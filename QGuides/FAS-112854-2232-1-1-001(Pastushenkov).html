What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course is absolutely incredible if you're looking for a better understanding of Russian culture or politics. Note that there is less emphasis on grammar, so if that's a weak point that you're hoping to work on, this may not be the best course for you. With that said, Russian students looking to bolster their listening comprehension, slang/pop–culture vocabulary, or exposure to Russian cultural staples should take this course.
This is a wonderful course with a great professor. It's one of the two Russian classes I've taken at Harvard that taught me relevant, useful, and current Russian language and culture (the other was with a visiting professor). The class is tailored to student interests and has a fantastic syllabus of popular culture materials mixed with analysis. I'd highly recommend it to all students of Russian (and anyone who speaks Russian and wants a great class)! It strikes an excellent balance of having enough homework to keep you thinking outside of class, but no busywork and enough time to really examine new topics in depth instead of just memorizing vocab for a quiz and then forgetting it
This class was all about media, from YouTube interviews of Russian politicians to contemporary graphic novels in Russian. I enjoyed our honest discussions about the war in Ukraine and our weekly dissections of state and independent Russian news. I felt like this class was more about learning through media than learning through rote grammatical exercises. This is helpful in its own way, but even in 4th year Russian I wished for more grammatical emphasis, but this is my own opinion, since students had varying backgrounds and Russian levels in our class. The instructor is one of the best I have encountered at Harvard. He is kind, respectful, knowledge, and open minded about discussing the complications of modern Russian society. He makes everyone feel comfortable and included in class, and I couldnt ask for a better Russian teacher!
A must take course for anybody interested in studying Russia!
Highly recommend this class, and you will take away whatever you put into it!
This class will prepare you to excel in modern conversations with Russian speakers and help you to understand modern Russian culture rather than just Soviet or 90's culture.
