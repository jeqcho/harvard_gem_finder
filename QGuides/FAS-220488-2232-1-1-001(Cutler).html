What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
ECON 2925 is a worthwhile course with wonderful professors that have engaged in cutting edge research on the subject of health equity. They generously share their wealth of knowledge with the students each week during the research presentations. The course equips you with skills that will enhance your ability to deliver professional talks and write your research for future presentations.
Seminar–based course where papers are discussed and students/faculty present on early–stage (or completed) work related to health equity, broadly defined. Was fun to be in class and hear the discussions, and felt very informal (in a good, relaxing way).
