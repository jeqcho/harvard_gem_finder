I would like to tell future students that this course with Professor Jardine offers a rich and enlightening exploration of the diverse voices and experiences of women writers in the French literary tradition. I gained a deep appreciation for the distinctive perspectives and socio–historical contexts that shaped these writers' artistry as well as the importance of reading and writing to form a perspective on identity. Through engaging discussions, thought–provoking assignments, and Professor Jardine's expert guidance, students will develop a more nuanced understanding of the complex interplay between gender, identity, and the written word in 20th–century French literature. This class was an incredible opportunity to expand one's literary horizons but also an inspiring journey into the powerful world of women's voices and their enduring impact on the literary landscape. Thank you so, so much Professor Jardine for an incredible semester!
