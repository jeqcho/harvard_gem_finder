Comments
This class is a lot of work (~15 hours a week), but you can definitely learn a lot out of the course from fantastic lectures and sections. I believe both lectures and sections delivered the essential physics intuition very nicely.
This course was great. The homeworks definitely took a lot of time, but I had so much fun bonding with students in my cohort while doing the psets. The take home exam felt fair, and the content of the course was amazing. I always wanted to understand general relativity after having heard so much about it in popular science, and this course did a great job of exposing me to it. GR is really beautiful, and it's not just for cosmology or high energy theory; I really feel like I have a better understanding of gauge theories in general now, and the way I look at E&M, quantum geometry, and QFT in general is totally different than before I took this course. 10/10 would recommend.
GR under jafferis is a real struggle. He goes through material very fast, and by doing so he skips a lot of the derivations for the material that he covers in class. even so, jafferis is a genius and understands GR very well, so he always manages to answer questions well and straight to the point. Often times, he teaches the class as if he already assumes you know GR, so it really is difficult to try to keep up with him. I did not read the textbook unless absolutely necessary, and I felt that reading through the material beforehand would have been so so helpful.

the material is really really difficult, and anyone who comes into this class must be prepared to suffer to some extent. It will definitely not be a smooth sailing class. everything was going well until we hit black holes. after so much time spent on black holes, I still dont understand blackholes, and I think i never will. Learning about all the pop science stuff like wormholes and white holes and blablabla started to make me think that physics is a fake science. idk.

homeworks literally consume your soul, be prepared to suffer lmao. and alek is the best TF i've ever had hands down. hes a beast.
This class is definitely worth it!
Be prepared to put in a lot of time and learn a lot of physics!
