What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
There is a lot to know about Latin 2. Taking Latin 1 is incredibly helpful, and it is unlike learning any other language. Prior to this, I had no experience with anything like cases or declensions, but it is interesting to know some of the origins of English and other romance languages. Overall as far as fulfilling the language requirements go, this is a pretty good class to do so with. It really gives you an appreciation for the etymology and origins of other languages.
Go to office hours regularly throughout the semester, and keep on top of the vocab.
It’s kinda hard for a language class but that’s only because of the grammar, I feel like it was less intensive than other language classes and honestly a good way to fulfill the language requirement.
I went into Latin 1/2 with no experience at all, and was amazed at the progress from taking just these 2 courses. Both are very well structured and similar to each other in terms of homework/quizzes which will help you get into a weekly routine, and both are taught by Dr. Livingston, who is a very knowledgeable and enthusiastic teacher. The grammar/morphology could be tricky for me at first, but the homework and readings provide ample practice. Additionally, Dr. Livingston and my section leader were always open and quick to respond to my questions. Learning a new language is always an extremely rewarding experience, and I am glad I went out on a limb and chose Latin!
