What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I cannot recommend this class enough. Introduction to Archaeological Science manages to be both accessible and comprehensive. Weekly readings are grounded in both methodological overviews and fascinating case studies. Labs are brief and to the point, and you will be introduced to some of the most interesting labs on campus. Students who are not confident in your chemistry or biology, have no fear––Professor Warinner is excellent at breaking down complicated concepts, and, by the end of the semester, you will be able to read and comprehend a broad range of scientific papers.
This course also could not be more organized. The Canvas site is immaculate and the assignments are well–defined and well–paced. The readings and workload are both manageable and rewarding. Take this class!!!
I learned so much content and practical skills. The things archaeological science can do just blew my mind.
This class is wonderful! Tina is a great professor and the class is very well organized. There’s a fair amount of reading but it’s not crazy, and all the assignments are clearly described and pretty chill. Definitely would recommend!
