TAKE IT! Best decision I made this semester.
This is a wonderful introductory course for the Levatine dialect. It starts off with very basic vocabulary which I found very helpful and the weekly homework assignments are entertaining.
If you are able to take this class, I highly recommend it! The teaching staff is passionate and kind, and the lessons are interesting and enjoyable. This class was one of my favorite courses I have taken throughout all of my time at Harvard, and I am so grateful for the chance to have been a part of it. The work for the course is not "busy work" but is actually relevant and interesting, and can be tailored to fit your interests and goals. Meeting other students in the course was also really fun, and the classroom environment was consistently great! Teaching staff was very available, in office hours and over email, and supported us throughout the semester.
If you want to learn Levantine Arabic, do not hesitate and take this class. Richard's passion for and knowledge of language acquisition is unparalleled, and it is reflected in this course perfectly. Your confidence in speaking, listening, and learning Arabic will improve dramatically, with the coursework being carefully built around improving those three skills. Moreover, it is not nearly as time consuming as the MSA classes.
