This memorization was not interesting in any way, and I spent all night trying to memorize this info only to forget it immediately after the exam.

Ultimately, that stuff wasn't that big of an issue and a lot of the lectures were genuinely interesting. There is a wide range in how good the lecturers are from week to week, but some were great.
