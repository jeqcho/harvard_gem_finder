What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is an excellent survey of Afro–Latin American histories and a good discussion space to workshop ideas about those histories.
I would tell future students to take this class if they want to dive deep into how an art history or history can be radical, how it can undo and remake and care for narratives.
This is a fantastic course that is really at the cutting edge of scholarship. Be prepared to work hard – all of the readings raise complicated questions that you are expected to engage with in your weekly responses. It may also be useful to have some background in Afro–American or Afro–Latin studies going in, but that is not necessary!
