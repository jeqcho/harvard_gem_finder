Comments
The breadth/scope of this course is quite large, so students will definitely be more engaged/interested in some units over others. Overall, I think the workload is reasonable and does a good job assessing students' understanding of the material. The course improved throughout the semester, and lecturers were strong.
Try and avoid taking this class unless absolutely necessary
CB201 is a great class if you're enthusiastic about cell biology or are working in the field of cell bio. If this is not the case, given the nature of the very specific lectures, this class may not be as useful.
With a lot of restructuring, this course has the potential to be good.
I would tell future students that there is no point in going to lectures, that they should not expect the homework content to be covered, prepare to be embarrassed in front of the class because you don't know everything about a field you don't study and aren't even interested in. Don't take the class if you don't have to.
Try to just form a good study group and work together!

I wish I had taken BCMP 234 (Cellular Metabolism) instead of CB 201 to fulfill the course requirement for my program. I heard from my peers that BCMP 234 taught material that was far more relevant to my research interests, and I didn't know that it was a viable substitution for CB 201.
Expect lots of work, but overall rewarding.
