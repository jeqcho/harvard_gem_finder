What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this class, a gem! It is not very demanding and Gutierrez is such a supportive and knowledgeable professor. It was really fun to dedicate time during the week to speak in Spanish.
Take this class if you want to simultaneously develop your Spanish skills at a really advanced level and gain an introduction to the field of public health. The readings are very difficult and will take a lot of time (took me entire afternoons sometimes), but if you put in the time, you will gain a very nuanced perspective on how different conditions like AIDS and diabetes have affected Latin America. In addition, Profesora Gutiérrez is very flexible and supportive.
You will learn a lot in this class. The readings can be a bit complex and there are more assignments in this course than in other Spanish courses I have taken, but it's not too bad.
