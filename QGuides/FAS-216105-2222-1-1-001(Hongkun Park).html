I strongly recommend that you take this course if you are interested in learning more about broad concepts in science and technology. Hongkun gives fun and engaging lectures each week as well as creates a welcoming class environment. The presentations are voluntary, so you get to choose which ones you are particularly interested in. Overall, the class is enjoyable and you will become more aware of the science that affects your daily life.
This is a great freshman seminar to take if you are looking for low stress, and Hongkun is the best! I had no experience going into it, and it was great to be able to just absorb information about different science and tech topics without having to worry about retaining facts to be tested on. The assignments of the class were mainly low stakes presentations, and weekly readings which were also pretty optional. Would highly recommend!
