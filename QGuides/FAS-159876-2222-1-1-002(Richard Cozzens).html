What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
It's a good opportunity to learn a new language, as well as the cultural exchange you get along with it –– in addition, you get to meet awesome people who want to do the same.
A great way to continue your Arabic learning.
Prepare for a much larger workload than Arabic AA, especially in Cozzen's class.
This class is very enjoyable, supportive, and eye–opening, with special thanks to the great teaching team
Be prepared to learn a lot of vocabulary, but eventually –– rather quickly actually –– you will be introduced to ways (methods/patterns) that make vocabulary acquisition really interesting and much easier. Be prepared for daily homework and make sure to study your verb conjugations! I found it very helpful to repeat things back (say them out loud) whenever I did al–kitaab drills online. Doing that made my ear and pronunciation much better. Ustaadh Richard is awesome and you would be lucky to have him as your Professor.
You should definitely consider taking this class if you want to learn another language! Arabic is beautiful and although many say it is a very difficult language, you will get it if you put in the work. I really enjoyed going to class everyday and practicing speaking with my classmates. By the end of Arabic AB, you should be able to sustain conversations with people which is very impressive.
This class has a high level of daily workload (often an hour or more of homework per night) but that work really pays off and your Arabic will improve dramatically. For me that was very rewarding.
