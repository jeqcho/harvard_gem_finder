What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Very good class, informative and important
If you want to know about all health economics concepts (especially US focused), you should take this class.
Great survey course on health economics topics taught by a giant in the field. Covers a range of topics from health and development to insurance markets and competition. Requires a good amount of reading but assignments are interesting, fair, and overall the course was not as much work as I was expecting. Dr. Cutler is very available to students and obviously puts a lot of effort into teaching the course. The course does not require you to be an expert in economics already, but familiarity with microeconomic theory and econometrics will provide a good foundation to understand the topics discussed. Definitely recommend if you work or do research in health economics/health policy/health systems.
