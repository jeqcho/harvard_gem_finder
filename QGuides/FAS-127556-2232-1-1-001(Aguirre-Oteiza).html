Loved this course; highly recommend! I think the biggest strength of the course is that it is taught by Daniel who is a very knowledgeable professor who makes class engaging, encourages participation, and generates enthusiasm since he is very passionate for the subject matter. He really makes you excited to come to class and especially to be prepared with your thoughts on the poems since it is a very dynamic class where everyone gets the chance to speak and share comfortably. This was my first poetry class, and also my first poetry class in Spanish, and I believe that it was very accessible to me as I was provided with the necessary context and background required to read the anthology and formulate critical thoughts. There was a very interesting variety of authors and poems and I really enjoyed exposing myself to such works by choosing to take this class! I loved having the weekly freewheeling journal to be able to freely express my thoughts on the poems we read without worrying about a restricted format or structure. The final project (worked with a partner) is a translation exercise and it is similar to the freewheeling journal in that there is no restricted structure and you can focus on corroborating your choices effectively.
