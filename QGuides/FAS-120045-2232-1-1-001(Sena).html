What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
It's a lot of work but equal amounts of fun and gratification.
Classical Chinese with Dr. Sena was unforgettable and incredibly rewarding. You will learn how to interpret texts at the microscopic level, dissecting individual words and their grammatical functions; you will also learn how to interpret them at the macroscopic level, seeing how individual words, clauses, and paragraphs work together as functions of a greater whole. Indeed, working at varying levels of analysis and abstraction is absolutely essential for reading pre–modern texts in the Chinese tradition, and this course equips you with the tools to approach these texts as a critical, engaged reader.
If you want to study Chinese history or literature seriously, you should take it. The earlier, the better.
