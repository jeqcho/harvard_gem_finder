What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great class! Took it for my political theory requirement. Not a lot of assignments, only one lecture/section a week, time commitment relatively low compared to other classes.
Professor Unger is an extremely knowledgeable person and you have a lot to learn from his class. That said, you should be prepared to spend a few hours per week grappling with texts, and I would recommend going to every class so you can ask questions when necessary (this is a class where you really have to pay attention – if I tried to multitask I got behind). You have to be ready for a relatively impersonal and strict learning experience, but there’s a lot to gain if you engage with the content thoroughly.
