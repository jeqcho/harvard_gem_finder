This course fostered some of the most interesting and worthwhile discussion I have experienced on this campus. It has been my favorite class thus far and I would highly encourage people to take it.
This was without a doubt one of the best classes I took at Harvard. If you are at all interested in how Christianity effects ethical issues in todays world, you should take this class! Not a stressful class and super interesting readings and discussions. Professor Lockwood is also super cool and it seems like he really cares about the students in the class.
I highly recommend this class if you are looking for a space to learn more and have a space to discuss Christianity and important topics within Christian ethics like marriage and family, abortion, war and peace, feminism and gender, and the environment. This class is such a great opportunity to grow in your faith and speak openly about crucial issues that you rarely get the opportunity to speak about at Harvard. Everyone in the class is very kind and genuinely wants to learn more about Christian theology, morality, and ethics. Professor Lockwood is such a great moderator in the discussion and he always highlights key themes on the board. He is always willing to meet to discuss your papers and he helps you grow as a writer with very thorough, clear feedback. There are only weekly discussion posts and 2 essays for the whole semester. The second essay can also be an extension of you first one if you are passionate about the topic. This is definitely my favorite class that I have ever taken at Harvard, please take it if you are interested!!
