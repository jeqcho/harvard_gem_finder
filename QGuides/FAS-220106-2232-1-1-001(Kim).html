If you're at all interested about Asian American theater, this is the best class I could imagine for it. Ju Yon Kim is incredible, and provokes great discussion on material that is relevant, profound, and ultimately really interesting. Could not recommend this class harder enough!
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! I'm not even a Humanities concentrator, but this was the best course I've ever taken at Harvard. Ju Yon Kim is such a gift to mankind. I have never felt so seen in my life. I have cried so many tears because of this class and the emotional whiplash of the plays we read. This class will break you open and force you to acknowledge parts of yourself that you've kept closed off from the world for so long––especially if you take it with friends who are working on the same theater production as you that semester... This class was OUT of this world <3
This class is AMAZING; definitely try to take it if you want a great seminar course experience with wonderfully curated material and perhaps one of the best professors on the campus (Ju Yon Kim is always engaged, engaging, and incredibly empathetic and tuned in to the students)! You will be able to read, listen to, and watch so many diverse pieces of Asian American theater that cover an entire spectrum of different Asian and Asian American experiences and culturally or historically positioned narratives (as well as covering a lot of different media), and be pushed to really think critically about how these pieces are constructed and received by audiences. The class itself was also able to build a great community among the students by the end of the course.
YOU ABSOLUTELY MUST TAKE THIS CLASS! I absolutely loved it with all of my heart, read so many phenomenal materials, and learned a great deal. Would 100% recommend!
Interesting class for those interested in Asian American theater! It's also chill in terms of workload, and there is a creative component to assignments (e.g. rewriting a scene, restaging a scene). It's probably more an English class than a theater class (coursework mostly consists of readings, discussion posts, papers), but there is a significant portion of the class who is involved in theater more than English literature. Professor Kim is also super nice.
Definitely take this class! The workload is manageable (although a decent amount of reading), Professor Kim is great, and the grading is fair.
This course is so wonderful! I cannot recommend it enough if you have any interest in Asian American Theater! The plays are so enjoyable and the discussions are fruitful!
Take this class!!! You have to be prepared to do the readings or else you'll be kind of lost in class, but if you come prepared and ready to engage, it's a really really great class. Prof. Kim is AMAZING and a super sweet, understanding professor. I also know that this class is a first try and will be tweaked in the future which will make the class even more exciting!!
This is a fantastic class even if you are not super interested in theater!
