What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course is absolutely incredible––one of my favorites I have ever taken at Harvard. We explored so many forms of art: photography, painting, film, dance, performance art, theater, literature. Class was always engaging and welcoming and low–stress (just a group of people excitedly and happily talking about art!). I looked forward to class every time. We also took multiple trips to the Harvard Art Museums storage facility to view specific works in person, which was definitely a highlight of the class! Professor Stewart is one of the only professors who has genuinely blown my mind with his spontaneous analysis of the readings and art we looked at in class (I hope I can analyze stuff like that one day). He is also a very compassionate, caring, and understanding person. One day, before we watched a film for class, he got us all lunch and we just chatted for awhile about how we were all doing. I would take this class again if I could, and I highly recommend it (and any other class with Professor Stewart). He is a good human.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!!!!! Especially if you're from a STEM background!
This class was excellent! Definitely changed how I will walk through art galleries and encounter art/writing/film/photography in the future. It's given me lenses with which to look at the world that are really important and formative. Professor Stewart is amazing –– he has this incredible ability to drive conversation in such interesting directions and really made such profound points about the course materials. All in all, this just has been really fun. Everyone in the course is quite engaged and it's a really enjoyable experience.
