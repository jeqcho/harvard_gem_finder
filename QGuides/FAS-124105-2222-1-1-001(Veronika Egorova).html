This course is great. You will learn Russian while reading works by some great authors like Chekhov and Dostoevsky. I've long heard of Dostoevsky, but I've never read any work by him. Of course, it wasn't easy, but I really liked the reading. The reading by Chekhov was also very interesting. The film is good too. Something that we didn't have in Russian 101 was that in Russian 103 each student was partnered with a Russian student and we had five calls throughout the semester, which were very helpful for practicing the language and learning about the life of Russian people nowadays.
