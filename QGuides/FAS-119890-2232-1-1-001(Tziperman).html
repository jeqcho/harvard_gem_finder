What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is great! Professor Tziperman is an excellent lecturer and every class is interesting and engaging. The problem sets can be difficult but manageable by attending section and asking questions. I also took this course as an undergrad and Professor Tziperman made it clear that he was available to help me through what was, at times, some of the most challenging material I have learned. I would highly recommend this to any college senior in Physics (my background) or perhaps Applied Math who is considering climate science for graduate study. It especially went well having taken EPS 208 with Professor Kuang in the previous semester to get an introduction in geophysical fluid dynamics.
