I am so glad I took this class. At the same time, it required a lot of work: I put in three times as many hours in outside of class work than I originally anticipated. That generated a bit of stress, but also made the results very rewarding.
Start PSETs early
I would encourage them to fully use their resources for this class! This class is the most famous class at Harvard, and plenty of people often go in with high expectations that are often not met––I believe this is because, for beginners, CS50 is only rewarding if you put in the effort to talk to your TF at the end of section and attend tutorials/office hours.
Spring is quieter than fall, so if ou want more support then take this in the spring
CS50 is a very chaotic class, and while there is a lot of value in the concepts this can sometimes get lost in the sheer complexity of handling the range of assignments and commitments. Keep this in mind when choosing to enroll.
