Go to section, Katie is a very energetic TF who really understands the literature discussed in class. The assignments and workload are quite light given how much you work in the class. For anyone on the fence, I would incredibly recommend taking the class.

I absolutely recommend this class, it was a highlight of my semester and a highlight of my time at Harvard!
