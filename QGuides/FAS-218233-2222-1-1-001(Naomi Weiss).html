This is a really nice Gened if you are interested in theater. The readings are interesting, the lectures are engaging, and sections are as good as can be hoped for with a Gened. The assignments are not overly taxing—though there is both a final project and final exam, the project is due at the start of reading period and neither is too difficult.
Take this course for Professor Weiss. Not the easiest gen ed I've ever taken (a lot of reading) but definitely not the most difficult.
Such a great class! The readings are interesting and not too long and the assignments were all fun to complete.
This is a great introduction to Ancient Greek Tragedy and a good demonstration of why it is still important to study Greek tragedy!
You get out what your put in. The amount of reading is a bit of a time commitment.
This is a fantastic course! I really enjoyed this course and learned so much. Professor Weiss is an engaging lecturer, and the course covers a wide variety of modern adaptations. The workload is pretty consistent every week, though keep in mind that the major assignments are relatively back loaded if you're planning a busy semester. Would definitely recommend it!
This was a really enjoyable and illuminating class covering several influential Greek tragedies and some modern interpretations. I really enjoyed learning about how interpretation of the plays might have changed over time, and how they related to different social issues, or to the field of psychology.
Have fun with it!
This was a phenomenal course! If you like Greek tragedy, or even just stories in general, you will love the class. Engaging lectures by Prof. Weiss, intellectually–stimulating discussions with Emily (TF), and overall a great pairing of ancient Greek plays with diverse modern adaptations. Essays and final exam were very reasonable, and feedback provided is timely, informative, and helpful. Take this course, and you'll see how many of the themes covered truly span time and space.
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! it has a very doable workload with really nice grading, the lectures are FANTASTIC and the class community is really nice. i really liked my section and found that it was a great way to explore the texts more deeply. there are field trips, guest speakers, movies, and very interesting and short reading each week. it's a gem of a class!
