This was a really wonderful course—Claire is an extremely thoughtful and engaging teacher. The students who take this course are also very passionate and very engaged—everyone has always done the reading and workshop is always extremely fun and very helpful. The course starts with with a series of very interesting readings focusing on character, as well as short writing assignments each week. A couple weeks in, we start reading a couple student short stories a week and everyone writes two of their own. Claire always gives extremely detailed feedback and really pushes everyone to their best writing. She also always brings snacks to workshop!

People from all concentrations/experience levels, take this!
