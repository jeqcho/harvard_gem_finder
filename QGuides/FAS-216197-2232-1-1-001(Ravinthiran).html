What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this class no hesitation! I really loved my experience –– the work is manageable, the poetry is engaged and analyzed in a luminous manner by Professor Ravinthiran, and you'll get a much better sense of 20th century poetry after taking it.
If you care a lot about Elizabeth Bishop –– take this class. If you aren't willing to do a lot of intellectual work with a poem on your own time, especially for the papers, don't take this class. The seminars were enjoyable, but at times it felt like very little new intellectual work was happening during the class.
I really enjoy Professor Ravinthiran! He does a great job connecting with students in a way that makes the poems feel relevant to our own lives. I learned so much about Elizabeth Bishop's complicated life and her poetry. I'm very happy I took this class. You get what you put into the class. The reading is manageable, but you can make it far more meaningful if you really invest in the material and try to draw some conclusions about the poems before you come to class.

My only complaint is the structure of the class. A final paper worth 50% of a final grade is simply too much. However, Professor Ravinthiran is super accessible for office hours and is readily available for help. I wish I had had the opportunity to take more classes from him!
If you enjoy poetry and can write and you're looking for a relatively light–weight class to take, this is for you. If you aren't interested in poetry and aren't confident in your writing skills, this class may be painful for you.
An excellent class about an excellent poet taught by an excellent professor! Is it a challenge to discuss poetry for two hours straight? Yeah. But it's worth it for the Bishopy revelations.
Take this class!! Professor Ravinithran is absolutely amazing––– he's so knowledgeable and supportive. If you have the chance to take a class with him, especially this one (because who wouldn't want to take a seminar reading Elizabeth Bishop poems), take it!!
