My favorite class at Harvard so far. Provides students with insight into the world of economic research through various papers discussed in class, as well as a great experience writing a research paper. Great introduction to research for those interested in writing a thesis. Course staff was incredibly accessible and helpful throughout the process and Andrei puts students in touch with practically anyone to get additional feedback on papers. 10/10 can reccomend.
I absolutely loved this class! It's probably one of the best opportunities you will ever have if you are considering economics research or a thesis down the line. Shleifer is incredibly helpful when you are working on your paper, and the fact that you get to meet with him so often helps relieve any uncertainties about the process (especially if this is your first time writing an econ research paper!).
If you're at all interested in econ research, 100% apply for this class. The opportunity to write a research paper with such close guidance and investment from both Professor Shleifer and TF is one of a kind. Additionally, the students are all engaged with the material, which makes for interesting lectures and course discussion. And in addition to learning some of the hard skills associated with econ research, the lectures from both Professor Shleifer and selected guest speakers provide a comprehensive introduction to both the classic papers/concepts in political economy, as well as what is on the cutting edge of the field. This has been the true highlight of my time in the Harvard economics department, and I guarantee you that you will probably feel the same if you take this class!
This is a great class. If you are interested in academic economics, you should definitely take it. The course content itself is interesting but not spectacular. The biggest value comes from the extensive interactions you have with Prof. Schleifer, who makes himself widely available and spends a lot of time personally meeting with you and advising your final paper. The level of feedback and engagement with a professor is unlike any other class I've taken at Harvard.
This course really pushes you to take ownership over your work and research. It’s incredible self–driven but a lot of support is given along the way. Definitely don’t leave it all until the last minute and work closely with Professor Shleifer and Suproteem throughout the semester so you’re proud of your work product. It’s challenging but Professor Shleifer cares so much about all his students.
Great class. You'll learn a lot of interesting things about political econ, read some great papers, and get to spend a lot of one–on–one time with Prof Shleifer and Suproteem. Small number of students really adds to the experience.
Take it. It is the best class i've taken at Harvard.
Start working on your paper early! It's obvious, but will enhance your experience 10x.
Really great opportunity to pursue a research interest with support and guidance from an incredible professor. Professor Shleifer cares a ton about his students and it shows throughout the entire semester. Phenomenal, consistent feedback was a big part of what made this course great. The course helped develop my research skills in preparation for my thesis (even more than my junior tutorial).
Take this class if political economy is a possible senior thesis topic for you, but only take it if you are truly passionate about political economy. You're expected to write a 25–page paper which is really non–trivial for a semester's work (finding a good topic, finding good data, finding significant results – none of these are guaranteed). I liked that Andrei made the course meeting times (once a week, 2 hours) as chill as possible, and he really gave us maximum space to pursue our research interests while making sure to meet frequently to ensure we were on track. It's hard work, but the good part of it is, if you find something good, you would already have done significant legwork for your thesis.
Take this class. Andrei cares so much about students and their papers and will go to great lengths to help make sure your paper is as good as it can be. A truly unique opportunity
