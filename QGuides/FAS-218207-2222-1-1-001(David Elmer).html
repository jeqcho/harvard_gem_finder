take this class!!! professor elmer is awesome, very very helpful and kind in office hours. the class itself is really informative and i found myself thinking about what we were studying in class in my day to day life
Classics, Race, and Power is one of my favorite courses that I've taken at Harvard over the past three years. The topic is relevant, engaging, and fascinating. The reading assignments are quite interesting and engaging, and the graded assignments are very manageable. The guest speakers (and lunch afterwards with them) were amazing and made the course that much better. Elmer is a legend, and everyone should ideally take a class with him at least once before you graduate. Highly recommend the course – you'll come to love it in the same way I did.
If you are interested in the classics at all, even if you have no background, take this course to get a good sense of the field and literature
Also if you want to try something new and dive into a topic that is not heavily discussed within classics, take this course
Take this class! It's really a must given the conversations and shifts in the Classics discipline throughout the globe and I think any Classicist should take this course to understand the power dynamics and history of the discipline as they begin to engage with the ancient world through their coursework.
CLS–STDY 123 is a great class. You will learn a lot about the ways that the Classics have been appropriated by all sorts of interest groups throughout history, how ideas of race and gender are represented in the Classics, and more. The array of guest speakers is great, the readings are interesting and reasonable in length, and the assignments are spread out such that the course never feels like too much work. Professor Elmer really treats all of us collaborators, and there are lots of opportunities for discussion in class. There were also several class lunches and dinners at restaurants and Professor Elmer's home, and these were a great way to get to know others in the class and in some cases the guest speakers.
