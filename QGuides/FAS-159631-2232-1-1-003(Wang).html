BEFORE DECIDING TO TAKE THIS COURSE, evaluate if you will genuinely have the time to do well in the class. Chinese 120 is much, much easier than Chinese 130, both in speed and content. Chinese 130 content is very niche, and you likely won't use it if you're not planning to eventually work in a Humanities field in a Mandarin–speaking country. I personally only studied Mandarin here because I wanted to be able to communicate with family members and potentially survive alone in a Mandarin–speaking country (for studying abroad/interning purposes, etc.), and the content of Chinese 130 went way above anything I expect to ever use in those contexts. On the positive side, Chinese 130 fulfills the Humanities and Arts Divisional Distribution for any STEM concentrators :)
