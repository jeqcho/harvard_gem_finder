This year, the class was quite small, which resulted in a very nice learning environment. The material is also very interesting, and Prof. Mazur is great at teaching it. There is some algebra background from Math 122 that is definitely helpful to know before taking this class.
Subject and professor great. PSets a bit dry.
Math 124 is a good class to take to know number theory. This might sound like the most abstract and theoretical math course since it is literally about numbers, but there is a great deal to learn that can help with studies in other fields that are not obvious at all when you first look. But after the class, you will realize you learned an incredible amount of ideas that genuinely are different from other classes. It is almost like jumping into an entirely different area of learning, which is where the most learning can happen.
