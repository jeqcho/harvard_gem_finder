Great lectures and really interesting content! It can be a bit difficult it you don't know much about late imperial China, but you learn so much from the lectures.
Read the syllabus and ensure that you are interested in the topics covered. Attend classes during the add/drop period and ensure that the teaching style suits you.
Sometimes need to explore more to engage in the course
I took this class with absolutely no preparation or background: I had never taken a history class before, did not know Chinese, and knew very little about the history of China. However, I am so glad I took this class, I just had to do extra reading/ watch modules about the Chinese history of the time more generally and went to office hours when I had questions. If you are interested in history at all and willing to do a little extra work take this class!
This class is very interesting and provides you with a very solid understanding of the social fabric of pre–modern China. Prof. Szonyi is fantastic. He is extremely engaging and knowledgeable. His lectures are eye–opening and deal with many topics typically overlooked in history. His lectures draw from a wide breadth of disciplines, including historiography, archeology, visual studies, and economics. His analysis is consistently very nuanced. I strongly recommend the class to someone interested in understanding what life was actually like in pre–modern China.
This is a really fun class, you'll learn a lot without having to put in too much energy outside of class. Lectures are really engaging
This class is extremely disorganized: professor would always show up late, no reminders for assignments coming up, moving due dates, TF hired last minute and didn't even study Chinese history, would often not finish content in lectures, very obvious that Szonyi does not prioritize undergraduate teaching. The content was mildly interesting, but because it was social history, you learn extremely specific details (such as what violence looked like at the time) but lacked an overall understanding of Chinese political history.
