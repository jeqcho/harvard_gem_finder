What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
A thesis is a unique experience, I won't prescriptively recommend it to everyone. That being said, if you have any interest in doing one, I would generally say you just should. It is entirely unlike any classroom experience; you can closely interact with you advisor (and see their enthusiasm for the field, which sometimes doesn't shine through in typical lectures as much as it will when you are adjacent to their research area) and may go arbitrarily deep in any subfield of your choosing.
