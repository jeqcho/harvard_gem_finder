Hum 10 is a really unique experience and I don't regret taking it, but there are a few things I wish I had known before. 1) Getting good grades on the papers is really really difficult, much more difficult than you would expect. 2) I don't think it teaches you writing like expos does, even if expos might be much more annoying. 3) Reading that many books is actually pretty manageable, but you might find it disappointing that you consume them more than you have time to appreciate them. This is not necessarily a bad thing, because if you don't like a book, you don't have to spend more than a week on it, but it might also feel sad at times. 4) Your experience depends a lot on your seminar professor.
