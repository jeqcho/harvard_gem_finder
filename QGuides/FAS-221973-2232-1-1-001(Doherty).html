What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I encourage anybody who is interesting in magazine writing, journalism, and exploring new forms to take this class. Maggie is an extremely thoughtful, attentive, and present teacher—she is always available to chat, provide useful feedback, and talk about the ups and downs of writing. The community she fostered in the course made me a much better writer, reader, and editor. It also introduced me to the pragmatic tools needed to embark in the world of writing, though plenty of time is spent focusing on craft as well.
Take this class!! Out of four creative writing workshops I took at Harvard, this was my favorite. Maggie is brilliant and will help you grow so much as a writer, and she also facilitated a tight–knit community within the class. You'll read a lot of great magazine journalism and get really attached to your classmates' pieces and watching them develop.
This class was phenomenal. Maggie is an amazing instructor who guided our discussions with such grace and eloquence, and she offered incredible feedback on all our pieces every week. You read some incredible pieces and have rich discussions about what works and what doesn't. Also, there's nothing better than getting workshopped by 11 of your peers — a bit scary but SO helpful for my writing.
This class gives you great breadth of exposure to lots of non–fiction writing, as well a sneak–peek into the magazine world in terms of pitching, writing styles, and what they look for. I'd recommend you take the course if you'd like some encouragement in getting your work out there in the world :) This was the class I looked forward to every week!
This was one of the most fulfilling courses I've taken at Harvard. Maggie was great. My peers were great. Discussions were so thoughtful and insightful. The vibes were so good. If you're looking for a creative nonfic class, take this one. I've taken a number of poetry and fiction workshops and this class was different in ways that really made me grow.
