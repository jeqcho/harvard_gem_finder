What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Make sure that if you only have time to do some of the reading –– READ THE THEORY! You will take so much more from the course if you actually survey the theory, and it will give you the strongest possible grounding for moving forward in your study of literature.
You get as much out of this class as you put into it. I think the extra readings are very informative enjoyable & worth your free time if you have any
While English 97 may be a required course, it is truly a joy of a requirement. Professor Wilson introduces you to some of the most remarkable chapters in the history of literary theory, and each of her lessons equips you with a tool of literary criticism and interpretation that is exciting to behold and powerful to wield. Every class was a wonder to look forward to––I highly recommend this course! And do the readings: they're fun, interesting, and might just set you on the path that leads to an eventual thesis (if you're thinking of writing one).
This was a very manageable course. Detective fiction turned out to be a more varied genre than I previously anticipated. We were guided through the process of writing a research paper step by step and I felt I got a final product I could not have produced at the start of the course– growth! If you don't like detective fiction though.... beware... there will be a lot of it.
