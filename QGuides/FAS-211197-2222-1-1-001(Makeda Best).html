What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take it! If you're at all interested in a museum career or in curation, this course will provide some seriously invaluable hands on experience that no other course at Harvard offers.
Definitely take this course if you are interested in curation or museums as an institution as a whole. The knowledge and expertise that Makeda and Mary brought to this course was so special and I learned to expand my thinking beyond just art in a theoretical or academic sense and dive into the museum as an institution.
I'd tell future students to take this course if they want to engage in a deep, semester–long deep dive into the work that museums do and the politics/poetics of that work.
this is such a unique course, especially for those who want to pursue working in art museums (curatorial, exhibition design, collections management, etc.).
I would take some time to consider the requirements of this class before enrolling. While it was a good class for most of the semester (manageable and interesting readings, manageable assignments, lots of time in the galleries and doing experiential/hands–on classes), the final project for this class is ridiculous. It was also apparently shortened from past iterations, which was startling to hear. It was a ton of busywork that could have been condensed into a much shorter and more manageable project. Additionally, we did not receive the guidelines of the project until spring break– had I known how extensive it was going to be, I likely would not have enrolled in this class. These guidelines were unclear in places, some parts were left unexplained and up to interpretation, and other parts of it changed up to a week before the final project was due. If you are really passionate about museum curation and that kind of project excites you, go for it and take this class. If you are an undergrad with no art history experience and a latent interest in museum curation, this class probably is not for you. Seriously, the cool parts don't outweigh the final project. That was ridiculous for no reason.
