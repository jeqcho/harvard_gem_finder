This course was very interesting and provided a nice mixture of dancing and discussion of the cultural significance of the dance style. Jeffrey was so great and really encouraged us to dance without fear and fully immerse ourselves in the learning experience. The dancing was difficult but fulfilling, and the class really helped at expanding my dance vocabulary.
TAKE THIS COURSE Jeffrey is amazing. He is a bold personality when we are actually dancing and warming up in class––he will get you hyped and yell at you (with lots of tough love). Aside from the big energy when we are dancing, he is very gentle and kind and amazing to talk to. I had never done any dance class prior to this and could not have had a more fun, enthusiastic, and welcoming introduction. I feel more flexible and aware of my own body after this class. Learning about the history and meaning of koteba and the traditions of the region enriched the class and meaning of the dance greatly. We started each class with a discussion recapping that weeks discussion post which was nice. I still do the warm ups I learned in class throughout the week and in the gym. Jeffrey really encourages you to learn how to pick back up where you fell off and never be defeated when you cannot get a dance. You will get out what you put into this class and Jeffrey puts his all as well. The final project allowed us to put together what we learned and just have fun dancing with others in a group.
This class is a lot of physical work, but truly a gem besides the soreness and sweat.
Definitely take this class! It is not stressful at all and you gain so much insight and knowledge from the topics discussed. There is also a lot of physical activity involved so be prepared for that, but the instructor is very understanding if you are unable to participate sometimes and the physical activity does help your body feel better in the end. You also get to meet people you probably would have never crossed paths with which is also very exciting.
take it and keep an open mind
This class is a lot of cardio/exercise so BE PREPARED! Probably 65/35 split of exercise and warmup to actual dancing but if you're with your friends it's fun.
There will be times you will NOT be feeling it, but the mental challenge of conditioning the body we all found extremely rewarding. The emphasis on getting the movement rhythm really helped me progress and eventually flow into the movement better. He is quite intense but you can feel the passion behind his approach. Overall I am so glad I took this course and I feel like I learned a lot about body control, allowing myself to move outside of judgement, and the meaning of dance in certain practices.
This course changed me in a multitude of ways, but to summarize: my appreciation for dance and bodily movement as a form of resistance and freedom has multiplied tenfold. My body is stronger. My movements are more intentional. My love for dance has only blossomed even more. And I have no professional dance experience. This class was enlightening. You get to learn so much about the culture of Mali and tradition as a concept. This class was my Monday workout and my weekly de–stresser. It's a MUST TAKE. Jeffrey is unmatched. Come in with a good attitude and you'll have the time of your life!
