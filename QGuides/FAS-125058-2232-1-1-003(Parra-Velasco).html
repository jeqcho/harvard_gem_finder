What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
the course was easy for me because i took Spanish my whole life. i noticed that the class moves fast though, especially for the students who had only taken 1 spanish class prior to this one. in high school, we would spend weeks on just one piece of grammar (preterite vs. imperfect, reflexive verb conjugations, present perfect conjugations). in this class, we briefly went over them . be prepared to do a lot of practice outside of class if you have not taken spanish before.
This is a good class to take! A lot of it depends on how chill your individual prof is, but it isn't too hard to get an A if you attend class and turn in your assignments. The exams can feel like they aren't always the most straightforward, but they aren't that big of a percentage of your final grades, so it doesn't matter too much.
I would tell students that this course is hard. It is not an easy A unless you are super comfortable with your Spanish. You will learn a lot of Spanish, but the assignments feel largely unfair and are very harsh.
