This class is the most rewarding class I’ve taken. There’s nothing like the feeling of being able to sight read and understand ancient philosophical texts, and learn from Professor Sena. Only take it if you’re willing to put the effort in because your commitment to the coursework affects the classroom environment as well. The assignments are reasonable given your growing skillset over the term, the exams are good measures of mastery, and Professor Sena is gracious in his willingness to help at office hours.
Dr. Sena is phenomenal in every respect that I can count. If you're motivated to learn Literary Chinese for any reason, then you can't do better than this course.
You can get whatever you want out of this class— Professor Sena is so helpful and truly passionate. The discussion with peers is a wonderful place to grow with colleagues.
If you have a deep interest in Classical Chinese, this course is a great way to do it.
