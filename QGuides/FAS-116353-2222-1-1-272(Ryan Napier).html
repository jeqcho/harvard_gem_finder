Austen & Us is an absolute gem of an Expos! During the fall, many of my friends complained about the workload of Expos and how boring the average class could be. I never felt this way with Dr. Napier. Although the class gets sort of slow by the end (since everyone is working on their own research projects, as opposed to reading one book or watching two movies), I never really found myself wishing for class to be over (or even looking at the clock). Dr. Napier really fostered a fun and engaging learning environment, so while I felt like I was learning, I didn't feel like we were taking ourselves too seriously. Dr. Napier is incredibly understanding about extensions and is frequently available out of class for questions and discussions. Even if reading Jane Austen doesn't relate to your field of concentration or you're worried about literary analysis, I strongly recommend taking this course! You have to take Expos one way or another and doing it with Dr. Napier is the best.
I was dreading Expos but I genuinely looked forward to each section!! The workload was reasonable and I felt I gained something from each class. Dr. Napier’s feedback is really thorough and thoughtful; the entire class feels really intentional.
