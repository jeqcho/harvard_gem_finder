Comments
I knew replicas and other stat phys techniques, but this course helps me deeply understand what they are doing, with a specific concentration on spiked SK model (or MLE or Bayesian estimator in stat view point) for a long time in this class. Now I am confident in reading many replica–related machine–learning papers in recent days, which is a great gain for me. Also, as an advanced course, the workload after class is not too much. Thanks the course staffs for this wonderful course!
Please take it if you are interested in statistical physics but be prepared to spend a lot of time outside of class to grasp the material covered. Not that it is important for problem sets, though.
You must take this course if it is offered. The content is too interesting to not be exposed to, and I think it has entirely shaped my view of what I want to study in the future. There were only 3 psets, and they were each only 2 questions, but they were a good deal harder than anything from another 21x class. My understand of how this differs from the physics version of this class is that it has considerably more statistical intuition.
