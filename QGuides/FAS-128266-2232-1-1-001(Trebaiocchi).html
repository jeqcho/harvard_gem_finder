TAKE THIS CLASS! It's the perfect fourth class because it isn't too intense and its really fun to be in a smaller class.
Take it! It improves your Italian, is much less work than Italian 20 (although be prepared to do some work outside of class every week), and the topic is very relevant to your lives.
This class is not only an effective Italian class and great follow–up to Italian 20, but it is a fascinating and engaging course in sustainability, environmental issues, and Italian culture. The class is much better than Italian 20, with less work, less demanding exams, more projects/group work, and globally relevant assignments that required creativity and were super interesting (such as visiting a farmer's marker!). Definitely take this class!!
This class was great in terms of learning about sustainability in Italy and practicing the skills we learned in previous Italian classes here. If you aren't confident in your Italian, I would probably recommend taking an earlier class in the sequence because this class does not discuss grammar or conjugations or anything, you are just expected to know and correct yourself as you go. In that sense it's more challenging because you need to draw on previous semesters a bit more, without relying on a review at the beginning. It's worth taking!
If you want to continue your Italian journey, take this class! Much more manageable than some of the earlier classes & has a very interesting subject matter that is very relevant to our daily lives.
