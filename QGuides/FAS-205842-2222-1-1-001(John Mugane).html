TAKE THIS CLASS! Taiwo is an amazing instructor who will put your needs as a student above all else. The class is fascinating and is equally about the culture of West Africa (which I had very little knowledge of prior to the class) as it is about West African pidgin itself. The assignments are fun and not overly difficult, but also useful. I recommend taking this class with a friend or two as it makes the group assignments/projects really enjoyable. Overall, taking West African Pidgin (more affectionately known as WAP) was the best decision of my freshman year, hands down.
