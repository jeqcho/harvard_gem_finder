PLEASE take it. I'm a psychology concentrator and took it on the recommendation of a friend – best decision ever. Professor Madigan is the sweetest ever and does an incredible job of teaching a very dark time in human history. As someone who have zero prior knowledge, I was still able to understand the material and get a good grade. You will learn so much about the Holocaust – things that will genuinely shock you and make you wonder why you never got taught it in high school. Couldn't recommend it more.
This was a really great course if you are interesting in delving into the details about what happened during the Holocaust, how we think about each of the different groups of people involved, and historical debates over what happened. Professor Madigan and Michael are both incredibly smart and knowledgeable on this topic and I really loved how they got to know everybody in the class so well. This class has a light work load besides the final paper but the readings and movies assigned are great and you should definitely spend the time reading/watching them to get the most out of the class. Overall, if you are interested in history and how we understand the Holocaust in particular I highly recommend this class.
HIGHLY RECOMMEND. This is one of the most impactful classes I have ever taken. This class isn't just about the Holocaust – it is also about the lead–up, fallout, and relevance to other instances of injustice. The books and movies are really well–selected and will stick with you for a long time. Professor Madigan is thoughtful, awesome, and extremely kind. If you do take this class, make sure to speak up and participate in section because it's worth a good portion of your grade.
This is an amazing course. Professor Madigan is extremely knowledgeable about the material and he brings in experts when the class reviews certain materials that are really engaging. Work load is not difficult and the exam is very fair. There will be a new TF for this course but I'm sure they will be great!
Interesting content that is applicable outside of the Holocaust and low work load made for a great class. Highly recommend if looking for a low stress class to fulfill the gened requirement
Take. This. Class. It's important for everyone to learn about the Holocaust, and this is the best opportunity you'll ever get to do so.
Definitely take this class– Professor Madigan and the course staff are excellent and will make sure that you have everything you need to succeed. This is a great class to explore a very specific topic in a meaningful and fulfilling way, and if you're like me and figured you'd hate a history class– take it, this class was definitely one of my favorites this semester!
