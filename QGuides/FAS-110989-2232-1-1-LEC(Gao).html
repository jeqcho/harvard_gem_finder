Do not take the class if you do not need to. Do not take the class if you will not make your life surround this class. This class is VERY demanding, and it is VERY challenging. I hadn't taken math since junior year of high school and I struggled A LOT. It moves very quickly. But if you're willing to actually put in HOURS and HOURS of work to do well on psets, go to office hours, understand the content, I think it's a very good learning opportunity. But if you have a lot on your plate during the semester (like I did), then this will definitely be a struggle and at least for me, take a hit on your grades. I haven't felt more unprepared in my life to do the midterms/exams and I hadn't done worse in my life. This class is humbling as heck! There are some really smart people in this class that will make you feel dumb, but just know that it's because they probably had a different math background, something that set them up for success. But seriously, don't take this class if you don't need to, and please plan your semester so that you are given time to do well. I was doing 7 psets a week and also in two–preprofessional clubs while enrolled in this course and it was actually one of the hardest things I've ever experienced in my life. don't do that. Don't hurt yourself like I did!
