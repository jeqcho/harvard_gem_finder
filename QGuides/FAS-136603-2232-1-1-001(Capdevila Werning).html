Just read the materials and discuss.
If you are looking for a philosophy or feminism course, TAKE THIS COURSE!!!! I accidently stumbled upon this course and I LOVED IT and I am so glad that I did. The professor, Remei, is a wonderful human who wants nothing but the best for everyone. She has a true passion for the subject matter and teaches it in an engaging way. The class is very low stress and low stakes (many of the assignments are completion based) so the only true grade you receive are your papers and they are fairly straightforward. I look this as a sixth class and highly recommend it. SO TAKE THIS CLASS!!!
Obviously if you are interested in feminism this class will probably appeal to you. It is more philosophy than I thought it was, but I ended up finding it interesting having never really been exposed to philosophy.
This class is interesting at times and not super difficult, but I did find sitting in class boring sometimes since the slide shows and discussions of readings were not always super engaging. However, if you do the readings carefully and participate a lot, you can change the vibe of the classroom and make it more dynamic!
Incredible class!!! Amazing choice of topics, readings, and class discussions. A very applicable philosophy class –– I learned so much and it had the right amount of work.
