Whether or not you are interested in sports, take this class. It will make you a better writer, strengthen your powers of observation, and teach you about life as a professional writer. The small class environment is very engaging.
I had so, so much fun in this course and learned a ton about sports journalism, journalism as a whole, and writing in general. Louisa is such a wonderful instructor! She clearly cares so deeply about all of her students — she provided immense amounts of feedback on my assignments and was always willing to chat or email with me whenever I had any questions. She made the class a super fun, supportive learning environment. She also brought in all sorts of wonderful guest speakers. As an avid sports fan myself, I found the readings super fun. I also enjoyed doing the assignments, especially since they were so different from most of my other work (and about topics I found more interesting).
Take this course! Louisa is an unbelievably kind professor who brings amazing guests to class, provides thoughtful feedback on writing assignments, and encourages you to explore your interests as a writer.
this course is exceptional. Great environment, great teacher in Louisa, and truly helps you read in a different way by teaching the formal techniques of outstanding sports and games pieces. Good job of variety and so much leniency to write about what you would like.
Take the course for a fun time where you get out what you put in – and you'll want to put in a lot, since the professor is amazing. :)
Take this class!! Louisa is absolutely amazing as a writer, editor, discussion leader, and teacher (in the most literal sense of the word). Course readings are valuable and the class offers amazing opportunities to learn from a fantastic writer and to speak to many other writers with varied writing careers at varying points in their journey. I learned so much from each of the writing exercises/assignments. Coming to class each week was joyful – such a fun group to be a part of. Cannot recommend this class highly enough.
Professor Thomas is a phenomenal teacher – for anyone with even the remotest interest in becoming a sports writer (or even just a writer), TAKE THIS CLASS!

Again, could not recommend this course more highly to anyone thinking about taking it!!
