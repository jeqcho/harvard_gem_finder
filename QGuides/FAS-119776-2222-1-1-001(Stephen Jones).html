This is a wonderful opportunity to take a class on a very understudied region with one of the leading experts on it. The course covers critical and timely themes and includes multiple guest speakers with a wide variety of perspectives, which help provide a very nuanced understanding of a complex region
If you are interested in the region, want to travel/work there, or want to learn more about post–Soviet transformations, this is a course I would recommend. Professor Jones also provides excellent feedback on assignments, that helps you improve throughout the course.
Given the number of assignments, it might be good to consider how you can balance the workload considering other courses you are taking. Overall, I am delighted that I took this course.
