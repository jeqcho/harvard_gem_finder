What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Prepares you very well to write your senior thesis
This class is so incredibly helpful for if you are planning to write a senior thesis! Although at first the 25–page paper may seem daunting, the assignments you complete throughout the semester really set you up well to write the paper, and ultimately you end up producing a very through and well–researched piece of academic writing that you can build upon, if you so choose, for the senior thesis.
This class is required, so you'll have to take it as a History of Science student, but the instruction is great. The expectations for assignments is clear and the support available from the teaching staff is wonderful. I always felt like I was being set up for success and had the tools necessary to complete the various portions of the research and writing process. Overall, this course felt worthwhile and beneficial to my education. If you stay on top of the deadlines and ask for help when you need it, you'll be in good shape!
25 pages is no joke so put maximum effort on all the preliminary assignments so that the transition from the rough draft to final paper is smooth and you feel prepared tackling the remaining 10–15 pages. Definitely reach out to your TF at any point for assistance; Iman was so helpful when I was identifying sources and developing my argument so I'd say she was a great resource for support. This class is not easy since it's a lot of writing but try to pace yourself with everything, it's definitely doable. Good luck!
If you have never written such a long pice of work then this course is great because all the work you do is gearing up for this paper. Especially having the rough draft due earlier helps so that you don't procrastinate the work and can really be workshopped in depth.
