This class is very much a “get out what you put in” kind of place. You could coast and baloney your way through a semester and end up with nothing to show for it at the end…OR you could invest in your cohort and take advantage of the awesome advice and resources and get a huge leap forward on your company. Treat it and give it time like you would that thesis or hard CS course and RAD will help your company in ways you wouldn’t expect and help develop you as a founder.
Take one of the other courses on startups offered either at the college, MIT or HBS. You will learn more and not have to undergo useless busy work assigned by a bully. Simply useless.
You get as much as you put into it. You can stay engaged and learn a lot from Paul and your classmates or you can work independently on your startup. either way, this class was a great experience with a lot of perks bedcause of a phenomenal teaching staff!
Take it if you're looking to further sink into the Harvard startup milieu and want some dedicated time each week to work on your startup, facilitated by solid peer accountability.
If you have a startup, take this course.
If you're genuinely committed to spending your time on your startup, take this.
If you're working on a startups seriously and want to write it off for credit this is an excellent choice
