What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The course was well–intentioned but often felt like it had no direction or overarching theme (we were supposed to be studying phenology but never properly learned what that meant). The assignments were graded FAR too harshly though, especially given that we had very little direction on them and got virtually no feedback.
Take all comments with a grain of caution as this is the course's first iteration. I came into Hum93 excited to continue on the fantastic experience I enjoyed in Hum90; unfortunately, not only did the course connect poorly with its predecessor but it also did not appear as a fully formed course. While many of the weeks were enjoyable and taught valuable lessons in how to look at the world in novel ways, the course did not have an arch with central take aways. The assignments were vague and rarely did we receive feedback. Engaging with the other students is the best component. I hope that the next cohort receives a more complete course.
People are going to complain about this class because of the way in which expectations for assignments were not clearly communicated and work was often returned without any comments. However, the course material itself is really engaging, especially if you are interested in the subject matter, and the professors are both great. The workload was very manageable and office hours was a great opportunity to learn more from the professors. HUM 93 is one of those courses you will get nothing out of if you do invest time in thinking critically about the material. I recommend it, but you need to make an effort for it to be meaningful.
Although I enjoyed HUM90 more, this second course is a great way to continue lively discussions with the same friends from HUM90. Lots of space for creative exploration. Is it worth to take it for the Mahindra Scholar name? If you're deciding between this and another class, take the other class. If you have an empty spot, take this class.
I loved HUM 90, but I found HUM 93 to be dissapointing. It wasn't that big of a time suck, so although I found the content to be not very interesting, I wasn't particularly bothered by the course because it wasn't particularly demanding.
