The nature of the language is pretty difficult but the teaching staff does its best to try to help you succeed. The most amazing teaching staff with the greatest personalities, resources, and eagerness to really want you to succeed, that I really have not seen elsewhere at this school. I learned so much Chinese in every sense of the term – reading, writing, speaking, culture, etc., while not feeling that the course was too overwhelming in terms of work. Of course, with any other rewarding class, you should put in the work to study, complete your homework, and prepare for the constant barrage of dictations, but at the end of the day, these teachers want you to succeed, so just be sure to get support if you feel that you are struggling. One of the best courses I have taken thus far, and inspired me to continue further!
