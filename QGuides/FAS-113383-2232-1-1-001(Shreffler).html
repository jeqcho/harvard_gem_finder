What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
**Really great class! Super manageable and accessible no matter your concentration. Don't need to know all the scary intense music theory stuff (but if you do you can def incorporate it into discussions and responses). Weekly discussion posts were low–stress and everything else worked toward your final project for the end of the sem so it didn't feel like a major ordeal.
If students love considering diverse approaches to making music and a way to learn about the ways in which culture has shifted (since 1945) through a creative lens this is an excellent course to take
This class is not a big time commitment and is useful for navigating significant pieces of work and composers of the past 70 years.
