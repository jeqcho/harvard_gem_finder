What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great course with real–world applications; definitely take it if you get the opportunity.
Make sure you choose the right project. Sometimes, the subject matter matter less than the methods; I was assigned into a project in a subject matter I had zero interest in, but the methods used (NLP for non–language sequence data) ended up being very relevant to my interests and I learned much more than I had expected. Overall really fun class if you find the right project and team members.
Keep an open mind, ask questions when in doubt and be forthright in your communication – this will save you a lot of time and hassle when working on the project
Be sure to talk about the work assignment as the first task in the first meeting, so that everyone can get an even amount of work, and later on there would not be someone who is self–motivated and claims they have done more work than the rest.
I loved this course. So exciting, pretty low stakes, not much stress.
Establish a good rapport with your team. When the project felt overwhelming, it was nice to know I had support from the people I was working with. Also, keep your expectations low for what you'll actually be able to accomplish in the time, since it's truly a very short amount of time.
