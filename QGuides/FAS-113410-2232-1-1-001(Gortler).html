It's a pretty straightforward class. A surprisingly high number of psets that aren't too exciting, but it's about what you'd expect in terms of content.
If you enjoy programming and debugging, this class is absolutely the class for you! Shlomo is an awesome, entertaining lecturer, and the PSETs are really fun (and satisfying) to complete. The grade breakdown is through PSETs and a final project (no exams!), and I found the grading to be overall fair. The time this course takes can vary greatly depending on the student, but I personally loved saving the weekly PSET for a day when I needed a break from my other classes.
