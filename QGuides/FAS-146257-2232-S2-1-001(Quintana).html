What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
You have to prepare two papers per class and comment/criticize them. They are interesting but very complex papers. If you don't have a background in experimental biology and immunity, it will be a challenging class. The discussions in class are very high level, which I enjoyed, but also saw many lost faces. This is not an easy class to take lightly.
Take this class only if you have at least an intermediate understanding of immunology.
