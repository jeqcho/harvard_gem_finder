Take this class if you want to find out how Russian literature fights back against the neo–imperialist society that faces Russia today.
Take this course! Even if you have only an inkling of an interest in contemporary Russian culture/media, this is an amazingly fruitful class. Professor Sandler is wonderful and will help you gain a ton from the material, even if you don't understand anything the first time you read it. The class covers some sensitive topics (especially given the ongoing war in Ukraine), but framed by that war you get to cover a lot of really powerful and interesting material by Russian dissidents and intellectuals. Loved this class, was an amazing way to close out my senior year.
Professor Sandler and Anna (if Anna still happens to be the TF) are both WONDERFUL. I would highly recommend taking this course solely for them. But beyond that, the movies and readings are all incredibly interesting, especially later in the course. The discussions have been very fun and engaging, and there is generally ample time to talk over paper topics if you want to.
An amazing class!
