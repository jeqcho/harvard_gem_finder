Take any class by Harris!
This is a very exciting course that will teach you many things about algebraic curves specifically, but also many other topics in algebraic geometry in general, as well as important techniques that you can apply to various aspects of math.

Definitely take this course if offered, or any other class with Professor Harris. He is a spectacular, very charismatic lecturer with an unparalleled understanding of the material, including its conception and valuable insights into its application.
