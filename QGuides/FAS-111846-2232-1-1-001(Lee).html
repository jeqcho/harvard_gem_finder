This class is basically identical in structure and difficulty to 130A, so you probably already understand the workload, etc. The added projects and presentations are really fun and you will definitely improve a lot (just like with the previous classes). Be warned it is a pretty time–intensive class and there are a lot of small assignments, so you never really get a break from the class, but they are not too long or terrible, just constant. Overall amazing course staff and really a great experience, especially if you take it with the right cohort!
