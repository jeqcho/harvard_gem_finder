Spend a good amount of time on your papers. Also, Elizabeth is a great TF!
Do not stress about having quantitative skills going into the class– I had none, yet did well because I tapped into the many available resources (for example, the Harvard library offers incredibly helpful research consultations). Make sure to choose a topic that you're interested in, at least skim the readings, and take the early assignments seriously. If you put in the work early, the end of the semester will be a breeze. Trust me. Put in the work on that pre–prospectus!
This course is a great opportunity to learn about research as a social scientist. Specifically, the section on Inequality and the American Welfare State with Elizabeth Thom opened my eyes drastically to the true state of America’s inequality and the hidden welfare state.
