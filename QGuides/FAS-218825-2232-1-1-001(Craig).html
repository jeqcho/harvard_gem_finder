Be prepared to experience intellectual transformation. This course challenges assumptions about race, institutions, and social relations that we often take for granted on a daily basis. It requires being able to sit with uncomfortable truths, confrontations with privilege, and an open–mindedness to alternative wonders beyond the worlds of settler colonialism and capitalism. I highly recommend this course to all students of all disciplines.
For me, this class was simply the best. I learned so much from Seokweon and Eleanor (material and more importantly ways of thinking that I don't think I could have taught myself) and also from other students in class. I think this is one of those classes at Harvard where you can see how deeply thoughtful and smart your peers are and learn from them. Section was amazing and productive and the essays (not stressful or punishing felt very rewarding and cathartic. The readings could be very specific at times, but that's actually a part of the course I really enjoyed because I became knowledgeable about different ethnic issues I otherwise would not have. Thank you Eleanor and Seokweon!!!

The essays are surprisingly difficult and I didn't do great on them, but the class experience itself made it all worth it!

As a STEM student, I learned a ton in this course and would recommend you take it if you are even a little bit interested in these topics!
TAKE THIS CLASS!! YOU WILL NOT REGRET IT!

As an Asian American, I came into this class with some understanding of my identity and Asian American history, but I've honestly learned so much—not only about myself but about other Asian communities and Pacific Islanders. Eleanor and Seokweon are incredible, extremely knowledgeable, and empathetic people who truly want the best for you. They put an emphasis on learning and vulnerability that I haven't seen anywhere else at Harvard. I wish that every class was like this. Honestly, I feel like everyone needs to take a class like this both because of the incredible learning environment that Eleanor and Seokweon provide but also because of simply how much we learn in this class.
This has been one of the most meaningful classes I've ever taken here at Harvard.
