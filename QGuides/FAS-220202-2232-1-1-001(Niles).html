What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is great if you are interested in the Tudors (as the title suggests). We studied both the original texts and modern adaptations, and watched movie clips alongside reading. We also had two Houghton visits and did a bit of paleography, which was super cool and interesting. Dr. Niles is really nice and willing to read over drafts or brainstorm ideas. This course may be a bit dense content wise, especially for someone who is not familiar with Shakespearean English. And there definitely are so many things I missed out in the readings because I cannot fully understand the language. Overall this was a good class, definitely challenging when it comes to reading materials but really interesting!
This is a great class! You are able to pursue parts of the Tudor period that interest you the most. For instance, if you are more inclined to history versus literature, you are able to tailor your assignments to that. I love the flexibility of this class. Professor Niles is a great teacher, and I have really enjoyed the readings. I definitely recommend this class! You'll learn a lot and it's a relatively low stress, which makes the course even more enjoyable!
This is a great course but requires dedication in terms of reading and understanding large selections of older texts
This is a cool and relatively chill english class with a great prof. Assignments could be better and it's worthwhile meeting with Prof Niles ahead of time because guidelines aren't published.
