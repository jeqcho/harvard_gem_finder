The course staff has seemed to improve this course significantly since it was first offered. The TFs were amazing and Brendan is extremely enthusiastic about what he is teaching. They are very accommodating and use section to help us work through homework and other things we learn in lecture. If you are scared of all the other CS courses at Harvard, this is a great one to take because of how great the course staff is.
Take this course if you're intimidated by coding! It is extremely accessible and beginner friendly!
This class is for you if have no experience coding and are looking to satisfy the QRD/Divisional Distribution requirements, but I did not learn much. There are some kinks in the flow of the class. But, this might be because it is a new course. The first half of the course was basic Python and the back was entirely AM105 material. Very disjointed, and I did not learn much in the back half. The workload/psets were easy though, and I got a lot of good feedback ***
God i loved this course. The lectures were engaging, the psets are VERY fair. The TFs care so much, and the prof is amazing. The prof had so much enthusiasm for comp sci and data science! It is definitely a huge improvement from the previous year (according to the Q guide comments i read). I def recommend this class!
Solid class, very beginner friendly. Highly recommend to take, it's very approachable and a great way to learn some programming and data science especially for those that don't want to take a straight up CS class.
If you need your CS requirement as an engineer, take this course over AM10 or CS50 for sure!!! Brendan is such a sweetheart and his lectures are hilarious & captivating, and the teaching staff was also very dedicated to helping out and making sure we understood the material. There were multiple times on weekends or etc. when I was struggling with some bug in my code, and the TFs responded to my email with helpful information within a couple hours!!! The psets were well–formatted (maybe a bit tedious at times) and definitely helped reinforce the Python knowledge we learned in class. My Python skills have definitely gotten stronger as a result (and for engineers, the course does give you a chance to practice using numpy, pandas, scipy, and odeint), and I really enjoyed learning in lecture as well. I would definitely recommend this course!!!
Great Course!!
