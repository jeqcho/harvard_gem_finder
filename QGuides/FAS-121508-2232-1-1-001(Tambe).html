Not only do you get to directly experience making an impact aligned with your partner org's mission, but the guest speaker lectures and workshops in this course will inspire you with perspectives and stories about prior and current AI for social good being done. Prof. Tambe is incredible, the TFs care so much, and you'll be surrounded with like–minded peers :)

This class is likely one of the most realistic and relevant CS courses you can take at Harvard. It teaches you how to work as a team, work with a third–party client, and how to manage ambiguous expectations and deadlines. I would recommend taking it to gain all of the aforementioned skills. I would be wary of treating this course as one that *teaches* you machine learning however, as that was not covered.
