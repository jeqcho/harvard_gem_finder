This course was a great addition to my busy schedule because the workload was light and enjoyable. I got to practice thinking about art and storytelling in a different way. One thing that I think was unique about this course is that I felt quite connected to the other students in the class; there are often chances to be vulnerable or share personal things about yourself if you so choose, and the overall environment of the classroom was inviting and wonderful.

Our class was unorganized, and often felt as if we were meeting for no point. The assignments were often unrelated to what our course was supposed to be covering. For a pass/fail creative writing class, I wasn't expecting to have such strong feelings about this course. Most importantly, this class did NOT foster my love of writing. It fostered a hostile environment in which I felt as if I never want to write again.
