Arabic BA/BB is definitely a challenge. I came into it knowing that a lot of people drop out of the course partway through — but it wasn’t as bad as expected. My advice is to go to office hours as much as possible (just build it into your schedule) so that you can practice speaking each week and discuss the feedback you get on the longer essays. I loved this course, it is the best part of my schedule this semester, I only wish I had partnered with other students more outside of class to practice speaking. If you have the chance to take Arabic with Ustaadh Madi, you are very lucky!
Keep up with the daily homework!
Please take this class. I have really loved Intermediate Arabic at Harvard. Prof. Amr Madi is the best person that you will ever meet. First, his humor in class, and his very sweet Egyptian accent, while he is speaking, will always be in my heart. His attention to detail and willingness to assist always is sth that should draw you to this class. Also, the infusion of dialects into the class, Egyptian and Levantine, spiced up things in this class for the better and exponentially improved our Arabic.
