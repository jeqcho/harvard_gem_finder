Ran is an excellent professor who truly cares about his students and their progression in Modern Hebrew. He is always willing to meet with you outside of class and talk about your interests. He looks to connect with you not only as a student but as a human, making you feel comfortable in class and ready to learn everyday.
The class is really great. Morah Ran is there to work with you, and he is incredible. The class does not necessarily teach all the skills (it assumes some basic Hebrew knowledge), but you will figure it out and be fine! it's really great. Parts of the course could be improved, but Ran will be teaching it again and will be improving it.
This class is made by the person who teaches it. Ran is great, passionate, and accessible, but is new at teaching Hebrew Language. Hopefully this will change with time, but I don't think he yet has a perfected intuition for how people learn and what is reasonable to expect of students in learning a new langugae.
