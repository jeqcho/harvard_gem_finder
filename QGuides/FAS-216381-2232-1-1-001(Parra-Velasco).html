What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is a mix of many students with all different approaches and needs. It can be difficult to then weave all conversations together (some students more interested in language pedagogy, other in pedagogy overall, and some other in cultural aspects of teaching a course). Professors Greene and Parra provided useful feedback in assignments. However, I wish the head courses take more leadership in the course, and give it shape and form to conversations more consistently. They rely quite heavily on their TFs, who were incredibly resourceful, but lack of leadership in the course was evident. Future versions of this course may become better, but it'd be hard to say now that Dr. Greene is retiring.
What you bring to this class matters––the better you prepare, the more you get out of it.
