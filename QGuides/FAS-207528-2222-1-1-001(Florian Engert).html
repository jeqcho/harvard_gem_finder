What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

If you have a neuro/MCB requirement, take this course. Florian generates lots of enthusiasm in his weekly lectures, Monica and Hanna host great sections, and Roy will help you with any coding problem you may have. On a whole, the course staff is very accessible outside of class.

The MATLAB assignments may seem slightly daunting after the first few weeks, but Roy is an extremely valuable resource–if you have a question, ask him for help. Attending the weekly coding session is another resource I recommend taking advantage of.

The sections begin with a brief quiz to ensure that you read that week's paper(s). The quizzes are normally less than three questions.

The final is very reasonable and the final project is driven by your own interest.
Absolute must have in your schedule. Florian and the TFs Hanna, Monica, and Roy make this small class educational while being low pressure and fun. I can't stop telling people about the things I learned in this course (Marr's 3 levels of understanding, desert ants, etc) and I loved the format of everything. Plenty of resources if you need help – hard to get a poor grade.
