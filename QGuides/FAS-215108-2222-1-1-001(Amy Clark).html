Take this class. That is an order.
I took this class for the great pun, but stayed because prehistory from the Paleolithic to the Bronze Age is really interesting, at least when professor Clark tells it. The assignments are really fun (although not entirely easy) and the small class size really helps to encourage interesting discussions about course topics. You won't regret taking this class!
This was a great course. I learned a lot and Professor Clark is super approachable and easy to learn from. She also made it really easy to engage and be interested in each lecture's topic. It was awesome to get to learn about the creation of hand–axes, for example, and then in the next class to get to actually hold real ones from the Peabody's collections.
This class is relaxing without being boring. You'll learn a lot without being overwhelmed by readings and assignments. Amy is also absolutely fantastic!
Definitely take this course if you are at all interested in the past and the foundations of human culture. This course is so well run and fun!
