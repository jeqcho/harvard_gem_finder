Take this class! Osnat is a fantastic teacher (and better person) who will tailor the course to suit exactly your needs. Whether you're looking to improve conversational skills, reading comprehension, writing skills or something else, this class really has the potential to help you grow and improve. Osnat is also an incredible teacher and gives fun, low–stress assignments (like read any short story in hebrew and write a one page reflection on it), and really cares about your own personal health and growth in college.

For some reason language classes are sort of looked down at Harvard and not seen as valuable/impactful as other courses, but I can definitely say I grew more in Osnat's class than any other class here at Harvard so far, as a student of hebrew but also a person. Please take her class!
Mod–Heb 130 with Osnat is an EXCELLENT course. I looked forward to it every week. The workload is not very intense and the grading is friendly. That said, I found that my ability to read, write, and speak Hebrew improved dramatically over the course of the semester. I could not recommend this course more.
