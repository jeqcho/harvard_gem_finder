TAKE THIS CLASS! Dennis and Julia are the best instructors –– engaged, thought–provoking, expansive in their modes of theoretical analysis, and full of true and abiding respect for the work you do. The course is built around modern cinematic discourse, so having an eye for film criticism helps, and so does being an avid viewer of films –– you will get a sense of cinema as a universe while you take this.
This class is pretty good if you like to talk about film theory and watch a wide variety of films each week. This being said, the class was quite big for a weekly discussion about film theory, which made participation very intimidating. Also, if you're not too interested in theory, this will be a slog. The best part of this class though is the ability to make a film for the final project, which not all theory classes offer.
