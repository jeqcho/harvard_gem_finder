Comments
Incredible overview of two great texts, taught by the man who likely knows them better than anyone in the world
This course provided a great exploration of the Federalist Papers and Democracy in America. I would highly recommend this course if you're interested in those texts.
The Political Science of American Democracy is one of the best courses at Harvard College. As usual, Professor Mansfield shines through as an excellent lecturer. His expertise, especially on Tocqueville, is unmatched. This course will bring about a greater appreciation for American society and its institutions, while simultaneously exposing its weaknesses.
Needless to say, Professor Mansfield is an expert on political philosophy, and particularly on Tocqueville's Democracy in America, which he translated. This is an experience that you can only get at Harvard, so if you have the opportunity, definitely lottery for the class. The readings can be quite long and abstract, but class consists mainly of lecture, during which most (if not all) of the key concepts and arguments will be explained.
Feel sorry for yourself you did not have the chance to learn from the wise Mansfield and his noble steed Dimitri!
Mansfield is a legend. Nothing else to it. The materials are great– books everyone should read, but are hard to really understand on your own. Fabulous class.
