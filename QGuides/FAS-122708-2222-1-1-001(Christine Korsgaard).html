Great class with very limited workload. TA is really annoying, but the professors and guest speakers are great. Very small class, attendance is expected at every class. Lots of discussion
This class is great for discussions and for meeting new people outside of the College. The class was diverse in both schools and identities which made for interesting conversation. I also think that this class was great for analyzing issues that biologists (especially wet–lab) do not get to think about frequently because they are more focused on their experiments. I am grateful as a biologist to have taken this course since it will now inform my thoughts and decisions in the lab.
This is an awesome class for anyone interested in working in the science/health/medical fields! The teaching staff are great at facilitating conversation, and the guest lecturers are top notch. Highly recommend—this is a gem in the best sense of the word: no stress, just pure academic engagement with interesting topics.
