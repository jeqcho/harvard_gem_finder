Awesome course and great readings – take if you want to read more fiction!
This is a great course with a wonderful syllabus and reasonable workload! The discussions are fascinating and very thorough, so it definitely helps to take notes and come fully prepared for each meeting.
This course is extremely accessible if you have very little background knowledge in the Arabic language, literatures, or middle East/North African history. The reading list is very enjoyable and the workload/readings are manageable. Most classes are discussion based and the prof does a very good job with having a list of points to discuss which is ends up functioning really well since she can still make relevant and useful points about the text without having to lecture.
The course is incredibly fascinating and you will learn a lot. Although a background in Arab history, Arabic, or literature is definitely helpful, it is not necessary because the professor explains it all in the class. The assignments are reasonable and it is overall a great introduction to Arabic literature.
