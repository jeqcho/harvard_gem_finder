What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I feel like this course not only helped me understand how electron microscopy works and what can be done with it, but I now have a super marketable skill which I can apply to my own research and help others in my research group. Take the class if you may use EM in any capacity–– you will learn to take publication quality images and the training will be much more comprehensive than you could get elsewhere. The staff are totally amazing. This class is honestly one of the best resources for SEAS students at Harvard. I wish every class at Harvard was like this! There is work for the class, but the fact that it will be tied to your own research makes it fine.
This class is a great way to learn and get comfortable with using these instruments (as compared to simply getting trained through the cores)! Would definitely recommend.
If you have an idea of what you would like to accomplish with electron microscopy in your research, take this class!
Please take the class if you will use TEM, especially early in your PhD, I think I'll look back on taking this class as one of the best decisions I made in my time at MIT.
This course is a great opportunity to get to know & work with staff scientists at CNS. They are amazing experts who are willing to help your research in any possible way.
