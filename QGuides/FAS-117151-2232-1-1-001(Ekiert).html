This is a really great class if you are interested in the region. The course goes into a lot of detail on East Central Europe. The Professor is very nice and interesting.
If you are like to grapple with history, government, social movements, central Eastern Europe, the Soviet Union, and you like reading about these topics, take this! A lot of reading, classic lecture style, and so much (interesting) information.

As someone who didn’t have as strong a framework for various comparative politics and political science frameworks, this was an amazing survey of these through the lens of a fascinating region and time. Though a lot of reading, I found it to be very engaging.
If you are interested in Central and Eastern Europe broadly, take it!
