Take this class! Especially if Professor Aguirre–Oteiza is teaching it. You will spend the equivalent of breakfast time each morning for a week to get though the week's reading, and all other work is done in–class. Really, it's not a burden to your schedule at all and is an excellent course to satisfy requirements. It will teach you more about translation than you think you know already! The midterm and final are also straight from some part of the readings you will translate in–class, so as long as you show up and actively participate, you are well–equipped to get an A.
Low stress course that teaches you the art of translation and allows you to think about how translations/translators influence readers and influence how aspects of Spanish history and culture are perceived. Plus, you take it with Daniel, who is the best. Highly recommend
