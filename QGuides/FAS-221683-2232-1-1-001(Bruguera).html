Take It! fun way to fulfill arts and humanities req
It's a gem.
This class is very fun without being too much work. You get a lot of freedom when it comes to what your final podcast looks like, and it's mainly just up to you to put into this class what you aim to get out of it.
I would take this class only if you have podcasting knowledge or genuinely want to start your own podcast and have an idea for it. There isn't really much class structure except for submitting your episodes. If you don't genuinely want to put in time to it, editing and recording can be a chore and frustrating to do. If you want to start a podcast or already have one then I would definitely take this class.
I would tell them that if they have ever wanted to learn more about a subject and teach others they should take this class as it is such a great stepping stone.
