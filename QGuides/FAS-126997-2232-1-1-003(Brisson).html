TAKE THIS CLASS! Dr. Brisson is an AMAZING instructor. The best professor I have met at Harvard! She is understanding, kind and yet, you will learn so much in this course! The assignments are creative and you will not get bored. The grammar is tricky as you are in a higher level French course but it is manageable!
I would highly recommend that you take this class. Dr. Brisson is an absolute gem, the sweetest lady, and very understanding and flexible if you need extensions on homework, projects, etc. The course load is not bad at all, and the final project (where you create a short French film in groups) is relatively low–pressure and super fun and creative. You’ll learn loads about Francophone cinema from a range of really interesting movies, which is not only a fun homework but also teaches you so much about Francophone culture – it’s really interesting. Definitely take this class – it was my favourite class this semester and you won’t regret it.
Take it :)
This class is phenomenal. Dr. Brisson is an amazing teacher, super nice (she literally cancelled our last test, gave us a chance to re–do at least one test throughout the semester and also gave us an opportunity to earn extra credit.) The writing assessments are super relaxed. The class is super educational, pretty and genuinely so much fun. 10/10 would recommend.
Worth taking! The film component is interesting and thought provoking, and leads to a valuable conversation about race and class across geographical and lingual barriers.
It's a great class! You watch a few films throughout the semester and they all deal with important issues relating to Francophone or contemporary society. You get as much out of the course as you put into it. I would encourage everyone to use the opportunity to practice and utilize French at every opportunity––in class, when writing the redactions etc. Also the final film project is really fun! I would highly recommend French 40 to anyone looking to continue their French education here at Harvard.
You will learn French by watching movies and commenting about them. Highly recommend.
French 40 is a really wonderful class: although it is a lot of work at the start, you'll eventually fall into a pattern of checking the workbook and logging into Canvas to submit discussion responses, VoiceThreads and critiques. The volume of assignments is quite high though, and you'll need to juggle both the bigger essays and the smaller weekly responses, so it's really important to keep track of these.
