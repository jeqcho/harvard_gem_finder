Professor Kelly and Wenfei are an incredible teaching team, kind, brilliant, and thoughtful, welcoming students into a niche area of literature in an engaging and supportive way. This course introduced me to a fascinating new world of literature and beyond that I will happily continue exploring on my own post college. It also gave me the space to exercise my creative self with every class assignment and serendipitously brought together students of all different backgrounds. I'm so glad I found this on my.harvard and chose it as a final semester elective!
I could not recommend this class more highly. I came in with no background in Chinese literature, but I feel like I learned so much about this facet of the Chinese literary tradition (and its evolution into modern media). It's so much fun getting to read these strange stories. Usually I dread weekly course readings, but I genuinely looked forward to these. They were often quite reasonable in length and so fascinating. The class discussions were also very interesting and lively. If you're excited by the prospect of reading ghost stories and discussing the boundary between human and other, I highly recommend taking this class!
Prof Kelly and the TF Wenfei are wonderful instructors and they put in a lot of effort to make the class engaging. The comments on the assignments are very thoughtful and productive as well!
This is one of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard as a STEM major. The stories chosen for readings are short, relevant (and referenced in other readings), and most of all fun to read. The discussions in class were super interesting, and this is one of the few classes at Harvard I would have been sad to miss a day of. We also had really fun field trips to the Art Museum, CAM Lab, and Yenching Rare Books collection! Assignments never felt like busy work, and both Professor Kelly and Wenfei were always there to give helpful feedback on drafts, and really great feedback on the finished assignments as well. Could not recommend this class enough! It was both fun and rewarding without being overly stressful.
