What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I am so happy I wrote a thesis. It was a lot of work and stressful at times, but a hugely rewarding process. Important to pick a doable question and make sure data is available early in the process.
The seminar itself is not super helpful BUT your TF can be a total lifesaver if you have an advisor that is not very involved. The weekly meeting with the TF are super helpful, make sure to go prepared to them so that you can get as much as possible out of every meeting. The presentations we have to do (mostly first semester) felt like a huge waste of time but I guess it was nice to have a little group of people who were also thesising even though we were not very good at helping each other out.
Take this class if your primary research interest is in economics and you would like to learn how to write a lengthy research paper
The format of the thesis seminar is helpful and my seminar TF, Shreya, was amazing! Would definitely recommend writing a thesis, you learn a ton
