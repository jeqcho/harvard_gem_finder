What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This was the most productive class of my semester. Can be curated to fit individual interests.
TAKE THIS CLASS!! The psychological theories I learnt in this class gave me a new framework to understand the messy and dynamic international system that cannot always be captured by pure rational choice theories. Professor Powers is an amazing instructor who leads classes with excellence and really cares about students and their development. The exciting discussions and new ideas developed in this class will be a highlight of my time at Harvard that I will look back upon with fondness. Don't miss it!
I would encourage students interested in psychology and international relations to take this course. It will be a very rewarding experience.
I would highly recommend the course. I think it is useful for people doing policy or scholarly work. I think you could also introduce an intermediate course that sits between the undergraduate version and this one. You could also teach a parallel course that talks about IR behaviours through a psychological lens such as deterrence, signalling, etc.
It is a great course, with a great cohort and seminar leader.
