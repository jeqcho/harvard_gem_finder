I want to preface that I thought the course was exceptionally interesting, the instructor, and the TFs during OH and section were also excellent and knowledgable. If you are looking for an intense GENED that will improve your critical reading, writing, and general knowledge I would definitely recommend in that regard. However, I will say that for a GENED the workload is very intense, and if you are looking for an easy way to fulfill the H&S requirement there are better alternatives. If the course still has 100 pages of reading a week and three/four papers when you read the syllabus then it will be considerable work. It really depends what your preferences are as a student, if you are academically minded and want to learn new perspectives from the GENED program this is a hard yes. If you want a gem and an easy A I would probably not recommend.
