I definitely recommend this course! It is on the light side in terms of workload, but it definitely makes you think. This class certainly requires thought unless you already know advanced statistics; Yue does a great job at introducing the topics both for those with no prior background and for those who have taken statistics before like me. He also does a good job of keeping stuff interesting in lecture. He is good at giving lectures, and he gives lots of interesting examples about the topics we learn. He also gives fun bonus problems that encourage us to think more about the material. Also– the exams follow pretty directly from the homework and lectures. Na Li is also great if you are taking the class with her, although I think Yue usually teaches it.
This fulfills the Probability and Statistics requirement for the engineering SB degree. Very straightforward class – one pset a week, two midterms, and a final. The content itself can be very hard and unintuitive at times, but be sure to work with other students and reach out to teaching staff. Professor Yue is great.
This course is fantastic. Professor Lu (Yue) is a great lecturer and can explain everything in a way that makes it very easy to follow. The course material and problem sets aren't too difficult to follow and there is plenty of support available through the professor and teaching staff in case you do get stuck.
ASK QUESTIONS
Definitely a very good alternative to Stat 110 if you don't have a particular interest in statistics/probability and are just looking for concentration requirement. I wouldn't say that you learn "less" than you would in Stat 110 either; the class covers statistical inference whereas Stat 110 does not, and everything you learn is taught in an application–based manner, so you walk away with a good grasp on how to understand and utilize probability/statistics.
I think this was a great class, I personally liked it a lot !
The course is tough, but pretty fair. Would recommend.
This is an excellent class. I highly recommend you take it over STAT110 or in addition to it. This class is very practical and the Professor (Yue Lu) is very dedicated to his students so you will succeed and learn a lot.
