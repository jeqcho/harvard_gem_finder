Incredible workshop — best I’ve taken (of 4) at Harvard. The kind of learning you can’t do from reading a how to book on writing, and the community is so lovely and supportive. Adored this class and really enjoyed how each piece was treated with such care and ambition, challenged through conversation, and reimagined. Not a lot of classes teach revision skills, and this one does.
ABSOLUTELY A MUST TAKE! Paul is incredible, supportive, and a wonderful writer himself. Highly recommend reading his works alongside the class seminars, as that will put into perspective some of his comments and feedback, since he often employs a lot of the broader concepts he points out into his own work. The peer–to–peer feedback is an excellent process for improving your writing, since you get 12 different people sending you feedback on what you write, and you can write on anything you've wanted to write on. By far my favorite class of the semester.
