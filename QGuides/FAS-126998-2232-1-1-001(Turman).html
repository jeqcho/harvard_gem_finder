Comments
This course was organized much better than previous French courses I took at Harvard. I personally liked the stress placed on learning vocab this semester, since most other classes focus on grammar instead. There is quite a bit of work but it isn't too difficult to complete and provides enough practice to actually improve. Would recommend taking if you need to fulfill a language req/finish a citation.
This is a must take if you want to improve your skills
This course is high in quantity of work, but the work that you are given is very manageable and allows you to solidify skills that you already have. You willl have to sit down with your packet for hours every night, and as soon as you finish one big assignment, another one is already in the works. The level of work in manageable, but it is an extremely high quantity. I would also advise that I entered this class with the same speaking abilities that I entered french 40 with and was fine. You just have to work harder than you do in french 40. I also think that if you are truly at the 50 level/test into 50, you could take the next higher up class.
There's quite a bit of work so manage your time well, do your best to start using the vocabulary as soon as you learn it and try to immerse yourself in the language as much as possible.
It's fine, I had fun with it because I like politics, very much take it if you're pursuing your citation or degree.
