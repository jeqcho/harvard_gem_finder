What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
There is a lot of reading, so be prepared.
Come with an open mind. You will be exposed to lot of readings. If you invest on it, you will learn. Class discussions are major component and participate in it. If you don't do that, then you won't learn much.
The class is challenging. There are a lot of readings to do every week, and you will invest a lot of time engaging with the material to prepare for weekly discussions. The final project is also extremely time consuming and requires a lot of commitment.

That being said, this was a VERY rewarding class. I learned more applicable concepts from this class than I have from any other class I've taken at Harvard. If you are willing to engage in discussions, read every week, and ask questions, I think you will feel the same. I think the time commitment is worth it.
Great class – invest time in the readings and make sure you engage in the classroom!
