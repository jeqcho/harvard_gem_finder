Comments
Take this class!! Vijay is the best, and you will learn a lot by having to collaborate with people you might not otherwise play with or know. It is a significant amount of work because of rehearsals, but it is worth it! There is no homework other than rehearsals and very short weekly reflections.
Take this class.
This is such a special course! I had the unique opportunity to collaborate with and learn from my peers in an academic context and I truly grew so much from this experience.
My favorite course and one of the best all–around experiences I've ever had at Harvard. It's something really very special. Professor Iyer is a legend and remarkably brilliant and kind. Samora is as well; the feedback they give together is always constructive but honest and mind–opening. The small nature of the class and the way that ensembles move around also created a musical community that I was extraordinarily grateful to be a part of, both because they're all great musicians from all different sorts of backgrounds but also because many people I met in the class have become very good friends. Would 1000% recommend if you want to grow as a musician and an artist.
Great class –– take it. You won't regret it. This class offers something for everyone, even if you are not a music concentrator –– I am a mechanical engineer and yet still found this to be my favorite class this semester by far. The community you build with your groups and with your classmates is unparalleled, and the compositions truly turn out to be things of beauty once they are workshopped and perfected. Even if you aren't confident you are experienced enough with music or composition, it is definitely worth applying and taking regardless –– Prof. Iyer and Samora were very understanding in my experience.
Do not hesitate for even a second to take this class if you are interested in composition and small ensemble playing. It is not a big time commitment but you gain so much insight into the process of making music and working with others to create something beautiful together.
This class can change your experience at Harvard!! Take advantage if you can of this amazing opportunity to be exposed to some incredible new peers, professors, and performance/composition collaborations!
