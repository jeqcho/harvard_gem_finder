I learned about a lot of things, from basic physical oceanography to the ways that research is done, from shallow reef ecosystems to the deep sea, and from sharks to phyla of invertebrates that very few people have even heard of. I think I am more curious about the natural world, and also more poised to understand it as a result of having taken this course. I also think that I have a greater awareness of the physical and chemical processes which underly the ocean's functioning, as well as conservation issues affecting the ocean, and as a result I am equipped to become a more responsible, engaged citizen.
