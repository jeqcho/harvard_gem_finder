It's hard! I like STAT 104 because it emphasizes math over coding and (speaking as someone uninterested in math) presents the topics in an engaging, relevant way.
This class is a decent amount of work, but there are plenty of resources available to help you with it. If you put in the time, you will see results. Make sure to always do the practice tests for the exams, since those give you a very good idea of what to expect on the actual exam.
I would recommend this course. Sections and Study Breaks are especially helpful.
This class is challenging, but if you go to section, stay on top of the material, do the readings, actively complete the psets, and review lecture slides regularly, you can succeed in this class.
Despite the negative reviews about stat 104 in the past, the class is statistically significantly better under Professor Rader.
