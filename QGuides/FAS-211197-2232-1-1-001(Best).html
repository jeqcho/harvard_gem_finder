Everyone should take this course! I know the instructors may be changing, but I hope Harvard still offers this course because it is truly amazing! Going into the Harvard Art Museum to discuss displayed works, gaining exclusive access to pieces that the professors acquired themselves, talking to the experts in the art conservation and restoration labs are all unique experiences that you cannot really get anywhere else! You'll learn quite a lot about the political and ethical questions facing curators and get to practice them through presentations. Regardless of your major, if you have ever wondered about the work it takes to set up a museum or how the process of art/object acquisition works, if you have any appreciation for art or museums at all, you should take this course.
