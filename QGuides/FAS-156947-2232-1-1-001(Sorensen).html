TAKE THIS CLASS! One of the most fun electives I've ever taken at Harvard. The course staff (Pia <3) is INCREDIBLE and are so dedicated to the class–their excitement about fermentation is infectious. This is the gem of gems–expect minimal work outside of class (10 minutes every other week on average tops to answer a discussion question). The final group project is super open–you get to choose what fermentation you want to investigate and other folks in the class are super bought–in, so it's fun to do it together. Truly when else do you get to brew mead and make yogurt and take field trips to cheese caves for a class?

This class was one of the highlights of my weeks and I highly recommend it to anyone looking for a fun course that approaches food from a scientific perspective.
