What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
If you have the opportunity to take the course, do it and do it as soon as possible.
This class was the highlight of my semester. I highly recommend Josh's immediate poetry workshop!
Josh is maybe one of the best teachers I've ever had and I feel so lucky that he lead my first experience writing poetry in a classroom setting. He set an amazingly encouraging precedent early on, asking for "only positive feedback" in the beginning of the semester to get folks out of their shells. Each week, he provides a thick packet of poems that are examples of the weekly poem assignment (you write a poem a week). These poems are awesome –– I've read, reread, and will continue to reread them. Reading a lot of poetry with intentionality is a cheat code to writing better poetry, and these packets really hone the skill of pinpointing when an author utilizes a certain technique. The pace of writing a poem a week is challenging, but soon the expectation of writing polished poetry sloughs off to reveal authentic poetry. Seeing everyone come into their own styles was a joy and each class felt almost spiritual. I trusted that my classmates would leave no stone unturned as much as I trusted them to turn them gently and articulately.

As someone who was embarrassed to show anyone my poetry, this class couldn't have been better for me. I almost didn't take it as I read some of Josh's poems online and wasn't certain my style aligned with his. Thank god I did. Josh is scary good at articulating what a poem is doing and even more scary–good at pinpointing what an author is trying to do. He writes paragraphs, no PAGES, for every person in the class every week in response to the weekly assignments. And the stuff is pretty much pure gold every time. He'll write something about you that you didn't even know was there and the effort he puts in to his comments is heroic (I would get some of these emails at 2 or 3am). TAKE THIS CLASS. APPLY NOW.
Please take this class if you are interested in poetry! Basically everyone in the class shared they were nervous about sharing their work/being good enough, so give it a shot and take it! I grew and learned so much.
***TAKE THIS CLASS. Professor Bell is amazing and he really truly helped me develop my poetry and the way I viewed different styles of poetry. It's almost guaranteed that your style will change just by taking the course and being exposed to a myriad of poems.
This class changed my life. Josh Bell changes, and touches, lives. If you're a poet, take this class. If. you're not a poet but want to become one, take this class.
This is such an incredible class, and you should absolutely take it if you can! Josh gives such kind and constructive feedback, as do the other students in the class. It's definitely work doing a poem a week, but it's such a rare opportunity to get such helpful feedback. Plus, everyone's in the same boat as you, and it's just invaluable to be able to hear how people interpret your poems. Take this if you like poems!
