What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this class only if you have a strong interest in philosophy, religion, and its applications to, well, anything. The essay prompts are general enough that you can write about any topic in just about any form as long as you show that you’ve thought critically about it. Take the lectures as they are—long–winded and incomprehensible at times but occasionally resonant—and do the readings if you actually want to learn philosophy. Michael Puett is wonderful!!! He makes himself very available to students outside of class and generates significant enthusiasm for course material. You get out what you put in.
