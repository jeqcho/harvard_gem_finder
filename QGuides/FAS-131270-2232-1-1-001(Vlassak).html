ES 120 is a great course. The lectures are very engaging and always packed with course content. If you put in the time to learn the material, many of the concepts become intuitive and fun. Be aware that the homework assignments will take a lot of time and the exams are quite difficult. Though it is difficult, it is not impossible, and it is certainly possible to enjoy the course. This class is HARD, I'm not gonna lie. Support from the teaching staff is super helpful (and honestly necessary to get through the coursework). PSets are really long, so start them early, and unfortunately there are PSets during exam weeks (which I hope they change for future semesters). Exams are way too long to reasonably complete in the allotted class time, so practice doing problems quickly if you want to do well on exams. Joost basically teaches exactly from the textbook, so follow along with the textbook during the semester (the book is well written so it's helpful).
