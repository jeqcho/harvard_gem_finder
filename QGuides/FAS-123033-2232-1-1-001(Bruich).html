This is an intro level of econometrics course that applies the raw mathematical and statistical tools of regression to answer causal questions. As a result, the methods learned are broadly applicable in social science fields.

Another strength is that students get to learn coding in Python/R/stata to solve these econometrics problems and write reports by the end of the semester, all very valuable tools to have even for non–concentrators.
