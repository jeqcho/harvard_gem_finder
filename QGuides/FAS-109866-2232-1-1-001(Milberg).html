This has undoubtedly been one of the most unique academic experiences I've had on campus. Whether or not you plan to study psychology or neuroscience, this course was incredible in terms of developing a universal interest in the subject, encouraging student participation, and moving the focus from grades and completion of assignments to fully engaging with the material and just learning to learn. I highly recommend this class for anyone remotely interested in the field.
Take this course if you have the chance to! Professor Milberg makes the class so engaging and it's a great way to learn about the brain in a non–traditional way. This class has been one of my favorites all throughout college, and I am so happy that I got into the lottery for it this semester.
Take this class if you want to dive into understanding hallmark neuro cases that gave insight into cognition and the brain. Professor Milberg is a great instructor and passionate about what he does. The course gives you a cursory understanding of neuroanatomy for the first portion of the class then moves into presentations of hallmark cases.

This is a really great course! The first half of the class is going over neuroanatomy which is super cool and not at all like what you learned in neuro 80. Prof Milberg brings in a brain that you get to examine. There's a midterm but we got to take it as a class which made it very stress–free. The second half of the class is just student presentations, which were lots of fun! I definitely would recommend this course if you're on an MBB track or if you're able to take it as an elective!
