What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course taught me how to approach math in a new and better way! The teaching staff were amazing!
If you are even slightly shaky on your math, take Math M! It is an extra year of math if you need to eventually take 1b, but if you really want to understand and learn the material instead of suffering through content you don't understand, Math M is the way. I've made so many friends in the class as well because everyone is just great in this course, I swear.
Be able to solve the problems without help before exams!! Collaboration is really helpful but when it comes to test time being able to do the problems and knowing the material individually is crucial!
This course is really well thought and organized in order to ensure you succeed in the class. The 3 Psets a week can be tedious and sometimes challenging but there is enough support for you to be able to complete them and be able to understand the course.
The course is really good, but be sure to pick a TF that you like and fits your learning style. Matt, the course head, is really a great person and leads the course well so staff communication is good. It is not terribly hard, but it is still calculus.
Be prepared for a lot of work. The teaching team is fantastic and they want nothing more than for you to succeed. Matt is a fantastic coursehead and does everything in his power to make the experience reasonable and fun for the students. I would take this even if you have taken AP Calc AB because they teach more in depth application based.
It is an excellent course to take if you have not taken a calc class before, or if you struggled with calculus in high school and could use a better understanding before moving on to higher level math courses.
