A good class to take as long as you are good at attending rehearsal
If you're already in HRO, take this class! Easy A and little readings to do.
Definitely recommend to take if you are in HRO. The readings are fairly interesting and relevant to the music we play, and Fed will personally respond to each of your discussion posts.
If you are very passionate about music and an experienced instrumentalist, this course is perfect for you. You will get to collaborate with likeminded musicians, learn incredible repertoire under Maestro Cortese, and understand where the music you are playing comes from (via readings, rehearsals, etc.). I love it!
Please take this class! Gives you accountability to go to HRO rehearsal, and the assignments are very straightforward. I wish this class was 4 credits!
If you are in HRO, I'd highly recommend enrolling in Music 107! It's a great and manageable way to earn credit for attentively going to rehearsal and doing the Canvas assignments. The readings, while sometimes long, do provide a really great background to contextualize the music in the program, so it makes rehearsing the music a lot more interesting and meaningful in the long run.
Show up to rehearsals! HRO is super fun, and there is very minimal work outside of rehearsals, which consist of readings around twice a semester.
I highly recommend Music 107 to all members of HRO. The assignments outside of scheduled rehearsals are very reasonable in scope and truly add to the experience of performing the music. It is an excellent way to gain a deeper understanding of the composer and music we play while also earning credit for HRO.
