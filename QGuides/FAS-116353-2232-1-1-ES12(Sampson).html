Be prepared to read a lot
Overall, I enjoyed the course. The two big writing tasks were fun to do, and the readings were interesting as they discussed many societal problems regarding success in life. The professor was fantastic, very accessible, and provided a lot of very useful, specific, and thorough feedback. That being said, the only downsides are that you might have to allocate more time to the course than you like to, and sometimes the readings can feel very overwhelming because of their length. However, I am sure don't this class instead of the regular Expos 20 was the right decision for me.
