What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The course is a relatively easy and low commitment way to be introduced to the field of survival analysis. LJ Wei is really knowledgeable of the field and shares lots of stories of his experience in academia. He makes lectures entertaining, though he does tow the line of what's uncomfortable sometimes.
While this class focuses less on the practical implementation of survival analysis, it's a great introduction to general methods and the theory that justifies them. The theory part of the class is presented clearly so that's it's not overwhelming, even if you've never seen martingales before
