kincaid is cool
It's amazing
Although I really enjoyed learning from Professor Kincaid, many parts of this class were ultimately really frustrating. Other than the first assignment, our creative writing work was never driven by prompts or any other kind of direction–giving assignment, which would have been very helpful for first–time creative writers like myself. Professor Kincaid is a remarkable writer with so much experience and hearing her takes on everything from fiction writing to politics was always entertaining and informative. However, she was absent so many weeks and we had to cancel class so many times to the point that knowing whether or not we'd have class at all felt like a game of tossup. I feel like I could have gotten so much more out of this class if she had been present more and if there was more structure to assignments. I really wanted to love this class.
It is an incredible experience. I honestly could not recommend this class more.
This class is so lovely! Basically an opportunity to workshop your writing and receive feedback from Professor Kincaid and your peers. Was one of my favorite sections of the week.
Be warned – if you are looking for structured instruction/guidance on how to write creatively, this is not the course for you. You really have to just dive in and see what you write, and then you receive feedback in class after you read aloud. It's a great exercise in bravery and taught me to be more comfortable putting myself out there and sharing my writing—accepting imperfections and being open to constructive feedback.
This class is amazing! The community fostered in this class is just wonderful. I learned such a great deal from my classmates and from Professor Kincaid every session. Professor Kincaid is such a fount of knowledge, and you gain so much from every conversation. I've grown as a writer simply through listening to my classmates and participating in this workshop. And it's just so much fun!
