Please take the course to truly get to know the professors of HAA and have a once in a lifetime experience.
best class ever. i wish i could take it again next year!! the readings and lectures are super interesting (professor roxburgh is great!) and the structure of the class is such that you end up becoming good friends with ur classmates. plus, free trip!
To be completely honest: the excursion makes up for the lacking structural, community, and organization of the course component of the course. The excursion is highly experiential, exciting, and well–planned—and it’s during the excursion that you really get to know your classmates and professors’ thoughts and arguments. During the actual classes during semester, this class is so uninspired and long — the lecture format often is just one professor reading slides at us, and the homework assignments revolve around group presentations and short papers in which you barely have the space to really dig into material, or the time to get to work with your group members on really understanding the area you are assigned. Take this course for an incredible, unforgettable, magical excursion experience, but be warned that the semester class experience is borderline awful.
Take this class and take advantage of office hours :)
