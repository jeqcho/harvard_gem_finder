What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is fun and interesting! I would highly recommend for anyone curious about topics in planetary habitability.
This class is a really lovely review of the past and current literature of atmospheres of terrestrial planets, and Robin is great at facilitating conversations in this! Overall not too stressful while still learning a lot :)
This class is particularly hard if you come with a weak background in atmospheric studies. The more you know about atmosphere before, the more you get out of this class. Nonetheless, the reading are very interesting and Prof. Wordsworth is an amazing teacher. You'll learn a lot from this class if you decide to take it.
If you are a graduate student looking for a fun reading course, I definitely recommend this class. For undergrads, I would advise only upper–level students with a background in physics or EPS.
