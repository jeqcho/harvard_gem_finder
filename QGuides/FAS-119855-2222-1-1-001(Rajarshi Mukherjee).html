What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Only take this course if you are really interested in the theory of inference and enjoy proofs. I took this as someone interested in applications of statistics to real problems, thinking it would be good for me to understand more statistical theory and push myself. In the end, this course did not help me in any way for applied statistics, but made me better at doing theoretical proofs.
The course itself is not designed to be stressful, as Rajarshi is a very understanding instructor. Nonetheless you will be challenged, as you will see a lot of things you've never seen before and go through very thorough proofs. It's a good deep dive into statistical theory and concepts!
