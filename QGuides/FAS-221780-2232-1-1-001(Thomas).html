Do not miss the opportunity to learn from her.
Please take this class! Louisa is incredibly nice and a great teacher, and the readings are not too extensive and very interesting!
TAKE THIS CLASS! It is a fantastic course with an even more fantastic professor. Louisa is such a supportive professor that genuinely cares about you and your improvement as a writer. She is so approachable and understanding. And the readings she has picked out are just phenomenal.
This is a great workshop! Louisa is super knowledgeable but also casual. Emphasis is on learning and adjusting rather than mass production. I really enjoyed it.
I absolutely recommend this course to anyone with an interest in creative nonfiction or creative writing in general! The course provided a supportive environment to not only learn about nonfiction as a topic but also grow as a writer. Professor Thomas is an amazing writer and teacher, and the lessons I learned in this course will definitely stay with me.
Take this class. It will change how you read about people and how you portray them! Louisa is a fantastic teacher who is able to see your writing and your goals even better than you are. She is constantly understanding and compassionate while still effectively driving progress and learning goals throughout the semester. I genuinely loved showing up to class, even at 9am! I looked forward to every week and every reading. Truly a treat of a class, and the first time I think a class at Harvard made me a genuinely better writer in such little time. I wouldn't hesitate to take the seminar again if I could!
