What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you are ready to engage with the readings then you will not regret taking this course. The syllabus is so comprehensive and well structured and Vishal is one of the most supportive and attentive instructors I’ve had at Harvard. For the final paper, he is extremely open to a vast range of topics that his students find inspiring and will provide so much support in helping his students find a topic they are passionate about if they don’t know exactly what they want to focus on.
Do not take this class if you want simply a lecture class! This course is one you have to prepare for, engage actively with the material and participate–– and that is how you get the most out of the class as well. Also, start thinking about your final paper topic early and start researching early!!
If you want to learn about the latest methodological strategies operating the intersection of art, urbanism, architecture, and design from an extremely knowledgable and engaging professor, this is the class for you!
