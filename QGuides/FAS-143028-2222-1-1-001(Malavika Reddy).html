Mala is a fantastic professor. She does a wonderful job of making social theory seem less intimidating, sharing her expertise, and facilitating discussion. The readings are not too intense or long. Throughout the course, you will have the opportunity to apply the theories you are learning to contemporary issues/problems/events.
Mala is an incredible lecturer and also fosters intimate and wonderful interactions between the students in the course. You will learn so much about different social theories and how they are applicable to the world, whether it's looking at Lil Nas X's Montero video through the lens of Foucault or Beyonce's Homecoming through an understanding of the erotic.
This class is really amazing. Mala is an absolutely incredible professor. She is so caring and thoughtful in designing the course. Theory seems really daunting and it is not always the most fun, and can be a lot of reading, but Mala makes it really interactive, interesting, and relevant.
This course is a great introduction to anthropology through theory. You'll learn a lot, and the teaching staff are great.
