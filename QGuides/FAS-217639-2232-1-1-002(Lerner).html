ES94 is cool class ***if*** you're interested in entrepreneurship.

Josh is a good professor, but he's chaotic at times, and can be hard to follow.

The cases are mostly interesting, but are sometimes not well written, and require effort to fully read. They will make you pay around $100 for the cases.

The teaching staff is sometimes out of touch with the real world, as is typical for HBS. The class is basically a networking opportunity. If you're into the world of HBS, venture capital, and serial entrepreneurship, you will have a blast. Otherwise, you might realize that entrepreneurship is not for you. The class helped me understand that I'm in the latter group, and I consider that a good outcome.

The workload is very manageable, so if you need a class to balance your schedule, go for it.
