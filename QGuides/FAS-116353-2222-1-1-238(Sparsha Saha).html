I would ABSOLUTELY TAKE THIS EXPOS if you can!!! Rank it first! Sparsha is one of the most wonderful professors that I have had at Harvard and she moved with much flexibility for us, yet also made sure to ensure that we were really improving our writing. I feel like I came out of her class with more tools in my toolbox for writing and have even begun to implement some of her techniques in my other academic writing (for example, setting up a puzzle in our paper, this is something you'll come to learn if you take her class)! Sparsha is one of the kindest professors as well and genuinely cares about every single student and wants to see us all succeed. In addition, she is so obviously passionate about all of the work that she does and very obviously cares about the issues that she teaches in class. However, I do want to note that this is NOT some kind of class that is trying to turn everyone vegan, that is actually the last thing Sparsha wants to do. All she wants to do is educate us more so that we can make more informed decisions about our individual choices in life and give us better writing skills. Absolutely take this Expos, Sparsha is by far one of the best professors that Harvard has to offer and you will take away so incredibly much from this class.
