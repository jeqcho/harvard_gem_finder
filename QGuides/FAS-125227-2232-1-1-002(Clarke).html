Best course at Harvard! You get out what you put in
Expos 40 changed my life, and if you have underlying confidence issues that manifest in a discomfort with public speaking, it will change your life as well. Kate strikes that miraculous balance between kindness and helpfulness in her feedback, and she has an endless number of suggestions and exercises to combat seemingly every kind of presentation flaw. This class will give as much as you put in — do the work, and you'll discover passions, strengths, and a love of sharing your knowledge with the world that you never knew you had.
This is an overall great course, but it's also a "what you make of it" class. I was a weak public speaker going into this course, so this was not a gem for me. I spent a LOT of time on it during speech weeks, like 10+ hours. I chose really personal topics for most of my speeches, which made it easier to write my speeches, but much more emotional to deliver them, but I think I got a lot out of that. While harrowing for me at times, was an overall valuable and fun time. Kate is the greatest preceptor for this course, and she is so caring and kind. She did wonders for me with her suggestions, feedback, workshopping, and general support.
Most useful class I've taken at Harvard. I learned so much about public speaking. Really helpful work that doesn't feel like work. Just take it.
