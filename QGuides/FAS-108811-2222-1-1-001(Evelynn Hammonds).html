Comments
Save yourself, take another grad seminar, literally any other one. Hammonds is extremely intelligent and well–read but just does not want to be there. Be ready to never get your emails responded to and to just take a guess if she has read your reflection paper or not. Just save yourself the frustration honestly.
The course material is really interesting, so you should definitely do the readings. But you'll have to be self–driven, since you can also easily slide by without truly engaging with the material. We never received any feedback on any of our submitted work. Even though it's a weekly seminar, the structure of our class on a weekly basis was a hefty lecture delivered by Professor Hammonds and then we would go down the line and present what we wrote about in our weekly response paper. We never truly had a full group discussion or addressed each other's points in class.
