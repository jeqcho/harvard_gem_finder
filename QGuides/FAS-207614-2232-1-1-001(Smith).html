Comments
Take this class as soon as possible – it will pay off in the later years of your PhD
This course is extremely helpful if you have a project in mind that you wish to complete this term. The final term assignment is due earlier in the semester than usual, which helps tremendously in getting the project squared away early and not interfering with the busy end of term assignments. The guest speakers were phenomenal. I highly recommend this course to anyone with a desire to improve their writing or challenge existing norms surrounding scientific writing (mainly, that it is painfully dull). Feedback received on assignments is particularly strong and instructive, assisting in the revision process tremendously.
Absolutely take this class if you're a senior going to grad school! It will really prepare you! I learned a lot from the reading materials assigned
This course really deconstructed how I view science writing and communication, and the process of getting grants. It also reframed what kind of researcher i'd hope to be. I'm really glad I took it! It's stuff that anyone going into science/academia should know.
