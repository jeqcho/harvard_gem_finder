Take this class!! Weekly assignments are engaging but low–stress and lectures are super interesting. The exams can get pretty specific but they're always straightforward and fair. The primary research articles you'll read are very interesting and the paper presentation (with a partner) is very manageable. Final writing assignment was super fun to write! Overall, a great class to take for any Neuro concentrator or secondary. Laura and Helena are really wonderful instructors and give great detailed feedback on everything. Highly highly recommend!!
Awesome class, loved every lecture. PLEASE study for the two midterm exams. The questions are all taken right from the lecture slides so if you know what's going on you'll be in a great spot. If you don't study you won't do well on the exams.
This course is so fun! You get to learn all about a bunch of pathogens that attack the brain, read cool papers, and develop some scientific writing skills while youre at it through manageable weekly assignments. There are exams, but they are very straightforward, and its just such a cool topic. As an added bonus, there are a lot of lab techniques/ microbiology stuff you learn about which I have found very helpful for the MCAT. Laura and Helena are both super nice, and I promise you that youll learn a lot without it feeling like a heavy lift.
I would tell this student to take this class if they are interested in the immune response, and how pathogens are able to enter/disrupt function in the central nervous system.
Overall a very fun and interesting class! Exams are heavy memorization so make sure to study ahead of time!!
This course is really interesting and Dr. Magnotti is a fantastic lecturer. I fully recommend. Really study hard for the two midterm exams and pay attention to specific details. Also, really put forth effort on your weekly responses because the feedback you receive is really helpful in furthering your understanding. I think this is a good course to take for people interested in medicine, too, as it emphasizes the importance of the biological mechanisms of certain infections.
The lectures and content are great, but the course is very memorization heavy. At review sessions, when students asked what they should remember, the advice seemed to be memorize every detail about everything. Probably very applicable and helpful towards anyone who is looking at medicine as a future career, but a little stressful for me personally as I was studying for exams. Sometimes, I felt like the grading was a bit harsh, but the comments and feedback were very helpful towards doing better on future assignments!
Highly recommend this class!! Very interesting subject topic, and you will definitely learn a lot! The exams were fair, but you will need to study hard for them.
Awesome class. I really enjoyed it.
This class takes some work, but you learn so much from it! It is truly a great way to get an introduction to some of the more fascinating aspects of the brain and neurological diseases. It also facilitates a greater comfortability with reading primary literature and understanding scientific experimentation, especially for brain specific techniques.
I would say one caveat is that the exams can be specific, and will require studying.
This class is genuinely the best course I have taken at Harvard. I've enthusiastically recommended it to so many of my friends to take next year. The material is SO incredibly fascinating, and Laura is a truly amazing lecturer. I'm one of the few non–pre–med Neuro concentrators, and this felt like a really amazing foray into some medical microbiology that I wouldn't really get a chance to explore otherwise. Every lecture, and student–led lecture/discussion, sincerely felt like a really elevated, academic YouTube video I was watching for fun and discussing with others in my free time. Helena is such a great TF, explains the papers in a really succinct way, and really advocates for her students' learning. Every week, I was so passionately excited to come to class and learn, and I had been missing that kind of academic enjoyment for a while! I cannot stress enough how amazing of a class this is! If you're on the fence, take it!
