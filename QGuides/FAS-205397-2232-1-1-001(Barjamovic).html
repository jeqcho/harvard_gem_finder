You should take this class!
If you have any interest whatsoever in ancient history, then take this class. You will have a good deal of autonomy to choose reading materials, and you will give talks to your peers. Dr. Barjamovic is a fantastic leader and moderator.
This class is best suited for someone who has interests in particular research subjects, but hasn't yet had the time or opportunity to explore them. It is better to have a general idea to explore than to simply read books to have said one has read them.
Take it! The course will help you appreciate what's special about your own field while also seeing the necessity of studying all Mediterranean cultures within the same lens.
This class is amazing. The materisl are student–directed and the discussions are student–led but Dr. Barjamovic will happily and easily help explain concepts, historical context, whatever he feels is needed to push the conversation. There are almost certainly going to be higher–level students auditing the class (sometimes for the 2nd time) but dont' let them intimidate you! Learn from them! Engage with them. You will learn more than you ever thought.
