Hello First–year trying to figure out how to rank expos classes. I remember being in your position, looking at the Q–scores. I remember basically there are three categories. Expos classes people say they loved and improved their writing, expos classes that were fine and just a whatever way to get the credit, and the rare expos class that people really do not like. I'm here to tell you this one is the second one. It's fine, you'll survive, maybe even you'll have some fun discussions in class or a paper you're proud of, but it's not great. The subject isn't super interesting, the papers aren't fun to write, and you probably wont get much better at writing. I would rank it lower down personally. Among all the mediocrity, here are some thing I did like. The movies you watch and the one book you read are very enjoyable in my opinion. Dr. Lane is a very nice and approachable professor. If you have cool students in your class like I did there will probably be some really fun discussions.
