Take this class because it sets you up to understand literature from the bottom up—as a form of science, even. That is how much it is broken down.
For English concentrators, this required course is definitely shaped by the instructor. But overall, the readings across forms are generally interesting and the assignments are not horribly difficult. Do not fear English 20, and try to enjoy the assortment of texts it has to offer you. For non–English concentrators, I'd recommend choosing an English course that speaks to your specific interests rather than this general topic course.
