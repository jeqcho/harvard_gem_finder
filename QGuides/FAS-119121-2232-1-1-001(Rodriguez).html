Comments
Fantastic class! Super thought provoking readings. Assignments (response papers, one presentation, and essays) were manageable, enjoyable, and helpful in analyzing course material. Class discussions were great and Prof. Rodriguez is a a great instructor!
Some of the readings are more dense and difficult than others, but overall, the workload is manageable. The smaller discussion posts get you to start thinking critically about the texts before the larger papers. Our class was a small group, so we had a lot of opportunities to talk and practice our French.
