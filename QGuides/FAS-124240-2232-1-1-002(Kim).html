If you are motivated to learn Korean, this is the place to be! You may start Ba without knowing how to read hangul, but by the end of Bb you'd be able to do a whole 5 minutes presentation in korean! You do need to put effort every day to interact with the language, but you really will learn a lot. Love the community, love the teachers. take this class!
The best thing about the course are the people–– the teaching staff are incredible, and care so much about the students academically and personally. I have also met some of my best friends in this course. There is a pretty demanding in person requirement, but it's definitely paid off because no other courses here allow you to get to know your classmates so well. If you are thinking about learning Korean, I absolutely recommend taking the BA/BB series.
Korean BB is a great way to continue enhancing your Korean language skills as a beginner and teaches you about the Korean language, music, and culture in a fun and creative way. The work in this class may seem overwhelming in the beginning because daily attendance is required, and there are weekly assignments/tests but it is manageable and professors are always willing to help you improve.
Korean is a fantastic choice for the language requirement. The staff is amazing. Prepare to do plenty of memorizing and studying but very worthwhile and graded very fairly. Because it meets daily, class community is excellent.
