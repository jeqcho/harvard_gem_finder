If you want to improve your writing, taking a creative writing class is a great way to do that. Every time I have taken one of these classes at Harvard I learn so much. Professor Messud is very kind and welcoming, so her class would be great to take especially if you haven't taken any creative writing classes before. I highly recommend any of her courses.
Take it!!!
If you get the chance, take this class!!!!!! Claire is brilliant, kind, and honest, which are the best qualities to have in a professor, especially a writing one. Her classes were my favorite at Harvard, and she was one of my favorite professors in my 4 years here. What a gem of a human being. What a gem of a class. I wish my whole course load were her classes. She has taught me so much, and my writing has made leaps because of her notes.
Take this class! You will be so inspired by your peer writers –– including older voices from Harvard's (and other school's) graduate programs. The quality of their writing will drive you to new heights of your own.
It's a lot of work but all of it is useful.
I would tell future students to prepare to read. Read a lot. And talk about reading a lot.
take this class!!! Your writing will improve, you'll learn so much from Professor Messud (and your classmates!), and you'll have such exciting discussions in the process!
Take the course with Claire Messud! She cares a lot about your development as a writer, is a close reader, and will give you honest feedback.
This is definitely an advanced fiction workshop, and you should be prepared to dedicate a very large chunk of your week to this class, but if you love writing you'll love every moment of it. Reading other students' work and responding to it is also a large time commitment, but there's so much to learn from them too. You have to be very passionate about writing to thrive in this kind of environment, but everyone else will be as well, and so it's an insanely rewarding experience. Professor Messud is absolutely brilliant, and she's so thoughtful and purposeful about her feedback to you. It's an amazing and inspiring experience to get to write with her guidance. This was by far the highlight of my semesterI wish I could take it forever.
