What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course is really special and I highly recommend taking it. This may be the only course I've ever taken that really could only be taught at Harvard because it makes such extensive use of Harvard's collections and examines their history in depth. I learned to think much more critically about museums and other academic institutions and the debts they owe to indigenous people. Professor Klassen is amazing. She is so kind and welcoming while also being such a brilliant scholar and facilitating such thought–provoking discussions.
Take this class! Getting to visit Harvard's museums and contemplate how to remediate insensitive or inadequate portrayals of Indigenous cultures was an invaluable experience.
Take this course if you want a closer look at Harvard's museums!
