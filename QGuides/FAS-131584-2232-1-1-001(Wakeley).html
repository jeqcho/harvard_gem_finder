What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
As always, John is a fantastic professor to learn from and have discussions with. I really enjoyed the course and found the time I put into it each week to be time well–spent. I would recommend the course to those who are interested in diving into specific topics of evolutionary genetics and in strengthening their skills in critical reading of the literature — this course is a great space to develop those skills in a comfortable, non–judgmental setting among peers.
Pick papers that you’re excited to talk about! Don’t feel like you have to choose from the list. Also, don’t be too afraid to pick something you don’t 100% understand
Definitely take this course if the topic for the year looks even remotely interesting to you!
This is an excellent seminar course. John fosters an environment in which students can discuss papers in a relaxed way, comfortable to voice confusion and ask basic questions. The goal of each class was to understand papers together, including where they went right and where they went wrong. This kind of class is useful to anyone in science, not just evolutionary genetics.
