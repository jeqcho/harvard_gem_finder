This course is an absolutely amazingly–taught course! Professor Calmon is a wonderful professor and gets everyone really excited about the content. It was really cool to see how signals and communications is ubiquitous and essential for modern technology. The mathematical content is definitely difficult, but the way the concepts were explained really helped keep in mind why the content was important and how it all fit together. It was also really fascinating to learn about the history of the content as well as the future directions of the field. Would definitely recommend this class!
Very organized course. Very fair HWs. Very fair HWs. Very clear lectures. Very good resources (book).

Amazing Prof! AMAZING! So so so nice! And a super good teacher! A+++++++++++++
Flavio is the GOAT. Although the class material is remarkable on its own, what made ES–156 one of the best courses I have taken at Harvard was Professor Calmon. His love for the subject matter was contagious, and he was very accessible and flexible outside of class.

The PSETs progressively became more difficult, and the final exam was very tough, but I felt that I learned a ton in class. I have nothing but positive things to say about my experience.
*As a disclaimer, I took this class with Professor Calmon, so this advice might not hold true for a different professor. This class is not easy. The problems sets are difficult and math heavy. I recommend starting early and going to office hours. With that said, this class is also very interesting. We learned not only a lot of the math behind signals but also a lot of the math behind communication systems. Professor Calmon is the best professor I have had so far and likely the best I will ever have. He is incredibly knowledgeable and his lectures are clear, organized, and engaging. He is also down–to–earth and very understanding.
This is an incredible course whether you are concentrating in EE or not. You learn a ton about signals and communication. The course content is difficult but very rewarding to understand. Professor Calmon is one of the best lecturers I have had.
This is a good course. The more math–adjacent parts of EE are generally quite fascinating and powerful, with signals being no exception. Flavio is very kind and knowledgeable.
