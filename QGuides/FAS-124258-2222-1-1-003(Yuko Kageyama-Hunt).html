What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is simply amazing. Unlike most other classes at Harvard, I am never looking at the clock during Japanese class and wondering when the class will end. Kageyama Sensei, Nakazato Sensei, and Takehara Sensei keep all of us engaged throughout the entire class, and I always can pinpoint what exactly I learned or improved upon after class. My language skills have improved immensely. As someone who has only taken STEM classes at Harvard, Japanese BB allowed me to meet a diverse group of people who are all extremely passionate about Japanese and make a lot of friends. The instructors put a tremendous amount of effort into the class and exhibit a lot of passion for the material. As a result, you will find that your peers also put in a lot of effort and actually care about the class material. The workload is very manageable and spread out. If you are on the fence about Japanese, please take it! You will not regret it, I promise.
There is no way that Harvard's JAPANBa & Bb courses are not the very best introductory Japanese courses in the nation (and probably the world). If you have any interest in the language, then I would say the best time to start learning would be now.
JUST TAKE IT YOU WILL NOT REGRET!
Such a fun class. Definitely difficult but it is so fun to progress from learning Japanese in BA to finally feeling like you are able to start speaking in BB
