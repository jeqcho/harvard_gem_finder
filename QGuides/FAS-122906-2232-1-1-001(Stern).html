What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
A great course that pushes you as an actor! Requires time outside of class to find material and rehearse with scene partners, so be sure you can commit to this time. The class environment is very supportive and it is very rewarding!
Take it!
Take this course if you are at all interested in improving your acting! You will get out of this class exactly what you put in. I spent a lot of time and effort in finding material, memorizing it, and practicing it, which meant this was actually one of my more stressful classes but it meant that I got so much out of this class and feel like I have really grown as an actor. Getting constant feedback on your acting and getting to use interesting techniques was so meaningful to me and the community of the class was so supportive and great. Couldn't recommend this class enough if you are at all interested in acting and getting constant opportunities to work on acting!
Most useful TDM class I've taken at Harvard hands down. It really forces you to be purposeful in all of your scenes and monologues, and Marcus and Therese are immensely insightful on how you can grow. It is quite the time commitment –– you are performing basically every other week off–book –– so know that coming in. But if you can put in the hours to memorize, it's very rewarding.
Take this class if you are passionate about acting, want to improve your delivery, or know how to be more in touch with your emotions. Marcus and Therese are so understanding and kind – if you would like the course to be more engaging and collaborative, I think you could ask for it. They are the type of teachers who care about students and their feedback.
