I would tell future students to take this course and try to have Israel as your TF because he is amazing.
This course is a review of Spanish 20 in terms of grammar, but AT A FASTER PACE for the sake of focusing on SPEAKING. Would TOTALLY recommend taking this after Spanish 20 because you TRULY solidify your skills in grammar while most importantly getting more comfortable speaking on the spot.
This class is great. The workload is really manageable, you get more practice with grammar and learn some new concepts. If you have taken Spanish 10–20 (or a couple years in high school), you shouldn't stress about taking this class. It's great practice and you'll be prepared for it. Not much different in structure from the earlier Spanish courses. I would strongly recommend!
I think this class is a great way to improve your Spanish speaking abilities, but you have to be willing to consistently put in work to stay on top of weekly assignments and the periodical exams.
I highly recommend this class if you want to continue developing your Spanish, you won't be disappointed. It feels more like a launchpad than a stepping stone as you come into the class with a more thorough understanding of the language, thus, you'll find yourself developing your voice in the language and have more complex conversations.
