What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is hard work, but is very illuminating and well worth the effort. Carefully designed pedagogically, Professor Perry is an amazing professor who will really help illuminate your knowledge of comparative politics in China. It has helped me gain a deeper appreciation for China as a site for studying comparative politics, and opened up a broader range of new research ideas to explore.
For students genuinely interested in contemporary Chinese politics, this is a class must take. The workload is heavy but worth it for those who want to do serious and in–depth studies on Chinese politics. The framework is pretty political science style, so basic familiarity with such a discipline would be helpful.
It is a challenging class and the reading materials require a lot of time to digest. But you can learn a lot from them.
