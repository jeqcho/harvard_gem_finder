Really really enjoyed this class –– definitely one of my top 3 classes at Harvard. The topics we covered are so important and it's really cool to see how economists have been approaching the problems. This is one of the only classes where I've felt like the content actually /mattered/ in real life. The final paper is super tough and it's a good idea to get started early. I would definitely recommend this class if you're considering writing a thesis –– the class gives you some experience doing your own research if you haven't done that before and also is probably a good indicator if you'll actually enjoy the thesis process.
