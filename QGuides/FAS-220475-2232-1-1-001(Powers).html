I would recommend taking this course because it is interesting, there's a fun mock trial, and it's not a stressful workload at all. I do think though that sometimes the discussions felt stagnant and the course material didn't always follow what I felt I needed to complete the case studies. So just having patients and doing outside research was necessary. Also, I would be on top of checking on assignments and the syllabus (just being proactive)!
I recommend! I got to see another side of Psychology that I had not otherwise been exposed to. Learning about psychology in a professional, legal setting was incredibly fascinating, even if I don't see myself going into law in the future. **Content warning: violence, trauma, SA, school shootings. These topics were not too difficult for me to handle, but I think it's worth mentioning in case any of them may be upsetting for you.
This course is a light workload and incredibly interesting. Dr. Davis is amazing and cares about the work that she does.
Take this class!! I want to go into law, and this class changed the way that I think about so many important issues within the criminal justice domain. It also showed me how I might be able to integrate psychology into the work that I do. The field of law always benefits from having more people who understand psychology (as we discuss in class!), so if you want to go into law, this is a great opportunity to learn things that will help you make a positive impact. That said, ANYONE and everyone can benefit from and enjoy this class – regardless of what you have studied in the past, and regardless of where you want to go moving forward.
Definitely take this course, Dr. Davis is extremely knowledgeable about the subject matter and you will learn about so many different aspects of the criminal justice system and how psychology is relevant to each of these. You will also get to participate in a mock trial, which was one of the most fun assignments I've done at Harvard.
This is a great class. The topic is very interesting, and the professor has so much experience to draw on and share. The assignments can take a little time (about 2 hours a week), but are not hard if you have done the readings and paid attention in class.
This was such a great course! Dr. Davis is an amazing teacher and has so much professional knowledge and life experience to share with us. We had such interesting conversations about all the topics on the syllabus and I'm really looking forward to our mock trial at the end of the semester. Hands down my favorite class that I took this semester and honestly the only class I've taken this semester where I haven't been distracted by other work (because the class material and the discussions we have are just so interesting)!
I highly recommend this course. Dr. Davis is extremely knowledgeable and experienced in this field, and the mock trial we did at the end of the semester was a lot of fun.
