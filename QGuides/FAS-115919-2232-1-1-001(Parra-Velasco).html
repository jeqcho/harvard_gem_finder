Take this class to connect to the community and to learn Spanish in a different and better way than you are used to
This class is time consuming. It's 3hrs of community service per week on top of the class work. That being said, it was an incredible experience that I wouldn't trade for the world. Working in the community gives you such a unique experience and really gives you the chance to use and improve your Spanish skills. I can say, without a doubt, that I can speak and understand much better now than at the beginning of the semester. I can also say that I feel like I was able to make a difference in the community because of this class. Don't let the commitment scare you away because this class is worth every second of it.
If you are interested in the culture, history, and hardships of the Hispanic community, take this course! You get to meet wonderful individuals around the community, and you might finally be able to feel comfortable enough to use Spanish outside of the schooling bubble. :)
