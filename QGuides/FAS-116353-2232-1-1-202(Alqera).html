TAKE THIS CLASS!!**** You will learn so much about mindfulness that you never knew about, but what makes this class so special is that it really translates directly into your own world. I took this class second semester and I was stressed but this class taught me amazing strategies that I was able to apply to my life to learn how to deal with life in general and struggles that are out of your hands. Each class starts with 5 minutes of meditation, so keep an open mind with the process because you will really take a lot away from it. Also, there are some weeks with a lot of work but generally it seems much fairer than other expos classes. Its a diamond in the rough. Also, the discussion are always meaningful and generally fun.
Of the expos classes you could take, this is one of the best! The course readings are shorter but very interesting/varied compared to other classes, and the discussions in class are always really good. Unit 2 (research paper) is probably the most work you'll do in the course for a couple of weeks, but as long as you're on top of it you'll do well as the third unit is a capstone project instead. There's only a short assignment each week to turn in, and classes feel very fun to be in. There's also a meditation to start each class which is super fun and useful for daily life. Sheza is very kind and gives a lot of feedback on papers, so there is a lot to work with when revising. She also bases your deadline of the final draft off when you receive your paper, so you'll definitely have a fair amount of time to revise. Sections were probably my favorite compared to my other courses. Definitely take this expos if you're interested in self improvement, reflection, the mind, or sports!! You'll have a great time.
