I heavily recommend taking this class!!! I always thought that the border was simply a physical barrier separating two countries, but I learned that there are many types of borders such as those of language, culture, race, education, health, socioeconomics, etc. I learned a lot about the history of Mexico and the border and how people in power have taken advantage and spun the narrative to their advantage, while framing indigenous and Mexican people as inferior. I learned a lot personally and became aware of some of my own biases and how to change them.
