What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Mora is an incredible instructor and generates great enthusiasm for the material. He always encourages intellectual discussion and creates a space for students to really develop as philosophical thinkers.
I highly recommend this class. Not only is Professor Mora one of the best teachers on campus, but this class in particular is amazing at developing your view of how groups should function. It has changed and informed my views on many aspects of my life, and going forward I will make sure to interrogate the issues around me in the same way this class has taught me to.
Cool topics. Emilio is a very engaged instructor and really cares about your success. Expect to receive a lot of feedback on your writing. The workload is really up to you since you decide when to turn in reading responses, which is nice.
If Emilio Mora is teaching any class, TAKE IT. He is an absolute gem of the Philosophy department and of Harvard in general. This class was another excellent one and helped me think more deeply about minority group rights in relation to liberal political theory. In a political climate with so many heated arguments, this class was a breath of fresh air, helping us to think about these issues calmly, rationally, and critically. Outside of class, readings and response papers do not take a lot of time (2 hrs. per week just readings, 3–4 hrs. per week if writing a response paper as well as doing readings), and the flexibility in the assignments is incredibly helpful. Anyone can take this class, no background required. However, some writing experience may be useful because there's not a ton of formal scaffolding around how to write the response papers (however, going to office hours or talking to Emilio would surely help if you find yourself needing guidance). Take this class if you want to engage in interesting discussion, receive extensive feedback on your writing, and grow as a thinker and scholar.
