What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you like research, take this class –– you'll read and discuss lots of real studies and real research. But that's it, if you're hoping to gain knowledge like you would from a traditional lecture class (i.e. coming out of the course with a grasp on new material) I don't think this class is necessarily for you. Just a different approach to studying science!
It is a really good class for the methodologies of the life sciences but the content can be a little dry at times.
Please keep in mind that this course is for half credit. You have to take this for a full year to get full credit. You are assigned an article every week, and have to annotate the article on perusall, and have one assignment per week. This is on the neural basis of feeding behavior –– you have to be really passionate about this topic to engage with it on such an in–depth level. Sam and Sofia are great!! Be prepared for a lot of feedback on your assignments it is designed to prepare you for your final assignment and be prepared to talk about the article in class. Understanding the figures helps!
