Take this class! Aaron is the best, and he does a great job of interrogating conservatism through a fascinating historical lens without spending too much time on Trump or the current Republican Party (though the class does touch on the present a bit). Still, if you're interested in politics or American History, I really can't suggest a better class or instructor than Aaron.
If you are conservative or not, take this class
Aaron is honestly a pretty good guy, and class discussions are super fruitful. That being said, unless the readings changed, I'm hesitant to recommend. 200–300 pages of reading is pretty on par for a history seminar, but there are literally 0 primary sources, which is a massive turnoff; you'll be reading randomly selected chapters from dense history books instead of real primary sources, which is quite frustrating. Additionally, the class could have been constructed a lot better; much of the class was structured in its build–up to Ronald Reagan, but we never quite got there. limiting the scope of the class and introducing more primary sources would go a long way.
