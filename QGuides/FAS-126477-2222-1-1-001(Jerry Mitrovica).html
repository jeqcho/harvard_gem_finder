What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is great. It teaches a lot of interesting details about the science of sea level change. Jerry and Marisa are both great, and create a really friendly class environment. The assignments are not very difficult, and the teaching staff is always willing to give a lot of help.
The math can be a little heavy but the instructor and TF will give you more than enough help to get through it.
I would tell future students to not be concerned about the math or coding because Jerry and Marissa did such a good job of teaching everything you need to know. I would also tell future students that even if they aren’t super enthusiastic to learn about sea level physics, to take the class anyway because it was fun and enjoyable.
Unless you have an amazing TF, don't take this course.
Interesting and engaging class. Jerry is a funny, dynamic lecturer. So many interesting questions to ask about paleo and modern sea level rise that I always left with more questions than when I went in. Light work load, with the bulk of the work being writing a script to calculate a sea level fingerprint from a melting glacier.
