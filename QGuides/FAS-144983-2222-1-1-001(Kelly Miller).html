If you're on here, you have to take this course for the SB, so I'll just give some advice on how to make this experience less painful for you. As stated in previous Q guides, I think the main factor that will determine your experience in ES 96 is your instructor. Depending on the instructor, you will receive more or less support throughout the project, more or less encouragement, and more or less work outside of class time. The last point is the most important because with a great instructor your section will be very efficient with doing work for the class DURING class, and you will not fall behind or need as much prep work outside of class. You will most likely not get to choose your section, but you will have a great experience if you're in Kelly's section because she's super nice, super sweet, very encouraging, and has taught the course so many times that she knows where the class is supposed to be at a specific point in time. That means that you won't fall behind in the project and class time is used very effectively to meet goals, compared to other sections that have had to meet multiple times a week outside of class to catch up with the syllabus. Also, hopefully you are able to get along with your teammates really well (or learn how to).
