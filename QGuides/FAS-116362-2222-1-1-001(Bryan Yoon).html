I had SUCH good conversations with my classmates about environmental issues which is typical of most environmental communities.
ALSO, ESE6 doesn't have any quizzes or written assignments which is so so nice because it emphasizes that you really are just there to learn not for a grade. It takes all of the pressure off. The only assignments are a few coding–based collaborative homework and a final project which you can tailor to any environmental interest that you wish to explore. I would definitely recommend this class.
