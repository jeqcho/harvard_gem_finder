This course truly prioritizes experiential learning like no other. Before this course, I had a bit of a fear of critters (as someone who didn't really grow up around them). Through our lab component––where we got to hold and interact with animals on a weekly basis–– and our epic trip to Costa Rica, I felt this fear transform into pure joy and curiosity. I learned how to open my heart to the critters and experience them in all their glory. There is really nothing like learning from the animals themselves and experiencing first–hand the wisdom that they have to share.
This course forever changed my views on herpetological critters and opened my mind and heart in a totally unexpected, but beautiful, way.
I am a graduating senior and this is the best course I have taken at Harvard and the best class I have ever taken in my life. Jim is such a wonderful person and is an incredible lecturer. He is humorous and generates great enthusiasm for herpetology. The teaching staff was amazing. I can say with confidence that the spring break field trip to Costa Rica was the best week of my life, and I experienced many beautiful moments with amazing people. The whole structure of the course and the sequence of lectures was super engaging and made it easy to follow along. The guest lectures were super cool. Labs were incredible, as we got to work with dozens of incredible specimens. Please take this course. I am begging you.
