Great class with great structure, though the workload can be tricky if you have no Chinese background. Similar to a high school style language class. The drill sections are super fun, you will make friends. I was always excited to go to drill section, and even enjoyed lectures (though there is less student–talking)
This class is SO FUN! Just like Chinese Ba, there is a fair amount of work that you need to stay on top of, but if you do so you will improve so so much. Also the professors are all incredible and so kind.
– Spend the week before classes start reviewing all of semester 1. You hit the ground running in BB with no handholding or review.

Take this class! I knew no Chinese before the BA/BB series and am now able to write short papers, read passages, and converse with friends in Chinese. The progress that you will see in this course, especially coming out of BA is great. You will definitely feel more comfortable and confident with the language after the semester. The class is a lot of work, but finding a good study method that works for you (I really liked quizlet for the new vocab) will help with the dictations and preparing for class. Reach out to your teachers if you feel behind! The teaching staff and overall community is amazing and I have made great friends through this class.
