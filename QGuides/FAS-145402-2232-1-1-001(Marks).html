Fun class if you want to do some playwriting!
take if you can – you will grow and learn so much
Absolutely take this class!! Sam is a great professor and it is so useful to have a class that makes you do the writing. Writing a longer piece and doing the Harvard Playwrights Festival is scary, but it is so so fun and so useful. You learn so much from the workshops. I fell off of reading the plays at some point during the semester, but they are so worth reading – it's only like an hour a week of readings and you really do learn a lot from them.
tl;dr TAKE THIS CLASS
If you have any interest in theater or playwriting, you should 100% take this class! Sam is a wonderful professor and the class is very well–structured. Every week you'll read a play and having a writing assignment. At the beginning of the semester, it's shorter writing assignments and then you get to write a full one–act play which gets read at the Harvard Playwright's Festival. Getting to write every week and then getting to see your work performed is such a rewarding experience and I learned so much from the whole process.
