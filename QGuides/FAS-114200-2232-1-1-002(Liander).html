What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is a great Spanish class! It is not super challenging, and you will learn so much about the four countries of focus. If you have a requirement to fulfill or just looking to maintain your Spanish skills, this is a fun class to do just that.
The workload consists of a reading (or analysis of a movie, song, painting, etc.) 3 times a week, commentary on current events 3 or four times a semester, creative compositions/essays, 2 oral exams, and one class presentation. While it seems like a lot, I think the course heads to a pretty decent job of spreading everything out, and it's truly not bad at all. Highly recommend!!
The class seems to depend a lot on which TF you get. The homework readings definitely range in difficulty but do not get discouraged. Your Spanish speaking skills will improve a lot.
