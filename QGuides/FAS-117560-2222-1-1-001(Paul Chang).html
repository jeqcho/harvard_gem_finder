This course is a pretty easy introduction to the various methods of Sociology. Paul Chang is an amazing and engaging lecturer, even on Zoom. I also think he does a good job to pick really good readings for the syllabus. It is honestly worth while to read as much as you can. The assignments were a bit boring since you could not pick the topics, but they were overall easy to do. The course mainly focuses on the pros and cons of each method rather than the in–depth intricacies. This maybe a good course if you are still trying to figure out which methods to use for a thesis. However, if you already know what methods you want to use, maybe other in–depth courses would be better.
