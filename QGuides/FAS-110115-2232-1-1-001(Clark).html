JOIN GLEE CLUB
Joining the Glee Club was the best decision I made coming to Harvard. Not only is the process of singing in choir tremendously rewarding, it's given me a great social network and sense of belonging. Join the Glee Club – you will not regret it!

Nobody's reading a Glee Club evaluation on the Q guide, but here goes: Join Glee Club if you like to sing, and don't mind the bro culture. It's not as demanding as a cappella, and you'll be able to sing all the fight songs and anthems on a big stage and go on free tours. Unfortunately, you'll have to come to rehearsals on Friday afternoons and occasionally sing awful songs, but if you can like the culture and don't mind three–rehearsals–a–week you'll love it.
I found an amazing community of singers, mathematicians, computer scientists and more. I got to sing a great repertoire—I actively loved every piece we sang. Touring in the Dominican Republic was an amazing and eye–opening experience. Amazing performance Verdi's Requiem. Very grateful to have the privilege of singing in the group.
Glee Club is a great opportunity to see friends quite often and improve on singing and general music making abilities on a consistent basis. I loved learning and growing through singing in this choir and the yearly tour is awesome and a great opportunity to learn more about the world and other people in the Glee Club. Highly Recommend.
If you have ANY interest in singing on this campus at all, check out the Harvard choruses!!
