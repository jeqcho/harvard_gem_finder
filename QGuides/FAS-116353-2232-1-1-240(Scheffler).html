This is a top tier Expos section. Adam gives great feedback, the readings are short and interesting, and the discussions are laidback and fun. Take this class!
Great class, Adam makes the class exciting and never fails to disappoint with his in class activities. He also gives great draft feedback and really helps to point you in directions he thinks will work out well. The readings are also very engaging, I've never had a boring read. When writing your papers, you get to choose from a wide selection of texts, except for the second essay, where your lens is required to be Falconer, though you get to choose which ideas you want to borrow from her. In general, there is a lot of freedom with how you want to approach the papers. There is one required movie viewing which is a bit gory, but besides that, you don't need to have a particularly strong stomach.
Take this class! At points it can definitely be challenging, but every expos course is to a certain extent. As long as you participate and really engage with the material, the professor will be very helpful. I was hesitant about this topic at first and was not happy, but I grew to love the course.
This class is a must–take for Expos. Adam is an amazing professor who understands you are busy outside of his class, so he does not try to overwhelm you with work and is very understanding. He offers several extensions that you can earn and gets rid of excess assignments that are found in other Expos courses. The discussion is extremely engaging and fun (it's about Hell after all), and honestly it has been one of my favorite classes from freshman year. Definitely take it
Definitely take this expos it’s one of the few ones that people really enjoy and Adam is really lovely and all the readings and discussions are very interesting.
As a STEM concentrator, I adored this class. Adam is such a fantastic instructor; he gave me some of the best feedback I've ever received on any writing I've done. It is a course with quite a bit of reading, but I still highly recommend this course; you can make your Expos requirement much, much worse.
