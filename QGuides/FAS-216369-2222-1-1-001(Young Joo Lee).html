Have fun! And be brave
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! Manageable course load with a lot to learn. You will literally be working with cutting–edge technologies.
Take this class!!!! Young is a wonderful instructor and our TA this year (Aubrey) was really helpful. Young is really insightful in critiques and her feedback really gave me great guidance when I was stuck on my project. I really loved the screenings and the guest lectures too, though I will say the course is slightly heavier on the guest lectures and lighter on actual workshop/studio time (except for the last three–ish weeks).

Like all AFVS classes, this class will swell to fill up as much time as you can possibly dedicate to it. Unity/C#/Blender experience isn't *required*, but I found that having programming experience in the past REALLY helped me (both in terms of understanding online tutorials about how to do a certain thing and also adapting that tutorial to my project). There's great flexibility in how technically ambitious you want your project to be, though, because what's most important is just the cohesiveness of the vision. This class was definitely one of the more formative experiences of my academic life here and I would highly recommend it :)
The class is an exciting introduction to virtual reality, but it covers a broader breadth of creating immersive experiences. The class covers multiple tools to achieve this. It can be a fulfilling course to get adequate exposure to attaining technical still and historical context to the various mediums of XR.
