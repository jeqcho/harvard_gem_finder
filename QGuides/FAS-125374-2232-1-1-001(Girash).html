What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
One course you got to take when at Harvard is this one. This is a fantastic course that gives you a break from technical materials, and focuses on teaching, you have lots of interactions with other students, you get to see how they teach and you learn a lot from that experience. You have lots of opportunities to practice short teaching on the board and get feedback
Would strongly recommend! This is a relaxed class that gives nice teaching exposure and John is a fantastic person.
I strongly recommend taking the course for the following reasons: 1) The skills you learn are transferable to other areas of academic life, such as interviews and conference presentations. It is worth the investment 2) Some of the skills, such as learning to organize your thoughts in a way that is easier to process for others, are helpful in your personal life as well. You explicitly practice it in the form of whiteboard organization, etc. 3) The course workload is very low 4) If you are an international student, it might improve your speaking confidence as well. The class atmosphere is healthy, and you will not feel judged but only get honest feedback.
