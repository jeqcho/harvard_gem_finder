This course does cold–call, but the professors are excellent and the structure of the class really allowed me to practice speaking spontaneously. This was my first Spanish class since taking AP Spanish in high school (it had been 2.5 years since my last course) and this was a great reintroduction. Grading is very low pressure and there are opportunities to fix all of your mistakes in writing assignments.

Movies were sometimes hard to keep up with every weekend, especially without Spanish subtitles (this would be really helpful for future iterations of the course), but they are all great picks.
