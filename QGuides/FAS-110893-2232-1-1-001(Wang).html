This was an amazing course and I definitely recommend it to anyone, even (perhaps especially non–concentrators). Prof Wang's informative, engaging, and insightful lectures did an amazing job of not only teaching us the material, but reinforcing the historical context of Chinese politics and why it's important to study this nowadays. Assignments were very chill, and while it was sometimes a bit difficult to stay up to date with all of the readings (but when is it ever easy?), the well–organised structure of the overall class made it simple to understand the motivation behind the topics we were studying as well as their application to our understanding of Chinese history and current events.
