What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course is a fantastic introduction to Korean and a great first step to take if your goal is to eventually become fluent. At the same time, this means that the class will take a decent amount of effort and work, but in opinion is ultimately very worth it. You also get to meet a bunch of nice Korean (and quite a few non–Korean) people!
This class is very rewarding. It is very engaging and fun. It does take a lot of time commitments with 5 classes a week, but classes are interactive which makes it worth it. One of. my favorite classes. Highly recommend!
This class is worth taking! It's been one of the most rewarding classes I've taken. The staff will help you push through some of the challenging conjugations in such a fun and encouraging way. I've had so much fun laughing at the final projects you get to create in this course. I wish I had started taking Korean classes at Harvard sooner!
There will always be students who have significantly more or less experience with Korean than you do, so don't feel bad about reaching out for help. Everyone comes into the class at a different point in their learning journey.
I recommend knowing your learning style and memorization style for this class. The class isn't hard to follow, so long you have a set study schedule. The faculty is very welcoming and helpful, so don't be afraid to ask. It is a bit of a learning curve at first, but once you establish yourself, you should do well.
This class is a lot of fun, make sure to throw yourself in without hesitation.
The Korean language is no joke, and you shouldn't treat this class like one. It is intense. If you're prepared for that and are willing to put in the work, it will be a very rewarding class. Otherwise, you may feel pretty overwhelmed, especially at the beginning of the class.
