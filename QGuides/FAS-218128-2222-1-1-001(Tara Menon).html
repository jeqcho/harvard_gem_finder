This class is a fun English elective and the books we read were all super interesting. Professor Menon is really great at choosing just the right things to talk about in each novel, so class is always extremely engaging and there was never a dull moment. The assignments were mostly creative assignments, so it was definitely nice to read a classic novel and not have to write a 10–page paper about a central theme. Highly recommend taking this class, or even just any class with Professor Menon.
This is a delightful class about the Victorian Novel but more than that, it is a class about writing, making arguments, and developing the capacity to understand/critique a text. More than any other English class I've taken at Harvard, this class is accessible to students new to the humanities or seniors concentrating in English. The class is rigorous but establishes a set of common terms and strategies for working through difficult texts that are useful for a student at any level. The assignments are manageable and very clear expectations for the amount of time one should spend completing them are set. The reading load is heavy but enjoyable. Most importantly, Professor Menon is the most exciting, encouraging, supportive professor I have ever worked with. Her lectures consistently brought students out of 9am fog and into complex arguments and were a highlight of each week. I couldn't recommend taking a class with her more.
