It's an awesome class overall using the world as a framework for speaking and using Italian. Know that there won't be many in the class so willingly grow closer with your class and prof
This class is excellent. It not only improved my knowledge of the Italian language, but also opened my eyes to issues of sustainability in Italy and beyond. We covered a range of topics, from sustainable fashion to the migrant crisis in Europe. At the same time, the coursework and small group discussions helped me grow as a writer and a speaker. I highly recommend this class!
Take this class if you want to be challenged and continue learning Italian! Chiara is a fantastic professor, but be warned she will push you and it will not be easy. Assignments took me at least an hour each to complete– usually longer– and she grades quite strictly. However, this is not a reason to be deterred! You will learn a lot in only one semester. Best language class I've taken so far.
This class is eye–opening for sure! It will put your Italian to the test, as you will have to present and write often, but it really strengthens your ability to apply different grammatical aspects and talk about topics that intrigue you!
Italian 30 is a great course for those looking to improve their Italian. I would really recommend that you engage with the compiti assignments; they allow you to approach the language in a unique way relative to what you may be used to. I would also recommend taking good notes during the class, especially on the words, phrases, and grammatical structures that come up during discussion.
This is a wonderful class in which you learn about several aspects of Italian culture and sustainable living while having fun and meeting great people. Since the sections are generally small, you get very close to the people in them and it is much easier to study something when you are in a friendly environment rather than in a large classroom. Additionally, the course had a great structure for learning grammar and vocabulary, making for a smooth and enjoyable process.
