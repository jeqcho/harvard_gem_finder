What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This seminar was amazing!!!! Professor Oja was absolutely amazing and I wish there was a way to take another one of her classes. This subject matter is so interesting and was such a breath of fresh air to take.
This class was definitely the highlight of my spring semester. How often do you get to take a class where all you do is listen / watch Broadway musicals and discuss their cultural impact? Not only was it a lot of fun, but I also felt that I took so much away from every single conversation and discussion that we held in class. Professor Oja is incredible; Getting to learn from her and hearing her perspective on some pretty significant moments in Broadway history was such a pleasure. There was also something so beautiful about having such a small, intimate class setting where everyone was able to share their thoughts and participate in meaningful conversations. Getting to learn about such historically significant productions in such an interactive format – watching clips, reading analyses, exploring interviews, and sometimes even getting to experience our own staging sessions – was an incredibly rewarding experience. If you have any interest in Broadway, musical theater, or just want to step out of your comfort zone and take a new class, I 100% recommend this seminar!
This class is AMAZING!!!!! Please take it if you have any interest at all in musicals, theater, music, or history. You get to just talk about musicals all class long and you also get to participate in acting/singing while staging some scenes. It really is a great course and a very supportive environment. Plus, all of the assignments are extremely manageable. TAKE THIS COURSE!!!
