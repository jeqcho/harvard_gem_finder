If you are interested in learning more about the ways in which you can use your voice when performing (spoken text rather than singing), I highly recommend this class. Erika is a fantastic teacher and the environment she creates in the classroom is so encouraging. I never felt uncomfortable taking risks in trying out new things and I learned so much about how to use my voice when performing. This class is a great option for anyone who wants to hone their vocal skills in public settings, whether that's on the stage in a play or delivering a presentation at a conference. If you're willing to be a little silly and see where your voice can go, this class is for you!
