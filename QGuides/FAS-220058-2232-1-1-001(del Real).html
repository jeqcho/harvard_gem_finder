What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Plan ahead! Archival visits require scheduling in–advance.
If you are passionate about archival work, architecture, or (ideally) both, this class is for you. It inspires days upon days of only thinking about your chosen research topic and returning again and again to the archive with more questions.
This course provided a perfect balance between theory and practice, exploring the topic of the archive—in particular the architectural archive—through readings, archival visits, research, and a workshop–style curatorial project. Through engaging conversations with students and professionals in the field, this class expanded the perspective on the history, function, types, uses, and implications of the archive in relation to the past and the future of architecture.
Professor del Real was a fantastic instructor. He created a very collaborative environment where every student supported one another and was very engaged with the work of everybody in the class.
