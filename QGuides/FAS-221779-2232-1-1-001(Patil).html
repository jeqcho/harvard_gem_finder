If you are interested in metaphysics, epistemology, philosophy of language and philosophy of mind and want to explore these fields in the South Asian context, you should definitely take this course.
To the future student who knows of Indian philosophy, I say two things:
The material covered in this couse will be much more similar to Western philosophy than you expect.
Depending on your interest in the particular topics covered, you may find the course to be anything from very interesting to very boring.
Really excellent class with an excellent professor.
Dr. Patil will really help you to achieve your learning goals and do well in the class. Be mindful of your philosophy knowledge, though – a lack thereof can be a setback.
You should take this class!!!
This course covers some super interesting material and class discussions are always great. Professor Patil is very responsive to questions and does a good job of breaking down concepts. The course was a bit structurally disorganized, which sometimes made it difficult to gauge how well I was grasping the material (e.g., feedback on assignments could have been a bit more timely & organized). But I would definitely recommend if you are interested in Indian philosophy at all.
