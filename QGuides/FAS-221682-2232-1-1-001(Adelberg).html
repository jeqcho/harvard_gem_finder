Jeff and Amanda are super fun. It feels less like a class and more like a fun learning club with fantastic mentors. Go ahead and try something new! They'll be happy to help you.
SUCH a fun class, highly recommend to anyone who wants to get familiar with the practical implementation of lighting and/or learn more about the behind–the–scenes of theater. Instructors are working professional lighting designers who have a great perspective because they're outside the Harvard bubble, and they're super welcoming and willing to work with students of all experience levels. Course is harder if you have no experience or familiarity with theater or design, but still very doable. Project–based work, so the more you put in the more you get out.
Great class! Take this class if you're interested in thinking about lighting design and how to represent abstract designs in concrete terms. Jeff and Amanda are wonderful & an absolute dream team.
