What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I absolutely recommend this course! Professor Scarry is absolutely lovely and the class brought together students from an array of backgrounds which generated fantastic and thought–provoking discussions. The readings were carefully chosen and varied, and the class had just the right amount of structure and organization. There really is no one better to learn from about beauty!
This is a course as much if not more on aesthetics and philosophy as it is on literature. Elaine is extremely learned and creative and class discussions are wonderful. Dense reading.
Because the course covers such a breadth of texts, some of the readings will resonate more or less with your interests, and that's okay. Don't feel pressure to write a paper in response to a text if it's not an idea you aren't interested in pursuing. But, I would also suggest using the short papers as an opportunity to ask questions and test out different arguments.
I believe you'll get the most out of the class if you engage with it on a personal as well as an intellectual level.
An amazing course on aesthetics!
