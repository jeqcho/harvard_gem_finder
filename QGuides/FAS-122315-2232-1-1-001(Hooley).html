Class is one of my favorites. The discussion was always nice, and the professors do really care about your writing and your future in Psych!
I was nervous initially about taking 975 because I had no neuroscience background, but the only way I saw neuroscience incorporated into the course was through the readings. So if you're nervous about taking 975 because of the neuro aspect, don't fear because really this class isn't about learning content but rather how to write psychological research papers. With that being said, make sure you manage your time well because especially if you're taking 1901 simultaneously, you'll have two big papers due around the same time during the semester and you only have four 24–hour extensions to use on specific assignments.
I actually really enjoyed this course. It's a requirement for all psych concentrators so you don't have a choice, but the small class size is really nice, and it builds towards the final paper really well. There are a bunch of assignments but not an overwhelming amount, and they build on each other and prepare you for the final paper really well. I feel like my writing improved in this course, and it was a helpful course. Very TF dependent, but I loved mine. Feedback provided is also so helpful and detailed.
