Take the class! Professor Wilson is a great teacher! It's such an interesting subject and really exciting to engage in discussion. The readings are totally fair and the assignments feel genuinely productive towards your final paper. **
This is a great class. I have never felt so excited to do readings and talk about what I've learned. Prof Wilson is great at facilitating discussion and community. You will feel heard and challenged.
Cold War Science is amazing! Professor Wilson selected fascinating readings spanning the Cold War to really give you a sense of not only the history but all the ways in which history and science are woven together. The readings were all fascinating and well–selected but not overwhelming (there is no reading just for the sake of assigning reading, everything we read was genuinely informative and impactful). Also, the topics each week led to engaging conversations and Professor Wilson did a great job moderating, bringing in new opinions, and encouraging people to connect various readings or ideas from the course together. I have never had a seminar–style class this effective at generating thought–provoking discussions in my time at Harvard. Overall could not recommend this class, or anything taught by Professor Wilson, more highly! Whether you just want to learn more about the cold war (I knew nothing going in), are interested in nuclear physics, or any other reason, you will walk away from this class with new understandings and perspectives!
amazing course, best seminar i've taken at harvard.
This was a really fantastic course that was excellently taught! I think that Professor Wilson did a really great job facilitating discussions.
Take this course! This was one of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard –– one of those truly 'Harvard' classes full of fantastic readings and brilliant discussion. Professor Wilson has a brilliant mastery of the subject matter, and has curated an excellent set of some of the most interesting and thought provoking readings I've seen. You will learn so much about not just the Cold War, but the history of American science and foundations in technology, and how the Cold War shaped America in the 1940s –1960s all the way to today.
An absolutely fantastic and interesting course! Understanding how much of the basis of modern science is tied into the military–indunstrial complex of the Cold War is really interesting, and I think important to anyone trying to understand how modern science works (scientists and those on the outside looking in). Professor Wilson is an absolutely fantastic teacher, and one of the most talented moderators of conversations I've ever had the pleasure of taking a class from. He strikes the perfect balance of gently guiding and pushing conversation, while really letting students drive most class conversation.
I strongly recommend this course. It's thorough, but not crushing in terms of requirements. You will learn a lot about Cold War science but also have the flexibility to explore areas which interest you individually.
