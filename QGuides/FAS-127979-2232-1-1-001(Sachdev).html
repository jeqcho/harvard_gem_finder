Comments
Great course! It covers a lot of important topics in condensed matter / many body physics, and Subir is a great lecturer. He motivates the physics well, even if he skips a lot of steps in the derivations ("when the dust settles, you get ..."). The final project — a 30 min presentation — takes a lot of time, but I thought it was a valuable experience. I feel more confident reading papers and attending talks after taking the course.
Great course, Subir and Maria are excellent instructors. The content is very challenging but interesting.
This is a very vigorous course and covers a very good spectrum of topics in condensed matter physics. If you can learn all the techniques then you would be very prepared for research
Despite the 3 hour lectures once a week, this course was still really valuable. Learning Hartree–Fock properly, finally seeing Feynman diagrams in solid state physics, seeing a proper treatment of BCS theory, and getting an introduction to quantum spin liquids and highly entangled states were all essential components of my understanding of CMP in general. 10/10 would recommend
Consider whether the class is relevant to either your research or future class interests. The prerequisite knowledge should be taken seriously, as it may be difficult to grasp the physical intuition behind some of these concepts without them.
