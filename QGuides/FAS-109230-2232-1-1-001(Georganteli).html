Professor Georganteli is not only the kindest Professor I've ever met at Harvard, but is also an amazing instructor whose enthusiasm for the subject matter infects everybody. I learned so much about the history of the Crusades and medieval art history in this course and also experienced once in a life time field trips, such as a day trip to the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston and a weekend excursion to Dumbarton Oaks in WASHINGTON DC.
