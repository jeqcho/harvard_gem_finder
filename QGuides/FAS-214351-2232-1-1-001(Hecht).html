The final project was also a very fun way to integrate information from the course in a unique and creative way, and it was really fun to see other students' presentations.

AMAZING CLASS!! Erin is a fantastic professor. Youll learn so much about human brains and about other animals brains in a fun, low stress, engaging environment. The final projects were so creative (you get to do a brain evolution thought experiment of a fictional creature) and I loved seeing what other students came up with. Additionally, I think what sets this course apart from other brain–focused neuroscience or psychology courses is its emphasis on evolution and WHY brains of different species are the way they are. I found this focus unique and particularly interesting.
