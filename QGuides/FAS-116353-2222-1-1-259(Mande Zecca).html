This course will be so amazing if you truly are interested in horror and gothic literture, if not, it can still be a good course as the work load is very manageable and Dr Zecca produces such a nice, supportive and relaxed learning environment. Dr Zecca really makes expos feel like an enjoyable non–strenuous experience and will support you with your writing in any aspects you see fit. Do beware the assignments are demanding and perhaps a way to get through this course is avoiding to read and try engage with all the sources. Instead focus on one that you really find interesting for each unit and build your essay from that. Of course read them all briefly but try decide on the one quick it will be helpful to get the essays done.
This expos is S tier. If you enjoy horror stories or movies in any capacity, I would highly recommend this class. None of the assignments were cumbersome and Dr. Zecca is an incredible preceptor who's also a ton of fun. Can't recommend it enough!
