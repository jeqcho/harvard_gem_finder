I could not recommend this class more enthusiastically. This class is one of the only political studies classes engaged with indigenous studies, and it does so in a manner that is both reflective of classical and contemporary scholarship and deeply respectful of the sensitivity and vital importance of the subject material. Prof. Carpenter approaches the subject material respectfully, drawing on the knowledge and insights of a wide range of guest lecturers who significantly expand the class's breadth of knowledge. As a Mindich Programme class, the work is deeply rewarding and has a tangible, practical output that serves to provide real benefit to stakeholders while enabling students participating to grow at the same time. This is the most important Government class you will get the chance to take at this school.
