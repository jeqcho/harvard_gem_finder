What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
An amazing survey of systems biology! Cluzel is a kind, compassionate and deeply knowledgeable professor who will bring you through a series of fascinating substudies, combining physics math chemistry statistics and more! Really cool connections and mind blowing revelations will be made in this class, and the small size makes it intimate and easy to participate! Get prepared for a seminar style class and be ready to be wrong and right while learning!
The problems discussed in this class are actually pretty interesting, I think often problems in system biology can be solved just by a paradigm shift. For me, I got the most out of the class by this.

The class also has minimal time requirements as you do most of the assignments in class. I would say the class would be better if the professor shared the notes in class so that in case someone misses a class, they can follow it.

The French way of learning with about 5 peers is also very interesting.
Fantastic course. The professor is super engaged and down–to–earth. Classes are interactive, meaning you'll be engaged and won't fall asleep, but also really get a chance to work with the class material. The professor 'tests' us by having one–on–one conversations, where he really gets to understand each of us and how we work. Fundamentally, the class is about working together and exploring making models in biology. I normally don't write too much for these evaluations, so when I say this class is a great time, I really mean it!
This class covers very unique and interesting content!
