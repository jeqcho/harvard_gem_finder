What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
All astro concentrators should take this class! The content feels super useful and applicable to astronomy, and the assignments are super chill. The small size of the class made the learning feel very personalized, and Edo and Marissa (our amazing TF) were always available to help. Also there's a FREE TRIP TO ARIZONA!!!!! You literally get to observe in the mountains for 3 days –– it's so much fun! The final project is great too –– you get to analyze all the data you just collected (on the FREE trip to Arizona) using the tools you were taught and write up a report that feels like a culmination of all the work in the class. I cannot recommend this class enough!
This class is a really hands–on approach to learning, between doing worksheets in class to learn the astronomy software (Pyraf and DS9) and going to the observatory over spring break to collect the data. There is not very much work, so you don't have something forcing you to learn the material. You get out of this class what you put into it.
Edo and Marissa are incredible. The material is also really cool if you take the time to embrace it. The trip in spring break was also incredibly fun. Definitely recommend if you're interested and astro, and an engineer because its got the best of both worlds.
