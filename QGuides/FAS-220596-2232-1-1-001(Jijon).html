I would advise students that they should not expect to receive timely feedback from Professor Jijon nor should they expect clear or consistent expectations for coursework. However, much of the material is interesting and Professor Jijon invites students to participate class successfully. Students should be concerned, however, that many of the students taking this class are just looking for an easy grade rather than having an interest in the material, or having anything intelligent to say.
Great classroom environment, readings aren't too dense & class is discussion–heavy
**This is an amazing class period. Professor Jijon is such a good teacher and cares a lot about her students. She goes above and beyond to provide support and I really appreciate that. She provides good feedback, answers emails in a timely fashion, and wants us to succeed. The discussion posts were really low stress, just submitting 2 questions on the readings. Also, the readings are super interesting and fair in terms of page number (3 readings between 10–20 pages each a week). The questions are shared in class and are used for discussion which is amazing and engaging. Also, all of the assignments all lead towards the final paper, so even though it might seem like a lot of work and demanding to write a 15–20 page paper, most of the work is done by the end of the semester which is great. If you are lucky enough to take one of her classes I promise you won't be disappointed!!

Dr. Jijon was a wonderful lecturer and made the space enjoyable and welcoming for all, regardless of concentration.
