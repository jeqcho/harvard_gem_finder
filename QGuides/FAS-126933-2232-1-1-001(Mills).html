What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course is a great intro to french. you learn a lot of french through fun applications and activities, but if you really wanna build your vocab, its kinda on you to memorize it on your own, as you won't learn every single word in class. the workload for this class is not bad at all. the homework is always due on the day of the quiz so you have about a week and a half to two weeks to finish the packet and it only takes a few hours to do and grade. the compositions are a good way to study for the quizzes because they have you practice the vocab and grammar that will be on the quiz, so I recommend finishing them before you take the quiz. This class is also fun and has a couple of different field trips within Harvard square that you go on. I'd also recommend that you go to the French table that they host once a week. besides getting a bonus point on the quiz, you also get to meet other people taking french and practice your listening skills (though it does remind you how much you still have to learn in the language.) Overall, it's a good class that I am glad I took!
