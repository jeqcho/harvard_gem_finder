You are invited to push yourself, it is up to you to push beyond what is required for the class and if you do, you will be supported by your teacher and your peers. It is a fairly horizontal playing field.
This course was a lot of fun! You’ll get to do different performances yourself while learning about performance in general and how it can be applied outside an artistic setting. I feel it is good for people of any background, but I think you’d be able to contribute more if you or your classmates have some performance experience already.
TAKE THIS CLASS! No questions asked. Don't even ask "what's the difference between performance and performance art?" because you'll figure that part out – and in my opinion, it's better that you don't know the difference, so your world can be torn apart, and then you can rebuild it.
If you enjoy performing and want a pretty easy, low–stakes, supportive environment to do so, you should definitely take this class! If you want a more structured or challenging course, though, this class may not be for you. ***
