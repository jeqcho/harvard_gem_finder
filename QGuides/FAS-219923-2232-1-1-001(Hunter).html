What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is one of the best classes you'll take. I feel more prepared for my required TFing and am now interested in potentially teaching more. Be engaged as many of these skills are transferrable outside of teaching too.
I would tell students to take it seriously even if they're not interested in teaching beyond the program teaching requirement. I believe that pedagogical training is not widespread enough in academic science, even though many scientists will have opportunities or even expectations to teach by virtue of working at a college or university. I think that it's extremely valuable to learn about how to teach effectively and create an inclusive space for students and to have some awareness of the academic structures in place at the University where we work.
