This is great if you're at a stage in your writing where you are spending more time and effort on fewer poems. It's pretty unstructured, as opposed to the Intermediate Workshop, which runs on weekly assignments.

This was such a wonderful class! Being in community with other people that care about engaging with your work and expressing their most honest and sincere feedback in a caring and effective way significantly improved my writing. Josh also does such an incredible job of reading through my poetry and finding specific aspects of my writing (such as theme, structure, tone, composition, etc.) that I'm not even fully aware of until he shares them with me and helps me bring them out more in future poems. I'm so glad I got the opportunity to write poetry in this class and workshop it with Josh and my classmates. I also learned so so much about engaging with different texts and voices simply by reading and discussing other students' poems. If you've ever felt hesitant about sharing your poetry with other people but still love to write, then please take this class. You will learn so much and grow abundantly
