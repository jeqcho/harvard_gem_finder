This class is incredible. It covers the most interesting, practical, and surprising topics in personality psychology, and is a great class to take if you are at all interested in personality psychology. Professor Jones is incredible and super supportive of her students. The class for me was not at all stressful, but I still took away so much from the class. The assignments are very fair and helped to review the main concepts. The projects were fun, too! Take this class if you are considering it.
Pretty standard psych elective. The lectures were ok, with weekly readings and assignments. There are no exams which is great. If you like personality psychology this is a great class.
Lots of really interesting tidbits, and I can see myself coming back to notes I took and concepts I learned in this class 15 years down the road– not something I can say about most classes.
I would encourage future students to definitely take this course! It is very well–taught and covers really interesting material. The creative assignments push you to synthesize material in a different way, but they are not super difficult. Professor Jones is a great teacher and is super receptive to students. The amount of weekly work is also super manageable**. I recommend this course 100%, especially for people interested in getting a taste of psychology.
this has been my favorite class so far at Harvard. everything from the teaching style & organization of the class, down to the content itself, the types of assignments we had, and the professor, were amazing. personality psychology is one of the most interesting aspects of psychology, & as somebody who didn't come from a psychology background, I felt very well–oriented because of the class slides, and it encouraged me to participate more. there are weekly assignments that vary, from taking a personality quiz to making a video or writing a diary report, so every week is something new and fun. there are also weekly quizzes based on the content of the previous week's slides, so make sure to thoroughly review those lecture slides before the quizzes. the quizzes are low stress anyways, and will take at most 15 minutes. The professor was also amazing, very kind and always willing to walk through explanations several times. She had a very warm but calm spirit, and she was very open to feedback, even changing the class around a bit after mid–semester feedback. The content from this class was very applicable to real life, to the point where as I was learning, I was realizing that a lot of it explained different aspects of my personality. This class has made me more interested & confident in branching out from the STEM field towards social sciences.
Take the class! Professor Jones is great!
