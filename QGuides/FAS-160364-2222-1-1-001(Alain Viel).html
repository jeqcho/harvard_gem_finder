What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course is an excellent option for those who do not want to work in a lab outside of class but are nonetheless interested in lab work. Having an instructor means you don't need to generate your own questions or procedures but still allows you to be responsible for your own element of work and have your own contributions to the scientific body of knowledge.
Take this class! It’s a great way to learn what being in a wet lab is like, and it’s nice to get practice working in a research group. You will be in the lab a lot, and will need to coordinate with your group members, so this class helps you develop communication and teamwork skills
