specific tips:
1. do pre–reading before section, don't skip sections and really use CA's to be sure you understand concepts during class
2. do psets first alone then go to office hours/MCQ every single time –– this means making it a big part of your week (devote 2–3 hours 3x/week)
3. go to quiz sessions every single time even though you're tired and have already spent enough time on the class and have better things to do (I wish I had done this)
