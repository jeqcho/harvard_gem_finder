Take this class!!!! I liked the creative freedom on each assignment, as well as the final project. It was very manageable and Ruth and Sarah are both so so incredible. Ruth and Sarah have made this class the best it can be by always being so willing to help and offer feedback. I loved their attitudes and their love for animation rubs off on students as well. I enjoyed the variety of topics and mediums that we explored. Animating can take a while, but if you budget your time well, it is doable! Personally, I enjoyed the process A LOT so it did not feel like I was laboring. I felt a great sense of accomplishment after my final project because brings together everything you've learned over the course. I learned so much about sound mixing, how to use Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, DragonFrame, etc. I enjoyed seeing everyone's creations as well. As an upcoming senior, this class has been my favorite class so far!!
