This is a good jumping off point if you are trying to become conversational in Spanish, but it will require some work on your end. Fantastic literature selections, with a focus on learning a lot of the key tenses that you will use in everyday speech. Highly recommend putting in time each week to learn the new vocabulary and review it as the course goes on because it will help tremendously with being able to express more complex thoughts. Also highly recommend actively seeking out everyday situations in which to practice, which the course builds in to some degree but there is simply no replacement for the real thing.
