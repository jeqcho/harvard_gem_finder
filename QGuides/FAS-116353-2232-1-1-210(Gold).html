TAKE THIS CLASS!!! I went into Expos with a lot of apprehension because I am not super confident in my writing abilities, but this course was probably one of my favourites. Dr. Gold is literally THE BEST prof ever. She has picked out amazing texts to analyze, runs every discussion and lesson in such an inclusive way that I felt encourage to share my thoughts, and provides genuinely helpful feedback on our writing. She has fun ways of teaching concepts to make them stick (i.e. using Tinder bios as evidence to make a claim, which taught me about how evidence from text can be used) and is able to guide you through the writing process flawlessly. While writing is still not my favourite thing to do, the discussion in this course is also amazing and something that everyone will be able to participate in. And she is fairly lenient with grading as well!
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! I know literally everyone in this Q Guide is probably saying the same thing but LISTEN!! This is literally the best expos ever. I have never understood why anyone else complains about their expos (and people complain a lot) because this is literally my favorite class (AND THIS IS COMING FROM A MATH MAJOR WHO HATED HIGH SCHOOL WRITING). Every expos class will teach you how to write but most classes have you read a text or two in order to have something to write about and then you are just stressed about the writing (from what I hear :) ). In this course, you get to read SUCH INTERESTING texts (really cool short stories that make you feel seen in a way I never have before and feminist theory that isn't actually that hard to read because Dr. Gold will take you through the important parts and you can literally apply to everything ever). It feels like you are reading things BECAUSE THEY ARE ACTUALLY IMPORTANT and then just writing as another way to think about these ideas more deeply. It takes all the stress out of the class and just makes it fun and worth–while. And you are SURROUNDED BY PEOPLE WHO ARE ACTUALLY PASSIONATE. Everyone in the class most likely put it first choice (that's why there are two sections) and really really care about the issues at hand. You aren't just in a room with people who are taking the class because it is required. This doesn't feel like a required class. Despite my rant about the content of the course, I also think Dr Gold is incredibly good at teaching you to write. She breaks everything down into steps and gives lots of examples and even has a "Gold Guide" that she wrote that tells you everything you need to know about writing. I don't think she is the easiest grader in all of expos (if you want an easy A gem this may not be the course for you) but when you actually care about what you are writing it literally doesn't matter. And she will give you so so much feedback (20 minute videos going in depth about her thoughts on your essays) and always be willing to meet and talk about it, that you will truly learn a lot. She will also listen to you all the time and is so easy to come talk to. I sometimes mention to other people (upperclassmen!) that I am in the class and they will light up and start talking about how awesome Dr. Gold is. In conclusion, I took this class because my friend took it last semester and told me to take and I will be forever grateful to him :)))
If you're a guy don't be discouraged from this class, I'm a guy who took it and it was still very interesting and I had fun taking it.
