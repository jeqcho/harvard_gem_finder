This was one of the best courses I took at Harvard!
Take this seminar!! There is so much to learn about community building, effective listening, social movements, community organizations, and more through this course. There is a place for anyone in this course whether you're interested in theater/politics or not. Even if you can't take this course, take any TDM course because not only do you learn so much about the content, but you also learn so much about your classmates and feel closer to the Harvard community.
Without a doubt, take this class! It will change you in more ways than you know, equip you with tools for practicing art in a meaningful and intentional way, and teach you to show ip in communities and listen so you can do the real work, whatever that means to you.
Community building is highly prioritized, especially through thorough analysis of civic justice events around the world. The daily check–ins were a bonding experience that allowed the privilege to hear about each peer on a personal level. I fully recommend this course to all! Leah and Dayron are two of the most supportive, kind, and intelligent people at Harvard, and this class will encourage you to think and reflect on a more human, philosophical, and grounded level.
Honestly, you NEED this course. I’ve never taken a TDM class or explored the arts before, and this class was my first entry point. Leah and Dayton are so so good at building honest community through check ins and encouraging everyone’s reactions to be grounded in their experiences. They also are TOP TIER at hyping you up so that you feel like you’re really into something unique and new. I’ve loved this class so much and would take it again 100x over.
