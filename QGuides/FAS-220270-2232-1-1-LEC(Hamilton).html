Take this class if you have even the slightest interest in German literature or history! Make sure to do the readings before lecture!
YOU MUST TAKE A CLASS WITH PROFESSOR HAMILTON IN YOUR TIME HERE!!! As for this class specifically, Professor Hamilton created such a great syllabus – with a broad enough overview of German literature so as to give breadth, but also focused enough to make you feel like you were taking concrete ideas/ themes out of the class. He is an engaging and witty lecturer, and the 2 hours passed by in no time. He is also super kind, and that will come through in office hours and even during the lecture itself. The work was just a few (very, very short) papers and the reading assignments were minimal. Professor Hamilton also knows so, so much, and fills every second of the two hours with insight. I think this was one of my favorite classes I've ever taken in my college career –– learned SO much, minimal stress, engaging lectures. I've heard this is true of all his classes.
It is such a great opportunity to be trained in close reading and developing ideas with the aid of classical texts.
Professor Hamilton is the most engaging lecturer I've had at Harvard.
