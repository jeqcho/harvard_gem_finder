In terms of a tutorial, this class was quite good. The topics were interesting, Emilio is a great teacher, and you learn how to think rigorously about moral issues, which is essential honestly for anyone.
Take this class! The readings are great + challenge you without being overly difficult. The subject matter of the class is engaging and the discussions are always interesting. If you are interested in the ways philosophy can be applied to contemporary social justice debates, I would take this class!
Great class! Covers a great range of material while also giving depth for each piece of subject matter discussed. Further, Emilio is great and you should definitely take a class with him – super kind and approachable but also very serious about the subject matter and cares deeply about the course.
I took the course with Emilio and would definitely recommend ranking his section highly. He keeps the structure of the work really flexible, so its easy to fit this class into any schedule. The course touches on some interesting ethical debates, though some of the issues do feel slightly dated. Overall though, I felt like I had a lot of freedom to focus my thinking and writing on the areas that I found most interesting.
