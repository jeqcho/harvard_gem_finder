Take this class! It is incredibly interesting and well–taught. The course is so well–constructed and you'll walk away as an expert on American conservatism.
Fantastic seminar option. Topic is super engaging and Aaron is the best. Highly, highly recommend for a seminar because work load is also not as bad as other seminars. Discussions went by quickly because they were very engaging and interesting, and Aaron facilitates them excellently. The assignment structure is super effective and nice too because you build towards the final paper.
This class is a good way to fill your seminar requirement, but would probably be intense for a non–concentrator. The readings are on par for a seminar but on the long side, and with little variety. Professor Bekemeyer is great, and very knowledgeable, but make sure you are very interested in the topic since classes can feel long. Overall would recommend with reservations.
Aaron is great. The class is a great one to get your 20 page paper if you are a history major looking to write a thesis. It is also does not assume you know how to do archival or independent historical research.
I highly recommend this course for history concentrators. I would plan ahead of time for the final paper –– there's plenty of opportunity with the build up assignments. I would recommend that students have a topic in mind early on and stick with it otherwise it can be a lot of work to start from scratch in the final few weeks.
