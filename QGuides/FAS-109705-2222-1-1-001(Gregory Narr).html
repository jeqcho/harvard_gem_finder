Narr is a very sweet teacher. He is certainly one of a kind, but the type of teacher you grow to love. He fosters a great classroom environment. He is a little all over the place, but I highly recommend this class. The class is not that challenging and the conversations and class dialogue were always interesting. There is a 3000 word final paper, but it is manageable.
This is a great class for anyone interested in the course topic. To get the most out of this course, you need to be willing to share personal experiences, engage with the texts, and connect with classmates. The class almost takes on a seminar form so participation is crucial. The work is very manageable – not something to stress about. Greg is the best if you get to know him. It is worth it to make the effort and will make the class even more enjoyable! A great class for someone who is interested in social media effects, likes to discuss with classmates, and is willing to wake up for a 9am (participation is big!)
