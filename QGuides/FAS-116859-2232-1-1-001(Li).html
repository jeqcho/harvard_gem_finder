Great class in every way
I am so glad that I chose to take this class instead of Stat 110. I feel like I learned the underlying concepts of probability much better because it was a smaller class size and I got to have way more interaction with the amazing teaching staff, particularly the two legendary TFs Zhaolin and Xiaomin. I have never felt more supported in a class before at Harvard, and the teaching staff is always responding to Ed posts in like 10 minutes and answering our often very late emails as soon as they can. They also go out of their way to make time to meet with us to explain the concepts, and make sure we fully understand the material. While the lectures may be a bit dry, the teaching staff are the saving grace of the course. I highly recommend this course to everyone.

Additionally, the teaching is phenomenal, and you can receive a lot of individual instruction by attending office hours. Xiaomin and Zhaolin went above and beyond to help students learn the material, sometimes extending office hours by a full hour to answer questions.
