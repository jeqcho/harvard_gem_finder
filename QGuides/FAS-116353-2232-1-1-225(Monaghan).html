What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Dr M is a great teacher and a fair grader; if you do the readings and correct your papers thoughtfully, you will do well. I didn't find the class to be as depressing as other students suggest, in fact, I thought the classes were very interesting.
First unit is extremely boring. The rest of the course is more open, allowing for students to elect what they want to study. I came in much less enthusiastic about the course than when I left – enjoyable overall.
The grading in this class is super tough. It’s a hard expos.
Do not take this course unless you are absolutely certain that it is for you topic–wise. Dr. Monaghan is extremely regimented and extremely strict in her grading . You will grow, but most of this growth will come as you are forced to meet her expectations.
Don't take non–combatants for total war! There are more interesting classes with teachers that grade easier. At the end of the day, you want an A which is hard to do in this class.
I would tell future students that this class is definitely not the easiest expos class (and if that's what you're looking for, I would not recommend taking this class). However, I do also think that this class is very conducive to strengthening and enhancing your writing skills. Also, it is important to have some semblance of interest in war and topics that pertain to war– otherwise, the reading will feel very boring and long. Overall, I came out of the class having learned a lot and having a good expos experience.
This class was, in my opinion, a great example of what an expos class should be. We had many lessons, any many peer assignments, that worked to teach us the fundamental parts of writing. We had timely schedules to do work which forced the class to stay on our game. Dr. M grades harshly but fairly, and this forced all of us to put in lots of work into the class. We got interesting and valuable comments from Dr. M and our peers, and I personally improved my writing over the class by a reasonable amount. Yes, there is dark subject matter, and this isn't a gem, but it is informative and valuable.
This isn't an "easy" Expos, but it's a class that you will absolutely learn from and grow from. To be completely honest, this was my last–choice Expos, but at the end of it I could not be more happy that I was placed into it. This class requires hard work and dedication, but if you are willing to put effort in, you will get a lot out of this class. Dr. Monaghan is amazing, and she gives very valuable feedback that will help you grow as a writer. If you're looking for the kind of transformative writing experience that Expos advertises itself to be, I would highly recommend taking this class!
