Comments
It was a pleasure to learn from Dr. Hankins in History 1301. The course opened up new avenues of interpreting history and examining my own ideas, and I heartily recommend it to everyone and anyone.
For the philosophically–inclined, this course is a great way to spend a semester. Professor Hankins offers lectures to provide grounding in the topic, while Konrad's sections always provide the opportunity to probe deeper on difficult questions. The midterms, quizzes, and finals are all fair and test your understanding of the material. However, getting through the readings is a challenge if you put them off for too long.
