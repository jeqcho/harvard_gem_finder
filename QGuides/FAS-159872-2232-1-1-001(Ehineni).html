If you're weighing taking this class, just do it! It was a very enjoyable reprieve from the hustle and bustle from other classes. Professor Ehineni is a joy to take this class with because of his enthusiasm and commitment to the language and teaching. I was a bit nervous and out of my comfort zone in the beginning but over the semester I gained my footing and confidence in my language skills. If you're reading this, take this as a sign to take the class!
This course is great if you want to learn basics of how to speak Yoruba. I will say that this course will not get you fluent or close to being fluent, but the atmosphere that professor Taiwo creates is fun, lighthearted and relaxed. He is extremely understanding and a great teacher of language all–around. He's also very very nice, the nicest professor I've had here, and is clearly most interested in all of his students just having a great time and taking away the Yoruba lessons we learn about in class.
***Great course and Professor Taiwo is an amazing teacher
If you are interested in Yoruba and want to take it in approachable way, consider this class. It is relatively low–stress, and there are several parts built into the syllabus, but you can learn a lot if you put in the effort. Professor Taiwo is accessible and really prioritizes learning, not just getting though material for the sake of being able to say he did.
