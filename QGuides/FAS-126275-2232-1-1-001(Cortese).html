Take it! :)
This class has been my absolute favorite in college. Fed is so deeply invested in the growth of his students, and while he holds high standards, he knows exactly what will best help an orchestra/singer to better internalize and perform a piece of music. Fed is extremely reasonable in his assignments, and is arguably one of the most knowledgeable professors I have ever met–– the content covered in this course did not solely pertain to music, but also to history, philosophy, sociology, psychology, and much more. I learned so much about opera through this course, but also took several valuable life lessons away from this course as well.
Music 127R has been my favourite course at Harvard; I could not recommend it more.
A true honor to get to study with Fed. There is no professor like him at this school, and you will learn, not only about music, but about some of the most profound truths that you will remember for the rest of your life.
