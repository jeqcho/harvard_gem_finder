This class is great! I liked that I was held accountable by all of the deadlines for each of the mini assignments – it makes writing the final paper very easy and manageable. It also is fun to dive into a topic you are interested in.
This is a concentration requirement so you're probably not here by choice. Nonetheless, you should pick a research topic that is engaging and will make the work feel less laborious. The assignments all build up to the final paper so take that seriously is you create less work for yourself in the long run.
you will definitely have to put in some work into this course, but if you find a topic you are excited about, it is a great class. the assignments throughout the semester help you and prompt you to complete parts of research and writing so that by the time you write the final paper, you already have a large portion of the info/research you need! it's a cool opportunity to pursue a topic in–depth whether you are writing a senior thesis in another field or not.
