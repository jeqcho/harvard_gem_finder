This course is a phenomenal tutorial. While there is a lot of reading, the writing aspect is manageable if you devote the time. You should do your best to make the most of every class, as it can sometimes be easy to put it on the back burner during the busier times of the semester. However, you'll get so much out of the class if you are diligent about doing the readings. Don't stress about the final research project; you really work up to it.
Professor Felter is great teacher and really nice guy. However, this class is a lot of work, especially the readings. I enjoyed this section overall, however, be prepared to read a lot and come to class ready to speak. If you are thinking about pre law track this is the section is for you, however, if you are interested in other topics this class may be a lot of work for you.
