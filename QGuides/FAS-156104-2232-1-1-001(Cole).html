To echo a review of the course from a previous semester: "Enjoy what the professionals can offer you." Nevertheless, feel like I learned a lot while not being stressed out with an enormous workload.
It is really worth taking this course if you are interested in drug discovery
This course is a decent survey of a variety of areas within pharmacology and drug discovery, but lacks depth in mechanistic understanding and underlying organic chemistry principles. I would take this if you are interested in pharmacology without much background and curious about exploring different aspects at a broad view.
This class will have you looking at chemical structures of molecules. It will be helpful to review chemistry and organic chemistry before enrolling.
You will learn a lot about drug design and the target validation pathway which can be applied to many fields.

This is a great class for understanding the mechanism of different types of drugs and their development. It is a great class on the science of the pharmaceutical industry.
If you are looking for course that will give you a very comprehensive introduction to drug development, this course is what you need! It covers diverse areas of drug development, including cancer immunotherapy, pain management, gene therapy, and cardiovascular disease with fantastic invited speakers from industry. Don't miss out!
