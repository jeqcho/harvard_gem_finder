What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this course! It's a bit of a time commitment, but relatively easy if you have a mild background in Chinese.
If you're thinking about taking Chinese either to fulfill your language requirement or just because you're interested in learning the language (like I was) DEFINITELY take this course!!! Super well–structured, the instructors are among the best I've had at Harvard (very engaged, invested in your success), and even with no prior experience (like I had) if you put in the work you will improve rapidly. Assignments and exams are very fair and you get to apply the material from each week in a variety of ways (writing, speaking, etc.). A plethora of resources available to help you learn the material and improve week to week. All in all, a very fulfilling experience and if I had to go back I would choose to take this course 10/10 times.
If you have ever wanted to learn Chinese, definitely take Ba and Bb! You will have to put in some work, but the teaching staff is absolutely amazing and the course is really well structured. There are also a decent number of opportunities to raise your grade. By the end of the year you will have made so much progress!
