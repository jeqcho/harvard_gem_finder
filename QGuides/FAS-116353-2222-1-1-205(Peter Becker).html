Comments
Overall not bad, but if you're looking for one of the more "fun" expos classes where you get to watch movies and write about rap songs etc., this isn't it. It's very straightforward and to the point, which isn't a bad thing if you wish to improve your academic reading/writing skills. I personally don't regret taking it, as a couple of friends and I took the class together and managed to have a good time and have interesting discussions during class, but I wouldn't necessarily recommend unless you're very interested in slave narratives in a literary sense or love reading through academic articles.
don't push the essay writing/prep to the last minute, especially the final papers which collide with many other finals that you're taking, all of which are equally or similarly hard.
Read earlier than you think you should.
Listen to your predecessors. Becker is a hard grader. I thought my grades were low (B on the first paper) but then, after speaking to other stude. nts, I was not alone. The readings are tough when you have to go from one scene of tragedy to the next while expected to analyze them; I will always find this odd.
