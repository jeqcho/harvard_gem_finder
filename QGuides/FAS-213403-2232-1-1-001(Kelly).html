What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The readings and discussions are quite interesting, and I highly recommend this class for anyone interested in German Idealism. The readings are difficult, and you definitely get much more value out of them if you take the time to read them closely and try to understand them well.
Take this class if you want to learn about german idealism. Professor Kelly is absolutely amazing philosopher and an even better person. He will always treat you with the proper dignity due to human beings, and Professor Matherne is an absolute delight to work with. I sincerely look forwards to taking more classes with them both in the future .
