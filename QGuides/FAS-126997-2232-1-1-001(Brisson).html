What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is a great way to improve your French listening skills. The movies are enjoyable, and the assignments are very helpful!
There's a wide variety of course assignments. Some are more similar to p–sets– a short set of questions that are pretty straightforward. There are also 3 essays, three spoken essay assignments, and 3 exams spread throughout the semester. If you prefer the exact same structure each week, this may be a bit out of your comfort zone, but all the assignments serve a purpose.
This course was interesting because it gives you a very specific focus in terms of vocabulary, which can be positive or negative. The films were interesting and discussed a range of social issues. However, the course was also very messy and that meant confusion across sections and within sections about datelines, exam topics, requirements, and grading expectations. This can be quite stressful when taking into account other courses and the Harvard lifestyle in general. If it becomes more well organized, I would highly recommend.
