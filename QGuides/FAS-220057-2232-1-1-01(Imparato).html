This is an amazing course for any student looking to understand the real world implications of the innovations happening right now in the biological sciences, all while learning about important moral frameworks that can be used to critically evaluate the ethics behind such practices. With consistent, collaborative and engaging discussions with classmates and teaching staff both in the classroom and during section, the class allows for everyone to share their perspective about the topics we discuss, allowing for different points of views to be shared and a more holistic understanding of the topics.
Take this class! It will force you to question everything you have ever thought about technology, with specific focus on the Biotech field that will soon govern our lives. Sergio is a truly incredibly professor and cares so much for his students. He ultimately just wants you to engage in the course material and if you buy–in you will get so much from this class. Biotech Ethics has forced me to reconsider my career ambitions thanks to how inspiring the class is. Do yourself a favor and take this class.

Highly, highly recommend!
