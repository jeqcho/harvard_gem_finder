Best math class I have taken. Very fun and interesting. Lots of busy work, I recommend being on top of it.
I enjoyed this class. I think it's fair and there's ample opportunity to get help when needed. All of the TFs are great.
I took 21a after taking 1b and 21b, it definitely is easier than both of them. The psets are doable alone, but MQC is your friend and always helps. The class really isn't bad, just make sure to put some time aside to do the psets. Midterm and final were definitely very fair, though I do think off cycle 21a is slightly easier just because the course is smaller in terms of enrollment.
The class was not as difficult as I anticipated. I was pretty well prepared for the class (I took AP Calc BC online in high school and was not super confident, so I also took Math 1b), and the topics were very well explained and the workload was totally doable, given that it was still three psets per week. The exams were at a similar level, if not a bit easier, to the psets. What I think made the course run so smoothly was that the topics were fairly linear and there was a clear progression from one to the next. It was fairly easy to recall old topics for exams because many were very applicable to the more recent ones. There were a lot of resources available (TF's and CA's in class, office hours, and MQC) if there was anything I missed in class or needed help with on a pset. My only complaint was that compared to Math 1b, I would say this course was less engaging because there was a lot of content but the explanations and proofs were not always covered in order to get through everything.
Put effort into this class and it translates to grades that you love.
