What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this course, and try to interact with the instructors and students.
This course is a fun way to engage with many top minds in ML research. The discussions can be hit or miss, but overall you can get out of this course what you want to. It also is not super time–consuming outside of the final project, so it can be taken as a 3rd CS class as opposed to another pset class. The Google Brain researchers are all super nice and willing to give advice at the end of the semester regarding research paths in the future which is helpful!
The instructors of this course (especially the Google team) are amazing!
Take this class! It think that it is the best applied ML course at Harvard and I would argue the best ML course at Harvard as well. The instructors from Google are incredible – the provide a super cool perspective on the papers you read, really show you a lot of the inner workings of industry research, and are great project mentors. The course also has as really good topics list from applied deep Bayesian methods, to reinforcement learning, to causal ML, to cutting edge applications in bio and chem. Again, I would strongly recommend for anyone interested in ML at Harvard.
