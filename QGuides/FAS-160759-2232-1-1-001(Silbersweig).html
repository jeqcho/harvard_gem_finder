This class is a GEM! The professor is very kind and understanding. The assignments makes sense (so you are ready for discussion). Nothing is unreasonable. You learn lots about neuroimaging and the brain as well as mental illness.
This class is honestly such a gem. Though the readings can be difficult to parse at times, each class is filled with discussion that doesn't necessitate a comprehensive understanding of the structure of the brain. The professor brings in real–world examples to contextualize the issues he presents, and has a really refreshing view on mental health and the stigma that surrounds it.
Take this class!
Please take this class if you can! Dr. Silbersweig is such an inspiring and compassionate individual who will make this class so interesting for you.
Take this course!! I took it for my science distributional requirement (the last science class I took was in high school!). I was nervous going into it, but Dr. Silbersweig was truly the most incredible professor. He cares so much about the subject and he brings in such interesting real–world experience being the head of psychiatry at BWH. The coolest thing we did in this course was a hospital visit to see a real patient and hear her story. I cannot say enough good things about this class. Whether you are a STEM or non–STEM person you will learn so much about the neurobiology of psychiatric illness.
TAKE THIS COURSE for a deep (but non–intimidating) dive into the modularity of the brain and what happens to its "output" (i.e. the mind) when brain machinery faces a glitch. The class is held once a week for a couple of hours, during which we get to ask Dr. Silbersweig about the readings and his unique experiences as a pioneer in the field of neuropsychiatry along with engaging in a student–led class discussion about that given week's topic/readings. Assignments are sparse, simple, and straightforward. An all–around wonderful course which I highly recommend, ESPECIALLY if you are already interested in psychiatry, psychology, or neurology (and if you're not already interested in any of these, you will be after taking this course ;) ). Once again, TAKE THIS COURSE!
