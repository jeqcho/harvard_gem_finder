What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Professor Malik teaches you not only about the subject matter itself, but how to think like a political scientist more broadly. The course is heavy on material, but through it all you will gain an understanding of ethnicity and identity all while learning what to look for when analyzing an argument, what makes a paper convincing, what constitutes an effective research design, and so much more. These skills will be invaluable as you continue your gov career while writing research papers of your own.
Professor Malik is a great discussion leader, very helpful with developing a research topic and fleshing out your ideas and interests. The class and reading materials are super engaging and thought–provoking. The class is oriented more towards understanding research design which is very helpful for shaping your own research projects. Highly recommend taking this class!
