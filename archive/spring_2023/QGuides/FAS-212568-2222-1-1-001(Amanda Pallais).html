Great elective to take if you're a junior ec concentrator – the workload is relatively chill (including the final paper if you budget your time correctly), Professor Pallais is amazing, and the papers covered are all very interesting! Highly recommend!
Great course to learn how to write a longer econ paper, although the subject matter is pretty focused on just randomized experiments in the literature.
This is an awesome course. The teaching staff is amazing. The readings are very interesting and painless to read. The response papers are fun to do, especially in the weeks where the response paper prompt is about what your project plan is.
Start working on your final project early and have lots of meetings with Professor Pallais and TF Francesca!
It's a really good opportunity to put a whole semester's worth of work into a topic of your choice. Designing the experiment and writing the paper isn't easy, but it's a really rewarding experience.
I loved this class. Amanda is wonderful – talented and articulate and passionate and kind. She makes everything completely accessible and is completely open to questions and clarifications. Francesca is also extremely accessible and knowledgeable. The content is not going to change your world but it might equip you more skills as you pursue a thesis or research. Nothing crazy, but given the more limited workload over the week, it is worth it. The large assignment of designing an experiment is fun and interesting. I'd take with no hesitation again. It would be great to balance out an extremely tough workload. One of my favorites.
