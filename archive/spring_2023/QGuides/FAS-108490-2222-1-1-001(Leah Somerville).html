Are you interested in psychology? Do you have emotions? Are you just looking to fill out your class schedule? If your answer to any of these questions is yes (which I sure hope it is), then this course might be a good fit for you. In addition to hammering in some key psychological concepts (if that's your cup of tea), this class also includes information that is likely directly applicable to your day–to–day life. The fact that the work/reading expectations for this course are extremely reasonable is also a plus.
Take this class if you're looking for a relaxed, interesting psych elective, especially if you're looking to counterbalance some more time–consuming classes. I took this without having taken a foundational psych class, and had no issues. The focus is definitely on the science of emotions (research methods, competing viewpoints, a little bit of neuro, etc.), and less about how we can apply these findings in everyday life, although you will learn a few things that might feel useful in your life more generally.
