Wonderful seminar in which you not only get to travel to NYC to see some amazing exhibits for free, but also have the chance to improve your writing and close looking skills dramatically while discussing some incredible and underappreciated artworks and artists. Professor Gough seems intimidating but is lovely and supportive. Her passion for the work is palpable.
Don’t be afraid to ask questions as they come to you and do weekly responses carefully!
This class is amazing, you are exposed to beautiful and thought–provoking art, get to hear from an authority on this topic, Professor Gough, and get to contribute to an actual exhibition! Really cool.
I would tell future students that this class is an incredible opportunity to relearn modernism through fabric. It's a chance to think about how what we see is conditioned by media biases, which are often gendered/racialized. This course is a thought experiment into what happens when we reorient ourselves towards practices that have been marginalized––and it does so with clarity and great thoughtfulness.
