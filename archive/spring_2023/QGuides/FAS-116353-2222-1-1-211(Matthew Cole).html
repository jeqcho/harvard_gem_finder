Start early on the writing assignments, and do a little every day. You have plenty of time, but only if you choose to.
I would really encourage other students to take this class. I found the topic area to be very interesting and also very topical. 1984 was a great read, and I also enjoyed the opportunity to read some of Orwell's essays. Professor Cole is a great instructor who will do his very best to help you improve your written work. I took a lot out of this class and think that a good portion of next year's students would find the same.
This EXPOS section is a really great section. You take a traditional literary text, 1984, and not only dive very deep into it but also think about its themes in today's context. Dr. Cole is really knowledgeable on Orwell and political theory in general, so you definitely learn a lot about those themes in this section.
Really good expos course and good teacher with excellent feedback
This class is excellent and now you will be able to speak about such a famous book. Meet with Dr. Cole during the essay writing process so you clearly understand his essay expectations.
I would tell future students to not be afraid to take this class even if they have not read 1984. I would also tell them to take advantage of the resources this course offers. Professor Cole is incredibly helpful and available if they ever get stuck on any of their essays. The feedback he has given me has helped me to become a much better writer.
Students are capable of making significant improvements in their essay–writing, but doing so requires that they listen to Dr. Cole's feedback and continuously try to apply his feedback when revising their drafts and carrying on their learnings into future assignments.
