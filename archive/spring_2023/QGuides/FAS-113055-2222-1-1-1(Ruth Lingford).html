Apply for this class if you have any interest in animation!
You’ll get to try out different animation styles, like hand–drawn, puppetry, digital 2D, and 3D. The last half of the semester will be dedicated to working on an animation project of your own, which is open–ended and exciting. You also get to watch interesting films you wouldn’t otherwise see. This class can fit into a busy course schedule since there aren’t many hard deadlines, but if you're properly ambitious you'll still have a significant amount of work.
