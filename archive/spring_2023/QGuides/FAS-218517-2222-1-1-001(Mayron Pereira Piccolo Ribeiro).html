I would recommend this class to anyone who wants to learn more about the psychology of their listening habits, what music can do for their emotional and cognitive wellbeing, and the social and cultural roles of music. You don't need any technical training in music theory to fully enjoy this class, though some musical or neuroscience background might benefit you at times. The workload is not very demanding at all and the course staff care about making the course stress–free and meaningful for students, participation in solo performances and group music–making is encouraged, plus the content of lectures and readings is interesting!
