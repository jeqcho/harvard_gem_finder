The material is not difficult, but there are a lot of assignments.
This class is a lot of work! The language classes before 50 were more "fun" but this one has a lot of work. Don't expect this to be like 30 or 40, where you can get by with minimal work. That being said, the increased work does pay off. Your french will improve with the work you put into it.
I think this is a very good course to take. I would recommend making sure you start the assignments in the dossier with ample time (not that they're insanely long, but it's just nice to have them all done by class time). I also really appreciated how clear our assignments were and enjoyed speaking with other students during class. It's probably my favorite French class I've taken at Harvard! I'd recommend it!
The language exchange component is amazing!!
You should definitely take this class if you like French! It was a really interesting course and while it did require a good amount of time outside of class for homework and other assignments, it always felt like the effort I put into my work was worth it – the teacher seemed to really be able to recognize that all of us were really trying to do our best on these assignments and in response, she gave really supportive feedback!
