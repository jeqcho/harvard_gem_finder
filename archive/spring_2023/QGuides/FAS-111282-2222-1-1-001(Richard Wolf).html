What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course is very hectic and is very demanding
It's a very learn–by–doing course, but the professor gives really good feedback and sets up a friendly environment for you to share your work and improve. I definitely learned a lot and overcame (to some extent) my fear in sharing my creativity.
This course allows you to explore a wide combination of creative mediums in depth to communicate ideas.
You will likely find a community of students who are all interested in creating some kind of a multi–media ethnography. This course also enables you to take full advantage of the Derek Bok Learning Lab and their training on using various equipment for film and sound. The workload can be quite challenging, since you essentially have to create a short film every week, but if you want to be a better filmmaker this would be very helpful.
