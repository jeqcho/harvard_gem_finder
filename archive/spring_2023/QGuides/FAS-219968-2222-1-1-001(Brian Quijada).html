I'd really recommend this class if you're thinking of working on a sound–based project or story and want to have a deeper understanding of how to create those kinds of projects more effectively! It was also a great creative outlet for testing things out in a low–key setting, which is always a lot of fun!
Professor Quijada is unbelievably awesome. If you take the class seriously, put time into the assignments, and listen to feedback you will really grow as an artist and have a ton of fun while doing it. Best class I've taken at Harvard hands down.
Take it!
If you want to learn looping –– take this class! I feel like I not only expanded my musicianship, met new people, tried new things, etc. I really did come out of this class feeling comfortable with a totally new skill/"instrument", which feels really applicable to all of my other art. It's as intense as you make it, which at least for me, is a great structure, and gave me a lot of freedom week to week (some weeks more time, others less, the teacher is understanding) –– Brian is so cool and chill and also super talented and supportive. We need more classes with this energy at Harvard!
