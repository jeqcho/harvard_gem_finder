The preceptor is incredible, and always makes class exciting and engaging. I feel that the close environment of the class makes it easy to get to know the other students, and I had an overall good experience with the group projects. Conferences are useful.
Take this class! At times it might seem like a little more work than other EXPOS classes (with three papers + a tour that is more work than a regular presentation), but Willa is a super helpful and understanding instructor. The material is interesting and you will learn a lot!
Remember the skills you learn when doing your research paper.
If you're interested in public history, memorialization, or even just Boston in general, and you have the opportunity to take this course, take it. It was an amazing experience that I'm really grateful to have had. I think that humanities and social sciences people will probably gain a bit more from this class, but interest in the subject matter is really all that's required to have a rewarding and engaging expos experience.
