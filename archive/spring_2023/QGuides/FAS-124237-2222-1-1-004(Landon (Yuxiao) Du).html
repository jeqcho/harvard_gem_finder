What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Be prepared to stay on top of things in this course. There are days that are more dull and know that you will be answering some questions that you may not know how to articulate in the beginning, but this is all part of taking a language course.
This class is amazing! Although it's pretty fast paced, you learn a lot from the course if you commit yourself to doing at least 4 hours of work every week (which honestly isn't too bad). The teachers are great, flexible, and help create a community beyond the class. I've met some of my best friends from this course!
IF YOU TOOK BA, TAKE THIS CLASS! I know it can seem a bit intimidating, but there is truly nothing to lose. Every single one of the instructors are incredible, your Chinese will continue to improve dramatically if you put the work in, and class is just really fun! You'll see the benefit to every assignment you do, from homework to dictations – there's no busy work, everything contributes to improving your overall Chinese fluency. The teachers are the best part: they're amazing in every way.
Highly recommend!
The Chinese teachers are so kind and helpful, so I would recommend this class to anyone interested in learning Chinese from scratch or improving their Chinese if they've already learned some before.
TAKE CHINESE! As a half–Chinese American with no Chinese language background, I enrolled in this course because I wanted to learn about my heritage and learn the language. This class was a fantastic second half of the introduction to Chinese, and I learned SO MUCH. From the beginning of Chinese BA to the end of Chinese BB, I gained so much confidence and knowledge that I never would have thought possible. It's a lot of work, but you can feel yourself improving every single week if you put in the effort. I highly, highly recommend taking Chinese BB.
