What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Writing a thesis was super rewarding and I highly recommend it!!! It also requires a bunch of self discipline, so make sure you set reasonable deadlines for yourself and stick to them.
Listen to your advisor! Stay up with deadlines you put for yourself! Keep track of your sources and citations ahead of time!! Have as many people read your thesis before you submit it as you can! Good luck :)
The last few weeks of the thesis can be rough– a lot of things are coming together which is satisfying, but one is so tired and there is always more to be done. Find yourself a thesis writing buddy to lighten the mood while writing and to hold you accountable.
The History of Science department is helpful and will be supportive during the thesising process.
My advice is to get a lot of work done over winter break and ask for feedback from a lot of different places! Of course your advisor, but Professor Weidman gave such wonderful and insightful feedback and the Writing Center may be helpful as well.
Come in with enthusiasm and be ready to work!
