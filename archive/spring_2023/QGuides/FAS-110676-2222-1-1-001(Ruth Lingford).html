What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course will be exactly what you make it. The more time you put in, the more satisfied you will be with the work you produce. That time commitment can be quite intense (especially toward the end of the semester with the final project) but I cannot understate how amazing the end result will be. Ruth and Sarah are incredibly knowledgeable, flexible, helpful, and caring instructors. Highly highly recommend this course if you have the time to commit yourself to it.
I suggest that you take the class seriously in the sense that you block off a decent amount of time per week to do assignments. You can definitely pass the class with an A with little effort, but you won't get as much out of the class as you could. Depending on your field of study, getting to take this course could really be an amazing experience you won't get in other classes, so don't shake it off as something to just use to fill a requirement.
This class will take a lot of time, but if you are willing to put in the time to learn the techniques, it is a lot of fun and certainly worth it in the end.
Take this course if you are interested/curious about animation and sound!!! This is a course that will teach you the fundamentals of animation through fun, interactive in–class exercises and screenings. The teaching staff (Professor Lingford and Sarah) is very helpful! If you have any questions, whether they pertain to how to use a program – you will learn programs such as After Effects and Premiere Pro – or how to plan the storyboard of a project, the teaching staff is very good at helping out and proving feedback.
