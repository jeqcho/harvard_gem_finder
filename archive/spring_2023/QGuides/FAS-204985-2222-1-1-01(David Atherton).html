Take this class. It's absolutely remarkable. Prof. Atherton alone is enough of a reason to enroll because he is so knowledgeable, enthusiastic, funny, and warm. Each and every class is exciting and engaging, with tons of fascinating discussions. The reading list is incredible (get ready for some truly wild literature, in the best sense) and the assignments encourage so much creativity, which makes the work feel all the more satisfying personally. Prof. Atherton genuinely cares about his students (evident in his very thoughtful feedback on all assignments) and creates a wonderfully supportive classroom environment. Despite how much I'd encourage others to take this class, I only hope it remains somewhat small–natured in the future, because I think this course is so successful in large part thanks to the intimacy of knowing everyone in the room and being able to discuss things all together.
Absolutely take this class. The readings are incredibly interesting and you cover so much time and content but in a way that is not overwhelming at ALL. I recommend this course for anyone with interest in Japan, literature, or art.
The professor is also super nice and if not this class you need to take SOME sort of class with him at some point, I recommend this one.
