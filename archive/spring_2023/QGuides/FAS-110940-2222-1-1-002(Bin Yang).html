Mandarin is the best language program at Harvard and no one can tell me anything different. The instructors all have such a genuine love for their students and teaching. Class is always fun, and because the class size is small (around 10 people), it makes it a more intimate and welcoming learning environment! Also they make it impossible for you to not get an A, let alone fail. Literally, all you have to do is show up to class.
It is organized more or less the same as previous Chinese classes with the exception that you no longer need to learn to write characters (you can use computer for exams/homework), so it is actually quite a bit less time–consuming.
