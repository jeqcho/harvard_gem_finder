Comments
Chinese has always been one of my favorite classes every semester, if not my favorite. It was incredibly engaging and provides so many opportunities to practice. The Chinese department is wonderful and always has new ideas to improve the class.
If you've taken other Chinese courses at Harvard then this would be a great class to take if you want to continue sharpening your language skills.
If you're already here, then I don't think I need to convince you to take Chinese. But just know that 130b continues to be an amazing class with amazing teachers! It gets a little harder than 130a, and you discuss more nuanced topics, but the Chinese teachers really guide you along the ride. Definitely take!
It's great! If you're thinking about it, don't hesitate.
