What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is AWESOME for anyone who wants to study film scoring/film music, video game music, or be a composer or music theorist. It is also generally interesting for any film and video game lover. You will really learn a lot from the readings, from Frank, and through the discussions with your peers. The final paper/project is also an incredible way to pursue an area of study specific to you that you find interesting.
Professor Lehman is one of the best teachers I've ever had, in my entire life. He is probably the most qualified person on Earth to teach this class, and he clearly cares very deeply about his students. He's one of those teachers who is genuinely excited to hear new ideas in every class discussion, and it was always exciting to start a new topic. The assignments never felt like a hardship to me – they were usually just an opportunity to explore the new things we were learning and go a bit deeper than the readings. I highly recommend this class to anyone who is interested (with the caveat that students should have taken at least a feel year of college music theory – or the equivalent – before enrolling).
Take basic music theory and NRT first! This is not a beginner class.
