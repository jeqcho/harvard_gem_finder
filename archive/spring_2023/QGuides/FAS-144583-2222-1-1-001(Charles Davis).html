PLEASE TAKE THIS CLASS!!! Absolutely will change the way you view the world. It will somewhat difficult to memorize names and identifying characters. The teaching staff are supportive and passionate, making the learning process wonderful.
This is an incredible class and if you are interested at all in ecology, botany, field research, or just want a fun, experiential class you should take it. It is not an easy class, but the reward is indescribable. The labs and spring break trip are a hands–on and far more engaging method of learning that traditional classes and Chuck and Jacob have such a wide breadth of expertise and experiences that enhanced our learning and fun. I cannot emphasize how much I enjoyed this class and wish I had more opportunities in college to take more courses taught in this style. Even the lectures were engaging and lively, with frequent activities and a very clear roadmap of what we were learning. Compared to other taxonomy–based classes I've taken, the organization of this one set students up for the most success. Overall this is an amazing class and I feel grateful that it was offered this semester.
