Take this class. 100% worth it.
If you have any interest in Buddhist philosophy, no matter what you think that means, I would recommend this class. The readings can be hard, but the goal of the class is to understand something, not everything. Parimal is great at giving lectures that each everyone something, no matter what their backgrounds are. He'll also make sure that you care about the class if you put in the time to engage with it.
Be ready to analyze difficult readings. The philosophies/ideas are less difficult than the texts themselves.
Amazing class but also very hard due to the difficulty of the readings and the sheer amount of time needed to spend outside of class thinking about the readings and lectures. Will feel tedious at times, but in the end is for sure worth it, especially if you use the papers to explore something you found really interesting instead of doing what you think is most manageable.
