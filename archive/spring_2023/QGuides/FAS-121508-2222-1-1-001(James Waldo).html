April is an amazing course head who puts a lot of time and care into making a great experience. This class is challenging but fun and a good way to learn what it’s like to do ml or any kind of client facing project in real life, while also getting to understand the impacts of the work you do is.
This class is AWESOME! You get to just write code for an actual non–profit company that can really make a difference in meaningful areas such as climate change, health, and inherent bias in many machine learning algorithms today. No tests. No quizzes. Just coding and collaborating. Take it. You’ll love it. Trust.
Take this class! Truly was a great experience. Even if your team may have more experience than you , you will learn ALOT.
This course has the potential to be really fulfilling / enjoyable, but I think that since it was the first year it was being offered, some general kinks and organization still need to be ironed out. It is definitely a gem though, and your experience will depend a lot on the teammates that you have and the TF in charge of your project.
This class provides great opportunities in working with organizations that are seeking to do good in the world and hear from fantastic guest speakers.
This has been hands down the best CS class I have taken so far here at Harvard and is the closest you can get to real–world experience in the classroom. It was super cool to work with a non profit on a single task throughout the whole semester and to use what we were discussing and practicing in workshops to try and solve these problems. I loved the freedom of the course to allow us to pursue what interested us in our research and to further flesh out what our client wanted to get out of our final product, and I really appreciated how the TFs helped to provide some structure in helping us set weekly goals and giving us periodic assignments to see how we were doing. If you have any interest in machine learning or artificial intelligence and want to see what applying those interests can look like, take this class!!
Definitely enjoyed this course. Excellent speakers and awesome course staff. Wished the projects were a little more planned out but still was very satisfied with the end work we produced.
This is a rare opportunity in the CS department to work on projects with real–world impact!
