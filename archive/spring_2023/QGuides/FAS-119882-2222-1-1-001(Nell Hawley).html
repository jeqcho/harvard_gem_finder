Enjoy! Work hard! It's a beautiful language and has so much good philosophy that can be read using it!
Take Sanskrit with Nell! You won't regret it, it's a wonderful language. Nell is an exceptional professor, the course is exciting, and Sanskrit will change how you view yourself and life.
Definitely know this is a serious time commitment; probably more serious than other languages. But know that if you take it with Nell, you'll be in *excellent* hands, and she will have your back 100% of the time.
If you took Sanskrit 101A, don't stop there: TAKE THIS COURSE! You will go so much deeper on the Sanskrit grammar and vocab than you'd think was possible in four short months. You'll learn how to formulate actual sentences that can convey poetic and philosophical meaning; by the end of the course you'll even write your own short story/fable in Sanskrit! The class is also very small and tight–knit –– it has a great sense of community.
Hard but worth it.
Sanskrit 101B does an amazing job building off the concepts from 101A. At the end of the class you look back and go "woah!" The amount of hard and soft concepts I learned in Sanskrit was honestly very impressive. I could never imagine that I could go from not knowing a single bit of Sanskrit or Devanagari (the script) to reading and comprehending passages/short stories and writing my very own story/poem! Definitely take Sanskrit! As always, Nell did an amazing job this semester, and could not imagine the class being taught without her. Her expertise, enthusiasm, and flexibility made the class such an enjoyable time.
