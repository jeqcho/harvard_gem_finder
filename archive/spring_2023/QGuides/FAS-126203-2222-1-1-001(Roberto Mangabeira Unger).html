What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this class! The teaching teams brings a refreshing international perspective on the most pressing issues in the field of political economy. You will never think about microeconomics, macroeconomics and entrepreneurship as before!
If you know a little economics, this course will give you a bit of a new perspective while also remaining very chill throughout. Going to section is not necessary but will improve the experience enormously. Lecture can get a bit repetitive after a while, especially Unger's side of the contributions. Almost zero quantitative analysis, so if you want more Rodrik–style critique I'd look elsewhere. Class as a whole leans much more Unger in style.
I would take the course every year if I could.
Very up in the air discussions on political economy would be best if you have some understanding of the topic already but not necessary
This class is interesting, but you can probably get just as much out of it watching some Unger videos on YouTube
The readings are lengthy but are very good content for papers as well as for understanding the course material better
