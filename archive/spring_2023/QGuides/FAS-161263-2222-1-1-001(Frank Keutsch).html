What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course has an awesome lab component but was a bit disorganized on the whole (though this was the first time it was being offered, so I bet it'll be even better next go 'round!). For the final project, we sampled aerosol black carbon with a sensor we deployed out in Cambridge, which was such a cool culmination of our work. However, the expectations for this project and most assignments in the course were rather nebulous and generated a bit of stress. Frank is a hilarious and super knowledgeable lecturer, though, and I loved coming to class and learning from him. Similarly, the support staff for the class worked so hard, and it was incredible to spend time in the Active Learning Lab at the SEC as a non–engineering student.
I would tell future students this is a great course to better understand where environmental data comes from and how that knowledge can impact how to frame other areas of environmental science.
This is a great course!! Hands–on and you learn a lot about the field of environmental instrumentation.
I cannot recommend this course enough! This is one of the best courses I have taken at Harvard, with really cool assignments, a classroom environment that is simultaneously challenging and supportive when students ask questions or make mistakes, and a fantastic teaching team. I would encourage future students to start thinking about the black carbon project early on and to be really deliberate going into experimental design because there are a lot of unexpected complicating factors that can come up and ultimately make it hard to draw meaningful conclusions, but the process behind that project is incredibly educational and rewarding. This class is definitely graded fairly, and from a pedagogical perspective, I think it was taught beautifully. Frank is an amazing professor who really cares about his students and their understanding and appreciation of the curriculum. Take this course if you can!
Frank and the course staff are terrific people and I had a very enjoyable time taking this course this semester!
