What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I would tell students that it's a difficult class but if you receive a topic that you're interested in, you'll really enjoy it.
The sophomore tutorial is a requirement for economics concentrators and it is definitely one of the harder courses in the concentration. Your experience in the class will largely be determined by the tutorial you get placed in. I think that Applied Health Economics is one of the more interesting classes and I would recommend it to anyone who is interested in medicine and healthcare. Brad is also super knowledgeable and all the case studies he introduces in class are a great addition to the course material. However, I do think that he does not give as much constructive feedback as one might like so grading can feel a little arbitrary at times.
Take this class! It is so interesting and you will learn so much and Brad sets up such a positive environment for learning. It is laid back but you still learn so much without the stress of many other Harvard classes. This was an amazing tutorial.
I'm sure it depends completely on which section and lecturer. Health econ was my passion and choice. Brad is great and relaxed, it felt like a nice discussion each class. No regrets and learned quite a bit.
