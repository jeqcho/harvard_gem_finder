Comments
This is a great class if you want to know a lot about Zen! It's very focused on this topic so make sure you come in with enough interest to zero–in on it. I would say it's predominately made for people with existing interest, but I think the themes we discuss and the learnings are applicable to anyone's life, especially in an environment like Harvard.
This is definitely a fun, low–pressure course that I would recommend adding to your schedule! Though the readings for the course are quite a lot, they are not entirely necessary to understand the course and James' lecturing is very passionate and knowledgeable, so that makes it all worth it.
Definitely take it. You'll get a lot out of this class.
I loved this class! For not knowing anything about Zen, I felt like I got a fairly good grasp on the basics by the end. It is fairly history heavy, so keep that in mind. We also got the opportunity to talk with Buddhist nuns, which was an amazing experience!
Imagine: a professor just bursting with energy to shower his students with knowledge on Zen Buddhism, a chill classroom setting, and the freedom to ask any and all questions you have. If you're even remotely interested in a survey of Zen Buddhism, this course is the one for you.
