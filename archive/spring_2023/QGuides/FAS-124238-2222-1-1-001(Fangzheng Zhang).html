Take this class!!! The teachers are great, content is pretty engaging, and everyone who picks this class is quite enthusiastic which makes for an even better experience :)
Every Chinese class I've taken at Harvard has been amazing, and this was no exception. You will learn so much not only about more formal grammar structures and vocab, but also about deeper aspects of Chinese culture. It can get overwhelming at times with how much vocab you have to memorize, but the teachers are patient and will help you through it. Just make sure to actually invest time in this class and prepare for classes –– it will be worth it!
This is a superb course for heritage students who have long disliked Chinese language school. The range of topics provide a crossectional view of social and political life in modern day China, which are supplemented by thoughtful discussions during class. Vocabulary acquisition is, as always, challenging but the class is designed so as to consistently draw upon learned vocabulary. All this is work, yes, but this is one of the most comprenehsive language courses I've taken to date.
Really enjoyed this class. 张老师 and 王老师 make learning Chinese fun!
Teachers are amazing. It's a lot of busy work, but that's just how language is. Classes are always a good time; engaging and fun.
