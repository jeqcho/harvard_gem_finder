What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you want more practice with German grammar and to be able to understand almost any sentence structure, this is your class. Do not expect to become a much better writer or learn much vocabulary, though, as this class is definitely grammar heavy. The work is not overly challenging, but knowledge is thoroughly tested on exams, so do spend some time studying for them. Herr Burgard is also a character –– his teaching is a bit old–fashioned, but it is obvious that he cares a lot about what he teaches and about students' success.
A great class, would highly recommend – your grammar will certainly improve, as will your reading and translating ability!
German 61 is a difficult class if you don't have a very strong foundation in German grammar. I think it's a class that works well for people who have been studying German in school for many years but is a more difficult class for someone who learned german by living abroad or primarily by speaking. The exams are quite difficult and I found there wasn't too much explanation of techniques for some of the exercises that repeatedly come up in HW/Exams. Definitely don't take this course lightly.
This course is intense due it its comprehensiveness, so if your relationship with German is more casual, it might not be the best fit. However, if you want to work or study in a German–speaking country, this course is an absolute necessity and will offer extensive preparation. Definitely recommend to anyone serious about taking their mastery of the German language to a higher level.
