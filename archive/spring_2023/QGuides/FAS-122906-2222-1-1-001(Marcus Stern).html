What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
One of the best courses at Harvard. Marcus and Ashleigh care deeply about their students and about seeing them improve. You will get to work on a wide variety of characters, dissect complicated scripts, and select your own monologues and scenes. Do not underestimate the work it takes to memorize lines, but the reward is truly great as you feel yourself get better over the course of the semester. A lot of the workshopping relates to finding personal connections to the characters you play. Take this class if you have any interest in acting – doesn’t necessarily require prior experience!
I learned invaluable knowledge about acting. Marcus and Ashleigh are wonderful, and if you take this class, you will definitely become a better actor by the end of it.
This is an AMAZING class. If you have no acting experience, don't worry –– this class is so accessible, and Marcus and Ashleigh are incredibly supportive, kind, and there to help you succeed. This class definitely requires work: be prepared to memorize, think critically about the best roles for you, etc. –– but it is so rewarding. You will gain a better sense of who you are, what motivates/drives the world around you, and what acting is really about: acting from yourself/your life experiences. The class is small and intimate, and everyone becomes friends. I'm so glad I took this class before I graduated.
This class is a great way to dip your toes in the art of acting. If you've ever considered acting as a hobby or a career, I highly recommend checking this class out!
recommend so so highly!
I loved this course, and am pursuing a secondary in TDM because of it. It challenged me in a completely different way than my other classes ever did, and loved it. I'd say to really put in the work to completely memorize and find the right material. You'll get out of this course what you put in, and the more you try, the more you'll enjoy it and learn from it.
Try to at least pick your scenes a few days before your performance on Monday! It can be pretty stressful finding a monologue, memorizing it, and rehearsing it all within the span of one evening. So give yourself more time to feel comfortable with the script. Marcus and Ashleigh are absolutely incredible, so please be open to their suggestions so that you can improve and be the best version of yourself when acting! I 100% recommend this class to anyone who's interested; it definitely helped me improve as an actress and express my authentic self.
