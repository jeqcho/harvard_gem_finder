Great course if you are interested in game theory.
Econ 1050 gives a nice and simple overview of game theory. While it brings up a lot of interesting concepts, it does not go too in depth into the technical workings of them. As a math concentrator, the calculations and problem sets were not too difficult. There was not too much work outside of class, so it was nice to take as a light class along with some very difficult ones. Overall, the class was fun and interesting and I’m glad I took it.
