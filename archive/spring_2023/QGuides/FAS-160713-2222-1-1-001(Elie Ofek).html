I would like to tell future students that, if you have any remote interest in business, please take this course!! I had never explored the field of marketing before and have really gleaned a passion for the field after taking this course. Also, it was really a unique experience to take a course from a Harvard Business School Professor, which is something that is not very common at Harvard College. I really enjoyed it when we went over to the Harvard Business School for one of our sessions and held class there. It really gave us a nice feel for the classroom dynamics in HBS and Professor Ofek gave us a great overview of how HBS works. Professor Elie Ofek has so much passion for the course content and the case–based learning approach is a great way to learn the material. Please take it if you get via the freshman seminar lottery! If not, then email right away to see if there are any openings!

TAKE THIS CLASS! Every week there is a discussion about a different case, and each case teaches you something new and interesting. We also got to have class at HBS once, which was very cool.
