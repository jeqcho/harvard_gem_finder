Extremely solid class, should have Chinese Bx as preparation or 1 year of Chinese school back in high school.
Harvard's Chinese department is incredible, and this class further confirms that for me! The teachers make every class so much fun, and the community is amazing as well. You will quickly progress your Chinese speaking, reading, and writing level, and also gain a deeper cultural understanding.
This class is so incredible, and I feel like I improved so much within one semester. You learn a lot and also make a lot of close friends who will help each other out because you have class everyday.
Take this course! The teachers are amazing and overall the course is a lot of fun. The only downside is you don't get to learn how to write (mostly just reading / typing.)
Would definitely recommend the class, but be do remember that the course load is A LOT. You will make great friends through the class, but it does involve 2 quizzes a week not including all the regular assignments of a class (perhaps even more since the work is double as you are learning at twice the speed of a usual language class). Personally, this class ended up being the biggest workload I had, which is saying something because I was not skimping out on other classes. You will learn a lot of material, from Chinese culture, new grammatical structures (20 new ones a week since each lesson has 10 structures and you learn 2 lessons a week), and LOTS of vocabulary (90 words a week since each lesson is 45 words and you learn 2 each week). The grammatical structures are also much more difficult than Chinese BX and it's unlikely you've ever used them before, especially if you mainly speak Chinese since they're more commonly used in writing. The vocabulary does definitely get more difficult too, I speak Chinese with the majority of my family because they don't understand English and the majority of new vocabulary I learned in this course, I had never heard of before. In the end, the struggle may be worth it, but it definitely is a struggle. The instructors are also amazing, but am hearing that some of the ones I had are leaving, so you may not get the positive experience I had with them. Would overall recommend, but there are a lot of things people should consider before committing!
