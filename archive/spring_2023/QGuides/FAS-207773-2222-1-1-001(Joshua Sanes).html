I would recommend taking this class if you have taken Neuro 80 before and have an interest in the visual system! The class is small so you really get to know everyone in class very well, including Professor Sanes and Aboozar (the TF). The amount of work you put in in trying to understand the content, is the amount of knowledge and benefit you will get out of the class. Though this class is graded very generously, I would encourage you to really put in the effort in trying to get the most out of this really wonderful course! Hope you take it!
Take this class! A great upper–level neuroscience course that you won't regret.
Best neuroscience class I've taken so far as well as one of the best classes I've taken at Harvard.
You get out of this class what you put in. If you do readings and engage with the materials, you will learn a lot. The instructors are very cool researchers and are also pretty accessible, so take advantage and ask questions and get to know them!
This is the perfect class. Period. And I'm a little sad that other classes won't be like this. From the beginning, Josh set expectations that this was not going to be a class of harsh grading or pop quizzes or tough exams, or anything like that – you get out of it what you want. Basically, it's a participation based course that actually makes you want to learn by eliminating stress in any form. I wish every single class was like this.
Wonderful course! A Neuro background will help tremendously.
Take this class! You learn a lot of very interesting material, and it is very low stress.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! I absolutely loved it! Professor Sanes is one of the kindest professors that I got to know, and he structured the course such that you get out a lot without having to worry a lot about big deadlines and workload. As long as you are interested in the topic of the course, you should be able to get a lot of fun and insights out of it! The lectures are delivered very coherently and eloquently and have both breadth and depth, so there's something for everyone, whether you are neuroscience concentrators or not. There was a nice balance of lectures by Professor and presentations by students, and I liked how this format gave you the opportunity to both get to know topics that you weren't familiar with and go into a discussion with your classmates in a fairly open and flexible setting, where you are welcome to toss out comments and questions as they arise. The reading selection is also great, some nice review articles that get right to the relevant points and some very interesting papers with fascinating/pretty clean experiments! Also, Professor Sanes really makes himself available to get to know you and answer your questions. The TF (Aboozar) has also been amazing, willing to help students whenever they are in need of support! I am a senior and this really has been a wonderful experience, and I can't recommend enough!!
Loved this classvery laidback but also engaging! The assignments were worthwhile because they asked you to hypothesize about questions which did not have accepted answers. Professor Sanes is a great lecturer, and very available to meet outsider of class.
