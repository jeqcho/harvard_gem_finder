In this class you will learn how to listen to music in a very different way. I highly recommend it!
This course is very low pressure, I feel like the only thing that felt rushed was the final project portion of the course; but I felt relatively okay to tackle this portion. I think the only comment that I was I knew coming into this course is that you won’t learn a lot of music notation in the course, if you don’t have some sort of prior knowledge of music as either a musician (from playing instruments or making electronic mashes), previous composition experience, or just time to learn more about music, this might be a timesink. If not, it’ll be a pretty low pressure class.
This is a wonderful class that changes the way you think about music, and it allows you to compose the kind of music that you like but encourages you to incorporate different elements and themes learned from the class, which will definitely make your style of music composing evolve over the course of the semester through the exercises.
