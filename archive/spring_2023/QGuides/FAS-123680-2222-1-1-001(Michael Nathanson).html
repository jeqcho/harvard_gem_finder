The grading model for this amazing, as it emphasizes a learning–based approach. It extremely motivating that you can work on learning objectives throughout the semester and re–test your skills when you make errors on the last course. This method of test–taking, paired the with completion/effort–based pset grading made the focus of the course much less about the stress of perfect arithmetic, and much more about the calculus at hand. I learned just as much if not more than I would have in a course that had standard grading and test taking model.

While this class seemed a little difficult from the beginning, especially jumping right into calculus after not taking it for a while, as the semester went on, the pace slowed down a bit as I got the hang of the course.
