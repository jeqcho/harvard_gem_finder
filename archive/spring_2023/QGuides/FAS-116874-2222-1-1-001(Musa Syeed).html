What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is a great way to be introduced to screenwriting. You learn a lot about how to write a script, and the workshops in class were very helpful. The workshops help you get different perspectives on your scripts, and you get great feedback from Musa and your peers. The short films Musa also assigned were very impactful and memorable.
This class is really great. You learn a lot but it is really fun, engaging and low stakes. There is a really clear focus on your development as a screenwriter and you are not forced to write or think in a certain way. The community (the students and Musa) are all interesting and different and thoughtful and take your work to really great, new and exciting places. The assigned readings and films are all so different and really take an open–minded approach to short films as a genre. This open–mindedness is reflected n every element of the class.
I would take this class over TV or playwriting workshops if you want to write about something that genuinely interests you
This is such a great course!! I got exactly what I wanted out of it! If you want to learn the basics of screenwriting, draw inspiration from a community of writers and short assignments, and produce work in a low stakes environment, this is the perfect class for you!
If you get the chance to take this class, TAKE IT! It is so fun and Musa creates such a great classroom vibe.
