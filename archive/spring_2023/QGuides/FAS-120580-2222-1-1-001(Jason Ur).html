This is a really great course if you are interested in spaitail analysis and learning new software. You don't need to be a computer person to understand and take this course. The professors are really terrific and understanding. You do need to have a good sense of time management and it is definitley helpful if you come into the class with a research question already in mind that you can work on throughout the semester. Overall this course is fun and different and I recommend it.
This class is a great way to learn ArcGIS and you will come away with a great understanding of how to do both basic and complex operations. However, the class is structured for you to have a final project area in mind as you begin the class. If you don't have a project area in mind, it is also completely fine but think of a project closer to the start so you are able to begin right away to apply these processes to your own work.
