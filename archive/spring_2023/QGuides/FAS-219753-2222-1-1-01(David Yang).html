Take this class! David is a great instructor and his research is truly fascinating. The class really provides an unbiased perspective of China and there is always something for anyone to learn no matter how familiar with China you are.
I came into this course with very little background, so for me, mostly everything was new. Also, I would say that being taught by Professor Yang is extremely fun because you can see how dedicated he is and how he truly wants us to learn. I’m not sure how it would be for someone who already has a lot of background knowledge relating to China, but for me who only knew the basics and more general ideas, it was extremely satisfying leaving the class having learned these many insights.
