There are easier languages to fulfill the language requirement with. Not a bad class necessarily, just a lot more work than one might hope it would be.
I would recommend that you have much prior experience with similar languages, if not latin intself.
Latin I & II exceeded my expectations. I went in already wanting to learn Latin and thinking that I would give the courses a year to see if I wanted to continue––now, I certainly do. Ivy is one of the most dedicated instructors I have encountered and the course is constantly evolving to fit students' needs (though it retains a core rhythm that works brilliantly). Be warned, the course is not easy; however, it is well worthwhile and manageable. Further, if you have any hesitation about learning a dead language rest assured that learning Romance languages (as I have) will become much easier after Latin. And, unlike contemporary languages, learning Latin will give you a new view of the world from thousands of years ago! I cannot recommend this course enough if you are interested in learning Latin.
