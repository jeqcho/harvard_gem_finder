What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this class if you have the opportunity to do so. If social anthropology or politics interest you in anyway whatsoever, you will enjoy this course. Professor Fierman is a joy to learn from and you will have the opportunity to blossom academically in this class.
This course is hard, and at times confusing. But the readings are very interesting and certainly worth doing.
I would highly highly recommend this class! I felt my whole self expanding with the readings and will carry them with me not only in future academic work, but in life as I navigate institutions and systems of power, and in reinterpreting/reworking/making other modes of power in my daily life. The readings build on each other beautifully, and because the thread is conceptions of power and sovereignty, we traversed a lot of spaces and time periods in the readings and discussions, which is also really expanding!
