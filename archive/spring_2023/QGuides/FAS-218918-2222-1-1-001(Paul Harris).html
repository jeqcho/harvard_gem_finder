Comments
This was an awesome class on the psychology of education! I learned a lot and would recommend it to anyone!
Do not take this class. I took this class as an elective and was very excited about the class (I care deeply about education and was very interested in what this looks like from a cross–cultural perspective). I was disappointed. The readings were interesting but the discussion surrounding them was subpar. Professor Harris clearly cares a lot about this material, but he chose texts/questions that he had a pre–determined opinion about, which stifled discussion. I did not feel engaged in this class, and I did not feel like my classmates were engaged. However, if you are able to write clearly and lay out a solid argument in an essay, this is a very easy class with not much work (100% of your grade is essay–based). For me at least, that didn't make it worth it.
