This class is a step up in difficultly from 120a. But it is really amazing to see the complex topics you are able to understand. Different to other Chinese courses I have taken, it felt like I was taking a course on Chinese history of culture at some point, just also learning Chinese. That being said, sometimes I found the content to be a bit difficult to digest because of the increased volume of work.
This class is set up very well and ran by a group of very passionate teachers. They push you to be your best and help in countless ways. I would 100% recommend this course.
45 Characters + 10 Grammar a week. It is a decent amount of content but super manageable.
I would tell future students to really stay on top of the vocabulary. Studying for dictation will help you learn the material and if you feel comfortable with the vocabulary your time in class will be much more meaningful
Chinese 120b is a step up from 120a – but your proficiency increases materially, which makes the class very rewarding. As with all other Chinese classes at Harvard, if you put the work in you will find you make substantial progress, and will find support at every turn from the stellar Chinese program teaching staff.
