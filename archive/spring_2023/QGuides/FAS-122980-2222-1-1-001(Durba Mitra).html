Comments
Don't stress about the junior paper! Think of it this way: you have an entire semester to write one paper. It's hard if you hype it up and try to make it the best paper you've ever written. But if you don't attach too much of your self–worth to the product and focus more on the writing and research PROCESS, then it will be much more enjoyable
This is a great course to prepare you for senior thesising and a great opportunity to spend a semester working on a project you are passionate about. I felt so supported, personally and academically, in this class and was given the tools to create a project I cared about and was really proud of.
This course is a really valuable chance to learn about WGS methods and understand the important considerations that go into choosing a research method. Even though it is a requirement, this course is super helpful for building on past WGS courses to offer an invaluable introduction to doing research.
I would tell future students that this course is very rewarding if you are interested in answering your own questions about gender, and especially if you're ready to be challenged in answering that question. If you have the time and work to put into the class, it will reward you back
I think they are working out the kinks to this course. While the readings, Tutors, and Prof Mitra are fabulous, the rhythm of the course was off and it never felt central to my learning throughout the semester, despite it being my concentration tutorial. This disconnect caused a serious lack of motivation and lack of desire to do more than i needed to for the class because i felt not completely overseen by the prof or by my tutor.
I would suggest that students taking this course meet with the professor early on to get a sense of what the shape of it will be like! I felt a little bit thrown into things and I wish that I had thought to check in at the very beginning!
The success of this class may be entirely dependent on the professor teaching it. I heard from previous year's students that they hated it, and it was really shocking and scary. This yer had a new professor, Prof Mitra, and she is FANTASTIC. She brings a certain kind of intellectual curiosity to the classroom that is so captivating, and her style of teaching is amazing. The entire course is based on individual projects, so pick a topic that will prepare you for the thesis during senior year.
