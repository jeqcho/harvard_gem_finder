This is a solid choice for your Expos class if you're interested in these topics. Dr. Lane is great; very enthusiastic, understanding, and, creates a lovely classroom environment. The course materials were interesting and diverse as well. Dr. Lane is also a fair grader, so if you put time into your essays she will reward it! Overall, I really enjoyed this class this semester :)
This course is wonderfully taught and I could not recommend it enough. Professor Lane will meet you where you need to be met and if you approach the course openly you will get a lot out of it.
The topic seems interesting at the beginning, but the preceptor is not very skilled and there is a lot of open–end discussion, which can be okay at class but doesn't help you much for the papers. So, if you know how to write take the course, but otherwise, you're not taught much, and Dr. Lane is not a very generous grader!
This is a PHENOMENAL class! Given that it was the first semester this expos section was offered, I was not sure what to expect, but it was wonderful. The readings/viewings are fascinating, and I enjoyed the in class discussions. It is clear Dr. Lane cares about her students, and you will receive plenty of honest and incredibly useful feedback that will help you grow as a writer. TAKE IT!
