What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is a mediocre class. It's possibly the easiest 100 level math class at Harvard but it can be dry. The first segment of the class is about riddles and it's very fun, but after that the quality of the course material drops off significantly. You must have experience writing proofs and of coding using python to take this class. Also, be warned that there is no professor; the instructor is just a grad student.
You get exposed to a lot of topics in math that other classes don't teach, and the environment is really chill, so I ended up learning a lot
Okay so you’re playing a game with your friend where there is a round table and you each alternate putting a quarter flat down on the table, with no overlaps. The person who can’t fit a quarter onto the table loses. What strategy can you use to always win?

This is a type of fun puzzle that you might encounter in this course, and you will learn how you can use both math and logic to solve it. Keep up with problems sets and have fun on the final project, you’ll be fine.
This is as close as a math class comes to being a gem. You'd still learn plenty. Highly recommend!
Fun class– pretty low key for the math department. Not an easy A and you'll have to still do work, but all around a good class
Take this class! It's a bunch of fun math problems. The homework are challenging enough so that it's not boring, but not too challenging where it seems impossible when you look at it. Sasha is the best, and the whole course staff is very nice. Going to office hours is very useful. Would highly recommend.
It's a pretty easy class. Just psets and a final paper. Classes can sometimes feel boring if you don't like the material being covered, but the good thing is they will move on to new material in a day or two.
If you haven't taken number theory or have a solid coding background, problem sets will take you longer than you expect. Overall good course though.
