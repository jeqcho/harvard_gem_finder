There is a lot of readings so I wouldn't recommend reading through all of them page for page. You can definitely get away writing a response paper by just reading a little bit of each paper. Also, they want less of a summary of the readings and more of your opinion. I will note that they grade the response papers pretty hard (e.g. a 5/5 was said to be extremely rare and so I never got a 5/5 on any of the 13 papers the entire semester). The workload overall is not bad at all, expect when it comes to the quizzes, which you really need to study a lot for. If you're taking other classes that also have 3 midterms throughout the semester, I would rethink taking this class because it can be a lot.
