What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Honestly, I think this should be a required course for everyone. Learning how to write is so important in both academia and industry, and this course is a really great low stress way to learn how to write well. In particular Suzanne is really dedicated to the students and put together an amazing and diverse group of guest speakers and readings. I am so glad I got to take this course and only wish I had taken it earlier in my studies to produce better papers.
Take the class even if you think you know how to write.
If you have something to write, it is a great way to get credit for doing so. The staff are great people and build a nice community. The lectures run a little long and it is not a great course for learning specific writing techniques, but rather the greater framework to think about scientific writing.
This class is excellent for writing. If you have struggled with the research writing process, this class will give you many tips to handle that better. I came out of it feeling confident in writing my future science papers.
Definitely would retake this if I was working on thesis or some other big writing project.
You should absolutely take this class if you are at all interested in scientific research and scientific writing.
