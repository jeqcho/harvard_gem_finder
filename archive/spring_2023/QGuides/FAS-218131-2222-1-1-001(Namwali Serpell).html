What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course really made me think more deeply about science fiction and how it relates to race. The reading list was really, really good –– some of the novels and stories from the course have become some of my all–time favorites. Professor Serpell has a lot of interesting insights about the readings that she shares in lecture. Definitely consider taking this course, especially if you like science fiction.
Professor Serpell may be the most brilliant person I have taken a class with at Harvard.
Although the readings for each week may seem excessive at times, the books themselves are quite interesting and not unbearable to get through. Also, theres not many assignments due each week so readings are really the primary workload.
The syllabus is amazing, and it also provides a historical timeline of the BSF genre.
Please take this class! It was great and you will enjoy it if you like scifi as a genre. The writing assignments may be a bit difficult if you are not accustomed to the style expected in English classes, but they are well explained and you could always ask Prof Serpell or the TF for extra clarification. If you are a histsci concentrator or getting and EMR secondary it will count for both of those! Overall a great class and I highly recommend.
I thought this class was very well–put together. Professor Serpell is a really wonderful lecturer and I always left class with new ways of thinking about the course's texts. I also appreciated the mix of written texts and the occasional TV episode or movie on the syllabus. Highly recommend!
