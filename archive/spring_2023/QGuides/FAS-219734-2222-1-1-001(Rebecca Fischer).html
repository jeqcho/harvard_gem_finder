This class is something that really changes person to person. Lectures aren't the most interesting. Bur lab can be fun, especially because of the interactive and collaborative settings it produces. But if you're not that interested in minerals, or don't have a basic foundation in mineralogy, this course isn't really for you. There are a lot of minerals to go over, and crystal structures and other things to memorize. While the assignments are not bad, the tests can be really difficult and hard to prepare for (and you NEED to prepare for them). If you like minerals and have a passion for them, I would recommend this class. The field trip especially was interesting and fun. But if you don't, this isn't the class for you. I will say, the teaching staff are great. While it may not have been the best match for me, both JJ and Rebecca were a delight to get to know and made the class environment very positive!
I would tell students that its very memorization–heavy with not a lot of study guidance to help memorize the material.
