What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Brilliant course. Took it as it looked incredibly interesting and by chance it happened to become even more interesting due to the war going on in Ukraine. The course has a lot of reading that you have to do each week, but each is manageable and interesting. You very much get out what you put in. Some people in the class initially weren't doing the reading but as the weeks went on, due to how engaging Professor Skakov and the material was, they ended up wanting to do the reading so started to.
I highly recommend this course for anyone interested in understanding the arc and impact of Soviet and Russian cultural artifacts, works whose morals and motives destabilize standards and stereotypes.
Take it! The readings can sometimes be a lot but they take up the bulk of the work required with not much else involved. They are also very interesting. The discourse in the class is great.
Easy and enjoyable class on a fascinating subject matter. Prof. Skakov has an interesting perspective and a fantastic network (i.e. he knows many of the authors/ artists personally).
Really quite thought provoking and interesting discussions. Make sure to do the readings well.
