What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I really enjoyed this course, and I was able to learn so much from it, especially as someone who came in with no experience.
I would recommend this class to students interested in making things involving both digital objects and materials! There are definitely elements of both digital and non–digital objects, so I think an interest in both would be a great reason to take this course! I certainly found that the class was a lot of work at times—I'd recommend starting the assignments early and attending office hours—but the teaching staff is incredibly helpful with completing those, which is really nice.
This course is no joke—one week you learn to 3D print, the next you learn to create a Bluetooth device. It's a big commitment. But as a humanities concentrator I have never learned so many hard skills in so short a time, and the TFs and instructor are all wonderful people who will support you through the steep learning curve. I highly recommend—although DON'T be shy and miss office hours/fall behind on assignments by not coming into the lab enough.. you will regret it come finals week
Definitely keep on top of your weekly assignments. If you start falling behind you will struggle immensely at the end of the semester to catch up. Also, start your final project early.
Take this class!
