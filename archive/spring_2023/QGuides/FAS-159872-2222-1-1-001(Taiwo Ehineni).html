What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
In this course, you have a teacher that is actually concerned with you learning and your progress. There are different parts to learning a language: reading, writing, grammar, and speaking, and while these things can be overwhelming in a language, Professor Taiwo goes at a good pace, and really adapts the class to student needs. You will learn a lot, outside of the language, and about language in general, but make sure to do the work and stay on top of things so you can benefit and won't have to stress a lot when it comes to exams.
If you even slightly are interest in learning Yoruba, take this class. Everyone is excited to teach you and learn from each other and the workload is very manageable.
If you grew up in a Yoruba–speaking household, I highly recommend this course. My parents speak Yoruba but I never learned it and this course has given me the tools I need to be comfortable attempting to learn and speak Yoruba further at home.
