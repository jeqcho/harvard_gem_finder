What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this course!! If you are new to fiction or haven't written anything before at all, I would strongly recommend this course! You will learn so much from your peers, and the class is is a very supportive / non–judgmental setting to start sharing your writing drafts with other people to get feedback. Also, it's a pretty chill class where you can learn a lot in a low–stakes environment. There aren't that many assignments and Molly is very understanding with giving extensions if you can't make a deadline. Also, the most important thing is that you will be surrounded by a community of passionate individuals who are all trying to figure out their identities as writers! It's truly really inspiring and has pushed me to grow a lot as a writer ;)
If you have the chance to, definitely take this class! Everything was wonderful––the assignments, the reading, and the discussions. You also grow and learn so much as a writer and human being. This has been my favorite course at Harvard thus far.
This is a great class for learning about short stories and compelling fiction writing. Molly is a wonderful guide into this world.
This is such a fun and helpful class for anyone interested in getting into writing fiction! Molly is very supportive and friendly, and most importantly, cultivates a positive atmosphere among the students. The focus is on cultivating a safe environment for sharing your work and receiving constructive feedback. The readings are enjoyable and not too long. Overall, this class is not too much work, but it really is what you put into it.
Apply. Don’t hesitate because you don’t think you’re a writer. Hitting submit on this application is the first of many invaluable opportunities for self–growth and vulnerability that this course will offer you.
Take this class!!! It has been one of my favorite college classes so far and I had so much fun with the material, the people, the professor EVERYTHING! If you are even considering taking this class with the amazing Molly Antopol it would not hurt to apply (even if you are a first year, I took this during the spring semester of my first year) and if you get in consider yourself blessed because you are in for a fantastic ride :)
You'll get out of it as much as you put in.
