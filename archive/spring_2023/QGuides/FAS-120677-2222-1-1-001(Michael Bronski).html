What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
A great class. Bronski keeps things pretty open–ended and casual. Try to read everything if you have the time.
A great foundational course to seminal (and emerging!) authors in WGS. Manifestos, prose, theory, poetry–– a great variety and interesting syllabus for sure.
I would say that if anyone is at all interested in WGS, even if not to concentrate or secondary in, or if anyone is at all interested in gaining a deeper understanding of feminist frameworks (which they should be), then this class is a good introduction and survey of the material that primes you for further learning afterwards.
Read the texts carefully because you get exactly what you put in in the class.
