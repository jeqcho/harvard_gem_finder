Take this class!!! Fantastic instruction and mentorship by Andrew and Ali. The best way to learn about research is by doing it, and you will learn so much at the end of this course.
I had an amazing experience in this course. Wonderful and dedicated course staff that care about their lab experiments, so great to learn with them for a semester. Was nice to break up other work with experimental work. Take it!
If you are interested in this topic, take this class! It will be a fair amount of time commitment but you will learn a lot and it will be very rewarding.
HEB 1210 gave me a unique experience to work closely with post docs on their own research projects. This was a new and difficult experience, but I learned a lot and it is very fulfilling.
You will gain exposure to different fields of research and insight into the experimental method firsthand through data collection. You will also learn valuable research skills including how to write a research paper and develop models which can be applied to many different research areas. Experimentation will be scheduled on your own time, and can be time–consuming, but if you have the space in your schedule, this is a very valuable class to take esp. if you are considering a phD or doing research in your career
