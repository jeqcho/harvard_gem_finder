Stephanie is super knowledgeable about all of the subject material. Sometimes, however, the class feels a little bit too focused on a single reading of the poems, and it can be a little bit difficult to get a word in.
This is a fun intro class to poetry. The essay topics are atypical and challenging, but they definitely pushed me as a writer. Sometimes feedback can be a bit confusing but overall I enjoyed this class and it was not stressful.
This is one of the BEST classes that I’ve taken at Harvard!!!! I’m such a huge fan of Stephanie — she’s so cool and knowledgeable and understanding and brilliant. I also adore the syllabus and there’s really something for everyone. I was surprised because every week I’d come to class and find a new poet that I loved. I’ve taken other poetry classes at Harvard but this one is my favorite. The breadth of works and combination of modern and classical poetry is so refreshing. If you have any interest in English literature, you should DEFINITELY Take this class.
