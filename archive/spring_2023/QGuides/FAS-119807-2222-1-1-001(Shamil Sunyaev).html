I would emphasize to future prospective students that the papers read in the class are almost all method papers, not papers focused on new biological insights.
This course provides a nice overview of many modern genomics topics. The final proposal at the end of the course is the most useful aspect of the class.
Just be outspoken, be less self–cautious.
Understand papers thoroughly so that discussion flows more smoothly.
This class is a really great way to read literature in our field of research and get to know your cohort through vibrant research discussions.
