Take this class if you are interested in learning about weather and the water cycle.
This GenEd is great. Kaighin is a really kind person and knows so much about the water cycle. The class is relatively easy compared to other GenEds, and only has a few assignments plus a discussion post. There is a section component, but you don't do a ton of new stuff there, you only review topics. The midterm is fairly simple, and there is no final. It's not a pure "gem" since there is lots of work in the second half of the semester, but it is far better than any other GenEd I've taken here.

The only negative was that the class was "back–ended", meaning that the assignments were unnecessarily clustered near the end of the semester.
