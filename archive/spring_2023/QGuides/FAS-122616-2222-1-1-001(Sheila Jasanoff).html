What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
For me, Professor Jasanoff's have been grounding and eye–opening in the best possible ways. The readings are super fun and insightful and it's the only class I've taken at Harvard that feels like every reading and every minute is essential not to just get a "grade" but to become a better person and citizen of the world. The STS modes of analysis explored in the class will likely change how you view science and technology, possible futures, and your place in the world. A whirlwhind and tour de force of an experience I recommend to everyone.
You will not look at the world the same way after taking a course with Sheila. This class allows you to look at what makes certain social, political, and scientific orders hold, and what is gained and lost in the process. There is no class at Harvard that so helpfully and holistically considers science, technology, and society in conversation, and you will be the better as a human being and a student having taking it.
It's a great class. A lot of reading and sometimes intense discussion but definitely worth the rigor for the perspectives and analysis skills you gain.
