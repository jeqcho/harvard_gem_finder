What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I was so sad to learn that Russ is retiring after this year, because he's one of the best instructors I have ever had at Harvard and it's a shame no one else will be able to experience his teaching and mentorship going forward. I had a fantastic experience in this class – the content experience (Fiction By Other Means) is as promised, and Russ is such a lovely person with a wealth of knowledge and experience and a really natural teaching and mentorship style. While he wasn't the most critical or technically focused writing instructor, the amount of encouragement he offered and his listening ear (in contrast to some writing instructors, who let their own experience and expertise become the focus in one–on–one conversations, rather than giving the student space to talk about and ask question about their writing) helped me develop my confidence as a writer and the inspiration to write more postgrad. I plan to develop the short story I wrote over the course of this semester into a novel this upcoming year, and I have Russ to thank in large part for that.
Take this! The change you'll experience during the course of the semester is beautiful. Plus, this is a unique opportunity to combine writing with other art forms. I learned so much about writing and how to apply its tools to other disciplines. The course helped reorient my perception of things where I wouldn't notice them before. It's made me a better writer and overall person experiencing life.
This course provides excellent workshopping on fiction writing.
