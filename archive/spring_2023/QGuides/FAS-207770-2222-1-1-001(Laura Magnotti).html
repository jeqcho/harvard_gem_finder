For all neuroscience concentrators, I highly recommend taking this course. It's not easy, but all grading is fair and assignments/tests manageable, and I've not only learned so much brain/CNS diseases, but I also understand many neuroscientific concepts more deeply. This is a really great advanced neuro requirement, and the structure of the course is very conducive for learning the material and understanding outside reading extremely effectively. During the lecture before, you learn the background info needed to understand the paper for the week, which you then have to write a 1 page discussion on (instructions vary week–to–week). I can honestly say that I've never gained as much from outside readings as I have from this course, nor have I felt as prepared for discussions coming into class. The tests are tough but fair, and the presentation is also a lot of work, but also fair. The material is also SO interesting and Laura is AWESOME. Take this course!
