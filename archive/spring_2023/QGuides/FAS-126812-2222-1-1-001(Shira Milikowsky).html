TAKE THIS CLASS! (1) the class is enjoyable and engaging and the instructor & TF are wonderful; (2) the assignments are fun and the course material is really interesting and exciting; (3) this class will be very very challenging, intellectually, creatively, and in terms of time management, but don't worry, you will definitely get a good grade if you put in the work; (4) be prepared to not really know what or when the assignments are until like a couple of weeks before; (5) overall you will learn a lot and have a great time doing it! NOTE: the final assignment performance is like the second day of reading week instead of during finals... there is barely enough time to prepare. good luck! (Seriously, though, it's a great class and you should take it!)
Shira is the best! This course exposes you to lots of exciting directors and gives you a great pool of knowledge and inspiration to draw from.
Shira and this class are great! I think that this class is definitely more work than typical TDM classes but it is worth the work. The productions you make feel really rewording and you leave this class with products that you can be extremely proud of. In addition, make the effort to get to know your classmates. This space can be such a fantastic one if you put in the effort to make it one. Directing comes from the heart and so being able to discuss those inner thoughts with your class makes this class incredibly valuable. Some of the most human conversations I've had in a college class were in this one.
You will learn so so much form this class. All of the assignments are very engaging and fun to do. However, I will say that this class requires you to put a lot of effort and time outside of class into it. I spent a good chunk of each week developing ideas, spending time in rehearsal, and doing lots and lots of reading/research. However, if you enjoy theatre and the arts it is so worth it.
Take this course! Even if you've never done directing or don't really know what it encompasses. This course will challenge you in so many ways, give you freedom to explore your wildest ideas and bring them to life.
