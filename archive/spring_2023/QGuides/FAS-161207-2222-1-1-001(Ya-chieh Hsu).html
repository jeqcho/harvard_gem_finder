Incredibly well taught, and every lecture is engaging both due to Ya–chieh Hsu's energy, enthusiasm, and teaching and the nature of the course topics themselves, which I found interesting, relevant, and reflective of the state of stem cell biology today.
I would highly recommend taking this course. Ya–chieh is amazing and the information you learn is foundational in your career as a physician/scientist.
Take this class!!!! It was so much fun, I feel like I really learned a lot, and Professor Hsu was amazing. She has so much energy, explains things super clearly, and it shows how much she cares about her students. The content was really interesting and spanned a lot of different areas of cell bio (such as the circadian rhythm and how it relates to tissue regeneration). We also had 6 paper discussions throughout the semester, and while they're dense, as any publication is, they're really helpful for thinking more critically about science and not always taking conclusions at face value. Overall, I would recommend this course with enthusiasm!
