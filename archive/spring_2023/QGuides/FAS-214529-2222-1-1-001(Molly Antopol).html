Take this class! Molly is a really wonderful professor, and the class really bonds over reading and writing short stories. The workload is fairly light (reading a few stories per week and writing two drafts of your own), but you get a ton out of it if you take it seriously.
If you get the opportunity to take this class, *take it*!!!! It was hands–down my favorite class and a consistent bright spot in my week all semester. Normally, I can barely sit through a two–hour seminar, but this three–hour class flew by every week. It's so wonderful to be able to sit with a small group of brilliant, amazing writers, all of whom care so much about the class, and just talk to them for an afternoon. The assigned readings are fantastic and introduced me to so many new authors I'll still be reading for years. I grew immensely as a writer myself over the course of the semester; it was so nice to have a low–pressure, built–in way to spend time with my writing. Also, Molly is just an amazing human being. She always made sure we took a break in the middle of class, had class outside when the weather was nice, and is just so kind, understanding, and supportive. It was a privilege to take a class with her.
