This is a really hard class to put into words; my best suggestion to decide if it's a good fit for you is to spend a few hours looking through a few readings. Also, even if you find them challenging (not challenging to read but challenging in principle), stick with them! This class, like all truly meaningful classes, is meant to open your mind and that involves asking hard questions. Also, Christine makes a remarkably supportive learning environment, so you won't /feel/ challenged when it comes down to it. I'm a better person for having taken it for sure, so I can only recommend it.
