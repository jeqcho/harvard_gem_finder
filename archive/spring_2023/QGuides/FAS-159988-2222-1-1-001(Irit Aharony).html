What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class was probably my favorite at Harvard. Irit is the kindest and most caring professor, and trust me it is worth it to get to know her well. The course is always so interesting and you become good friends with those in your class. Irit is extremely supportive, and she understands that everyone learns at a different pace in their Hebrew journey. She gets to know all of her students well and wants everyone to find enjoyment in the language and culture. The TFs were also great and brought so much enthusiasm to the course and were supportive when I needed extra help. Irit also made so much time for office hours. I cannot recommend this course enough!
This course is a lot of work and time in class and the 9 AM start can be brutal but the class is such a warm environment and a great way to start the morning! Irit is so amazing and caring and just wants her students to succeed. If you have any interest in learning Hebrew at all whatsoever, this is your place to do it and to do it well. I learned more in a week with Irit then in 8 years of Hebrew school growing up.
