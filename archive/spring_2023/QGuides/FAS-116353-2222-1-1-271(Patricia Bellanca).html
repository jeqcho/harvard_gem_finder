this is the best expos class you could possibly take if you're a humanities student or even just really like gothic fiction/goth things :)
Gothic Fiction is a great class. Only three reading assignments (one for each unit) and three essays. Dr. Bellanca was great in guiding engaging discussions on the material, and we were well prepared to write our own essays on the works we read. Overall, the class teaches you how to structure an academic paper at the college level and beyond.
I highly recommend this class. The readings were quite gruesome, but Dr Bellanca is an incredible professor, and you will learn a lot from her guidance.
