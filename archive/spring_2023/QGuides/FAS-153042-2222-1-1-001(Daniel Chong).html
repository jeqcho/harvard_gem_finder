This is a great course as long as you are willing to put in the necessary effort. Your skill level and experience with chamber music will determine how much work you need to put in, but you should not take this class if you are not prepared to put in as much work as the rest of your group.
Take this class!
This is a great class, and playing chamber music with peers is a great experience. The fact that we can play chamber music for credit is great, so take this class if you have time!
Honestly, the experience from taking this course can be a coin flip. Depending on your chamber group, you may have to end up spending several more hours rehearsing compared to other groups. There will always be pre–made dual degree musician chamber groups that will always sound amazing, but if you just want to enjoy chamber music as a regular Harvard student, it may feel like you're having to catch up. Not that the actual level of performance matters that much towards your grade though. The teachers/coaches are very helpful and insightful, and getting to hear other groups play can be interesting.
This course is a gem of the Harvard offerings. Its nstruction is top–notch and its community is intimate and supportive.
MUSIC 189 is the best music performance class here at Harvard. Take this class if you want to play with some of the best musicians anywhere in the country and make some serious chamber music.
This is an incredible course. I wish I could take it every semester! It changes how you think about playing music with other people.
Very good chamber music class!
