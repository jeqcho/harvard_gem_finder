What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is challenging for sure. Professor Edmonds pushes his students to become more careful, intentional makers of art, and being able to take a class with him is a real treat. Like most AFVS classes, the people who take the class will also dramatically determine the experience, as well. Also, undoubtedly the unsung hero of the class was the TF Allison, who was absolutely amazing.
I loved the course. I thought it was a very meaningful and engaging exploration of many aspects of photography, including its history, theoretical concepts regarding photography, and opportunity to engage in studio work. I thought the teaching staff with Prof. Edmonds and Allison were great. Feedback was very helpful and definitely pushed me to become a better photographer.
The course is a bit more geared towards the history of photography/lecture based style that was a little unexpected. However, towards the second half of the course, it shifted to a more studio focused course.
Intermediate classes are great! Take it if you are ready to make work!
