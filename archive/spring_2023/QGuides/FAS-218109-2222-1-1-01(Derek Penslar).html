This is a great course! Professor Penslar is wonderful – he is super knowledgeable and intelligent and also cares about his students and their learning experience. He is also quite funny. This is a fantastic seminar! Highly recommended if you're at all interested in social/cultural history, psychology, or the study of emotions in general. You'll learn to approach a topic historically that we usually take for granted as an ingrained fact of life. The readings cover a ton of fascinating material, from ethnic hatred to compassion to fear to love. Penslar is a wonderful professor, genuinely cares about students, and facilitates really engaging in–class discussions.
If you need a history seminar to graduate, take this one!! Professor Penslar (and Erin, for as long as she's here at Harvard) are very nice, approachable, and supportive people, and you will learn so much not only about the history of emotion but also about what it lacks, how you can read/write better, and how you can do primary source historical research. Also I am so grateful that Professor Penslar is AMAZING about enforcing KN95+ masks in class – it was one of the few remaining instances after mid–March where I could go to class and not actively be eaten alive by the worry of literally passing away.
