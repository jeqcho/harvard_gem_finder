What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Deepen your movement practice in community, as you produce dance scholarship, drawing unimaginable horizons for movement artists and scholars of our time. In HDP libraries become dance studies; we spent time producing and reading our movement because we know, our queer knowledge is more present here, within our sweat, than in thousands of books stored in any institutional library. HDP reshapes academic culture; humanity is at the center.. as a result.. we walk out recharged, prepared to continue producing the hard, transgressive work that should be at the very own center or Humanities scholarship.
If you are even considering the course, take it. LROD is by far the best instructor I have had in 4 years at Harvard and she (and Jessi and the other students) make this course so worthwhile. Be prepared to work hard in the studio and plan ahead for tech week/performances because they are time consuming, but this is an experience that you will not get anywhere else at Harvard or even outside of Harvard. This was a highlight of my 4 years of college and I wish I could go back in time and take it every year. A kind, supportive community—so much stronger than I expected going in. I made friends through this class while also learning from people, which rarely happens in academics here. I can't even begin to express how happy I am that I took this class. I grew as a dancer and personally, met amazing people, and know that anyone who takes the class will grow from whatever their starting point is.
TAKE. IT.
If you danced heavily in high school and miss learning and performing choreo from choreographers, take this class. You will find a community in It.
I would like to tell future HDPers to please take this class!!! Be open, be curious, have fun, explore, move your body, learn, absorb, take it all in! It is truly a privilege to take this course and I know that you will leave having a whole new sense of what it means to make art.
Take this class!! Any reservations you may have–– about dance, performing, etc–– will be addressed, validated, but ultimately resolved. This class will give you a home.
The Harvard dance project is an amazing experience that can completely change the way you think about dance in the best way possible. I encourage anyone thinking about taking this class to do so. You'd be surprised how much you could learn throughout the entire process about dance, about choreography, and most importantly, about yourself.
Please! Please! Please take this course! It will forever impact your time here at Harvard. Classes like this one should be at the forefront of Harvard's educative style and mission. Everything about what we learned in this class came full circle to the extent that we as students were able to not only understand what we were being taught but also the process and pedagogy behind how we were being taught. This alone makes the class truly transformative.
