What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I would highly, highly recommend this class for anyone who wants to have space to critically reflect on research methods as a whole (not solely in anthropology, but relevant to a range of disciplines). The class offers structure (i.e. weekly reading responses, practical activities) if you want that, and also offers a lot of space/openness/fluidity in the discussions and in your way of going about the activities and assignments (there are specific prompts if you find that helpful, but also a broader orienting question that you can approach how you like). The readings are amazing!!! And Kaya is wonderful and really makes space for curiosity and genuine conversation in the class.
Great class
