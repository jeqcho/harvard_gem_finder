Comments
I would highly recommend Sociology 1130, especially if you've enjoyed other interactive classes like Expos 40 and freshman seminars. Manja and Catherine as a teaching team provide the most welcoming, helpful, and kind environment I have found in a Harvard class. Just as importantly, the group of students is incredibly inspiring, and the class allows you to learn from them throughout. This class really pushed me to reflect more on myself as a leader, my experiences at Harvard, and my goals for the future.
Take this! Especially as as senior or junior, reflecting on your experience at Harvard is such a valuable activity that busy Harvard students often lack the time to do.
This is a fantastic class to take, especially as an upperclassman, to reflect critically on your role as a student leader at Harvard.
This class is one of the best Harvard has to offer. In addition to learning from Professor Klemencic, you will be able to learn from your peers about their own experience, likely in organizations similar to your own. The workload is incredibly reasonable, and there is a ton of support if you ever feel overwhelmed or behind. Manja is genuinely excited about every single project that a student produces in the class, and it is so clear in the feedback that she gives, and the opportunities for publication or broader sharing that she offers at the conclusion of the class. Every student who holds a leadership position at Harvard should try their best to take this class.
You will not regret taking this course. Manja and Catherine are easily some of the best instructors I have ever had. They take every effort to get to know you on a human level and actually treat you like an adult. They make sure that you are doing research in an area that you want to be doing research in, and they make sure to hold you accountable to your own work. They are incredibly thoughtful, and seeing them always made my day. Finally, the work you do in this class is very practical and is intended to be that way.
Take this course! 100% Take this course. Don't waste time and enroll ASAP! Manja and Catherine are probably the best ever! You'll meet so many passionate people and learn so so so much!
Put everything you can into the action research project, it will be so worth it and so rewarding.
This course was the greatest class I took in my time at Harvard. Would highly recommend for ALL student leaders invested in supporting the development of their groups.
