What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I am passionate about clinical trials, but this class was not a good representation of the field or research that is ongoing in clinical trials. However, the class itself is not incredibly time–consuming.
Wypij is passionate. HWs are well paced. Learn a lot about the statistical ins and outs of trials.
Unless substantial changes are made to the course, I would advise potential students to consider other advanced core electives. As essential and wide–ranging of a subject that clinical trials is, the opportunity cost of forgoing another high quality advanced core course in order to take this one is not worth it in my opinion, given the current quality of the course. As it currently stands, students are not pushed very hard, the course is fairly disorganized, and the coverage of topics is too topical to leave the course with a strong grasp of many of the topics that are covered. I hope this changes in the future.
A chaotic course, but a reasonably light workload.
