Description:

A small group tutorial systematically guiding students in the writing of original, hypothesis-driven research proposals from initial topic selection through completion of a final draft.

Course Notes:

This course is open to second year BBS students. Others need permission of the instructors. Students will be placed in small groups using the sectioning tool in my.harvard. Dates, times and locations for Sessions 3 and 4 will be determined by the faculty running the small group sessions. Group assignments will be posted on the course website.
