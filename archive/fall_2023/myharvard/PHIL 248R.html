Description:

What does it mean to say that two things are equal? The answer differs depending on whether we are comparing two formulae, as in mathematics; two countries’ welfares, as in economics; or two individuals’ treatments under the justice system, as in law. We will discuss the concept of equality from mathematical, economic, legal, philosophical and other vantage points in this seminar course, which will be limited to about 25.
