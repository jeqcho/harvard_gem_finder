Description:

This tutorial will review substance use disorders (SUD) and addiction, with a focus on the ongoing opioid crisis and rising methamphetamine problem in the United States. Content will include: 1) basic neuroscience research on neural circuitry underlying substance use and addiction, 2) individual, community, economic and sociopolitical contributors to SUD, and 3) clinical trials and latest directions of SUD treatment. 

Students must complete both terms of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
