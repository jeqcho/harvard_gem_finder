Expos 40 is very dependent on your preceptor –– some sections have very little work, and others give more. I suspect I was in a section that gave more work (our speech assignments were more extensive, we had more guidelines to follow so were more limited in what we could speak on, which made speeches harder to construct, and we often had impromptu Q&A sections), but I also learned more than I would in other sections. I learned a lot about my speaking style, and how I can give more memorable speeches –– vocal variety, hand gestures, etc.
TAKE THIS COURSE! It can take a bit more time than you would expect but it's really cool to practice giving speeches over and over again. I got a lot better at public speaking just from this course
TAKE EXPOS40! Public speaking is such an important skill and something you won’t get comfortable with/ good at without lots of practice. The feedback is really helpful, the professors are passionate and well–qualified, and you’ll make great friendships in your class.
