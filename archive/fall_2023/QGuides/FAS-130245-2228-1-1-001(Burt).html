Class is a little bit awkward. If you do take this class, make sure to get a good role–playing group because that makes it fun.
TAKE THIS CLASS! So fun and the readings are actually interesting and entertaining. GEM for sure and worth the time! Plus Stephanie is a wonderful teacher and the TFs are amazing.
Professor Burt is excellent: very engaged, clearly very smart, and does her best to make this a fun and rewarding class for each student. Many of the works on the syllabus were also excellent, and the course staff is also flexible on extensions and making sure your outside–of–class health and wellbeing is preserved. Overall, wonderful course.
Great class. Great staff. If you get Wyatt as your section leader, you are in the best hands. He is incredible and I am going to recommend him to everyone. The readings can seem overwhelming at times, but there's some good stuff in there!
This course was one of my favorites I've taken at Harvard. I would highly recommend it even to students outside of the English department, as it is not too demanding with reading.
Great course, great instructors, great material, just don't get too caught up on doing deep reads on every assigned reading.
LOVED this class. I took it as an elective as a non–English concentrator, and it was maybe one of my favorite classes I've taken at Harvard. Stephanie is the most caring and thoughtful professor I have ever had, and is incredibly intentional about how she teaches the class. The reading load is a little heavy, which means sometimes you have to pick and choose what you read or skim (unless you are a super fast reader), but otherwise it is a reasonable courseload.
Ask for extensions if it helps you write a better paper! The professor wants to read good, thoughtful papers more than timely ones! AND UTILIZE SECTION LEADERS FOR WRITING ADVICE THEY ARE VERY GOOD AT IT!!!!
Rarely does a class feel both relatively painless and intellectually rewarding, but this class is both—it's a delightful, well–run course with a teaching team that truly cares about each student's learning. Stephanie's insights on texts are brilliant without ever delving into obscurity, and the teaching team was massively helpful in advance of each paper (which had very generous extension policies!).
Stephanie is so fun!
