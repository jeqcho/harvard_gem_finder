I loved getting to see all the musicals/plays in Boston. We also got to see live shows almost every week, which is an incredible opportunity. Luckily for me, the shows always landed on the afternoon after one of my big problem sets was due, so I got to decompress every week by watching theater. The small nature of the class ensures that you get close with your fellow students; creating community is one of the most important parts of the first semester, so for that reason alone I'd recommend this freshman seminar. I honestly don't have much to say aside from singing this class's praise. If you love acting and theater, I can't recommend it more!
