What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you are at all interested in engaging with South Asian literature, culture, and intellectual output, please consider taking Hindi–Urdu 102! Richard ji is an incredible instructor. He cares deeply about all the students in the course, makes himself available to support students outside of class, fosters a collaborative classroom environment, and encourages students at all stages of learning.
I grew up speaking Hindi but had no background in reading or writing it, especially for Urdu as well. I was scared going into the class that it was going to be overwhelming, but Richard ji made the class as manageable as possible while also giving us time to catch up or improve ourselves as much as we want. Fluency in speech is also not needed at all. Everybody was at a different level, and at one point, you really didn't notice it.
No matter where you are in the process of learning Hindi or Urdu, as long as you have some fluency, this is the class for you. It is extremely helpful for practice and building fluency. It's also just a really fun and enjoyable time, and I would look forward going to this class every day.
Great professor and class!! Finally became literate in my own language.
