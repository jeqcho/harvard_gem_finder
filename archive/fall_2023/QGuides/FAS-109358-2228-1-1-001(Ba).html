What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is incredible. While the content is certainly quantitatively heavy, the complex concepts are wonderfully explained by prof. Ba who won't move on from a topic until he sees that the class has gained some intuition about it. You are never expected to memorise useful proofs or grind out hours of busy work. Instead the focus is on learning about and discussing the many ways that we can represent and analyse data to obtain useful and interesting information. If you are a bioengineer or interested in how the body is quantified in any way take this class. Even if you're not, spending a semester being taught by Prof. Ba will never be a semester wasted. For BioE/BME students looking to fulfil requirements, I cannot recommend this class enough. As someone who didn't enjoy BE110, do not let any comparisions to that class sway you from taking this one. There might not be shopping week anymore but at least give it a try. If you put in the effort you will get so much out of it (and, unlike many engineering classes, you won't be spending hours wanting to pull your hair out only to get a bad grade).
Take it!!!
Take this class! Demba is a great teacher and this is probably one of the better–taught classes at Harvard
It's a great elective. The material is very cool, even though it's challenging. Assignments are designed so that it is fairly easy to do very well if you put in effort. The class size is small, and overall you have a good time during lecture
I loved this class. Prof. Ba is fantastic and is so knowledgeable and just very fun to learn from in the classroom. His structure of splitting the class with a break in the middle makes it so much easier to digest lecture, and the time flew by during this class period every time surprisingly quickly. The material is engaging, it isn't easy given the higher level concepts, but it definitely is doable alongside other difficult classes. The weekly work felt a little relentless possibly because OH were scheduled right before the work was due most of the time, so we all ended up cramming a lot of the work into the day before due dates, but the grading is nice, and getting an A is certainly not out of reach for most people. Just pay attention in class, don't get distracted on your computer, go to section, and take advantage of the time with the great course staff! Shubham, Manos, and Demba are gems and this class was fantastic. Sometimes the psets got a little disorienting because it felt like we had never seen the material before, but looking back, they were so helpful in seeing how the concepts are applied. Although it sometimes felt like the psets weren't aligned with the lecture schedule. Review your linear algebra before coming in. And your derivatives and series knowledge!
This is genuinely such a great class!! The course instructors are super supportive, kind, and care about all of the students. The concepts in the class are also very applicable to modern data analysis, and I feel like I learned so much. The labs were my favorite part of the course, and collecting your own (and friends) data was super fun for the labs and the final project. Really really recommend this class!!
