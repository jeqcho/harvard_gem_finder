Take this class
This course covers really interesting questions and arguments regarding conservatism as an ideology, as well as exciting readings (in my opinion). Nelson is a fantastic lecture who can break down the most convoluted of political theory texts into digestible argumentation. However, as in many social science classes, I believe your experience in the course is more tied to section than lecture, and I unfortunately felt section to be underutilized, often questioning what we were doing there. Teaching staff are not very accessible nor do they give significant feedback on assignments, overall leaving the impression that this was not a class I could learn and improve as a thinker in.
