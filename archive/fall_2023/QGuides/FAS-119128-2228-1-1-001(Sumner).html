Just to clarify –– this is the Celtic language of Ireland, not speaking English in an Irish accent. In general, anyone interested in Ireland should start with this course.
I would strongly recommend this course to anyone interested in learning Irish (obviously). It is taught at a very manageable pace and the small class environment is very enjoyable and a great opportunity for getting to know the instructor and the other students.
This is a great class! If you have peers who are willing to engage and a teacher who is interested in teaching, you will find yourself loving this course.
This class is really great even with no background in the subject. It is relatively easy given how difficult Irish is as a language, and I, as someone who only speaks English, thought it was very manageable. This class also brings together a really interesting combination of people, and because it is small you build a great community.
