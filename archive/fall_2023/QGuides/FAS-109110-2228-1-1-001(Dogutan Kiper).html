This was my favorite class of the semester for sure. The teaching staff is great and Dilek is a wonderful teacher and a great person to be in the lab with. The class is capped at 6 students, so you really get to know your classmates & get more individual attention. I think if you're choosing between this class and Chem 100R, you can't go wrong with either but I am happy I chose this class, especially considering I was not super interested in inorganic chemistry beforehand, and because of this class I've learned a lot of lab techniques & feel comfortable doing a lot of lab work independently.
TAKE THIS CLASS. Low stress, high reward. You learn so much and though the 5 hour classes seem daunting, it flies by so quickly.
My favorite chem class at Harvard. Amazing vibes and Dilek is just so very kind, funny, and overall great. The paper as a culmination of your work is really cool too.
My only advice is to do your post labs right after class to make your life easier.
This is a GREAT class! If choosing between chem 100r, chem 165, and this, definitely take this with Dilek!
This class is the best way to fulfill the experimental requirement! Teaching staff is amazing and so supportive, 10 hours a week in class is a lot but for me the classes practically flew by. This was a great introduction to inorganic research and just the research process in general from lab work to paper writing and academic presentations this class has it all. Very little time requirement out of class, they are very cognizant of how much time we spend in class and don't want to add more. Take this class!
Best chem class!! Dilek, Brandon, and Dan were great, understanding teachers and mentors. They care about your learning and well–being (i.e., you get a short break every lab). You learn many useful lab techniques regardless of your experience. The projects are structured so that your group writes a paper at the end. Lab reports aren't bad at all as long as you do them right after class and take lots of pictures! If you're willing to put up with the long class hours I highly recommend
Definitely take this class, you will not regret it. Dilek is a gem of the Chem department and although the time commitment may feel overwhelming, this class will be your favorite 10 hours of the week.
