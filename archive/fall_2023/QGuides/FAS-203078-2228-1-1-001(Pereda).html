What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Two assignments all semester–– a 10 page paper halfway through and a 20 page paper as the final. There was a printmaking workshop within the course this year for the first time that was a very fun component, so hopefully they keep that. I'd say overall this is a pretty good casual class with well–prepared lectures, and the amount of help you get on the papers is dependent upon how much you ask for help.
This class was life–changing. The interactive print–making was super fun and helped me to understand art history from an entirely different perspective. The work load might seem intimidating but the pace is very self–determined so it is quite flexible. Both Professor Pereda and Colin are readily available and enthusiastic about discussing ideas and papers. This class helped me realise how exciting art history could be.
Professor Pereda is the best lecturer I have ever had at Harvard. Super engaging, useful, and interesting! An amazing class!
