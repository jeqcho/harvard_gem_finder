Please take this class! I promise that it will be the best class you take your first semester here at Harvard. You will be able to go to the arboretum at no cost, which also provides an amazing way to get off of campus and spend time in a very peaceful space, which was a great asset to me during the instability of the first semester. This class is great for both STEM and Humanities students (coming from a Humanities student), just make sure you're willing to learn more about trees, yourself, and the world around you.
