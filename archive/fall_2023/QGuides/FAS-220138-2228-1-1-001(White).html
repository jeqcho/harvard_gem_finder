This class is for people who are genuinely interested in creative writing. You will get just as much out of it as you put in. I loved it!!
Take this if you want to do a lot of creative writing and read new things
I really enjoyed this course. Lots of learning about technique and craft. Lots of enthusiasm for horror as a genre. Lots of useful feedback as a writer. And Professor White was really nice and helpful. He started out the first class by giving tarot readings as a prompt for our first project. And he made sure to meet with us individually to give us feedback and talk about how we are doing. A fantastic class no matter where you are coming from in your creative writing journey, big recommend for me.
Just an all around excellent workshop. I'd been meaning for literally my entire Harvard career to take one of the english department's creative writing workshops and finally actually did it, and I am so, so glad I did. I learned a lot as a writer and also a lot about the Horror genre, one I was only just barely familiar with and starting to branch into. I feel much more confident with my skills within this genre. The professor and the rest of the students did a great job of creating an environment with amazing feedback and respectful, helpful criticism. Honestly just a great class and I'm so glad I took it.
