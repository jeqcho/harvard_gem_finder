I took away how religion is a huge part of human experience and what makes us human. And of course, the term religion is broad. It can be about the views of the notion of the numinous and the divine. It can be about the elevated sensations of collective effervescence. It can be about ritual practices and acts that are seen with a holy or sacred dimension. It can be about the process of making art. There is a transcendent element within all of this that calls us to pay attention. I highly recommend this class.
