What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is an amazing class. It is taught extremely well and very engaging. It has been my favorite class at Harvard (current senior). There was no busy work, and the work we did was informative and worth while.
Really fun and interesting class!! Some beginner –level understanding of environmental issues and photography is helpful, but not necessary. Coming in as someone who has never taken an AFVS class, this was a great way to get introduced to the department and what it has to offer.
If you're thinking about taking this class, you 100% should! I had never had the opportunity to discuss photography in tandem with climate change, but now having had the opportunity to do so, I've come to realize how incredibly important it is to view seemingly objective photos (like those featured in National Geographic) with a critical lens. Dr. Best is a wonderful teacher, and regular trips to the art museum make the class super fun and engaging. The class expanded my understanding of photography and artistic techniques, and the final project provided a great opportunity to explore an issue I cared deeply about.
Be prepared to talk a lot about photography and the many issues that surround landscape.
