The professor was so caring and intentional with her words. She cared not just about us as students but about us as presenters, writers, and people. She was always available to discuss our projects. Also the curriculum was superb; the interdisciplinary structure of the class was excellent and I feel like Ive learned a lot about different ways to approach history. The class can be a lot of work with a lot of reading and writing, but she puts a lot into you and you will get a lot out of it. You will get however much you put into this class out with practically no upper bound.
This is a great class, and it really is what you make out of it. I gained so much from the readings, discussions, and final paper, and it was very enjoyable. Professor Higginbotham was really nice and it was a small class so sometimes she would bring snacks and it was a very welcoming environment.
