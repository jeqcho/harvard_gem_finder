Great class!!! Work is minimal but very enjoyable and engaging. You have the freedom to make each assignment your own. The artist visits are so special and rewarding — you get to meet and speak to some incredible and renowned musicians in a very intimate setting.
Take this class!! As a neuro person, this is one of the only music classes I would take (I’m also Asian though so I’m biased)!!! It’s super chill and Professor Iyer is really cool!
If you have any interest in either music or Asian American studies, you should take this class. It's a very chill time to talk about identity and music with Professor Iyer and other students in the class, and the assignments really feel like they are for yourself instead of for a grade or class.
Take it, go in and be yourself. Be honest. Many great convos were had and interesting questions uncovered due to students speaking honestly in this class.
