What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class was filled with positive energy and was very relaxed. It would be a great class to express yourself without too much stress or homework. However, if you are wanting to take a class to truly push yourself as an artist, I would not recommend this course and suggest participating in more extracurriculars where you can further learn your art through practise since this class primarily revolves around talking about your art form (unless that’s what you prefer, then this class is perfect for you!!).
I would recommend the class if you have a passion project you're currently working on or dreaming of working on.
Take this class if you've always had an artistic project that you wanted to pursue but never had the time and/or resources.
