Comments
Great course for an introduction to historical linguistics. Do not take if you are interested in computational linguistics. The course material is a lot of reading and lecturing. It is fun if you are really interested in Linguistics and are considering a concentration/secondary in it.
Take it if you are interested in language or linguistics – very fascinating and very low work load.
This class is a GEM! Jay is literally one of the smartest people I have met in my entire life and he totally changed the way I perceive languages and where they come from. This class has a reasonable workload, is always incredibly intriguing, and is something I would definitely recommend to anyone interested.
As long as you have any interest in language, I one–hundred percent recommend this class. It is a relatively light time commitment, and the material is absolutely worth it. You will be a better student and a better critical thinker after taking this course.
