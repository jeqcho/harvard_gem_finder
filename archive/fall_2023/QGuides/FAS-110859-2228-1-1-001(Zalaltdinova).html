Comments
Take this class if you want to learn about the diversity of Russia while improving you speaking, listening, reading and writing skills.
Amazing class, definitely a great way to continue the work from third year Russian in terms of grammar, speaking, and vocabulary, but more–so in terms of learning much more about the Russian speaking world, which is so much more diverse that we are lead to originally believe. Just beautiful.
This is a critical course to improve your Russian language ability to discuss a range of topics, from politics to literature. I highly recommend this class for advanced Russian language students, as any student will make leaps and bounds with professor Liya Zalaltdinova's guidance.
