Expos 40 is a really strong class for gaining confidence as a public speaker. I've given important speeches all my life (student council election stuff) and yet I found myself learning and growing a ton throughout the process, mostly from other student styles.

It's a reasonable amount of work with weekly assignments and monthly speeches so it can catch up on you. But overall, this class is phenomenal. It's not "must–take" per–se as I've heard other people describe, but I think if you have space in your schedule you will be better for it and it's 100% more worth it than your 7th econ class.
Take this class! Especially before you graduate. You may consider yourself a pro at public speaking but there are always small things that can be picked at and improved upon.
TAKE THIS CLASS. It's been one of the most positive academic experiences I've had at Harvard. Our class formed a genuine, supportive community and I think my public speaking skills measurably improved as a result of this course. Strongly recommend as an elective for any seniors with extra space in their schedules.
TAKE THIS CLASS, one of the best at the school and great for a fun class junior or senior year
This class is amazing. Everyone raves about it for a good reason. By far the best class I've taken at Harvard. It's such a nurturing learning environment and everyone is so encouraging. Bailey was an outstanding preceptor. You won't find another class like this at Harvard. I've grown so much from this class, and I will be forever grateful I was able to take this class.
