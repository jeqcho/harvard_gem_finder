This is a great class for a true overview of all of the topics in Cosmology. The material is taught in a simple way, and Professor Chen generates lots of enthusiasm for the subject. I would certainly recommend this class to anyone who enjoyed the cosmological topics of Astro 17 and would like to tackle them in more detail, as well as anyone looking for an introduction to the subject, as Professor Chen does not make the course too difficult for anyone who has taken Physics 15a and knows calculus.
This course is a great introduction to cosmology! If you are interested at all in understanding how the Universe began and the current state of knowledge about it, even if you're not an astro concentrator, I highly recommend this course. Xingang is a fantastic lecturer, and Misha was an awesome TF...the course is well–paced, even through more challenging and mind–boggling concepts, so you're able to get out really solid knowledge about cosmology from the course. The exams are also very fair and the pset workload is super manageable. Overall a super fun and great course!
This was a fun, relatively low stress course. The homeworks were interesting but not overly time consuming. The final was take home and open book, which took off most of the pressure and allowed for the problems to be more in depth than a usual exam.
Very well taught course! Misha and Xingang are great educators. But the course is pretty boring and involved a lot of abstract math
Cool, not–to–hard course that is super interesting. Concepts can be difficult to understand, but it's well–tailored to undergrads.
Going to just copy/paste what I said in the "strengths of this course question" but: One of the best physics/astro classes I've taken at Harvard. Professor Xingang explains concepts in intuitive and easy–to–understand ways, and his lectures & lecture notes are very easy to follow. He always explains where he gets mathematical processes & results, and is willing and able to re explain concepts in different ways if someone didn't understand something the first time. He & Misha (the TF) were both incredibly responsive and helpful throughout the semester.
In addition, it's just a really cool class––the subject matter is super interesting and you get to learn about the formation of the universe down to within fractions of seconds from the Big Bang. We derived some large equations that were simply quoted in previous astro classes I've taken, and it was very cool to see where those concepts and equations came from. All in all an excellent class.
Take this course! One of the better courses I have taken in the Astrophysics department. You will not regret it. The teaching faculty (Xingang and Misha) are very understanding, and are able to explain concepts very well.
