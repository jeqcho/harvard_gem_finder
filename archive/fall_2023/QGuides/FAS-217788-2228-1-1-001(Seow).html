What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great introduction to the humanities if anyone's interested in the area
This class is very helpful for students who are interested in History of science, and also the modern world in reflecting where we're at right now! It is good for both history students and other majors in reflecting on the work we do everyday and some historical debates in thinking about labor, science, technology that is gradually transformed.
Professor Seow is amazing and kind, and the class is a fascinating look into the history of work, as it weaves in and out of the history of science. The discussion posts can seem like a lot, but they're actually a really great way to engage with the reading before class, and Prof Seow asks helpful questions to guide your responses. Some of the books we read are ones that I will continue to reference in other classes, and even for the rest of my life.
This seminar is extremely welcoming to students of all academic backgrounds.
