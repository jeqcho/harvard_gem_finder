What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course was honestly super fun. I really liked getting to delve deeper into Syntax, and I genuinely had a great time in class even though two hours of Syntax doesn't sound fun at first. If you're not really into Syntax you'll probably find the content a bit dull, but if you like Linguistics at all, you'll love this class. The course is super easy to keep up with, and the content isn't too hard to understand.
Please refresh yourself with a lot of syntactic knowledge before class!
It's a fun course and the topics are interesting (even though they change every year) but be ready to be a little lost sometimes if you have not read a lot of syntax work outside of this class.
If you have not taken at least a full semester of syntax, consider taking the undergraduate course first or spend some time before the semester to read a textbook, etc.
