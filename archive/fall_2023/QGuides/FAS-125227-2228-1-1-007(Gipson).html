If you are interested in improving in public speaking definitely take this class. It will temper your stage fright and give you tools on how to speak more coherently. You will also get closer to your classmates given the nature of the class.
This class is really fun! One of the few courses at Harvard where you actually become friends with your fellow students. It's a surprising amount of busy work if you're like me and aren't comfortable winging speeches. However, the work is very easy and usually pretty fun. The class goes by surprisingly quickly, although 2 hours of listening to other people can get a bit tedious and you have to attend. Would recommend if you're looking for a light course, but don't plan on skipping class/coming unprepared, it is obvious and disrespectful to everyone.
Not a completely life–changing course, but I found it to be useful to formally learn public speaking skills. I discovered a few habits I have––both positive and negative––that I'll work on in the future, and also spent a lot of time thinking about how to construct persuasive oral arguments. Not a ton of work, but make sure you have time to pay some attention to the class otherwise you won't get as much out of it.
Take this class! Even if you hate public speaking. It's a very safe environment to practice and become more comfortable public speaking. You get to know your classmates pretty well so you feel comfortable speaking in front of them and receiving feedback from them. I definitely feel more comfortable public speaking and I learned a lot of techniques for speaking outside of public speaking, like how to network and how to answer common job interview questions.
This class is amazing! Terry is fantastic – he will challenge you in class, but you will grow so much and make connections along the way. A must–take!
Good class, definitely puts you outside of your comfort zone if you're uncomfortable with speaking, but in a good way. Can be a bit stressful preparing your speech but feels good once you've given it. Your class gets really close and is enjoyable to attend.
