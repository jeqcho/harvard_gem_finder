take this class!!! it's not necessarily the most hands–on in terms of the teaching team walking you through tangible skills so expect to be learning and doing a lot on your own. BUT in that sense it teaches you how to be a filmmaker because it very much is a process of being on your own & trusting your intuition/building your practice. and the accountability of having to make something over the course of the semester and bring in new material every week is enough to force you to make something you really care about.
This is a really great class! The structure is very nice, with the first third of the semester spent mostly watching films, and moving to showing footage that students are filming in the last two thirds. It is fully conversational, giving feedback on each other’s work, and thus how class goes is partially dependent on the other students. Be ready to put in the hours, because you will be filming probably weekly, with lots of time spent in the editing lab. It is a very rewarding course, though, to get to spend so much time developing your project. Come in with ideas for your film!!! The class gets going quickly. It is a great class, and it was so fun to get to know people through the documentaries they were filming. I would recommend it to anyone who is serious about to try making a documentary! You don’t any need experience beforehand, though!
I'm wrapping up my final semester of business school and cross registered in AFVS 51A this semester. I've loved business school, and have taken some incredible classes, but I enjoyed this course more than all of them. Discovering true passions in life are rare but that's what I did this semester. I fell in love with filmmaking.
Although it can feel like a lot of work at times to film/edit, if you have the chance to take this class with Ross and Vera, you absolutely must!!!!
Make sure you have enough time outside of class to really dedicate to this film. A great film in this class requires attention to detail and a commitment to a good shooting/editing schedule which is all on your own time.
