Absolutely take this class if you can!
Sit in a room with curious and introspective artistic students for 2 hours a week. Read wonderful interdisciplinary papers from art, the liberal arts, and science; grow a unified understanding of the brain and art / beauty and everything in between. Discuss your life and everything around you. What is art, and what can art do for us? Paintings, literature, music, film, happiness, museums, … this class changed my view of the world and gave me a place to think hard about the art that surrounds us, as well as the art we create.
This class is a gem — fascinating topics, wonderful instructor. Coming to class every Thursday brightened up my whole week. If you have the opportunity to take this course, please enroll!
Definitely take this class! Nancy is such an amazing professor. This class is for you if you love discussions about literally anything. A lot of students in the class discussed it as being extremely therapeutic and insightful. I think that it is super important to take a course that is somewhat lower stakes but generates discussion that you can learn and change from. Neuroaesthetics is such an interesting topic and unless you take the class you will not believe how much there is to know≥
This is one of my favorite classes I've ever taken at Harvard! Dr. Etcoff is a leader in her field but she's super humble and down to earth. She seems to genuinely enjoy facilitating student discussions and can speak on a wide variety of topics. If you have any interest at all in neuroscience, art, architecture, fashion, medicine or just hope to gain a more emotionally informed life perspective you'll definitely enjoy this class.
Highly recommend this class! You learn a lot from the readings and class discussions. Professor Etcoff is incredible!
