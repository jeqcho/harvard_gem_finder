Take this workshop!! By the end of the semester, I felt like we had really bonded as a class, which is always a sign of an amazing workshop. Claire is lovely, helpful, and supportive without letting you off easy; she will push you to grow and be a better writer. The readings are great and several of their authors visited our class to talk/answer questions, which was so cool. And the fact–based aspect is really unique – not something you'll necessarily encounter in your average fiction workshop. Over all, this class was frequently the highlight of my school week; would highly recommend.
