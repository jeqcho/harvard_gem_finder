What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is one of the best courses I have taken at Harvard. Donhee is a great lecturer who really cares about his students. If you get the chance, you should take this class.
I would recommend this course if you finally want to learn E&M in a digestable format and with the best E&M teacher around.
No exams, just homework = a very nice breath of air compared to the engineering courses you may be used to. If you're interested in learning the fundamentals of Maxwell's equations and take things from electromagnetism a step further to understand the applications of this kind of physics... take this course!
Be sure to pay careful attention in lecture because we go at a pretty fast pace, but don't be afraid to ask questions even if you're just curious about how / why something works.
