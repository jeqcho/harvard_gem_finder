I really felt like I learned everything we covered in the class very well! It is very empowering to know how many of the common analyses we use in modern–day research and computer processes work under the hood. I also thought the class had a very positive environment, which was largely established thanks to Professor Tzipermans's positive and supportive attitude and banter in class. The homework was manageable and really helped me solidify my understanding of the course content. After taking this class, I would definitely recommend it to others, and I am personally considering taking more classes with Professor Tziperman!

My only small gripe with the class was the extent to which we were expected to do many operations by hand with a simple calculator, especially on some of the exams (e.g. multiplying multiple 3x3 matrices filled with 3–digit decimals together). I felt that the time spent trying to catch small mistakes in calculations like this could take away from learning the actual processes we were performing.

Otherwise, this is a great course, and I would definitely recommend!

All in all great class, good applications of linear algebra, not too hard, interesting as an introduction to machine learning algorithms!
