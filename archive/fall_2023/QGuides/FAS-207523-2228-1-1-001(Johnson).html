Professor Deloria is easily one of the best professors at Harvard. You simply have to take a class with him. This course was Deloria at his best, and 1 hour and 15 minutes has never gone by so quickly. Every lecture was engaging and important, and the course is well–designed. It is pretty much impossible to cover all of American indigenous history plus modern indigenous culture studies in one semester, but Prof. Deloria gave an effective overview/introduction. There are elements of history, literature, food, fashion, politics, museum studies and so much more. This is an important subject with a stellar professor. I LOVED this course!!!
This is a broad introduction to the field of Indigenous Studies, and consequently it's a little unfocused, but Professor Deloria is great and the class doesn't ask too much beyond your serious engagement. If you don't have a lot of experience in the division, it's not a great requirement–filler, but if you have something to put in you can get a lot out.
