If you are in need of a stats elective, this is a GREAT one. It is incredibly intuitive and just makes sense –– unlike the stats classes in which you stare at a question not knowing where to start, you almost always understand what is happening and why it is happening. The first 6 psets are very easy compared to every other stats class. The difficulty picks up a little bit towards the end but still, very intuitive and very doable. The final exam is a take home 10 hour exam –– it was decently similar to lecture slides with a few twists... it was certainly not easy but with 10 hours you are able to stare at it and figure out the few twists for the most part (I found the exam a lot easier than my previous stat class finals). Overall if you need a stats elective TAKE IT!! This is a gem of the stats dept!!
