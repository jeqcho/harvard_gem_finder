What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Good class to learn more about Brahms. There was no midterm, and the final was a great performance of chamber music by Brahms that we had worked on throughout the semester. The actual playing of Brahms was the highlight of the class, as we got to actually apply and think about how to use the knowledge and understanding that we had just read about and discussed. Readings were sometimes overly complicated and convoluted, but the general points they got across were important. Fed is very knowledgeable and extremely passionate about anything he decides to teach, which can lead to sometimes disorganized class sessions but informative and very interesting nonetheless. Someone with incredible insight if you are willing to spend the time to try to access it.
This class was undoubtedly one of the best I have taken at Harvard. This is a rare opportunity to combine the nuanced academic with the practical performance.
Take this class! It's a great combination of performing, discussing music, and reading.
