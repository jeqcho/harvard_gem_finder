Take the class! Great instructor, great atmosphere, great readings. Perfect introduction to the subject matter.
This course is fascinating! Every week we learned about a new type of scam in American history, which greatly improved my understanding of the culture at the time. While the reading can be long at the beginning of the semester, most of it is very engaging. Halfway through, we switched to watching movies each week (along with secondary source readings), which decreased the workload. Discussion was interesting, and Angela is a fabulous teacher. Make sure you go to her office hours and get to know her! Overall, I would highly recommend this course to anyone interested in this topic.
Take this course! The material is so interesting and likely a topic you will never otherwise learn about in a history or literature class. Also for the second half of the semester you watch a movie every week which is really fun.
This class provided a very interesting perspective on the history of the American con in many different contexts. The readings are superb–– very excellently curated and with great intention. The writing assignments build you up to writing a great final paper. It is a lot of reading and work, but totally worth it if you are interested in this unique slice of American history!
Take this class! This is not just a pre–1865 course. This is a course that's incredibly engaging, the materials are incredibly entertaining to consume, and Angela is a fantastic teacher.
