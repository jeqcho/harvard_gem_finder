What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Gem. Fun and engaging course in art and theater history.
Take it.
This course is a great introduction to theater design concepts for those who are interested in set design and costume design. It is, however, not a course that focuses specifically on one or two concepts or historical time periods, so I would not recommend it for people who want to learn more about a specific topic. I really appreciated the instructor's enthusiasm and encouragement to be creative. Overall, this is a great class to get an overview on the history of theater.
Take this class if you're looking for a fun, informative, stress–free addition to your week! You'll have the opportunity to receive really great feedback on design concepts and will feel free to experiment and explore your own aesthetic sensibility! Prof. Smeliansky is a great teacher and person and creates a really fun and positive environment for learning and conversation!
This is an important primer course if you have no formal training in set or concept design yet have done work in adjacent spaces. I find that my artistic gestures moving forward will be more confidently made because of this class and its presentation of historical context and critical analysis.
Absolutely take this class. It was my favorite at Harvard this far!
I would definitely suggest this class to students with an interest in set design.
