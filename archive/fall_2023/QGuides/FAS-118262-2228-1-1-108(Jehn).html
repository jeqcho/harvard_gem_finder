There are so many opportunities to get feedback on your work before submitting your final papers, allowing you to earn the highest grade possible. Readings are not as extensive as other courses, and the course is overall a lot less demanding than others. If you have reservations about your writing skills, definitely take this course.
It is extremely important to manage your time wisely. Give yourself enough time to complete assignments and try out new tactics in your writing.
