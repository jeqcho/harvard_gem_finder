This course is extremely helpful in keeping you accountable during the thesis process. I would have found it incredibly difficult to have a thesis that was completed without this course. However, I wished the timeline was more accelerated the previous semester to allow more of the thesis to be completed.
Don't expect to only get your thesis done based on these assignments.
I highly recommend staying on top of deadlines when it comes to your thesis. The tutorial has built–in deadlines, but it's also important to know your own writing schedule and spending time earlier will always benefit you later. Also, make sure to leave plenty of time to carefully proofread your thesis before you submit it.
A great class and solid way to ease into the thesis writing process, without it being too overwhelming. You will ideally get into a rhythm of writing, editing, and reading that will prepare you well (hopefully) for the rest of the process.
This class is really good for keeping you on track with your thesis!
