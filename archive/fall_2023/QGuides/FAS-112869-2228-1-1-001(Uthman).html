Ustaaz Nader SLAPS, take this class if you want to be in a fun joke–filled environment while actually learning Arabic and getting more fluid in your discussions! As an upper–level course, this class is not about language drills or vocab memorization, it's about fluidity and organic understandings of material. Ustaaz Nader is great at connecting with his students and helping them understand concepts in a way that's enjoyable, approachable, and very often brings in hilarious pop culture references. If you're on the edge about feeling "prepared" enough for the class, I'd urge you to take it — it will most likely be a pretty small class, and Ustaaz goes to great lengths to make sure content and discussion is understandable for everyone. Take this class if you want to keep up with Arabic in a more organic way with a teacher who really knows what he's doing!
This is an excellent class. Made me fall in love with Arabic even more. 20/10.
