THIS CLASS IS AMAZING! Probably my favorite seminar I've taken at Harvard – take this if you're interested in climate responsibility and want to candidly discuss these topics with a few other students once a week. There's not much work on a week–to–week basis, and I'm really excited about the term paper I produced at the end of the semester. There's not too much guidance in assignment instructions, but I think it's just because it's a new class and will develop over time. Peter is great and has such deep insights in this industry. Guest lectures can get a little dry sometimes, but overall I really enjoyed this class. It's definitely not limited to ESPP concentrators – one of the greatest strengths of this class is its diverse set of student perspectives.
