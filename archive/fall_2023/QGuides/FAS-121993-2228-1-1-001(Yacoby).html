What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Definitely take the class. Will let you play around and do some really cool experiments you wouldn't have the chance to otherwise. The student faculty ratio is great and you will receive help and supervision from very knowledgeable people. Your writing will improve a lot from the paper criticisms. Your ability to break down complex topics quickly without clear guidance also improves tremendously.
This class is a lot of work, but worth it! There is no substitute for working hands–on with an experimental setup. The structure of the class is straightforward— 3 experiments with a write–up for each (as well as oral quizzes at the beginning with the professors). You’ll get to spend a lot of quality time with your lab partner, and Joe/Jieping are amazing!
If you're looking for an introduction into experimental physics–especially if you haven't done experimental research– then take this course!
