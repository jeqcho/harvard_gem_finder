As an AM/Ec student – TAKE IT if you want to write a thesis in economics
The course makes the thesis process much more approachable. It provides great structure to the whole process.
Write a thesis!!! It will make you appreciate economics as a discipline that much more.
Might be the most rewarding experience of your entire academic career, but not something to take on a whim.
It is effective mainly as (A) a commitment mechanism to fight thesis procrastination and (B) a second advisor in case your thesis advisor doesn't see you often enough.
Martin's section is great and you will get plenty of good feedback. This class is required but is actually really helpful in terms of the structure it gives to the thesis process.
Figure out if your idea + desired approach is realistic very early on.
