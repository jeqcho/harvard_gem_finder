Take this class! It is one of the most relevant to real life and important ones that you'll take in college. The material is useful for everyone, regardless of what you study. There is not much overlap with other economics classes if you're a concentrator, and it's also very accessible to students outside of economics as well. Sometimes the problem sets can be a little tricky, but all the CAs are very nice and helpful. You may have to spend an hour or two per week in office hours. It's not necessary to do the textbook readings, but some of the books such as The Financial Diaries and The Index Card are eye–opening. This class made me much more informed on how to manage my finances and debunked a lot of misconceptions I had. You'll be really glad you took this class!
