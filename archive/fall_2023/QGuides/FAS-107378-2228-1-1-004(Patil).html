If you are wanting to learn a new language, THIS IS THE COURSE TO TAKE!!!! Prof. Evans is an amazing teacher who truly takes the time to make sure you understand the basics of the language but also in a fun and enjoyable way! The workload is not heavy, and he is always open to helping out to help you better understand the language. It is constructed very differently than other language courses and is not as stressful.
I recommend this class for the community it creates and if you have no experience but are enthusiastic to learn you will take a lot from this course.
