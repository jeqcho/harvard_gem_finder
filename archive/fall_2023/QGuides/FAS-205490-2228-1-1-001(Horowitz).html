This is generally not a course I would recommend taking. The readings are interesting, and the idea of zoobiquity is too, but it really is not well–taught in lecture and takes ideas of evolution a bit too far in quite an unsystematic way. Lectures were repetitive, boring, and dogmatic more than they were insightful or intellectually generative. It's not worth taking a course on all of this (although I guess if you're pre–med, this is better than no exposure to thinking differently about things?
