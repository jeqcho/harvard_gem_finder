Definitely be prepared to balance your time with this class in your schedule– it's an amazing class with a wonderful team of professors but it takes up a lot of time outside of class.
This is an awesome class! It takes you to new heights in your Korean speaking and learning and also gives much deeper insight into South Korean society and culture compared to elementary and intermediate levels. It requires strong speaking skills so prep in that department would be recommended.
I highly recommend taking this course. Unlike previous Korean courses, this one focuses more on content (readings, writing a paper, interpreting survey data). While it might seem hard at first, the teacher is super helpful and tailors the class to the students' needs. I also highly recommend the course because of the section. Instead of drilling grammar, you are simply expected to discuss and debate in Korean for half an hour.
Don't underestimate the workload, but you will definitely learn a lot!
This class is amazing! The professors are amazing and you will improve a lot, especially in how fluently and naturally you speak. It does take a lot of work and has a decently heavy workload–– you could probably manage it with another heavy class but almost definitely not two other heavy classes. The grading is not necessarily harsh but you're by no means guaranteed an A or A–. Definitely worthwhile to take!
