animation is such a unique art form and i couldn't have asked for a better introduction to making it than through this course and AFVS 53 before it. ruth and sarah are fantastic. you're going to need to stay on top of things to make your final project, so it definitely requires some self–organization, as a lot of this studio course is self–directed. animation can be incredibly time–consuming, but it's manageable if you're able to budget your time.
great chance to continue polishing animation skills
This is a great class to take if you want to dedicate a lot of time to the studio, and to a final project that will be yours from start to finish. It's worth noting that animation is slow work, but that Ruth and Sarah are very supportive and encouraging. I would recommend prior experience with animation. It's also important to know that this class is held and the work is mostly carried out in two windowless rooms, so if you're someone who needs to see the light of day to not feel anxious, this could be a tough class.
