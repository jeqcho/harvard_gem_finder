TAKE THIS COURSE! PROFESSOR LI JIE IS AN AMAZING EDUCATOR!
Really cool class! As the class title suggests, it's a broad survey of all sorts of media in roughly the 20th–21st century PRC (photographs, posters, livestreams, television, music, etc.). The only thing is that we don't really examine texts (fiction, essays, etc) as much as I thought we would. The professor really tries to adjust the class curricula to student interests since media studies is so broad and so much of the primary sources tend to be very new.
take it! prof. Li is great!
