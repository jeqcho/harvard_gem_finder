What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Come prepared with a project idea before the semester starts.
The final research project for this class is more like a continuous project that is conducted throughout the semester. It's a really valuable opportunity, so I definitely think starting to brainstorm research topics earlier and being mentally prepared to dive in and work ahead is a good call.
Get IRB approval ASAP
This class is quite demanding but worth it! I know Michele suggested we think about our project before the semester, but I can't emphasize enough how important this is, because the class moves very quickly, especially in the beginning.
In addition to providing a general appreciation for qualitative methods, this course was really helpful for broader professionalization. Several students used their projects to apply for fellowships, conferences, and grants, which is a helpful skill to develop as an early–stage graduate student.
It's definitely worth starting recruitment as early as possible. Even if you are still developing your blueprint and interview schedule, start as early as possible and think of alternatives. Always talk to your TA and Professor about your concerns so that they can give you alternatives. Talk to your peers who are struggling with the same issues.
Wonderful class but be prepared for a lot of work!
