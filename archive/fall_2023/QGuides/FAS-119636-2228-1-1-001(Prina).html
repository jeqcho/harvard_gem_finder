Take this class ASAP
This class is wonderful if you are looking for a way to pursue your art practice on your own while still having a community to convene with every week and come back to to share your thoughts/ideas/challenges. I would recommend that you already come with a pretty definitive project or point of exploration in mind though, given that this class is relatively unstructured. Stephen Prina is a fabulous artist and teacher and provides really valuable ways of thinking about art and the artistic process.
