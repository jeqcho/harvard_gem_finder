This course is a great introduction to the field of astrophysics, and Professor Randall is an excellent instructor for the course! She is incredibly knowledgeable about almost all things astrophysics and is more than willing to discuss anything within the field that the class finds especially interesting. This is a presentation–based class, so keep that in mind if you choose to enroll. Overall, you should come out of this class with a greater understanding of the history of astrophysics and the current state of the field.
