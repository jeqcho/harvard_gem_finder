THIS CLASS IS INCREDIBLE!!!! It was an absolute delight to be a part of and I would recommend this course to anyone who wants to get to know their own physical habits that they move through the world with (and then get to evaluate them and break them in cool and meaningful ways). This class is a must.
If they offer this class again, you should 100% take it! It was a really great change of pace getting to go to the dance studio twice a week and just dance. Ana is such a wonderful person and an excellent instructor. She really makes everyone feel welcomed and I learned a lot about the Gaga movement style from her. Every class was a bit different but it was all practice based, so each class is basically just 75 minutes of dance. There were only a couple of written assignments and they were mostly self–reflective, so they were relatively easy (and actually helped me think about my progress in the class). It definitely felt a bit weird at first dancing in front of others, but once I got over it, it was easily one of my favorite courses I've taken here.
If you are willing to put yourself out there and embrace the weirdness, I would totally recommend Gaga. As an athlete, it was cool to move my body in a different way. This is also a super low stress class in the sense that you basically just have to show up, so if you're looking for something to balance out your schedule, this is it.
Gaga is a great class and Ana is great! This class was a great part of my day and helped me find peace in my hectic schedule. It helps you become much more in tune with your own body and how it works. I will say that if you are a dancer, do not sign up for this class expecting to learn rep or any technique. It is an improv and exploratory based class.
Take this class if you need a class that doesn't stress you out! It's honestly like meditation and if you're a dancer/like dance it will make you think about dance in a new lens.

Best class on campus.
You get out what you put in to this class! I was open to the material and committed to being present in the space, and I had an amazing semester! You definitely do not need to have ANY dance experience to take this class (I did not).
If you take this course, leave your ego at the door.
Gaga dance class was an amazing experience that I would highly recommend to anyone. Despite being a dance class, I found that I learned more in this class than in some of my CS classes. This is likely due to the immersive and hands–on nature of the class, which made it feel like I was really learning in a meaningful way.

One of the best things about this class is its convenience and proximity to the quad. It was easy to fit into my schedule and attend regularly, which allowed me to make the most of the class. The workload was also manageable, with only a couple of short assignments to complete in addition to the dancing.

Overall, I would say that Gaga dance class is a must–take if you are in the quad area. It is a fun and enjoyable way to learn, and I feel like I came away from the class with a lot of valuable knowledge and skills. So, it is highly recommended to all the students.
