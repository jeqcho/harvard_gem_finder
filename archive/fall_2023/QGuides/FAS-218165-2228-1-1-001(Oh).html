What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This was a good class, and I would recommend it to Korean heritage students who want to challenge themselves to get better at Korean in an accelerated manner. There is a lot of homework – at least one thing is due every day of class. Although the homework isn't particularly "hard", it does take up a lot of time all added up, so keep that in mind. The speaking sections were very helpful, and special in that you get direct feedback after just speaking! I liked how this class focused on our needs and the teacher tailored the curriculum to help us learn the most. I did take some issue with the communication aspects of this class. Some assignments were in the syllabus but not mentioned again, and sometimes there was conflicting messaging on Canvas or no place to upload assignments. As it is an intense language class, there wasn't much lenience, which was frustrating at times. Overall, though, I enjoyed my experience as it helped me 1) get better at Korean 2) make Korean friends in a small class setting.
The class is definitely not the same as Korean 123XB, and it does have more assignments and expectations. However, as long as you pay attention in class and follow the assignments on Canvas, you will do well.
This course is requires a lot of work and there are few lulls in the workload throughout the year. There was also some frustrating communication of assignments. That being said, however, the course is very rewarding. Your Korean will absolutely improve significantly. Also, Oh 선생님 is great! She lets the class have fun, but is strict when she needs to be. If you are willing to put in the work, I recommend taking the class!
