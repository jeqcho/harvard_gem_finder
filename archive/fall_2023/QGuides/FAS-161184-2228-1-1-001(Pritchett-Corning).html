Highly recommend this course! Professor Pritchett–Corning is fantastic, and the lectures were super interesting and engaging. A great way to learn more about the mouse, whether you're a science concentrator or otherwise.
Although this course is focused on mice, the professor is such a warm soul that makes these quite detailed topics fun, applicable, and engaging. In addition to mice, I learned more about evaluating scientific research, and animal ethics as they pertain to research. I think I left with a more informed and nuanced perspective regarding the necessity and practices of animal research. Prior I was quite against or disagreeable towards animal research, but now I better understand the care existing behind the scenes and also the importance of it. I also loved being able to see the labs that exist at Harvard and the mice themselves.
