I highly recommend this class, even if you are not interested in business ethics particularly. You will learn so much, and should be ready to have some of the best discussions and conversations in any class offered here.
This class is great, I would highly recommend to anyone interested in Spanish, especially if you want to learn about the businesses across latinamerica and the way that history and world politics have influenced them. This course is super interesting and also a great way to get more confident and fluent with spanish. I improved my spanish speaking a ton. You have the chance to do research and present independently on a topic of your choice, as well as the chance to work in small groups on exams to learn from peers. The assignments and readings are manageable and Señora Gutiérrez is a great teacher and always willing to help out! You will bond with your classmates and learn a lot – highly recommend :)
Absolutely LOVED this class. The readings and materials were so interesting and covered a broad range of both time and places in Latin America. This course was one of the most engaging courses I have taken at Harvard and also incredibly amazing for my Spanish proficiency. Super interesting if you are in any way interested in economics, history, or just Latin America in general. TAKE THIS CLASS!
This course is great! Fully recommend. Make sure to keep up with all the readings.
If you have the option to take it, do it! Especially with Adrianna. She uses the socratic seminar method and it really helps you improve your speaking. She also is not a harsh grader by any means so I feel that I was more willing to take risks on assignments and challenge myself. However, I will say that even with easy grading, it is not a gem because it does require a fair bit of reading and writing. I spent the most time on this course compared to any other but it wasn't the most difficult by any means and I actually enjoyed the work.
