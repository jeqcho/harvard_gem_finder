It was an awesome class, so amazing. If you're a Chinese heritage speaker you DEFINITELY should take. The instructors are so caring and meticulous and respond to feedback amazingly.
Take this class!!!
This course is really fun because the classroom size is small and we all get to speak in Chinese (the fluency and skill level varies somewhat widely) and have lots of laughs in class. The teacher speaks in simple Chinese and it's a lot of fun! Homework can take quite a while though (4–5 hours for me), but there's only one per week! Recommend taking it!
Take this class if you're interested in learning Chinese! Honestly no complaints. The work may seem like a bit much on paper, but it doesn't actually feel like it cause the teachers do such a good job preparing you in class.
If you are a heritage Chinese learner but feel like your Chinese is rusty or you haven't had much experience reading or writing, TAKE THIS CLASS! The teachers are fantastic and always bring so much energy into each lesson, and I genuinely believe that my Chinese has improved dramatically over the course of just one semester. One thing I will say about this course is that it occurs every day and the building is pretty far, so it definitely is a big commitment to take this class and it will take up a decent amount of your week. In addition, the hand–written homework assignments every week can get pretty draining, so be prepared to spend some hours on that. Another thing is that the lectures are all done in traditional Chinese (even if the homework, drills, and tests can be done in simplified Chinese), which caused some of the material learned in class to go over my head. However, overall I am very glad that I took this course and I feel much more confident in my language abilities, especially going into tougher higher–level Chinese classes in the future.
This class is super well–designed, fun, and interesting! The staff is so supportive, and you will learn a lot. After taking this class, I felt more comfortable chatting with family members and reading Chinese. If you have a background in speaking Chinese, definitely consider taking this class. Note though, I can be tough if you don't have much background. I only knew pinyin and had some speaking background and this class was a lot of work. The class focuses on culture, speaking experience, and reading/writing in Chinese. Learning the vocabulary can be tough if you do not have much experience reading or writing in Chinese, as I didn't and had to put more time into learning them. It was stressful at times but the staff is super understanding, supportive, and amazing!
