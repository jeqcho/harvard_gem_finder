Act Natural is lowkey one of the best classes I've taken at Harvard. A lot of classes in the creative fields here can be intimidating, but this class felt super accessible thanks to the chill course staff and fun texts peppered throughout the syllabus. I had no formal acting experience going into the course, but was able to take away a lot of practical ideas from this class and honestly feel inspired to maybe try acting for realsies one day. In addition to acting, the class also digs into related subjects like performance theory and artificial intelligence. This course is an absolute GEM that I highly recommend. Zero acting experience necessary!
