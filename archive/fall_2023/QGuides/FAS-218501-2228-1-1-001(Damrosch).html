What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Taking a freshman seminar is a really nice way to meet people and this class was very conducive to getting to know your peers in a more formal setting. The class is not too much work, but is enjoyable if you like to read and have group discussions.
The texts in this class and the discussion can feel very over your head if you do not have a strong literacy background. The readings are interesting but hard to fully engage without background experience.
The readings are well–selected, but the discussions are what you make of them. Sometimes, I felt like the discussions went in a direction that didn't add much to my understanding of the text. That being said, the texts on their own were great–––I enjoyed the readings every week.
