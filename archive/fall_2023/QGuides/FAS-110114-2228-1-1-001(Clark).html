Collegium is one of the most supportive and committed groups of musicians I've found – with the leadership of Andy and Jon and student leadership, we were able to make masterful music while also learning to take better care of our voices and our community.
Collegium is a fun experience if you are looking for a fairly laid–back choir experience, however if you’re looking for something more challenging, I would recommend an alternative such as Harvard University Choir. The rehearsals (4 hours a week) are quite a big commitment, but if you’re really into music, it is worth it, as the concerts are always very high quality.
This class has a great sense of community, and you get perform and travel with your friends.
Absolutely take this course! It will be the highlight of your Harvard experience!
Collegium is a great experience. It can be challenging at times, but it's all worth it in the end. It's a great way to continue past musical experience while bonding with an amazing community of singers from diverse backgrounds and experiences.
Collegium is awesome!!! If you enjoy singing, I think you should definitely consider joining us. The community is unparalleled and has been one of my tightest groups of friends on campus. We do really interesting and often grand music, and the course really is a learning experience outside the classroom. There's an international tour once every four years, and this year we'll be going to Sweden!
Choir was really fun, and when it came to performing, even more fun! The conductors are super nice and people in the choir too. It is a great way to meet people with an interest in music. There is a lot of diversity. We have grad and undergrad and probably all the majors possible, from math to anthropology to linguistics.
Collegium is one of the warmest, most welcoming communities that I've found on campus, and it doesn't hurt that they're one of the most musically talented. If you like good music, friends, and performances, it's wonderful.
This is an excellent half–course. Isn't it great when you can get credit for an extracurricular?
Strong choir and great music. Really enjoyed the experience.
