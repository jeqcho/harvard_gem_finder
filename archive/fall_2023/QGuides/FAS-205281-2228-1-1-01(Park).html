What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Professor Park is the sweetest and most knowledgable professor of Korean literature. She has an obvious passion for the subject, and that helps to generate enthusiasm throughout the course. There are A LOT of readings, though.
Take this class if you are interested in translated literature and/or Korean history. Prof. Park is kind–hearted, knowledgeable, and open–minded – this made the class a comfortable environment for learning. I would say that class size matters for this course since the discussions were kept short, and might be even shorter if the enrollment for this class was bigger.
This is one of those GEM courses at Harvard that you will come across serendipitously in your course catalogue search, buried and hidden under other large classes. Take it if you have the chance! It is such a unique opportunity to get to know Professor Park, who is a fantastic professor and conversationalist. You will learn so much about Korea and Korean literature, and the things you take away from this course will undoubtedly impact the way you interact with Korean society and society in general.
This class is incredible, and so much work. You get out of it what you put in, and Professor Park really cares about the class and every student. If you are at all interested in Korean history, visual culture, and obvious, literature, you'll have no regrets taking this class. If you have a heavy workload besides this class, however, know that it could be quite a tricky one to balance with others. As someone who was also thesising this semester, I often felt the volumes of reading and writing required were too much.
You get out what you put in!!! If you want to have fun in this class, participate thoroughly. Let your reactions to the course material be genuine and emotional. This is your chance to talk about really cool fiction for two hours a week, and the workload is a lot more manageable than it looks at first glance.
Take this class!!! This is the best class I have ever taken. Professor Park is truly the most wonderful and kind instructor ever. You will not regretting taking this class.
