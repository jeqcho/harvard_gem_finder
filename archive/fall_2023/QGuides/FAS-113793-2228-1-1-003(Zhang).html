If you're going to take a language at Harvard, take Chinese! The teachers are all helpful and supportive, and it's really the most well–thought–out department you'll ever be a part of.
You will make friends and learn so much 中文！
Definitely worth it to take Chinese 120a! I took Chinese BA and BB, and it's pretty much the exact same expectations and workload, so if you've taken them, you'll know what to expect, which is really nice. I got into my study groove and just sailed through the semester on it. There were definitely times (many times) throughout the semester where I felt like I was WAY worse at Chinese than I'd been before, but it wasn't actually true ––– I'm just getting to the point where I know so much Chinese that I can also understand how much I don't know. But I'm truly getting so much better at this language, and it's a huge sense of accomplishment to feel like I get something tangible out of the work and time I put in. The class teaching staff is also amazing!! so it's not a stressful course and is well supported and fun!
Chinese is incredibly rigorous and is not for the faint of heart, but the class is such a warm and welcoming environment and the teachers are amazing. You really feel like you are learning a lot and you build strong relationships with your peers and teachers.
