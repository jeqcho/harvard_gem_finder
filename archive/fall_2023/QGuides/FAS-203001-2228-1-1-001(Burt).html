What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class isn't exactly what I thought it was going to be, but it was a good experience. It's definitely not a blow–off class though; expect to put work in (especially if your class does a comic) and really understand the readings and comics that we read. The discussions are really fun and interesting though, and there's a nice community among the small class size. Also, try looking for the books on HOLLIS (Harvard library catalog) before buying them because they can get quite pricey – the earlier the better because you want to get them before your classmates do (usually there's only 1 – 2 copies).
Stephanie is a great teacher who cares about the class a lot. Take the time to read the books and it's definitely worth it unless you really don't want a 2 hour 45 min long class.
