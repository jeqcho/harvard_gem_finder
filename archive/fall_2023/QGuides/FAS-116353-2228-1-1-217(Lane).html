If you love science fiction, this is the course for you. A perfectly reasonable workload paired with great teaching makes for a fun semester.
Dr. Lane is super accommodating. Class is a super welcoming space. We only had 3 papers total and for each we had a draft that she commented on before doing the final draft. Pre–draft assignments and cover letters could be tedious but got you thinking about the papers.
The Expos experience varies, so make sure you chose one you're interested in
Future students should expect to their views about what technology is to be broadened and revised during this course. The discussions are always engaging and the feedback given for each of the 3 papers is always helpful and constructive.
I would definitely take this section of Expos. Dr. Lane is extremely helpful in giving feedback to fix any loopholes in logic in your writing, as well as things like structure and organization. This specific class changed the way I wrote and did so practically. There was rarely "busy work" and the majority of time spent is with the major essays. However, it was very manageable and seems to me to be one of if not the best Expos classes in terms of workload as well as topic. If you love technology and sci–fi works/films/TV shows (like Black Mirror), I would definitely take this class.
