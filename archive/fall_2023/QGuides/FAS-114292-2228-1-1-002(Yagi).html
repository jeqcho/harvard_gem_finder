What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Another amazing course by the Japanese professors. I've never had a bad day in this class, and I wholeheartedly recommend it to anyone considering taking Japanese. Yagi Sensei and Nakatani Sensei are amazing and their chemistry works so well to create a course that taught me so much over the past semester while being incredibly fun. Be prepared to do a lot of work however: This was a steep increase in difficulty from the previous courses which I could often coast by without doing much at all.
I'm so glad I chose to take this class! I tested out of language requirements but decided to take this anyways and I don't regret my decision at all. The class is only demanding at times, and most of the work is very doable if you dedicate a little but of time every day. Yagi and Nakatani sensei are the sweetest.
This class will probably not advance your speaking skills much – instead, take it if you already have conversational skills and want to read advanced literature and academic articles, or practice specific skills like Kanjis. The instructors are, as all the Japanese senseis, simply wonderful and so kind. I just wish there was more speaking practice.
