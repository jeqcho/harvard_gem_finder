I really enjoyed my experience in Korean BA. I really loved getting to know my classmates too–– everyone who takes Korean has a real reason and truly wants to learn the language, which makes the course a lot of fun (perhaps as opposed to other similar courses that people take with the sole goal of fulfilling the language requirement). It is definitely more of a commitment than other courses since it meets every day, but if you can make it work and want to learn the language, then it is more than worth it.
Definitely take this class if you are interested at all in Korean language and culture! The teaching staff is great and the course materials are generally pretty good and easy to follow
I would tell them you can get a lot out of it, as long as you put in the effort. The course is setup very well to push you to really learn the language and it's easy to benefit from.
If you're interested in Korean or simply looking to fulfill the language requirement, I would really recommend the course!! Professor Kim and all the TFs are so amazing and helpful with whatever you might need, and foster a great environment in class and in section. The class itself isn't too hard, so it's easy to balance with other courses. Definitely take it if you're thinking about it!!
Be prepared for a fairly big time commitment with daily classes, quizzes, and assignments that you have to keep up with. These exercises and assignments help with learning and practicing the language, but it definitely takes more effort than expected to keep up with the class and do well.
