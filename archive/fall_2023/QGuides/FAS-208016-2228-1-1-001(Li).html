Comments
The lecturers are individually mostly excellent and you get some good research talks. But the overall lack of organization and structure makes it an overall less than satisfactory experience.
This course tries to achieve a difficult task of being both a foundational knowledge course while also being a research survey course, and it ends up feeling like it does a half–baked job at both. If you are interested in learning about a broad range of topics in bioinformatics but without going into much depth on any particular topic, this could be a good course to take.
This class is challenging due to its lack of structure and the various assumptions of knowledge made by the different faculty members. I would not recommend this class.
Important class in getting a foundation behind the computational framework of genetics and genomics.
