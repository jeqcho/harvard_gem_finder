This is an amazing course, absolutely take it! If you, like I was, are really interested in the impacts of Christianity in the United States in history, but want to know more about framing that conversation and understanding the long historical context for where we are today, there is really no better course than this. The ease with which Prof. Brekus can discuss both Jonathan Edwards and Kenneth Copeland is nothing short of impressive.
Excellent introduction to the religion department. Brekus is a great professor and cares so much for her students.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!!
Genuinely one of the most amazing classes available at Harvard. Catherine is simply an out of this world professor. Such interesting content, really incisive discussions. both historical and modern topics. So amazing.
TAKE THIS CLASS! Very few classes are Harvard examine the American South and this is the closest class I have found, – if you're interested in that! Proessor Brekus is the best!
I would recommend this class to anyone interested in Christian evangelicalism. I took it as an undergrad, so it was more difficult coursework than I am used to, but the professor and teaching fellows were so accommodating and helpful with any questions or issues we had. Very reading heavy (the readings were always excellent though), but light on assignments – just a couple of papers and discussion posts. One thing I loved about the class was how responsive the professor was to feedback. She truly did care about students and wanted to make sure everyone was succeeding in the class. Fascinating class, amazing lectures, amazing teachers – strongly recommend.
TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Brekkus is, hands–down, one of the best professors I've had the pleasure of knowing during my time at Harvard. I was generally intimidated by the fact that the majority of the class are divinity school students, but it just makes more for a more enriching student as an undergraduate, and a nice escape from FAS.
I simply loved this class. Prof. Brekus is a marvelous lecturer, so knowledgeable, and one of the kindest instructors I've had. Her syllabus includes so many fascinating readings and gave a rich overview of U.S. evangelical history. It was also so refreshing as an undergrad to learn alongside really passionate HDS students. Highly recommend this course!
