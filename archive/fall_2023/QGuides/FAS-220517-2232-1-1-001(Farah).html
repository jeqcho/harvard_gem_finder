Take this class! Absolutely amazing studio, Abdi and Jamieson are a fabulous duo, so talented, and have SO much wisdom to offer. This course taught me a lot about how to conceptualize drawing. It really expanded my interpretation of what "drawing" is, and how to produce a "drawing." I think this is important in art literacy, and I'm glad I learned it in such a non–judgemental, easy, encouraging environment. Flexibility, loose guidelines, yet fun and engaging assignments are strengths of this course. You won't be asked to provide much more than just showing up and willing to dive in.
This is one of the best classes I’ve ever taken at Harvard. Perfect way to start my morning and was honestly the only course I looked forward to every week. So grateful to have met Abdi and Jamieson. They were so supportive, fun, and kind overall. The class had such a good vibe to it, and I’m now considering an AFVS secondary as opposed to CS lol. The projects were super fun and a great way to explore art overall, as opposed to only exploring one medium like painting or sketching. Perfect class if you just want to do something fun/self–care while taking your toolkit classes.
