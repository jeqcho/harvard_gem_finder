This class is amazing, especially because of the social engagement element. Professor Mugane prioritizes hearing stories from people from African countries, rather than texts by white authors. He also includes some of his own experiences into the class discussions, which creates a more intimate classroom environment.
It's an amazing course and Prof. Mugane makes all the effort to make it enjoyable and rewarding.
This is a great class to take if you're interested in learning more about African language and culture. The class is a little longer than necessary and dull at times but it's not too difficult and you end up forming a great community.
