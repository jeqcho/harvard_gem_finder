This course gives a thorough introduction to functions and calculus, so if you are going into college unsure of whether or not you have mastered the basic calculus skills, I think this class is the perfect fit. It introduces you to college–level math but it is not overly challenging or work heavy like Math 1a. If you do struggle in the course, MQC and office hours are really helpful to get through PSETs or review for assessments. It is also nice because there is a final project instead of a final assessment, so it is more indicative of what you actually learned from the course. I would definitely recommend this course because it makes you more confident in your math abilities without causing unnecessary stress.
