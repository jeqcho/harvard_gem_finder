What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Class was very chill with a great instructor.
While the readings can be tough, it's not that much per week. The workload is on the lighter side with weekly readings, a few short papers (including one researching a psychological test of your choosing), and a final project (which you make your own test)
The midterm paper and the final project are really fun and a nice learning experience.
This class is an interesting introduction to psychology with an emphasis on its history and on testing. The material is interesting and the workload is extremely light, with only four assignments throughout the semester. The class itself is not always engaging, but it is a nice freshman seminar experience that is a good, low stress addition to a heavy class load.
I would recommend taking this class! It is super enjoyable and enables you to think more critically about psychological tests and better analyze them.
I highly recommend this class! Marla is so welcoming and helps create an environment where you feel encouraged to share your ideas. I learned so much about psychological tests and the final project was also very fun!
If you are interested in psychological testing take this course! However, do think about the timing of the class and if you can truly be engaged with the class at the time this course is taught.
