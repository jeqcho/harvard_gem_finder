Overall, I feel I have been able to enjoy my time at RCS. One of the things I value about RCS is that there is a relatively low barrier to entry, meaning that students don't have to have had any vocal training to be able to join. I also enjoy the repertoire we sing, as well as the incredible Holden Voice Program that accompanies the class. It can get a little intensive during concert weeks, and the attendance policy is strict (if a student is late for five minutes, it counts as an hour of missed rehearsal time that students have to later make up in office hours; additionally, I believe it is expected to take priority over every other extracurricular during concert weeks since it counts as a class). It can also feel a little slow and repetitive at times in terms of going over the repertoire. But overall, I think RCS is a pretty good way to make music, improve one's voice, and maybe make some friends along the way.
Join RCS!! Its wonderful :)
