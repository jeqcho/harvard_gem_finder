Take Chinese at Harvard! I was kind of on the fence about it at the start of the semester because it seemed like it might be a bit of waste to spend my limited number of classes at Harvard just taking a language that I sort of already knew. But it was very much worth it. It stands out among other courses at Harvard as much more fun and light hearted –– I looked forward to class and made a ton of friends in it. The teachers do cold call you, but the environment is so supportive and non judgmental, so you get used to it/ come to appreciate it really quickly. And, you really learn a lot, especially if you put in the time to study. This class is a bit of a grind. Don't fall behind a do a little every day and you'll be fine.
this is an excellently taught course!! Take it if you're interested in Chinese and want to further improve your language skills!!
Taking this class is really rewarding and the results are definitely noticeable! The workload may hard to adjust to at first, but all of it will contribute to you being more confident as a Chinese language learner.

The Chinese courses at Harvard offer probably the best way of learning Chinese short of total immersion, and 130xa is no exception. This class is incredibly fun and engaging; you'll learn a ton of vocabulary and grammar structures with some work (although less than you think!). The professors are incredible and go out of their way to make sure you understand the material. And the camaraderie between students in each class is unlike any other.
Take this class (or any other Chinese language class)! You definitely won't regret it.
Take this class! You will improve your Chinese by an order of magnitude, the teachers are incredible and honestly some of the best at teaching in all of Harvard, and the course is designed well. The Chinese you learn in this class is helpful for understanding slightly more formal/written language and will serve you well in any Chinese–speaking context. The teachers truly care about your learning and you get a lot of face time with them. They are super friendly and approachable and fun to talk to. The course is organized and the assignments are a decent amount of work, but reasonable for the amount you learn.
This course is phenomenal. The Harvard Chinese Department is truly amazing in creating courses that really help you improve your Chinese while still being fun and creating a comfortable class environment. Almost everything about this course was done right. The lectures and sections were always fun, yet informative. Structural boundaries were in place to make sure that lessons did not go off topic, but enough leeway was given to create a comfortable and fun environment where students felt willing to speak up and learn and make mistakes. The units were great in covering lots of vocabulary and content on real life issues. Finally, both of the teachers were very responsive, attentive, understanding, and relatable — our class even had a hot pot lunch outing after the class was finished because of how close everyone got and how much we loved our teachers :) Highly recommend.
