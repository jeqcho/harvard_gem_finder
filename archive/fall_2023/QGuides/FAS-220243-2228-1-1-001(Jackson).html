Comments
This class is an amazing course to take if you are looking to understand race in America and how American systems intertwine harmful racist rhetoric. Professor Jackson is very involved in the conversations, which not only guides the conversation, but also builds an intimate space for sharing ideas.
Definitely take this class. The professor is incredible and enthusiastic about every topic covered in class.
Don't miss out on the once in a lifetime opportunity to take this class. You will learn a tremendous about the history of scientific racism and the ways it was employed and by the time you finish the class, you will absolutely be able to speak on the subject with fluently. The class definitely deals with heavy topics, so make sure to exercise compassion and patience while hearing other peoples' stories and when sharing your own.
Read the readings, participate in class, and learn with one another!
The content of this class was very interesting, and Sebastian is a very friendly and knowledgeable lecturer. He definitely made the class more enjoyable. I definitely wish the discussions were more nuanced, and a lot of the learning done in this class is through the readings, but overall still a pretty good class.
I would tell students to take this class. It is such a great freshman seminar. It is a break from your normal classes, and it's pass/fail, so there is not too much stress that comes with the course.
TAKE THIS CLASS! While the coursework itself is flexible in terms of how much or how little you work on it outside of class without your grade being affected, you will want to read everything that Professor Jackson provides because it is so interesting. I found the workload to be 100% manageable and never was stressed out as a result of this class, and because there are no exams, you don't feel a pressure to absorb everything you read. The classes themselves are pretty much a loosely–guided discussion, so it depends on who is in your block, but my classmates were all incredibly bright and had interesting things to say about what we learned. Definitely recommend!
Go into this class with an open mind ready to learn some pretty hard realities about race. This class teaches you a lot about race and the justification of its makeup. Just go into this class ready to interact with some interesting texts and be open to discussion and you should be fine.
