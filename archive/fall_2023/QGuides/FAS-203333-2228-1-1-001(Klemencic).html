This course was amazing! I was hesitant to enroll in it at first because it looked like a lot of work and I was not deeply interested in the topic of higher education; however, someone recommended it to me because Manja was the instructor, and I am SO glad I took it. Not only did it make me really interested in higher education (there are so many interesting topics, which I hadn't thought about before this course), but I also felt so supported by Manja. She met regularly with every student, helping us on every phase of our research, was SO encouraging, and really kind and flexible. This course was maybe the best organized course I have ever taken. Overwhelmingly, if you want to take a sociology class where you will look forward to coming to class and seeing your classmates and instructor, this is the course for you.
This course was truly one of the most transformative courses that I have taken at Harvard and I recommend everyone to take this course!
