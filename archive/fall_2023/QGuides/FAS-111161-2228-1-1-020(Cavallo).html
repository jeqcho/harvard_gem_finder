This class was a lot of work for me, but worth it because it was low stress. I really liked not having big tests and having a project instead of a final. It's really set up to help you learn and succeed.

– The team has a very cool pedagogical mindset. Sometimes that can lead to more uncomfortable things but everything is very intentional and well thought
– The course instructors are very good at what they do and great at dealing with students
The course is still difficult even if you have taken calc, but it is good and the preceptors are nice.

It's a great way to understand calculus on a deeper level in a super welcoming environment. The number of quizzes and problem sets allows you to learn a lot without any of them being high stakes or extremely stressful. TFs and CAs are almost always available to help, so you won't have much trouble with your assignments at all!
