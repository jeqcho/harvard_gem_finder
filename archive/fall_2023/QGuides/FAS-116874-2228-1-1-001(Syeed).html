What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

A mustttt take if you have any remote interest in writing film/television.
so so so fun
I came into the class never having written any screenplay, and I came out with two short films I was proud of. That itself was an accomplishment and I was able to use this class as a motivator and facilitator to finally put pen to paper. Overall, the class is low–stakes and low–stress, a great place for beginners like me. That being said, I think discussions and lessons lacked real energy. The seminar was not the vibrant brain–trust, writers–room vibe I had in mind when originally applying, and I left the class always wanting a little more. I am not familiar with similar seminars offered by the English department, so I'm not sure if that type of atmosphere is offered elsewhere. Additionally, we were assigned weekly indie short films we rarely dived deep into, and there was only one assignment where we actually had to read a produced script. I hope that changes for future students. Though I'd recommend going in on one–on–one for personalized OH to get the most out of the course.
This is an amazing course. Its super low stress but you can learn so much, not just through the critiques of your work but through reading the work of your classmates.
Take this class! It was so fun and not a huge strain on my other classes.
