What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is what you make of it, so make it great! You have many opportunities in this seminar, and I encourage you to take advantage of it.
This is a great seminar to take! There are weekly readings (about 1 hour per week) with an occasional blog post. The readings are not mandatory, but you should do them because Robin is too sweet, and she does not deserve students who don't prepare for class. The readings aren't always directly discussed in class, but a lot of the content relates to the general topic of discussion. Every week, the topic switches to a different aspect of education (like implicit bias, testing, summer school, etc.). Robin leads the discussions for the first couple of weeks, but eventually, individual students will be responsible for presenting topics and facilitating class discussions. The class is great because the discussions are very thought–provoking, and you get to learn a lot from other people's experiences with math education. On top of that, Robin is a wonderful professor who asks phenomenal discussion questions! She brings snacks to class a lot too. :)
This seminar is absolutely amazing! Not only is Robin one of the sweetest people I have ever met, but this class brings a whole new perspective to math education. It highlights the faults of the methods of teaching majority of us were exposed to and allows up to think on solutions.
