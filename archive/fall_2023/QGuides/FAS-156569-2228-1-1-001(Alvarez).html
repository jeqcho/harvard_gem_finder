I would highly highly highly recommend that you take this class, if you have the fortune to do so. Wonderful professor who is so kind and caring and truly cares that you learn and have a chance to reflect on current advancements in neuroscience. An exceptional selection of movie clips that will get your mind going at the beginning of each class. Thoughtful and really really fun discussions about not just the movies but also neuroscience papers that use cutting–edge techniques. You really get to go deep into few papers, which I truly appreciated. All told, take it if you can. One of the few classes which I can recommend without reservation.
Definitely take this class! Professor Alvarez is so kind and passionate about the course material. He makes the sections really engaging and even buy snacks every week for his students. He also expertly explains very complex neuroscience topics in a way that is understandable and funny even.

I really suggest to try to take if you know you can be involved and interested.
