What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this course! Though some students may feel like they do not need to take this course after 140, I believe that 150 is absolutely essential. 150 truly stretches your ability to produce writing quickly and effectively, and to narrow down your communication techniques so that your speech not only becomes clear but also becomes eloquent. A must take if you want to use Japanese at a competent adult level!
This class is a natural extension of previous Japanese courses. It is more self–guided in the sense that there are no strict Kanji or Vocab homeworks, but there are still interesting readings that require careful reading and comprehension.
Be mentally prepared for the workload and challenges this course may pose to you, but you will come to realize you've learned soooo much if you make it through the end of the semester
This is a great course, but be prepared to work hard and stay engaged. Asakura–sensei is incredibly friendly and approachable, so don't be afraid to reach out. You can tell that she puts in a lot of time and effort to prepare the course materials, so definitely take advantage of all the textbook material you're given.
