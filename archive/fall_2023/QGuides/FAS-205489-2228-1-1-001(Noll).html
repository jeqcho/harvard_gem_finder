Definitely take this class! If you don't have a background in the topics, it might be a little challenging, but I wouldn't let that discourage you. It's also a class where you're encouraged to share your personal feelings and experiences, and can be extremely cathartic.
This class has really interesting material, and the professor (Dr. Noll) is a great professor! She knows the material very well and is very strategic in the way it is laid out. I'd say that there is a lack of diversity in the way of thinking of the material, so sometimes it felt like we had hit some dead ends or the conversation wasn't really going anywhere because only 4–5 people would be really engaged, but perhaps that was just the makeup of the class. In other words, I'm not sure how much I got out of the class in an academic sense. Dr. Noll is a very smart, thoughtful, and helpful professor, though.
