What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you're passionate about some historical question, write a thesis! Probably the most rewarding thing I've done yet at Harvard.
You'll have to take this class if you're writing a thesis. Talking to the other students is the best part of this course in my view.
This class is required if you (primarily) concentrate in history so you don't really have much of a choice, but even if you're just an allied concentrator in history, this class is still great because (a) it counts for credit, and your thesis is a LOT more work than you think and (b) Carla and Kenneth (and I'm sure whoever replaces Kenneth once he graduates) are great resources for writing your thesis.
The deadlines are helpful, sometimes the class time could be condensed but it is nice to chat with other people who are writing theses.
