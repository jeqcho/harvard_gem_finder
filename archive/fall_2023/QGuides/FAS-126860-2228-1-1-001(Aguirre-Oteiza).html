1000000% take this class if you're interested in furthering your studies in spanish. daniel is INCREDIBLE and you'll take away so much from this interactive and lively workshop in which you get to apply your skills.
This is a great class to learn about translation! Daniel creates a really fun and dynamic environment, and the reading isn't too much so most of the work you do is only in the class itself. The structure of the class has you on your feet/working with groups for a lot of it which is fun, and it was cool to learn Spanish in a more technical format like that. This class also teaches you how to find other words/ways to express yourself in Spanish even if you don't have the exact words that you're thinking of. I would definitely recommend it!!!
I strongly recommend this class; Professor Aguirre Oteiza is incredibly knowledgeable and enthusiastic. One of the top 3 most enjoyable classes I've taken at Harvard.
This class is so fun!! Daniel is amazing, the workload is EXTREMELY manageable, and you get to speak Spanish for 2 straight hours every week.
This class is SUPER chill. (really, it's a "taller/workshop", not a class). Daniel is consistently wonderful and entertaining. Your Spanish will not improve all that much in this course, but it will give you new perspectives on what it means to translate. You also get exposed to a fair bit of Spanish history and culture during the course.
This class was really unique in its use of translation to teach language. It felt really effective, even now when I use conversational Spanish I am more cognizant of what I really mean to say and have an increased ability to navigate around words I may not know. Daniel is also a really engaging, passionate, and fun professor. Best class of my semester
Take it! You learn a lot about the Spanish language and Spanish history!
Take this class!!! I cannot recommend Daniel and his translation courses enough. I look forward to them each week because he makes translating so fun. This course is a space where you can make mistakes without worrying about grades, which is rare for Harvard. It's clear that Daniel's main goal is to spark a love for the Spanish language in each of his students.
This is an amazing class. Daniel is extremely passionate about the course content and makes class interesting, engaging, and fruitful.
