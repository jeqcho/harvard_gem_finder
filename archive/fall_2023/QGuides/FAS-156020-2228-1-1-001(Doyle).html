When these issues are fixed, Jimmy has incredible knowledge regarding the course material and you will find this to be an engaging course, as long as you keep in mind that your essays will be held to an exceptionally high standard for success.

I'm a second–semester senior and this was one of my favorite courses I'd ever taken. Plato is so interesting, eloquent, and fun to read. They're all dialogues, like little plays or conversations, which makes it so much more approachable than I'd ever thought philosophy could be! Obviously you can't take everything at face value because these people genuinely believed in Apollo and reincarnation and stuff, but so many underlying themes are so interesting and some are applicable to life now. Professor Doyle is absolutely amazing! So knowledgeable, passionate, and thoughtful. I always went to his office hours and each time made me think about things in a different way.

If you ever got the chance to take one course, with no other distractions in life, and give it your all, then I think this course will radically change your life like no other course you could choose instead.
