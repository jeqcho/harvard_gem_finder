This class will help you develop your presentation style.
This class, although may seem super annoying and not worth your time, is actually so useful. I came in really confident about my presenting abilities. I know I am a great presenter. However, this class was still super useful and helped me further improve my public speaking abilities. There are so many ways to present well. So many different styles. Pay attention and figure out what you like and want to incorporate into your style.
The course needs work but with proper adaptations it can become quite useful for the first year MCO student; especially in discovering new ideas in biology that can be exciting for potential rotation labs.
Come prepared to the coaching sessions in order to take full advantage of them; they're very helpful!
