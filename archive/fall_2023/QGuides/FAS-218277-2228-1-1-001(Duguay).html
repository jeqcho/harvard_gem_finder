What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Take this class!! I had a wonderful time in this course and strongly encourage anyone remotely interested in music to take it. The course is not stressful and really allows you to focus on learning the analytical principles through readings, class examples, and homework assignments without the stress of exams. Professor Duguay is very knowledgeable and loves to talk to students during the class break or over email; it is very clear that she enjoys teaching and discussing music. The class has a pretty set workload with readings and a homework assignment every week which I found manageable even when taking harder courses (like organic chemistry) at the same time. Section is sometimes helpful, sometimes not so helpful, but only an hour long so not too much of a hassle. The grading scheme for the class is very fair and the rubrics for homework assignments and the final project are very clear. It's certainly easy to be successful in this course and get a lot out of in terms of learning about how to look at music through various different lenses. Professor Duguay is also very open to students analyzing music in other genres outside of strictly pop so it's easy to be interested in the course material and see how it applies to music you already listen to. I had a great time in the class as a stem concentrator so I highly recommend this course to anyone that wants to take it!
Great class ~ You make it what you want and get to talk about the week's theme with music you're currently loving! 1 mini analysis assignment + short reading questions a week, but it's manageable!
This is a fantastic class for anyone who really loves listening to music and wants to think more deeply about how it works! You do not need any background knowledge and you also don't need to be interested in current top 40 pop music because the songs you analyze are up to you, and can be from any genre.
Take this class if it is ever offered again. It was so much fun, low stress and Professor Duguay is the best!
Definitely take it if you love listening to music and want to elevate your perspective on it!
This course is great for you if you are a complete beginner at music and are enthusiastic about pop music/artists such as SZA, Kendrick Lamar, and a few others. If you already have some musical background and want to take a course that will get deeper into music theory, you might want to take a higher level music course.
This class will make you think about music in a way you never have before! There's a decent amount of reading, but the opportunity to apply what you learn to your favorite songs is cool.
