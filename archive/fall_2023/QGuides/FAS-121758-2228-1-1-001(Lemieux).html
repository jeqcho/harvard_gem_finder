What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you want to make multiples of your work do it before the final crit they change the locks on the cabinets.
This class was great if you want to learn to use the silkscreening medium to further your interests in other artistic/academic domains. I would encourage you from the very beginning of the semester to really start thinking about silkscreening as a medium, and not simply as just another form of printing the artwork you already make!
It is very challenging for art concentrators and non–concentrators and it is very time consuming. The TF and Professor are not on the same page. The TF is very nice but the Professor can be very cold and rude when approaching students about their work during class.
This class is a lot of work and lots of time–intensive work, but if you are interested in silkscreening and have ideas for big self–guided projects, you will like this class!
This is a VERY COOL class to take and if you can take it, TAKE IT. However, if you are thinking about printing T shirts for a project then you will be asked to rethink as the Prof will not encourage it unless you have a very good artistic reason.
Be prepared to spend a lot of time in the studio!
