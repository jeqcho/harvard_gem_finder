What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Photography with Dawn Kim hanged the way see the multiple worlds that I exist in, all at once. This course has allowed me to hold and probe the complexities, entanglements, and myths of being alive.
I don't know if this class will ever be offered again at Harvard–– I was among the lucky 7 who were able to take it. If, in some alien universe, this class is offered again at Harvard, I cannot even begin to describe how necessary it is for you to take it. You will be pushed in all the right directions, and grow as a photographer while having the most fun imaginable. Dawn and Claude are simply the best, and they create an environment that is both rigorous and demanding in the work you produce and extremely fun and light–hearted. God, this class is in the top–3 I've taken at Harvard, if not top–1.
This class will forever hold a special place in my heart. Dawn and Claude are incredible people and artists. It has been such a joy to make photos and talk about photos and look at photos together with some of the coolest people I know. There's no other class where you get to visit the Cambridge Water Department, go on a Cambridge ghost tour, listen in on a legendary Yale photo critique, and eat carrots 4 ways. Definitely one of my favorite classes that I've been in while at Harvard :)
