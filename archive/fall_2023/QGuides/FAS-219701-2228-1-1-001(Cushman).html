TAKE IT!!!!!!!!!!
This course is a phenomenal exploration of how different aspects of culture and socialization operate and can change between environments. I would highly encourage you take this course and spend time with the material to both criticize what it is arguing and look at what the implications are of what it is trying to say.
This was a great class. Fiery is such a great professor and held great conversations during seminar. He was really good at breaking down important concepts through conceptual exercises.
This course is absolutely incredible! Content is interesting and really makes you think about how different historical things or geographical considerations may have shaped current beliefs and behaviors. Fiery is also wonderful – the class is discussion based, and Fiery is really good at facilitating and bouncing off student ideas.
Fiery is well–known to be a stellar professor at Harvard, and this course is no different. I'm not sure if he'll teach it again, but I felt like I came away with a good understanding of what cultural conflict is, how it might arise, and how psychology as a field grounds it. I am not a Psychology concentrator (and, for that matter, have only ever taken 1 psych course), but I found the course very accessible (the readings might take longer for your to understand, but they're well worth your time). Most importantly, the discussions had in class were phenomenal, and I'm grateful to Fiery for being able to distill such complex ideas into simple, easy–to–understand explanations. Take this course.
I would highly recommend this class to anyone interested in cultures and/or cultural psychology. We learned about the origins of both small and large–scale conflict in the U.S. today. Our readings and discussions are really interesting and Fiery is an incredible professor.
Fiery is a legend and an excellent instructor –– a great way to explore a lot of interdisciplinary research on contemporary conflict in the U.S. (unfortunately, a perennially relevant topic)
Take this class it's a gem and so so interesting.
