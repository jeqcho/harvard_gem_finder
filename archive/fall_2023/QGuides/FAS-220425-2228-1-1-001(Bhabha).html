Comments
This is a great class! Prof. Bhabha is so knowledgeable and kind – definitely attend her office hours even if just to chat. There aren't really any assignments throughout the course so it is hard to know how you are doing. The lectures are very interesting and the readings are a bit long but definitely manageable.
Take this class! Was highly informative on topics related to migration and human rights, had incredibly guest speakers, and Dr. Bhabha is a great professor
Learning about migration and displacement is the root of developing a critical understanding about the past and present issues to help analyze and visualize future issues ahead of time so that organizations and individuals will be prepared to deal with the challenges.
It’s an excellent course! Very challenging but extremely impactful and helpful to understand all the dynamics that happen within immigration!
This is a great class to learn about all aspects of migration—from the way it intersects with technology and education to how it plays out in the international legal framework. The workload is very manageable and you learn just as much from the professor as you do from the other students in the class.
This class is an incredible introduction to migration and refugee policy. As an undergraduate, I thought that the overall workload wasn't too difficult to keep up with it. I loved how the class facilitated so many opportunities to not just learn from Professor Bhabha and the amazing guest speakers, but to also learn from other students in the class, many of whom have rich and interesting backgrounds in public policy and social justice.
This course was phenomenal, and it’s one of those kinds of courses you must take before you graduate. The sheer diversity of the student body in terms of background and experience was incredible — I sat alongside medical professionals, clinicians, teachers, refugee camp workers, and asylees. I am lucky to have been able to soak in their wisdom and knowledge through osmosis. Beyond that, Prof. Bhabha was a wonderful classroom moderator and clearly put a lot of effort and intentionality into class presentations and discussions, constantly asking us for feedback. She has a wealth of technical knowledge about the subject area and spent time at the beginning of each class discussing takeaways from the previous class, which helped draw a throughline through the entire academic experience. Take this class for a holistic learning experience where you can study pressing issues in the world and develop concrete solutions to implement from a variety of different fields.
