What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The class can sometimes be challenging, especially since it may require you to completely change your approach to writing. It's important to be open to change and constructive criticism. Martin is really sweet and supportive, and the content material is engaging and interesting (particularly if you're interested in environmentalism). Definitely recommend .
There isn't a ton of direction given to students for each assignment and the work feels very independent. Also, definitely take advantage of the response papers that are due before each first essay draft so that writing the final product is easier and can be more efficient.
This class is definitely helpful in improving your writing, but you have to be open to feedback and change. Sometimes things aren’t clear at first when starting an essay, but you have to just trust the process. Office hours are helpful simply for organizing your thoughts and getting a bit of extra help on essays.
This is a very instructive course. There are some readings that you have to do but they are manageable provided you start on time. In the first unit of the course, there is a trip to Walden Pond, which I recommend going. The course material is interesting and the instructor provides good feedback on your essays and response papers.
This expos class is one that I would recommend. The professor is a sweetheart, and you will learn a lot about the structural aspects of writing and tackle different forms of analysis throughout your projects.
