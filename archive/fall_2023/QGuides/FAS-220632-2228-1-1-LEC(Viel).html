This is a wonderful class to learn about social entrepreneurship. It just requires some creativity and excitement, and you will be great!
Take this course! This course has stretched my mind and it was very rewarding to pitch an ENTIRE company idea and receive THOROUGH feedback from course staff. The fact that this intellectual capital is right here at our fingertips... Please do not leave Harvard without taking this course, especially if you have ever dreamed of starting your own company. I took this course and realized that entrepreneurship might not be the right fit for me at this moment, but it helped me grow in my persuasion, marketing, and public speaking skills––which are vital in most career fields. It also helped me experience entrepreneurship firsthand, so now I know how to draft, edit, and produce an efficient pitch video if I ever start my own company. You're unlikely to get this level of training and this amount of room to explore and create in a classroom setting ever again––at least not at Harvard.

I recommend this course, with a reservation: beware that the speakers that the course staff in are not diverse (racially). As a black woman, it was disappointing to see that all the speakers for each class were white entrepreneurs, which is not at all reflective of Harvard's increasingly diverse student body. I will be holding the course staff/director accountable in increasing the diversity of speakers, but it may take another semester to see it in full effect.
