What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Do not take this class. If you are actually interested in econ, take something like ec 1010 or 1011. You will do probably about the same amount of work, but will get way more out of it. If you aren't so interested in econ but are curious about dilemmas in the world's economy, this course will be an utter letdown. After having taken the course and doing all of the readings and showing up to every class, I cannot tell you a single dilemma that we focused on specifically and we didn't even talk seriously about inequality until the very last week of the class. I am incredibly disappointed with this class and would strongly urge you to take something else.
Take if you have a deep interests in the mechanisms of globalization, including readings/conversations that are a lot more concerned with specific theories/formulas than talks about specific trade deals/talks in the modern international political economy.
Definitely apply for this class. It is a decent amount of work compared to some other seminars but is worth it.
This is a Socratic structured course that allows students to engage in the material regardless of their background. I recommend an interest in economics.
