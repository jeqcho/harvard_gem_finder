What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this because you work super close with Professor Sweeney who is SUCH a cool and interesting professor.
Unfortunately, this class was my least favorite out of the three technology science requirements. I felt this class was rather disorganized. In fact, we did not follow the syllabus beyond the first two weeks, resulting in us only having one assignment before the final paper. Class usually followed the similar flow: starting late, presenting where students were in the research process, then individual time with the TFs. There is nothing more stressful than knowing one final paper is worth essentially 100% of your grade. I also felt that the data acquisition process for multiple students, including myself, took way too long, which resulted in many students throwing together a project last minute the final two weeks of the semester.
You will be able to explore a topic of your choice and considerably research it.
