This was such an amazing, amazing course! I think the one of the best ways of learning about the world is by learning about a part of the world that you don't know much about. The course is mainly on Western thought toward China and helped me break down preconceived notions about China and "the East." Hands down the best course I've taken at Harvard so far. Professor Puett is actually one of the best people on the planet. He is so kind and understanding. He also has a way of always validating your thoughts even when you feel like they are dumb. As an undergraduate who has taken the class, I would say it's very doable and would highly encourage other undergrads to take the course.
