the instructor can make or break this class.
This course is incredibly educational and teaches you how to execute your own qualitative study! The intermediate assignments are well structured so they aren't just busy work but ultimately help you put together the final paper. You learn a lot from reading other studies and from listening to your peers talk about their papers. It's a required course for concentrations (so you'll have to take it no matter what), but I recommend going in with an open mind. The course is time consuming (particularly at the end of the semester when you have to write the actual 25–page paper, so plan ahead), but was probably the most educational required course I've taken thus far.
