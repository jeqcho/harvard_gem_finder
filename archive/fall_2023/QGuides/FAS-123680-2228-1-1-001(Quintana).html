This course is very good if you are considering diving straight into learning about precalculus. You don't need any prior experience with differentiation and integration to excel in this class as you will learn how to understand each of them in details as you go through the course. It has 3 Psets a week and plenty of opportunities for you to check your reasoning before each of them is due. It is also very exciting to be able to use Math to analyze real world situation from their data sets, propose possible solutions to identified problems and just have fun while behaving like data scientists.
It is not taught like a typical math class, and the assessments are structured very differently. While many students found this uncomfortable, I think it was a much more effective way of learning math than the traditional "lecture" style, where the instructor tells you everything, and you just study it like a parrot. Grappling with new material that we don't quite understand yet in sections made sure that when the concepts click, they were more likely to stay in my mind.
