What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course was a highlight of my semester. I've met graduate students from across the University that I otherwise might have never come into contact with. If you know nothing about market economics and want to learn about it in a setting that allows you to consider and challenge the history, applications, and ethics of "market faith"––take this class.
Cherish it if you get a place in this class and really put in the effort to learn and devote yourself to the readings / discussions – they are all intentional and it is the least you owe to yourself, your classmates, Professor Sandel, and all those who were did not get the chance to have a seat in such a class taught by a legendary professor
