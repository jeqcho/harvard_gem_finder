What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course is a really unique opportunity to gain a bird's eye view of the current field and Professor Caton is so knowledgeable. Also, the opportunity to speak directly with the authors of the books you read in class is invaluable to really understand the material. As a heads up, there is quite a bit of reading each week, and I think you really do have to do it to get as much out of the course as you can.
Such an excellent seminar! Highly recommend. Be prepared to read a lot, but the work pays dividends.
This course will allow you to learn more about some of the issues concerning the Middle East and also about the genre of ethnography itself. The texts are a highlight of the course and include some of the most current ethnographies of the subfield. Professor Caton explains ethnography very clearly in class which makes the material very accessible even if you are not already familiar with it! If the subject matter interests you, know that you will be able to fully enjoy and engage in the material.
