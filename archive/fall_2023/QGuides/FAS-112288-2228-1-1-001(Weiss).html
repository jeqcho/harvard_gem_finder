What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course can be quite hard, but adding vocab to the Anki app and reviewing daily can help make assigned readings less strenuous over time. It feels good to have read so much at the end of the course and you get an appreciation for the different dialects, genres, and styles. You might find an unexpected new favorite author, like Simonides!
Take this class!! I loved it! The professor was wonderful! You will find lots of people who love the same texts you love! You'll spend a lot of time on the homework and more studying for tests, but you'll be so, so much better at Greek for it.
This class is difficult, especially at the beginning. You will be reading ~200 lines of Greek per class, so if you are not used to such volume, it will be difficult to adjust. However, Professor Weiss is super understanding and was willing to highlight key sections for those who are struggling (bringing it down to ~100 lines). Again, challenging class at the beginning, but you will see yourself improve so quickly over this course. Take this class if you want to get better at reading Greek, do not take this class if you aren't willing to put in the work/hours to get there.
Be excited for the readings, but prepare for the workload
