This course was a lot of fun, and I learned a lot too! You get to learn just how music is connected to psychology and social life, and also get to see how psychological studies are done during the lectures. We also got to visit the Learning Lab at the Bok Center, which was also useful in our final project of creating a video based on a psychological study, which was a lot of fun! Mayron is very friendly and understanding, and is great at breaking down the course content so we could understand it. Therefore, I recommend this course to anyone with an interest in this subject.

This course was pretty great! We learned about really interesting research done (and still being done) on psychology and music. Professor Piccolo is amazing — an effective and engaging professor, as well as a pretty fun and cool person overall! He even takes the time to attend students' performances! The class was not a ton of work, just weekly readings plus 2 total quizzes and one final project, but the one aspect of the course I didn't particularly enjoy was the section assignments and section itself. They didn't feel really helpful to me, and took up an unnecessarily large part of the grade. Still, this course was really enjoyable, I highly recommend it!
