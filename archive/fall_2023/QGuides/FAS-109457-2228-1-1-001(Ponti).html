For example, she would draw an upside down triangle and I could not tell whether it was an accidently upside down drawn delta sign or weird V because she never introduced it as a "gradient" sign in class–– she just started to use it without explanation or context. I really appreciate the coding aspect, even though the assignments were not always clear — I had barely coded, and never in Python, before taking 12a, and I have learned so so much. Take this class if you’re doing engineering, looking for a more applied version of 15b, want to get better at Python, or don’t like exams; don’t take it if you want a theoretically rigorous understanding of the content.
