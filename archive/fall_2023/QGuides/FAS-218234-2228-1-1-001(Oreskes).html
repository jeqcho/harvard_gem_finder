TAKE THIS COURSE. It is definitely one of the best courses I have taken at Harvard so far. Professor Oreskes is obviously very famous and influential in her field, but she is so genuine and her lectures are always well–thought out and delivered. There are a lot of readings, but it's not like you're really tested on them so as long as you skim all of them or use your insight to pick which ones you feel like are the most valuable to you, the reading load is not that heavy. The readings are also all very interesting. The exams are super fair and are not memorization based at all.
