What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
That it is taught really well and goes into depth
Math MA is a superb introduction to calculus if you have not taken it before, or an excellent way to sharpen your math skills if you have taken calculus before. The teaching support is AMAZING and everyone wants you to succeed. The workload is also manageable and you will walk away with a greater understanding of calculus and appreciation for math.
TAKE MATH MA. I can't stress this enough. Ok unless you are a math genius and it seems boring to you. I took calculus up to integrals in high school and feel like I still learned a lot. You might want to take 1a or 1b to prove that you can take a higher math course, or to have the course requirements done early. Whatever reason it may be, if you want a solid foundation in math, low stress and supported class take this one. Sure, there are many assignments but that gives you a constant, sustainable working amount instead of the ups and downs and stressors that come with a lot of other math courses. You can always move up and get more intense, but especially as a freshman when you are figuring things out, this is the perfect class to start with. You wont regret it ! a lot of my 1a friends feel this class would have also been great for them. Also i met some of my best friends in this class ;)
As someone who was recommended to take this course but had already taken Calculus, I sometimes felt that this class went over things I already knew but taught it in a way that was much different and sometimes confusing from what I knew. If you think you have a strong enough basis in Calculus, I wouldn't recommend taking this class because a lot of it is focused on earlier things like limits, graphing functions, and derivatives. However, the support system is incredibly strong.
I highly recommend you to take this class, it does not require you to have any background knowledge on Calculus. You will really enjoy doing it
Just be patient and use the Math Question Center often as well as Office hours.
If you are in between Math MA or 1A, I would suggest MA if you think you need more practice to conceptually understand the materials more. What it means to think in Math. However, if you think you are a quick learner and just want to get math out of the way then I would suggest 1A because MA doesn't count for a lot of majors, you have to take MA and MB to make it equivalent to 1A.
Take this class for a strong foundation in calculus before moving on to higher levels. It will really help you to understand the rules deeply.
Don't fall behind. and take advantage of your resources.
