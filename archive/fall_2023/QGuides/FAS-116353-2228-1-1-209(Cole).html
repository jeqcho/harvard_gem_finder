This class is great if you are interested in learning about the consequences of an authoritarian society. Doing our own research was the best part of the class and helped us explore this idea.
This class has a very reasonable amount of work and interesting subject matter. It's an excellent way to fulfill the expository writing requirement.
This overall is a good class. It's not an amazing class, that will make you excited about taking it, but it's also not a bad expos class. It a good one to fill the requirement, you learn and the assignments are not bad. The only real reading you have to do is of 1894, analyze it enough to write some papers about it, and the second unit is basically researching and writing about a topic that you find interesting that relates in some way to 1984 (this means almost any political science topic will work). If you like the field you will definitely enjoy it, and if you don't is also fine. Good class to fufill the expos requirement.
Course is great. It is very interesting, if you have not read 1984 before. Otherwise, the first unit of the course might be a little too familiar.
This is probably the class from which the knowledge and skills you learn will be most vital in your future.
