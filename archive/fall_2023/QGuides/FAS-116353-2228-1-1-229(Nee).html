You should take this expos!! As far as the content, there is something for everyone. I came in being interested in exploring the third unit, but I found myself to be very interested in both the Icelandic family sagas and photography units. David presents the content in a way that is fascinating. Beyond that, David is an extremely approachable professor and fair grader. He makes clear what his expectations are and what should be changed between the revision and final essay stages. He also responds to emails and has frequent office hours. This is a really great expos class to take to gain a better understanding of college level writing and fulfill the requirement. I would highly recommend it.

As far as expos goes (for this class or another), my advice would be to start early on the essays and assignments because deadlines can sneak up on you. Also, the writing center is a great resource! Even if you can't get an appointment, I'd recommend taking advantage of their drop in hours because that was very helpful for me. Finally, when you receive feedback on your draft, expect to have to dedicate time towards implementing that feedback. The feedback is often more large–scale versus small changes, so you will generally have to dedicate a good amount of time to your essay after receiving feedback on a draft.
