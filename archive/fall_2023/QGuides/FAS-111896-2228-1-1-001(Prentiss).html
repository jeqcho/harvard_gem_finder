If Professor Dvorkin is teaching this class, TAKE IT!!! With Professor Dvorkin this class truly is a gem. Physics 15b is probably the hardest of the 15 series, but Professor Dvorkin truly wants to see her students succeed. I've honestly never seen a more dedicated professor who would take direct feedback from her students– if you found something particularly challenging, she would talk to you about it and work with you on how she could better support you. One example I remember is a group of students who came up to her asking if there could be an additional bonus/fun/not physics question on the final similar to those on problem sets to decrease stress, and she immediately agreed and began brainstorming ideas with students. Physics 15b could have been a bad experience because of how hard the class material is, but with Professor Dvorkin, the class has become one of my favorites.
