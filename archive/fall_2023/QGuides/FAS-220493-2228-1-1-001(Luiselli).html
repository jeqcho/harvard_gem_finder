This was such an insightful and amazing course!
If you are interested in questions of language, memory, imagination, etc, take this course! You will not regret having Professor Luiselli as your teacher — she is fantastic. This has been the best course I've taken here, and definitely the best creative writing course on campus. I've appreciated the great conversations she facilitated for us as a cohort; I left every class feeling like I'd learned something new. Unlike other creative writing courses I've taken on campus, this one pushed us all to think critically about our relationship with writing and the themes of the class. My writing has definitely grown, and Professor Luiselli made sure we had the tools and time to push ourselves with our work.
