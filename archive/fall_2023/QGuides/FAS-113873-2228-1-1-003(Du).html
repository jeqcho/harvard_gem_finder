Take the course! It's an easy introduction to Chinese and relatively easy to do well in this class. If you have some proficiency in Chinese you may want to skip and take a higher level course.
I took this class with no Chinese experience, honestly could not pronounce the compound vowels the first week, and now I can have full conversations and read paragraphs in Chinese. The class is very fast paced. You have to memorize 40 words a week and learn to write 20 characters. It is hard to catch up if you fall behind. But, there are a ton of support systems within the class. Since you have to memorize the words before class, class sessions are spent practicing speaking and listening. This is super helpful for memorization and pronunciation. If you are willing to put in the work, this class is really rewarding and it is possibly to do well without having any previous experience.
Take this class! As someone with absolutely 0 knowledge of Chinese, it is doable. A little hard, because it is a hard class to take but all of the assignments are worth so little and there's so many that one bad mark doesn't impact your grade as much. Also the teachers are extremely generous with their grading so it's very fair! I loved every minute of this class and it was nice to build a connection with the teaching staff because I got to see them every day. I loved that the staff switched weekly too – it meant I got to hear slightly different Chinese and different teaching styles which was awesome.
Chinese is a difficult language to learn, but the materials and teaching staff for this course make it fun, welcoming, and as easy as I think It can be! All the teachers are friendly and really want you to succeed and learn Chinese, and are always happy to help you do so. The small groups for leacure and section really make it easy to participate, and it doesn't feel like a big deal if you don't know an answer or get it wrong, so go for it! If you are at all curious about learning Chinese, you should take this course!
Be prepared to work. Its only a one hour long class, but it is a five day a week class with two quizzes each week. It is a fast moving class that covers a lot of material, so you have to be on top of your work if you want to avoid falling behind.
This class is FANTASTIC! Would recommend! It is definitely a lot of work, but you will learn so so much and very quickly! I found it so worth it! 祝好!
