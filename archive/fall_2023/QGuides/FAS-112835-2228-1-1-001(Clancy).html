What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course will force you to read significantly more Russian text than you would ever do otherwise, which is extremely beneficial. If you are interested in three hours of linguistics–related chatting per week, the class meetings are also beneficial. If you are looking to improve your reading skills in Russian, you are better off finding an accountability buddy and spending the three hours per week reading together.
