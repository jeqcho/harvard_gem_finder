What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Micro 202 provides an engaging opportunity to discuss bacterial pathogenesis, virulence, and host–microbe interactions with experts in the field in a small group setting.
This class is perfect for those interested in the fields of microbiology and how bacteria can cause disease. It does have a lot of scientific reading, and you will definitely become better at reading scientific papers from it, but it is very approachable and interesting.
– Excellent class to take if you have some background in bacteriology and immunology, and find microbial evasion of host immune responses fascinating
Read the papers in a way that lets you reflect on what led to the study, what it brought to the field, and what else could have been improved
Relatively reasonable course load
