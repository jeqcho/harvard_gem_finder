Take this class if you are interested in Islamic studies. The professor is super nice and the class was very structured. Make sure to do the readings beforehand so you would be able to focus on the response paper over the weekend. I highly recommend this class!
This was an excellent course overall. The professor is a great discussion facilitator and always had new questions from different angles or about the stakes of the issue. Having people from different religious and academic backgrounds kept the class interesting, though there were only six of us. However, there was a range of prior knowledge, which was sometimes hard to bridge.
The class is a far amount of work, but ensures you come away with a robust, useable knowledge of the subject. Prof Nasser is a great teacher and incredibly fun to learn from. The small class size means classes are made up of frequent discussion and commentary, which Prof Nasser directs well. If you want to learn a lot about Quran in a short time, this is the class for you
