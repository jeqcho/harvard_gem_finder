Totally take this class! If you're interested in the intersection of Roman history, culture, and literature around a complex but important topic, there's really no better experience than this class.
Enjoy the text and don't miss class!
This was an amazing course. Having started Latin in college, I was a bit worried about how my first 100–level Latin course would be, but Professor Love made this course extremely accessible. The Latin reading amount was very reasonable, especially as my first 100–level course, as it was complemented by secondary scholarship reading material. Professor Love is extremely supportive and always willing to help inside and outside class. Highly recommend, especially as a first 100–level course!
Great class if you're interested in race studies! Livy is a confusing author but you will grow to love him and his subjunctive clauses.
If you’re looking for a class that will legitimately improve your ability with the Latin language, this is it. This class wouldn’t be out of place in a modern social sciences curriculum, but your subject matter happened over 2000 years ago. I felt my Latin abilities get better week to week, and I also felt like I was learning so much more than Latin itself.
This class is highly recommended. Prof. Love makes such an effort to make students feel welcome and help you no matter your Latin knowledge coming in.
