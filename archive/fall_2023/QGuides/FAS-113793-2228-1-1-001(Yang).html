Great build up from the beginner class, definitely more relaxed with this smaller group of 120 students. A little more fast–paced in comparison to Ba/Bb, but also easier in some regards because hanzi writing (on tingxie, lesson quizzes) is replaced by typing on the keyboard. Many opportunities for bonus points so no fear for your GPA. Definitely take it to continue practicing and becoming more fluent at conversational Chinese!
Incredible teaching staff, they are there to support your learning as well as your well–being. If you want to improve your Chinese skills and are willing to/can commit to engaging 5–days a week, this class is so worth it. Opportunity to make great friends as well, since both lectures and discussion sections are so collaborative and relatively small in size.
TAKE THIS COURSE! If you took Ba/Bb you should really, really take this course. The teaching staff is truly fantastic: helpful, funny, engaging. The materials are fun (the stories in the textbook from Ba/Bb continue!) and really helpful, and the course materials truly help you actually learn the language.
To get the most out of the class, I highly recommend prepping well in advance. This will eliminate any stress for the 2 dictations a week, which are very easy if you've done the required pre–prep) and will make your homework go by very quickly. Most importantly, it will make lecture/drill sections a lot more engaging, as you will have the space to practice what you've learnt (as opposed to learning it for the first time every time.) I say this from the perspective of someone who didn't follow this advice, and although got by, often felt lost in lecture/drill.
