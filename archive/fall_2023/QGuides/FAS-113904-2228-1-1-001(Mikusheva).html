What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Go to Anna's office hours and ask every question you have, she's very patient and clarifies concepts really well
This is a wonderful course on time–series econometrics exposing students to a variety of tools widely used in the macro literature. As such, any student who is interested in macro economics and does not have prior knowledge in time–series analysis should take it.

One note: This is a course that focuses on techniques. Hence, it does not go as much into practical economic identification issues in the literature (e.g., what kinds of unobserved shocks can be a problem to invertibility in a typical VAR setting trying to infer IRFs to monetary policy shocks, etc.). For that, one needs to take a macro course.
Anna was a great lecturer and brought her expertise to the material. The class also very useful to people outside of macro and finance and made you more comfortable with many statistical concepts. She's just visiting for the year and not staying forever/teaching it next year, and it's a terrible shame!
If you can take this course with Anna and you are interested in econometrics or macro, 100% do it!!!
