This course will teach you a lot about animation, especially if you have never done anything of the sort. You will learn the process of animation, whether it is shooting your own stop motions, hand drawing frame by frame for a 10 second film, or creating your own soundscape. Most time is also spent creating your own films, which are your assignments. It can be very time consuming, depending on how much time and effort you put into your animations. The teachers can be intimidating sometimes, but they are very willing to help you on any questions you have or guidance you need. A lot of class time, especially towards the end, is spent working on your assignments or final project; I think this is nice because I think you learn more about the animation process from creating, rather than watching other films. I think this course is a good introduction to animation, but if you have already done animation before, there is no need to take this course. While I am not likely to take another animation course, I am glad to have taken this course as an introduction to animation.
