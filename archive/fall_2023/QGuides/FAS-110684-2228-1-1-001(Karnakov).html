Lectures were extremely boring to the point that attendance dwindled to around 10 by halfway through the semester. When you would go to lecture, nothing would be gained that couldn't be learned from just reading the lecture notes. Additionally, the PSETs were not at all related to the course material and instead measured your coding ability rather than math. Quizzes were all technicalities and didn't test your application of the material. Almost everything about this class felt thrown together. I wouldn't recommend this course unless the teaching staff changes it's been a few years.
