What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This isn't like a regular math class where you're lectured about how to solve a problem, you have to solve the problems themselves on a board, but there's a ton of support available for you in class, at the MQC, and in office hours so you'll never feel lost.
This class is for the student who wants a good introduction to calculus.
Be prepared for some psets that waste your time and some insane quizzes
If you have taken AB or BC calculus and you feel you're not strong in those subjects, take the course. However, I do think that if you understand AB calculus well enough, you can take 1A.
It is important to stop and understand the subjects which will help tremendously with p sets and quizzes.
Math Ma does not teach math effectively. While the staff are all very kind and helpful, the way the course is designed is terrible. They don't really teach the material, they just give you a worksheet and expect you to work on the board with a group and learn it yourself. This idea of "self discovery" or "self learning" or whatever they want to call it was incredibly difficult for me. Often times I felt like I didn't learn all the ways to approach a problem, all the rules necessary to do a problem, and the steps to do it. Most of my learning happened in office hours while I did the pset, not from the actual course itself.
I would say that this class is not that easy but it is helpful to prepare you for more math in your future career.
