What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This was the first time I’ve shared my poetry with others. Having the opportunity to write weekly and have my poems workshopped improved my writing immensely and helped strengthen my voice. Josh is an incredible teacher and gives such thorough feedback on each poem. It’s hard for me to be vulnerable with others, but by allowing myself to write what was dear to my heart, I was able to write about many different experiences I never thought I’d be able to put into words—or was too scared to previously do so. I learned a lot about my own writing, and I learned a lot from my peers. I have grown, as a writer, a poet. For those who have only written personal poetry and have never explored it academically, I truly recommend this class. It can be daunting not knowing a lot of the terminology, but trust me when I say that your writing will only grow stronger when you have avid readers and writers digesting and dissecting your work.
Take this class! Joshua Bell is an amazing professor. He cares so much about the students and you can see this in the feedback you're given. This class is for anyone who has an interest in writing poetry that has not been able to express that side of them yet or for people who already have and want to improve on their skills.
This has been one of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard & you will get out what you put in.
Take this class
The 10 poem application may seem a bit daunting, but this class is well worth it!! The small class size was fantastic, my group had a great sense of community (even gathered outside of class a few times to read poetry and get meals), and a really positive and constructive work environment was created. Josh is the best, runs class discussions well and gives very detailed and personalized feedback every week about your poems.
TAKE THIS CLASS IF YOU CAN! It was the highlight of my week and the feedback you receive from Josh and peers is always very insightful!
This is a truly joyful class –– perhaps the best I have ever taken. The classroom experience is deeply nurturing and honestly fun. Josh fosters a warm and inviting atmosphere while encouraging you to grow and find greater meaning in your writing and the work of your peers.
