I really appreciated the open and honest discussions he facilitated in this class. There are a lot of readings, but besides that the workload with the essays and discussion posts is very managable.
I really enjoyed this class. If you have any interest in how religion effects politics, this is a great course to take.
Prof Soroka is clearly passionate about teaching this class, and that's pretty infectious: it's really easy to become involved in the class discussions/debates and to feel passionately about the (incredibly important!) themes discussed. Totally worth taking the class, you'll learn a lot! I ended up having many discussions about class concepts with other students / family members, and it was always really interesting to bring up with different groups of people.

Overall, you should take this class.
