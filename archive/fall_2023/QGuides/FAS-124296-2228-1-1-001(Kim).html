The course requires a lot of time out of your week in order to prepare for all the weekly quizzes and exams. It is difficult to study for said quizzes and exams because the course (lecture and section) materials are not published onto the Canvas site, and retaking quizzes and exams is not permitted.
This class is a big commitment so only take it if you are really passionate about learning Korean
This class is a lot of work. There will be two assessments each week, and you do need to study for them. That being said, it is worth it. You will most likely become friends with your classmates and have a relatively good grasp of the language. The majority of people who take this class are either Korean or really like k–pop, so do with that what you will.
This is a great introductory language class. It is really well taught and they make sure you have a good foundation before continuing if you put in the effort. The head instructor as well as the section leaders are great as well.
This class honestly is a lot of work for students with no previous Korean background at all. Classes are everyday (which makes sense because it helps you get familiar with the language), and if you go and pay attention, it will be extremely helpful. A lot of your practice can happen during class, so you won’t have to practice much outside of class if you are actively engaged.
