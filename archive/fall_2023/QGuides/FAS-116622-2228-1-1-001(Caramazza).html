This was a fascinating class. The material sometimes was hard to grasp, but if you pay attention and ask questions, Dr. Caramazza did a great job not only at answering them but at pushing us to think about what the answer would be ourselves and reason through it. It's a good class to take if you need an advanced neuro rec, it's chill, with only a midterm and a final paper, but make sure to study hard for the midterm because it is a large percentage of your grade. Other than that, really interesting topic and highly recommend.
