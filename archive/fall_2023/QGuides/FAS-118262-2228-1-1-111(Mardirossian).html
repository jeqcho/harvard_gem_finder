What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this class! Professor Mardirossian is amazing. She is incredibly supportive, helpful, and a fair grader. She will humor even the most awfully structured and bad essay ideas and work extensively with you to make it coherent and a good piece of writing. This course is structured in a way that will help you succeed and grow as a writer. If you put in the work, it won't be hard to leave with an A. The workload is light and often the assignments build on top of each other. You will begin writing your unit draft only to find that you already have 1/3 of it done.
Attend office hours, be keen, constantly ask questions and start working on the assignments early enough.
I would like to let future students know that this course was really helpful with bridging the gap between high school and college level writing.
If you have any hesitation at all with writing at Harvard, I HIGHLY HIGHLY recommend this course. You will not regret it. The structure is something that is so helpful in building the writing skills you NEED to be successful here.
Take this class if you are debating whether to take Expos 10 or Expos 20. The individualized feedback you receive on your work is extremely useful, the class environment is great, and you will definitely leave this class a better writer.
