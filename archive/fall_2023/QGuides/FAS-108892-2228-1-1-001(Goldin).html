Comments
Very rewarding class if you put the time and effort into the research papers. Try to get to know Professor Goldin, your TF, and classmates early on in the semester. Really unique class in that it puts conversations around things as personal as relationships and family in an economics theoretical perspective.
You need some sort of background in research paper writing or you may find it difficult to get started initially. Prof. Goldin is very helpful in guiding you, but the vast majority of your grade rests on just the two research papers.
take this class! The research papers really give you an opportunity to learn about an area of your interest. Also, the teaching staff is wonderful!
This was such an interesting class to take! Prof. Goldin is amazing, and the class provides an amazing opportunity to explore the economics surrounding the family unit. As for the assignments, they aren't too time–consuming, just make sure to get an early start on the two research papers! I do wish there was a little more focus on developing the skills needed to write these papers. For example, it would have been nice to have the opportunity to go back through the 1st paper thoroughly before moving on to the next.
Loved taking this course with Professor Goldin, who is a prolific writer and academic in economics. Truly enjoyed learning about her research and this field.
This class is a little more work than it is advertised as (due to the weekly response papers) but still very interesting and fun. Professor Goldin is an amazing scholar and it was a privilege to be able to learn from her and learn about/discuss pertinent economic issues with her. However, I will note that this class is more lecture–based than most small courses and does not involve a ton of discussion between students. I did learn a lot regardless and appreciated feedback from Professor Goldin and our TF, Grace, on my academic ideas. Great experience!
A fantastic junior seminar. This class will make you appreciate economics as a discipline so much more. Also, Claudia is a gem of a professor; if youre an Econ student, dont miss the opportunity to take a class with her.
