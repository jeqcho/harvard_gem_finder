At home I would always respond to my parents' Chinese questions in English, so my speaking was weak and really slow going into this class. After taking it, I have noticed a significant improvement in my speaking abilities, and now I'm pretty comfortable speaking to my parents in Chinese. It's a great, fun, comprehensive class that will guarantee to improve your Chinese if your level is like mine. All grammar and vocab is really strongly consolidated through the 5 classes per week and pretty long weekly homework assignments (these generally took me and others around 4 hours per homework to complete) – and that's a lot of essential and useful grammar structures, since the course covers 12 grammar structures a week. The textbook is also really interesting – the stories aren't dry like you might expect from an introductory course, and there are super insightful Chinese culture nuggets dotted around throughout the textbook. I would really recommend this course if your speaking isn't that good and you want a solid accelerator course to improve your Chinese speaking and reading!
Teachers are super nice, lectures are very interactive/fun but the grammar parts are sometimes confusing (in lecture and on the slideshow), the two quizzes per week sound intimidating but they are pretty easy (they take about five minutes and are the same format every time), homework takes about 2–3 hours
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! I fell in love with Chinese culture, language, people, food, etc. while taking this course because it really does change you. Also if you're an American–Born Chinese student like me who doesn't really know how to read/write Chinese, it's EXTREMELY REWARDING. Knowing now that I can read what my parents text me in Chinese AND I can text back in Chinese is so fulfilling, all thanks to this course. The teachers are amazing, the course material is very well made, and you will not regret taking this course at all. Please do yourself a favor and enroll! It's quite demanding, but that's what makes you fluent faster! It's SO WORTH IT!!!
