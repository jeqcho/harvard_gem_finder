Take this course! Dr. Rubin is amazing and so are the TAs. Great learning experience, reasonable grading and assignments.
Dr. Rubin is a wonderful lecturer and person. He will do so much to try to make your experience in this course a good one. The course material is interesting and you get to hear from some pretty incredible speakers. Unless things change, this course does offer enrollment to MS/MBA students (they comprised probably half of the class this semester) so keep that in mind when it comes to selecting this course and the kind of classroom environment you want to be in.
Beware the final project. Get started on it very very very early. It is a group written 20 page paper. If you get bad teammates (unenthusiastic, unengaged, inexperienced), you will have to write this nearly all yourself.
Talk to the professor and TFs regarding the project. Guidelines are very unclear. In–class feedback practically does not occur. Feedback needs to be taken with a grain of salt though. Conflicting advice is frequently given.
Content is interesting, but frequently hindered by project/quizzes. Take this class only if you have friends also taking it. Perhaps you will get somewhat competent teammates and actually enjoy the class.
