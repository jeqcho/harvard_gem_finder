What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is a great introduction to privacy theory, and the assigned readings are generally interesting (and not *too* long). Assignments are graded quickly and with helpful feedback from the course instructor. Overall, this is a good choice to fulfill the Expos requirement.
You'll have to do a lot of writing (and reading) for this class, so if the subject matter does not interest you it may be harder to motivate yourself to do the writing and otherwise stay engaged with the class. That being said, i think most people would find the readings to be pretty interesting and thought provoking (although they can be long and dense). I'm not interested in CS, tech, or surveillance whatsoever and only took this class because it fit my schedule, but i found that this class forced me to thoroughly engage with an issue (privacy and surveillance) that i had not previously given much thought despite its impact on my daily life. Dr. Sinnott is an amazing, and experienced, instructor who does a great job of providing feedback (especially in writing conferences so make sure to take advantage of that) and interpreting dense readings so that you can understand them. Your success in this class depends on how engaged and committed you are. Also, note that expos 20 (in my opinion) is designed to tweak your writing, not overhaul it, so you may need to lower your expectations. Overall, I'm very happy I took this class despite its difficulty. Not only has my writing improved, I've realized the importance of a revision process, learned the tenets of a cohesive and effective argument and walked away with a greater awareness of surveillance that will change the way I think about privacy and how I act in real life.
Take it if you're interested in tech policy, or anything related!
Make essay feedback holy. Come with plans to revise your essay and be very attentive to any/all feedback.
Privacy and Surveillance is extremely Law/political philosophy – oriented. Be ready for that, esp. in the readings–– which can be lengthy.
