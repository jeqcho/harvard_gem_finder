What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
The lectures are long and relatively dry but very comprehensive with useful notes to look back to later. Comprehensive intro to empirical macro but not much intuition in the first half, which is on methods and is pretty dry.
This is a very helpful class for students interested in macroeconomics, or empirical work in labor economics (with a macro bent).
Take it if you want to work empirically and/or on macro topics!
