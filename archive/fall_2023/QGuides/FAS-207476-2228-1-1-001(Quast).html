Neuro 80 is a really great class. The lectures are pre–recorded so the class times are either an optional review of the week or a guest speaker. I found the optional reviews really helpful. It solidified and clarified my understanding if I had watched the recordings and was a good recap to get the important information if I hadn't watched them. While I found myself slip behind on watching recordings as the semester progressed, I really enjoyed not having to sit through in person lectures. It freed up the time for the guest speakers which I thought were great. The content from the guest speakers was not on tests but it was interesting to see real applications of some of the concepts we had learned about. Each guest had a different specialty and it exposed me to a wide variety of neuroscience related pursuits.
