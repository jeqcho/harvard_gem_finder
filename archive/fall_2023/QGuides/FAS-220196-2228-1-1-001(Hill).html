Future students, TAKE THIS CLASS! Dr. Hill is amazing and what you will read, learn, and discuss is super interesting! I have friends who had to write 10 page essays for their freshmen seminars, or multiple essays throughout the semester, and this class is way more chill. There is one essay at the beginning of the year, one project of your choosing (which is actually super fun) for the midterm, and then for the final you write a letter to your senior self. There are weekly readings, but as long as you do 1 or 2 and can contribute to the class discussions than you're golden. 10/10 highly recommend.
This course provides you with preparation for what becoming an adult means. It is honestly an amazing course 10/10 would recommend .
TAKE THIS CLASS!! Not only is Professor Hill a super fun and understanding teacher, she is also amazing at facilitating discussions around what the class is interested in. Honestly, this class is amazing regardless of how much previous knowledge you have. Professor Hill does a great job at incorporating psychology and theory in everyday experiences so it is very easy to pick up and learn new concepts. Also, if you want to go out for food not only once but TWICE in a semester, and have weekly candy on top of it, this class is for you.

Nancy is fantastic, the content is interesting, and the class is not too difficult. 100% recommend.
