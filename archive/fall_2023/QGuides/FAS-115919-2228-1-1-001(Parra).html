Highly recommend! Class discussions are relevant and low–stress, the teaching pair is excellent and supportive. The community service element is the cornerstone of your experience, so make the most out of your assigned organization! The class gets you out of the Harvard language bubble and into the actual Spanish–speaking world –– which I personally think is the place where we should be speaking Spanish anyway. Assignments are mostly movie notes and short readings, and the essays are brief and centered around the work you're doing with your organization. Find something fun and meaningful for your final project, the second half of the semester is all about your work there.
Take this course! It’s genuinely one of my favorite courses here at Harvard. The volunteering component is so worthwhile and class discussions not only build your fluency but are also very meaningful. The workload is very manageable and Profe Para is amazing!
This is a great course to take if you want an opportunity to really apply the Spanish you're learning. The community service is definitely a time commitment – make sure you have time for 2–3 hours of service and up to an hour of transportation time depending on where you end up volunteering.
