What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Expos 10 gave me a solid base for creating a thesis and developing academic texts. It is an introductory course, do not worry if you do not have previous experience because the professor teaches you the process step by step. Take advantage of the resources available, for example, the one o one conference you have with the professor. Take this course if you think you need to practice your academic writing skills, and want an almost individualized guide from teachers. I do not regret taking Expos 10. I would take it again if I could.
