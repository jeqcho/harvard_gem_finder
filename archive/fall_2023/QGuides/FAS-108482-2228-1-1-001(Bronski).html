This class is a great class to take as a first–year! There is a lot of reading, but if you buy in, the reward is incredibly high. If you are interested in the history of social movements and activism in America, this class is perfect for you. Assignments do not get in the way and do not obscure the real goal of the class, which is to gain an understanding of three of the most impactful social movements in United States history.
The course is better with a good understanding of the readings, even though there are many of them. This will make later assignments much easier. The course staff is so passionate and a great resource to consult with, too! Be creative with the engaged scholarship and do something that speaks to your heart. The class is super valuable, but is definitely one where you get what you put in.
Talk to Professor Bronski, he is an amazing resource.
