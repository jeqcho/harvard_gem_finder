I would highly recommend this class to my peers. The workload is manageable and it is both insightful and interesting. Additionally, the professor is exceptional.
TAKE. THIS. SEMINAR. Professor van Orden is an absolute angel and makes discussions feel easy and inclusive for everyone in the room, regardless of your musical background or lack thereof. All I knew going into this course was that I loved listening to music. I walked out of this course having learned some incredible history behind my favorite songs and artists. Whether you sing, play an instrument, dance, or are horrifically tone deaf but love music — there is something for everyone here. You won’t regret taking this class.
This course is absolutely wonderful. Professor van Orden encourages students to look broadly at the period while still making space for students to explore topics in depth that interest them. The weekly assignments were all interesting, including reading accounts of life in the 60s and listening to music from the time period. You'll learn niche facts about the culture of the 60s, as well as hearing countering perspectives to encourage you not to get caught up in the romanticization of the period. Nothing is off the table in this class, be ready to participate in discussions ranging from song structures to drugs to race relations. Also, the open–concept final project will remain a very memorable experience for me.
Take this class without hesitation. This was by far the highlight of my semester.
You can get a lot out of this class without even putting in that much effort
I would highly recommend this class to future students – it was a really fun, lighthearted, but also deeply interesting course, and I learned so much. There was a lot of flexibility around choices for the final project (either a research paper or a creative project, such as a dance or a song), so it’s an ideal class for creative personalities. The professor was so friendly, and brought fresh fruit for us every single week, which was very kind and generous. Overall, a fantastic class.
