This is one of, if not the best course I've taken at Harvard. Dr. Seymour and Alyssa are phenomenal, the content is easy to digest, and it is a low stress course that emphasizes more on you retaining and learning the information than on memorization and stressful test taking. Please take this class if you're even considering it. Could not recommend more.
I would recommend this course without hesitation. The skills you learn can be applied to any field, not just pre–health careers.
Genuinely one of my favorite classes I've ever taken at Harvard. This was the class I was looking forward to all week. This was the class that woke me up in the morning. The teaching staff were so kind and really fostered an environment in which we could all talk about relevant global health issues openly. This course is also constantly adapting to be as with the times as possible. I learned so much. You simply don't realize how important teeth are/how much you can learn about a person, a society, a country from their teeth until you start to critically think about it.
If you love global health, oral health, or are interested in medicine, this class is for you. Professor Seymour teaches the class with a lot of reflection and application which will help you have a non–traditional learning experience. There is no memorization to this course. If you immerse yourself in conversations, readings, etc. you will really enjoy this class! It builds a very good community too!
This class goes beyond the numbers of Global Health. This class asks you to design solutions and listen to the populations affected by oral diseases. It was an amazing course that I highly recommend.
Take. this. class. The class is very structured and the content is easy to comprehend, which makes it exciting to learn. And don't worry about the fact that the course is primarily about teeth; The class is taught through an oral health lens, but you still learn the fundamentals of overall global health.
Dr.Seymour is super inspiring! She is a great mentor.
TAKE THIS COURSE. Its a great introduction to global health with a dental lens, and it is so easy to do well in. Prof Seymour is also amazing!!
Take this class! You will learn so much about not just teeth but also global health and world development. It will change the way you look at health and policy. Highly recommend to anyone, regardless of your academic background. The workload is very manageable and group projects are fun.
