Comments
The professor reflected a personal connection to history which was embued in his lectures. Every session was important and passionate, and my ability to understand such vast and complex issues in such a short and comprehensive semester is surely attributed to his mastery.
Don't take this course. It's not engaging, mostly because it feels as though the professor doesn't care about teaching it. Bose gives me the impression of being one of those professors who cares much more about their own research than teaching students. The lectures were not engaging, as Bose isn't the best lecturer. Unlike other history classes, it didn't feel like the professor really had a purpose with the way he was presenting the information. There didn't seem to be much pedagogical thought put into it. The vast majority of the readings are written by Bose. There are literally zero primary source readings. Strangely, it felt like you needed prior knowledge to understand this course. It's just not a great class, so don't waste your time by taking it.
