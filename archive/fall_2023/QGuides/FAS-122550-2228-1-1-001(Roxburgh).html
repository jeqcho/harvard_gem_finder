Few courses are taught by faculty who are so actively involved in teaching the course material (in lecture and section) and who devote themselves to getting to know students and their work in the class individually. If you have any interest in the arts at all, this class will give you an incredible overview of many styles of artwork from different time periods. The assignments and sections are designed to be not stressful, and it's fun to just look closely at artwork and continue to draw more and more information and value out of it. TFs also give really thorough and thoughtful feedback.
