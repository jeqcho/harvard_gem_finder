TAKE THIS COURSE, even if you don't have a painting background! It's the most enjoyable course I've taken at Harvard. Matt is such a great professor and you'll learn so much about art (and life in general) from him.
This is a fantastic course. It is very self directed, but assistance is available when needed. There is great time to work in class, and a lot of encouragement to explore your interests and boundaries.
I would tell them that this is a class worth taking but that feedback is vague and minimal, and there’s not a lot of enthusiasm for your art unless it seems like you know exactly what you’re doing.
I enjoyed this class! I'd recommend starting on canvas–making early so the process isn't rushed before paintings are due.
It's a lot of work outside of class to keep up with, so be prepared for the demand of being in the studio to complete paintings.
I would recommend it if you're wanting to take AFVS classes or paint! Just make sure to advocate for yourself if you have questions or need support. And think outside the box :D
