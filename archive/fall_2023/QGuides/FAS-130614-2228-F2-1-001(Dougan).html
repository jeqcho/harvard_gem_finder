What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
While there are no assessments via homework or quizzes, this course requires active participation in discussions each day and thereby a thorough reading of all the assigned papers and figures prior to the class. Unless you already have a background in immunology, this may be time–consuming and challenging.
I think you should definitely take this course if you're interested in cancer immunology, cancer, or immunology. Particularly, it might be helpful for first–year graduate students trying to get their toes wet with actively and critically reading literature.
