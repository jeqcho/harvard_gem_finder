Meng is all one could ever wish for in a writing workshop instructor and more. She cares deeply about helping students improve their writing. She is gentle and kind but also doesn't shy away from expressing constructive feedback––which in many cases was revelatory. Take her workshop if you can!
You will learn so much about yourself as a writer (adapted from my final reflection). We read so many stories diverse in genre, author identity, and style, which led me to finally begin coalescing elements of the stories I wanted to tell. Meng is wonderful –– she encourages you to tease out your writing strengths while conveying areas of opportunity, all without a prescriptive lens.

And truly, all my classmates inspired me to explore beyond my current writing, and I'm so grateful for the supportive and honest writing community we've created.
I have much more confidence in my ability to experiment, and also draw upon my strengths, to surprise myself & others with the stories I have to share!
