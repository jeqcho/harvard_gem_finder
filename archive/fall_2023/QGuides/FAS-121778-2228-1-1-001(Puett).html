This gened will expose you to some really interesting ideas. Puett is a great lecturer (he is sooooo into it which makes the lectures really engaging), he makes the concepts really clear, and the philosophy aspect is so cool. The only wish I had taking this class is that we had more chances to engage with the material ourselves instead of just "learning" it. The essays will have you analyze the texts instead of asking what YOU think about them, if that makes sense. I don't regret taking the class though, it was very enjoyable and I learned a lot. The other thing you should know is it is a pretty easy class. There's a 4 page paper, a 6 page paper, and a final project (can be an 8 pager or a creative/art–based project), and weekly readings, and that's it!
This is an interesting course with a great staff. Good way to knock out a gen ed without too much work
