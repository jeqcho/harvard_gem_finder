That though the assignments and discussion elements mays sound daunting at first, the class is incredible and the instructor will help you if you are struggling. Teacher was extremely available and accommodating and if there is an interest in journalism it is absolutely worthwhile.
This was a great experience all around. The assignments were not too demanding and helped to sharpen my journalistic writing skills. Jill is very knowledgeable about all things journalism and she brings a great background to the table, not to mention all of the contacts she can provide you with to make your written pieces that much better. The guest lecturers brought a great deal of knowledge to the table and really changed my understanding of the journalistic profession for the better. All–in–all, this course is a fantastic one to take, even if you only have a slight interest in politics or journalism but lots of curiosity.
This was my favorite course of the semester! The class was really fun and filled with great people. The course does a great job of teaching you the fundamentals of political reporting and motivating you to become invested in the race that you follow.
The class is essentially one long term project, as you are following one election the entire class. With this in mind, make sure you track a race that will have ample media coverage.
Come into it with an open mind. Be willing to criticise the news you read. And be willing to argue with Jill – she enjoys it and the class needs it!
This course was awesome and VERY worth taking if you get in. It's a lot of work (about a 500 word article each week with a good amount of reporting you'll need to do for each, and longer articles twice in the semester). Jill is super interesting to learn from and has a great perspective, but she also doesn't give super thorough feedback and can be hard to reach outside of class. Overall, a fantastic experience and highly recommend. The class is what you make of it though, so you have to actually put effort in and care about the class in order to get anything out of it.
