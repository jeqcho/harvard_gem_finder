Comments
Take this class if you are interested in programming language semantics. CS 252r is a different class every year, but this year it was a pretty self–directed class. Most of the class was doing presentations and readings. Nada and Ana were great. The class is what you make of it!
This is hands down my favorite class at Harvard. I normally think that most courses' material can be learned on your own, but this becomes less true as a course's topic approaches the frontiers of current research. Then, it is often best to learn through seminars –– this class is precisely that, twice every week!
