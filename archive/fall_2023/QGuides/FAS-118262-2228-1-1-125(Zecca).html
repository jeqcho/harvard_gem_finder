There is a lot of work in the course and assignments, so keep that in mind, but having a close–quarter workshop–style class where you can receive peer editing and close mentorship from your professor is excellent.
Expos 10 is a really helpful course to make you feel extra prepared going into college writing. My preceptor (Dr. Zecca) was also the sweetest and always gave fantastic feedback on all our assignments.
