Comments
TAKE THIS CLASS, its so intersectional in so many different topics (evolutionary biology, feminism, anthropology, african/AA studies, and of course religion). As a conference course, there's a very good mix of short lectures for background context on the readings, and seminar–style discussion. The class itself attracts the most interesting people with different religious focuses different relationships with motherhood, providing a super warm class environment, very inviting of different perspectives. Very interesting and wide–spanning topics in the course across weeks (the syllabus gives a great preview)! Professor Patton and our TF Jessica were amazing, and really care about their students, and about dignifying motherhood.
This course is excellent. As a College student, the Divinity School was a beautiful respite from undergraduate courses. Everybody enrolled was so thoughtful, so kind, so intelligent, and so open to differing opinions and worldviews. Professor Patton is an expert lecturer and discussion leader, and always brought in fascinating case studies, reading materials, and theories for debate. I could not recommend this course highly enough.
