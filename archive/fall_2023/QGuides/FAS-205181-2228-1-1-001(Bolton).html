I 100% recommend this course! Professor Bolton has made my Harvard experience so much better! He is very passionate about the novellas he teaches and is the nicest Professor ever. The course load is not too bad. Every week we have to read a new novella and write a journal entry, but there is more than enough time to do this as the readings are not too long (as they are novellas). Professor Bolton fosters a very welcoming learning environment and the class was very enjoyable. Our seminar also got closer throughout the seminar which made classes more fun.
Professor Bolton is truly a wonderful and genuine professor, and this is a fantastic class. You have the opportunity to read many different novellas throughout the semester, and it's just a very good time!
TAKE IT IF: you get a good group, the class can be a blast. The readings are relatively short, and don't take up that much time, and have been, as it were, generally fun to read. Take it if you want an easy fun class outside of your other ones and have an affinity for literature.
