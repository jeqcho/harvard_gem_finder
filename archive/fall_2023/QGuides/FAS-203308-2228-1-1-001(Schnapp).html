What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you are interested in the ideological/artistic basis of Italian fascism, this is the class for you! This class is very much a case of you get what you put into it. If you invest time, you will learn a great deal of information.
It's a wonderful class with a great professor. The seminars can be long but the readings are quite manageable (make sure to clarify the page requirements for weekly readings!) and always informative. You will learn about fascism, Italian fascist history, Italian history, and more random tidbits from the fields of history, art, architecture, poetry... It is mostly graduate students so if you are an undergraduate do keep that in mind, but the professor is so receptive to undergraduates and flexible with the specifics on their requirements if need be that the experience was a real pleasure.
