French 128 is undoubtedly the best French class I've ever taken and will forever be one of my favorite classes at Harvard. Professor Kim not only helped us to improve our French reading, writing, and speaking skills but also taught us how to reflect critically on our society and how it has shaped us. Each class offered a rare and much appreciated opportunity to slow down and unpack my identity as well as purpose in life. I am also so grateful for the really welcoming and safe environment Professor Kim was able to create that allowed everyone in the class to quickly open up and learn from one another to the fullest. Please take this class!!
TAKE THIS CLASS. Literally the best French /literature class Harvard has to offer. Prof Kim is the most amazing professor she really wants to help you succeed and just generally cares about the well–being of her students. The material is so engaging, Classes do not get better than this, this is why I came to Harvard to take classes like this.
If you have even the slightest wish to take this class, please listen to that voice and take this class. I know that I was definitely intimidated by the syllabus, and even doubted my ability to be able to keep up with the readings. Professor Kim, however, makes sure that every one in the class feels confident in their abilities, and by the end of the semester, you will be amazed at how much you have been able to read, and how much your skills as a student of literature have progressed! The seminar format is perfect for group discussions, where you will ask questions, learn from your peers and your professor, and make wonderful memories. Professor Kim is absolutely incredible and someone you will be so glad to know. TAKE THIS CLASS! YOU WILL NOT REGRET IT!
This class is absolutely worth taking, especially if you are wanting to continue French but can't seem to find an exciting option!
