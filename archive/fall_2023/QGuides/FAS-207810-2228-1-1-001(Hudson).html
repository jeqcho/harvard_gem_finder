What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Some of the readings are a little heavy, but overall this class was a fantastic dive into the life of Cicero.
This is a good class when people talk. There is a good amount of reading, which people don't really end up doing, which leads to people talking less. I'm not sure how it was other years, but if a class strikes that balance effectively, I'm sure it can be wonderful. Jared Hudson is an INCREDIBLY kind person, and very very smart, although not the most engaging much of the time.
If you have an interest in the ancient world you should definitely take this seminar. Professor Hudson is a great teacher and provides a great curriculum.
For anyone interested in classics or the late republic, I highly recommend this class. For anyone not interested in either of those two things, I still recommend the class, but a bit less highly. Professor Hudson is incredibly passionate about the topic and extremely knowledgeable. I really enjoyed my meetings with him throughout the semester, and hearing all that he had to say about the topic. The class is very reading heavy, but besides that, there is practically no work besides the two essay assignments. Even if you don't have experience with the topic, you will be okay because Professor Hudson does a great job of helping you immerse yourself in Roman society.
