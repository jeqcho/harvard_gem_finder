What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this course if you'd like an opportunity to develop your writing skills and grow as a writer! This is a great place, including for beginners.
If you're interested in writing creative nonfiction, this is a great course! Not only will you read selections from notable writers in the genre, but you'll also produce your own long form piece that has been refined over the course of the semester. I found this course to be a great change–of–pace from my other classes because creative writingis such a unique academic discipline, and it really helped me to avoid burnout from my other work. Highly recommend!
This class has been one of the best I've here at Harvard. I am glad I got the opportunity to be in this workshop and learn and write alongside the most creative and interesting people. Darcy was a fantastic instructor, and through each weekly meeting I felt myself getting more comfortable with writing about myself and things that have happened in my life. This course changes your perspective on what matters in life, what's worth remembering and retelling, and how you choose to remember and retell. I think everyone should take this course as it pushes you to think introspectively about your past experiences and puts you in an environment where it's okay to feel vulnerable and speak and write about uncomfortable topics. Having classmates and Darcy give feedback on your writing is very rewarding, and you also learn just as much from your own writing as you do from the work of your peers.
This class made me such a better writer, and was truly a joy to be a part of. I cannot recommend it enough!
This is my favorite course I have ever taken at Harvard. The professor, Darcy Frey, is lovely – he is open to hearing your thoughts, work out a piece with you, and even encourages you to go for publishing afterwards, if you would like. I really feel like I learned a lot about creative nonfiction writing through both reading and the workshop discussion period. The class really held my hand through writing a piece I am incredibly proud of, thanks to the help of Darcy and my peers. Take this course, even if you're not sure you love writing.
Take this class!! You will have many opportunities to experiment with concepts, styles, chronology, dialogue, and more. Writing your longer piece will be almost as rewarding as reading your peers' pieces. This is a fabulous and well–organized workshop, and I would recommend this to anyone.
Take this class!!! Students of all concentrations, ages, experience levels, even colleges were in this course, so it really is open to anyone to apply, and Id take it over again in an instant
Please take this class! Darcy is phenomenal and you will get so close to the other people in your section. I looked forward to coming to class each week and wished that it had lasted longer. You will get such great feedback on your writing as well as such great inspiration from the stories that you read. I am neither an English concentrator nor a secondary so I took it for fun, and I have no regrets. All types of writers welcome!
Try it!!
