What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class was fantastic, and I would absolutely recommend it to anyone with any form of art background. This class really pushed me out of my comfort zone as a painter, and I learned to think about how the materials and process of production can help craft an artistic message. The teaching staff is super helpful and available, and they made me feel supported as I made artistic choices that were outside of my comfort zone.
Abdi is a great teacher and a wonderful person in general. If you want to deepen or develop your painting or multimedia art–making practice, take this course!
I don't know if this class will be offered in the future, but if it is, you should definitely take it (especially if you enjoy painting). Don't think you have to be an AFVS concentrator to take intermediate studio courses. Some of the most impressive work I've seen in this class came from CS concentrators.
This is so much more than a "painting" class. This is a mind–opening, limit–stretching, creative–juicing type of class. So many tools and tricks at your disposal and a great teaching staff to inspire you and help you learn to use those fancy materials. Definitely, a class you should take!
Take this class! But be ready to do things that aren’t traditional painting (like sewing and printmaking) as a significant portion of your projects.
