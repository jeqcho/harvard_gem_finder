Definitely take this class before you graduate! Your public speaking will noticeably improve. It's so fun to get to know your classmates and hear their stories.
Expos 40 is such a great class. Especially during the later parts of the semester, I was so excited for class every day. It's such a fun group of students and I highly recommend this class for the skills you learn and how fun it is!
TAKE THIS CLASS IF YOU GET IN! I cannot emphasize enough how much this class has made such an impact on my experience here at Harvard. EXPOS 40 is a great opportunity to practice speaking in a supportive, non–judgmental environment, and you gain so much valuable feedback from your peers and preceptor (which I think is the most valuable part of the class). Throughout the class, you also participate in a bunch of fun improv exercises that allow you to get out of your comfort zone and try things in speech that you haven't done before. Don't be scared of the speeches––that's why you're most likely interested in the class, and this is a class where the main goal is to give you opportunities to practice speaking (so don't be afraid to try new things in each of your speeches). And don't be worried about the grade––if you show that you are making a valiant effort and are engaged, the grade will work out for itself. Zach is amazing and really chill, so if you get into his section consider yourself really lucky!
I wish every student had the opportunity to take this course. You learn so much about yourself, but also your abilities about giving speeches and how you can apply Expos 40 skills to day to day things like a project presentation or a job interview. I wish every class at Harvard utilize the Expos 40 mentality: that in a fun and excepting environment that still challenges you, you learn more than just simply memorizing facts and dumping them onto two exams in a semester.
I loved this class and strongly recommend it, but I had amazing classmates and an amazing preceptor (Zach). I really believe that your peers/preceptor dictate your experience in the class, and I probably wouldn't have had the same experience if my class didn't have the same energy.
YES YES YES take this class if you can! A highlight of my senior fall. You learn public speaking skills but you also learn so much from the people in your class. Would highly recommend!
