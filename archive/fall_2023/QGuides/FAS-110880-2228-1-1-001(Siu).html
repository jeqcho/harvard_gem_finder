Do not take this course unless you're reasonably certain you want to do grad school in math. It is very, very hard, but also very rewarding if you put in the effort.
This course is very difficult. I think that unless you are extremely solid on complex analysis, you should not take any other math courses with this class –– expect this class to take all of your time. I made the mistake of taking two other math classes, which substantially affected my ability to learn the material. The benefit of taking the course, however, it that you will learn from Prof. Siu, a world–class mathematician, whose understanding of complex analysis is unbelievably deep.
