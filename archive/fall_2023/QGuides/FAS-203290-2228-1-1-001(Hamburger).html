There are good classes and then there are classes that change the way you think about the world. If you want the latter experience, take this class.
I would tell them to be as proactive as possible about finding a research topic, because the great joy of the course is to have Prof. Hamburger advising the paper. His conception of the diagrammatic is capacious and imaginative, and this comes out most clearly in research–focused conversations with him about how to build the project into something unique. The sooner they can do that, the happier they will be!
