This course is awesome, and everybody who has a chance to take it should take it!!!! It really helped me concretely improve my writing and expanded my horizons in terms of what I thought was possible with academic writing. Highly recommend!!!
Take this course! It is the most useful course I've taken at Harvard and allowed me to workshop my thesis writing in a community of critical and thoughtful readers and scholars. Yes it is a challenge to write at times, but it is so worth it.
I highly recommend this class, especially for history concentrators who are writing a thesis. This class is a great opportunity to work on the craft of historical writing in a very intentional way. You will both be introduced to new kinds of writing and have the opportunity to try them. All in all, a great course.
I was truly sorry when the course ended, because it was such a positive and motivating force in my semester. I not only felt like my approach to writing history was transformed, but I thoroughly enjoyed every class meeting. Prof. Jasanoff offers incredible insight into the craft of writing and is an exceptional facilitator of discussion. If you have the opportunity to take the class, you absolutely should.
I would highly recommend this class. Professor Jasanoff is incredible; she facilitated some of the best class discussions I've experienced, the design of the class is extremely thoughtful, and the feedback I received from both the professor and my peers was very helpful. I loved the class!
