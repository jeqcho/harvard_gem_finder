This course is great if you have a strong interest in startups and (even if you are in the ORB section, which is designed for people without startup ideas) some idea of a startup you'd like to pursue. In the ORB section, you are quickly asked to come up with an idea––with some help––and start working on it, so it's important that you care about what you're working on. Unless the course is restructured, I wouldn't recommend taking the course without a basic concept for a startup.

This was an awesome class and you will be introduced to REALLY cool people throughout the semester and join a network of brilliant peers in the process. This helps you really get your idea off the ground and connect with lots of qualified people to help you do so.
