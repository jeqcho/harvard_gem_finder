What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you are considering whether or not to write a thesis, I strongly recommend. The process is challenging but very rewarding and you will be very happy that you did. Find a topic that interests you and as you read more you will keep finding aspects of the topic that interest you. You will learn so many important life skills through the process.
It's a great opportunity to explore something that is really interesting to you
Write as you research!
The only reason you would take this course is if you're writing a thesis. It is not easy, and writing for such a long time is really challenging. Choose an advisor that cares about you and wants to see you succeed, and is also willing to put in time to meet with you as much or little as you want. My advisor and I meet almost every week, and that works for me, so try to know what you want before going into this.
Writing a thesis is a super rewarding experience, and I'm grateful to Dr. Baker for helping to walk us through it.
