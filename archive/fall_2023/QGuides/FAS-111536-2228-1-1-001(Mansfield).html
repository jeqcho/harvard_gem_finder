This class is unlikely to be offered again because of Mansfield's retirement. That said, the subject was pretty interesting, though very niche. I was a bit disappointed this class involved no student discussion as it might've appeared on paper; it was just Mansfield talking through his Swiftian musings, some plausible and others much less so, to a seminar–sized audience. As someone with very limited political philosophy background, I didn't take away that much from this class, but for someone with more prior experience, this could be a fulfilling deep–dive.
This is a very personal class with Professor Mansfield. If you enjoy him as a professor, you will like this class.
