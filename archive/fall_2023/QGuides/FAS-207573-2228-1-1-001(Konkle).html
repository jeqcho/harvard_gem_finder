Amazing course! Highly recommend. fMRI was made comprehendible even if you have no previous knowledge of the technique! I had no background of fMRI and I now feel like I know so much and could design many types of studies. This is definitely a methods focused class, so do not expect to learn about findings using fMRI, rather how fMRI works and how to design the studies themselves. Super useful if you are planning to do fMRI research in the future. Weekly quizzes are for you to test your knowledge on the week's material. Lots of the assignments in this class are mostly for you to test your knowledge and they are intended for you to learn rather than the be tested on. You will work through an fMRI paper and analyze its design in class, and you will also be able to apply concepts you learn in class on BrainVoyager software and have the chance to work with real brain data! You will also get to explore topics on your own when you do your presentation, which is focused on a teaching the technique to another person. Great class overall!
Dr. Konkle is deeply passionate about fMRI and that comes across through her teaching. You will absolutely learn the fundamentals of fMRI and more, which is really useful is you want to do a senior thesis using fMRI
