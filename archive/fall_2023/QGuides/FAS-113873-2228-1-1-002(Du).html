Not a light workload and lots of class time, but less difficult if you come in with some background. Probably more rewarding if you come in with less (or no) background as this class is wonderfully run and very well–structured, and what's taught is very applicable to everyday conversations. Teachers are wonderful, but be prepared to work (as it is with all language classes). Grading is pretty nice.
This class is a ton of fun, very engaging and educational, but unless you have a good knowledge of Mandarin coming in, do not expect this to be an easy lift. However, I will say that the teachers are pretty generous and kind when it does come to grading.
Be prepared to spend quite a bit of time studying outside of class if you hope to know the material and be successful!
Take this class! You will definitely have to be willing to commit a couple hours a week to keep up with the course material and stay on top of the vocabulary, but it is 100% worth it. The amount of Chinese that I learned in just one semester was more than what I learned after 4 years of high school. I highly recommend taking this if you are curious about Chinese language and culture.
If you are interested in learning Chinese, now is the time. Looked forward to class everyday with the awesome learning environment and class participation in learning the new vocabulary, grammar, and playing small games to learn the language even better. If you prepare for class by reading the relevant lesson in the textbook you will be more than fine and beyond that just keeping up with the homework and the lessons each week will essentially guarantee you a good grade, there are no surprises and everything is weighted very equally. Take this course!!
