What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
If you have any interest in American politics, you should definitely take this class! Professor Skocpol is incredibly knowledgeable so you learn something new every single week from her. She brings such an important, unique perspective. Each week builds upon the previous week so you want to stay on top of your readings to make sure you're getting the most out of the class. The readings are heavy, but they are all super useful and interesting that you should try to read as much as possible. The more prepared I was for lecture, the more fun I had participating in discussions and the more knowledge I got out of lecture. Use the final research paper as an opportunity to explore a niche interest of yours.
This is the best Gov class I've taken so far at Harvard, Professor Skocpol is obviously extremely knowledgable about American politics, and her leadership of the class is amazing. I learned something every single week that changed my perspective on American politics, and the small–size of the class allows you to truly share your thoughts and hear others.
I loved this class and Professor Skocpol! She's so knowledgeable and her passion for the topics we discuss is clear. She has so many fascinating stories especially about her time researching the Tea Party that you'll spend a portion of class time just listening to her which is where I got the most out of the class. There are a lot of readings for each week but you don't need to read all of them or entire books – skim the most important parts. The prospectus and research paper you write are lengthy but because you have to work on them throughout the semester they're more manageable and Professor Skocpol provides good feedback.
I would truly recommend this class! Professor Skocpol is iconic for a reason. She is truly so knowledgeable and has so much first–hand experience with the subject material and readings. It's definitely a time commitment and not a breeze. Prof. Skocpol does not accept ANY late assignments. The syllabus has EVERYTHING. Read it over before applying. But, it was very rewarding and one of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard so far!
This class isn’t easy and definitely requires thoughtful consideration of the material outside of class, but it’s so rewarding, especially the individual research component. Prof. Skocpol is also an utter gem and truly a joy to talk to both during class and outside.
This class is by far among my favorites that Iv'e taken here at Harvard! Be aware, reading load can be quite intense, but it is definitely worth it!
