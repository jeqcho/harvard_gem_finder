Graham Allison is a legend.
Take this class if you get in! Can seem intimidating because it's very competitive but you learn so much and it really gets easier after the first few weeks. Definitely a lot of reading and work, but so worth it. I learned SO much from the amazing professors and incredible guest speakers. One of the classes that makes you want to go to Harvard. Take it!!!
This course is the best overlap of practical and theoretical skills. There is no reason to not take it (aside for maybe grading, it’s pretty tough). Professor Allison is a living legend and you’ll never find a more knowledgeable person in the entire world. It’s like uncovering the world’s secrets every week.
Best course I have taken during my time at Harvard. Highly recommend to anyone interested in foreign policy or national security!
If you are able to, take the course!! It is one of the reasons we are at a school like Harvard. You do not have to be committed to a life in government, it is still useful in understanding the world and learning how to write in a way that gets all of your thoughts across coherently.
This is an excellent course. It is certainly challenging; the professors expect not only your writing but also your national security thinking to be operating at a high level, and anecdotally, many graduate students and mid–career folks also found it challenging. It's certainly not your average undergraduate class, and you're expected to come up with ideas and solutions that are more comprehensive and thought–through than most college classes. However, it's also an incredible opportunity to take what you've learned in more theoretical academic settings and/or extracurricular and professional experiences and apply it to practical problems under accomplished professionals. Professors Allison, Sanger, and Reveron have a wealth of experience that they share with you, and they supplement their own knowledge with a ton of incredible guests. I came away from the class with a better foundation for professional writing and clear thinking along with career inspiration that I know I'll take with me far into the future.
Take it if you are interested in foreign affairs especially while it is still taught by Prof Allison and David Sanger. You'll make great friends and change your understanding of how the world works
