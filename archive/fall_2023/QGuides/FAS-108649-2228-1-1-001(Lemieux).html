What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
More of a do–it–on–your–own class. Only 3 big deadlines so knowing how to do your work in class and set your own pace is important. Very fun class if you just love the process of making art by yourself since everyone else is doing something completely different.
Annette will not be directive at all––she provides very limited feedback and she seems impossible to please. Take this class if you want to make whatever you want––this will come at the cost of an art–making community led by a professional artist. Our class at to find our own way basically outside of class time.
This class is really amazing if you have sculpture or 3–dimensional art ideas that you want to make as you have the creative freedom to do whatever you want around interesting artistic peers. However, I would say that this class, at least when I took it, felt a bit awkward and unsocial. While the teachers were helpful with fulfilling the ideas of the students, there was often not a very encouraging and supportive vibe, as well as a feeling of disconnect between the teachers and students, which made the class not the most comfortable sometimes, however, it was still quite an amazing and unique class.
This class is very unstructured so you get plenty of opportunity to pursue the projects you want to explore.
