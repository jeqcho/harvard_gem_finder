What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course profoundly impacted me. Teju Cole not only teaches creative writing and analyzing photos, but how to learn and how to speak with intention. The class quickly became my most valued of the semester for how inspired I would leave the classroom, and how the reading, writing, and discussions were fruitful and enriching. I dove deeply into my own personal life with my projects, as did many in the class. It was beautiful to witness the growth of the confidentiality, trust, and connections among the students and the professor as we dealt with difficult subjects through the making of art and conversation.
Take this class!! It's an amazing opportunity to come in every week and read the incredible work of peers and noteworthy photographers/writers. The blend of writing and photography in the course is excellent. The writing assignments are great as well.
