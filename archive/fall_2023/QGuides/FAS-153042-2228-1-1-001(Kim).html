This was a wonderful fulfilling class. Come in with a group if you can. Ken is a great, nice, and encouraging coach. Chamber music is fun! I loved watching my peers perform as well.
If you are interested in making chamber music at a high level, there is no better opportunity to do so than in this class. The Parker String Quartet and Katherine Chi are phenomenal coaches (both musically and personally), and I've made some of my best friends through this course. Do not hesitate at all in enrolling if you are even slightly interested in chamber music, although do keep in mind that what you take from this class is how much you put in.
This class is an unmatched experience to hone practical performance skills, work with world–class musicians in the Parker Quartet, meet inspiring peers, and commit to sizable musical projects.
Even if you have not done chamber music before but are a dedicated instrumentalist, you should take 189r! One of the most beautiful things about making music is doing it together. 189r is a great opportunity to step away from studying and create art with other passionate musicians, and is very accessible to first–time chamber players!
I've said it before, I'll say it again. Best course at Harvard.
This class is a true joy and always a highlight of my semester. Take this for wonderful, thoughtful teaching and music.
One of the best classes at Harvard! The Quartet is fantastic and you will not regret it if you put together a good group and work hard – so satisfying :)
“Our goal in this class is to have fun and enjoy the process of making music together, as equals, and grow. Everything else is just logistics.”

This is what was said to us on day 1. And it was an incredible semester.

I've made friends who are some of the kindest, most perceptive, caring, curious, and interesting people I know. We thought about music. We learned about the music and in working towards a common goal, learned about each other, and I understood myself better.

The larger world may be chaotic, but it has never looked so beautiful as it does in that moment when you’re together and creating something larger than your individual selves.

The Parker Quartet is so cool. I don't know how they're able to sculpt such brilliant music. This was a great experience.
