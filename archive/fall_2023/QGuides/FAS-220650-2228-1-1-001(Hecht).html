My favorite class I've taken at Harvard so far!!
Take this course!! Seriously the best class I’ve ever taken. Erin is incredible and so knowledgeable about the field. I learned so much about dogs and animal behavior. If you want a truly fun class or are at all interested in animal behavior testing and research (or just really like dogs) I highly recommend.

Take this course! It was lotteried this time around, so make sure to take it when you get the chance! You learn so much and get to work with dogs too! Erin and Sophie are the best!
Amazing course!
The subject matter was fantastic, as well as the way the workload was distributed. We had to lottery to get in, and Erin and Sophie picked 12 of us who were genuinely interested in the topic and made the class super engaging and high level. My classmates generated a ton of enthusiasm for the course, and Erin and Sophie were there whenever we wanted to go off on a tangent and speculate about whatever was on our minds. This was the first class I had that had such an intimate seminar component to it, and, when coupled with the lab, I actually felt as though I a) learned something and b) took pride in what my entire class had accomplished. The class is for sure a gem, but it's the first "easy" class I've taken at Harvard that I actually took something away from because the subject was so interesting and the work was so low pressure. Very unique learning environment, and I bragged to all of my friends about how lucky I was to be apart of it this year.
10/10 take this class. Very little work and a lot of rewards. I loved getting to know the professors because they are so kind, informative, and understanding. Also, it's the only class at Harvard where you will get to play with dogs, haha. I wish more people could have the opportunity to take this class because it was wonderful.
Genuinely, this class was great! An absolute gem. Erin and Sophie are so supportive and I have learned so much in this class. Not too much work but when there is work, it is so interesting so it's really not that overwhelming.
