Take this course!!! Professor Madigan is an amazing professor, and this course will really engage you deeper into the aspects of the Holocaust that most schools don't teach you previously. Especially being able to have conversations with other students interested in the subject, this course will give you a much more "hands–on" experience than I have had with the Holocaust before. Many of the movies and books we read were fascinating, and I would recommend this course with total confidence.
This class allows you to engage with valuable resources that cause you to establish various emotional connections. You cannot hide from the truth as you begin to put faces to the Holocaust. I am incredibly grateful for the opportunity to take this class. It is an excellent opportunity to engage with a challenging subject area in a rewarding manner. With the class based on discussion, it is also valuable to debate, reflect, and come to conclusions on difficult areas that test your thought process in multiple areas. The Professor is incredible at stimulating discussions and providing insightful comments and resources that maximize your experience.
Absolutely take this class! The number of hours per week in previous Q guide reports scared me a little bit at first, but I am so glad that I chose to be a part of this Freshman Seminar. Professor Madigan is wonderful, and you will learn a lot about the Holocaust from this class, so if that is something that interests you, then you should definitely take it! The readings can be heavy and numerous at times but don't be afraid to use summaries when needed, and as long as you take notes and participate in class, you will be fine.
Professor Madigan is the BEST. Take this seminar. The materials are all super engaging and meaningful. Work load is very minimal.
