What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I would tell future students that this class does take time but is very effective.
This class is well suited for the student who wishes to get the basics of writing down and the student who is a good writer but wishes to further strengthen and improve their writing. It does require some work, however doing that work will benefit you in the end as it works towards the essays at the end of each of the three units this course consists of.
I would say that this is a great class, but it is tedious. Also, the quality and engagement might depend on your Professor, too. It varies.
If you are not sure about your writing skills, take this class.
