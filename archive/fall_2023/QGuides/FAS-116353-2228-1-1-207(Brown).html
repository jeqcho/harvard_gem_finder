This is a WONDERFUL class, definitely take it! Compared to other Expos classes, this has a much more manageable workload. The class is super engaging, and you'll get to study niche things about Boston and Massachusetts history that you might at first have not thought was interesting. The class is also super discussion–based and focused on critical thinking, but not so heavily reliant on scholarly articles such that it detracts from the actual conversation. Take this!
If you love history, you'll find the theories and content fascinating! Overall, the content is intriguing and the teacher is very receptive of whatever her students voice.
Willa is a wonderful instructor. You can see how passionate she is about history and writing, and she inspires that enthusiasm in all her students. Willa is an incredibly engaging and effective teacher. The final paper was also very fun to write.
This is an excellent choice for an expos class. It was interesting, different, and engaging. It taught me to think about history in an entirely new light, as well as write proficiently in two styles.
