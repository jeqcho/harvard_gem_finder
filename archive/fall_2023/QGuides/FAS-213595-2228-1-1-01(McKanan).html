I think this is a really good chance to get an exposure to what the divinity school has to offer. It's an approachable class on an interesting topic. The point based grading system I really like as it makes your target clear. I took it pass/fail though and that made it a much more enjoyable, low pressure experience
This is an incredibly well–put–together and interesting class and I would highly recommend it with one reservation. The difficulty level of the course is a bit high. It's nothing ridiculous but I would highly recommend taking the course pass/fail if you are unfamiliar with the topic and do not want to devote a lot of time to the subject matter. When taken pass/fail, the course is a bit more relaxed and you can focus more on the content of the readings and the discussions without worrying as much about your grade or completing tons of assignments. One of the most interesting and unique courses I've taken at Harvard!
