Overall, this course was taught extremely well. Despite the difficulty of the content, it was well taught and I felt prepared to do well on exams and the final. It does require a decent amount of time to be put into psets and studying, but the psets and classes prepare you well for the exams and for understanding the content intuitively instead of memorizing formulas. This class greatly increased my problem solving ability and I would recommend taking it for sure.
Math 21a is great! Definitely the best of the introductory math courses. As wish those courses, section makes all the difference! So don't just "shop" for a TF, "shop" also for a section where you comfortably able to collaborate with others. I highly recommend forming a pset group as well, since this is a great way to complete the psets in an efficient and accurate manner.
Get into Roderic's section. Best TF out there. And find a quality group to pset with because that is the best way to get better at this class!
