What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Do the readings early
Professor Colton is clearly the biggest strength of this course. He is super knowledgeable, kind, adaptable and a great mentor to have. The class is also well formed – a good mix of students, with different backgrounds and opinions. It’s a very discussion–heavy class, which I find to be the best way to develop one’s knowledge on the subject. Be prepared to start the final paper early, ask for feedback in October regarding your topic and start sketching a plan. It is rather overwhelming if you try to do it in a week or so.
The course comes very timely given the events unfolding in the region. It would be particularly useful for those interested in broader post–Soviet region but also those studying international relations, regionalism, and security.
