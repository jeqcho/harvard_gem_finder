Description:

The study of functions and their rates of change. Fundamental ideas of calculus are introduced early and used to provide a framework for the study of mathematical modeling involving algebraic, exponential, and logarithmic functions. Thorough understanding of differential calculus promoted by year long reinforcement.  

There will be required workshops Tuesdays.

Course Notes:

This is a lecture course taught in small sections. Please see the course site for section times and instructions on how to submit your time preferences. This course, when taken together with Mathematics Mb, can be followed by Mathematics 1b. Mathematics Ma and Mb together cover all the material in Mathematics 1a (and more).
