Description:

The war in Ukraine has dominated the news to the point where we risk becoming numb to its horror and significance. This course will discuss the causes and wider global consequences of the war. It will ensure you understand what is happening in Ukraine and Russia while challenging you to explore the difficult issues raised by the war. Students with basic or extensive knowledge of the region will benefit from the lectures, guest speakers, and activities. The course will be geared towards undergraduates, with an option for graduate students to do additional work for graduate credit.
