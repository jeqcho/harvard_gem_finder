Description:

This seminar introduces the methodology and practice of translating German into English. By working through the fundamental issues, strategies, and ethical responsibilities of the translator, students will encounter the delights and dilemmas of mediating between two different languages and cultures. Workshops address specific linguistic and cultural challenges across a range of text types, including journalistic, scientific, technical, literary, and consumer-orientated texts. Special attention is given to the many challenges of translation, such as audience and purpose, literal and connotative meaning, semantic and cultural distinctions, dialect, social and tonal registers, as well as issues of subtitling and dubbing.
