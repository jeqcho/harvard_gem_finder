Description:

In this course, students will continue to develop intermediate-level competence in spoken and written Persian. Emphasis is on reading comprehension, grammar, and writing, as well as cultural and historical knowledge. Through pop songs, modern Persian poetry, short stories, and Iranian movies, students will continue to develop their proficiency in intermediate-level Persian. Persian will be the primary language of the class. The prerequisite for this course is Persian BA (Intermediate Persian I).  

Course Notes:

Not open to auditors. Cannot be taken pass/fail.
