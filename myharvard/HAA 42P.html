Description:

The development of European architecture through the cathedral’s construction, transformation, and restoration in dialogue with its changing urban context, as well as debates over its reconstruction following the fire of 2019.

Class Notes:

An end-of-semester, post-examination period (May 4–13) excursion to Paris (May 14–23) is planned as part of this course. Travel, accommodation, site admissions, and some meal expenses will be paid by the HAA excursion fund. An earlier departure will only be considered if it does not interfere with any enrollee’s exams. Enrollment is capped at 15. Please submit a paragraph alongside your petition outlining why you are interested in taking the course and specifying whether you intend to participate in the excursion (which is not a requirement). Petitions should without exception be submitted no later than Monday, January 16th. Petitions will be approved by the end of day on Wednesday, January 18th.
