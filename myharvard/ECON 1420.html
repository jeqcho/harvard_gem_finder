Description:

Analyzes major issues in American economic policy, including national savings, taxation, health care, Social Security, budget policy, monetary and fiscal policy, and exchange rate management. Current economic issues and policy options are discussed in detail and in the context of current academic thinking.

Course Notes:

Prerequisites: Econ. 10a and 10b; API-101; or permission of instructor. Also offered by the Economics Department as Ec 1420. Please note, this is a jointly offered course hosted by another Harvard school and, accordingly, students must adhere to the academic and attendance policies of that school.
