Description:

This intensive seven week course in effective writing focuses on the strategies and techniques behind great persuasive writing in both politics and policy.  Unlearn dull academic writing and develop a writing process that leads to publication and persuasive power. We focus on narrative and image, voice and urgency--applied to opeds, advocacy pieces, reviews, and personal essays.
