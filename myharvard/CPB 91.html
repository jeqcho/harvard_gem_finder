Description:

91 is research for credit. It cannot be taken as a fifth course. To be eligible to enroll, you must have a Harvard-affiliated principal investigator agree to mentor you for the semester. For this reason, students must reach out to labs and interview with labs ahead of the start of the semester. Students are expected to work an average of 15 hours/week during term time. Please note, this course is only open to CPB concentrators.

Course Notes:

Limited to CPB concentrators. Students must have secured a position in a laboratory prior to enrolling in the course; the instructor will verify this with the faculty sponsor. Ordinarily may not be taken as a fifth course. Students must complete basic laboratory safety training and other safety training required by the host lab prior to starting work.
