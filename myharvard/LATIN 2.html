Description:

Latin 2 continues from Latin 1. Participants will continue to develop their ability to read Latin with increasing emphasis on literary texts.

Course Notes:

Students wishing to continue after Latin 2 should proceed to Latin 3, which concludes the normal introductory sequence. No auditors. May be taken pass/fail (undergraduates) or SAT/UNSAT (graduate students) with instructor permission.
