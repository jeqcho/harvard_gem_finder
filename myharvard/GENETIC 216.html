Description:

This course covers different topics in gene regulation, covering genetic, genomic, biochemical, and molecular approaches. A small number of topics are discussed in depth, using the primary literature. Topics range from prokaryotic transcription to eukaryotic development.

Course Notes:

Offered jointly with the Medical School as GN 703.0.

Recommended Prep:

BCMP 200 and Genetics 201. All students taking Genetics 216 should read and be prepared to discuss the papers for the first meeting. The readings can be downloaded from the course website.
