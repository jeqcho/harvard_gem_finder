Description:

The course is focused on neural structures and mechanisms mediating the detection, localization and recognition of sounds as well as speech production and perception. General principles are conveyed by theme discussions of cellular and circuit mechanisms of perception and plasticity within the central auditory system, human auditory cortex, pitch and auditory scene analysis, speech, cochlear implants and language.
