Description:

91 is research for credit. It cannot be taken as a fifth course. To be eligible to enroll, you must have a Harvard-affiliated principal investigator agree to mentor you for the semester. For this reason, students must reach out to labs and interview with labs ahead of the start of the semester. Students are expected to work an average of 15 hours/week during term time. Please note, this course is only open to MCB concentrators or those pursuing an MCB secondary.
