Description:

This course centers on the rehearsal and full production of the play Black Girl Nerd by Tori Sampson. Black Girl Nerd is an absolutely fun, totally wild, and wickedly funny new play with a subtitle that says what it's all about ..."One Nappy-headed girl's Superhero Journey to understanding her Blackness!" A brand-new, whiplashing contemporary comedy that shifts from auto-fiction to stand up to original game show -- this play is youthful and unapologetic in its love for superhero comic books and Black girl nerds. This is the newest, unproduced play by Sampson, a Boston-born Yale Drama School graduate, NY Times Critic's Pick, and Susan Smith Blackburn Prize-nominated playwright and screenwriter.

The course component of this Production Studio will place Sampson’s work in the broader context of contemporary Black theater in America, and will explore intersectional connections between comedy, media and experimental theater.
