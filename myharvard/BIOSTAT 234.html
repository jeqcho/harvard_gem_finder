Description:

Introduction to important computational problems in biostatistics and state-of-the-art algorithms for solving them. The course focuses on the implementation of data structures and algorithms to solve problems of practical relevance.

Class Notes:

BST 221 is the Applied section of Data Structures and Algorithms. It is not open to cross-registrants. Any non-HSPH students who are interested in this class may petition to enroll in BIOSTAT 234: Introduction to Data Structures and Algorithms (FAS). BIOSTAT 234 is the Theoretical section of Data Structures and Algorithms and is geared toward PhD students.
