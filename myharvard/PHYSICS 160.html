Description:

 

Quantum mechanics is the fundamental principle of the microscopic world. Quantum mechanics allows us to understand the motion of electrons, atoms and molecules. Only with such understanding, we can rationally design and engineer quantum materials, in order to realize quantum technologies such as quantum information, quantum sensing, and quantum computation. In this class, you will learn the fundamental postulates of quantum mechanics and their implications for the structure and behavior of atoms and molecules. In particular, we will explore the mathematical framework behind molecular bonding, vibration, and rotation. We will also discuss how to probe the properties of atoms and molecules using tunable electromagnetic radiation, more commonly known as light!
