Description:

Continuation of Japanese 120a. Approximately 150 additional Chinese characters.  Second-year intermediate level course aimed at consolidation of the basic grammatical patterns of Japanese and development of reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills to the level necessary for communication in everyday life in Japanese society.

Recommended Prep:
